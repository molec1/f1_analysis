<p>The <b>2008 European Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>LII Telefónica Grand Prix of Europe</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 24 August 2008 at the <a href="/wiki/Valencia_Street_Circuit" title="Valencia Street Circuit">Valencia Street Circuit</a>, <a href="/wiki/Valencia,_Spain" class="mw-redirect" title="Valencia, Spain">Valencia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>. It was the 12th&#160;race of the <a href="/wiki/2008_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2008 Formula One season">2008 Formula One season</a>. The 57-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> team after starting from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>. <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> finished second in a <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a> car, with <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> third in a <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a>.
</p><p>Massa started on pole position, with Hamilton second, and Kubica third. Massa got cleanly away, while Hamilton fought hard to fend off Kubica, but managing to keep second. The top three remained the same through the first round of pit stops, and after the first stop, Massa took a large lead over Hamilton. In Massa's second pit stop he was released straight into the path of <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a>, and had to back off to avoid a collision. The stewards announced that they would investigate the incident after the race. They later ruled that Ferrari would be fined <a href="/wiki/Euro" title="Euro">€</a>10,000 for the pit lane incident, but that Massa would keep his victory, as although the release was unsafe, no sporting advantage had been gained. Massa went on to take the victory, with Hamilton second and Kubica third. Hamilton's closest rival in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> going into the race, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>, failed to finish, after having an engine failure.
</p><p>Massa's victory in the race enabled him to overtake his teammate, Räikkönen, in the Drivers' Championship, moving him into second. He was now Hamilton's closest rival, behind by six points. Räikkönen moved eight points further behind Hamilton, due to his non-finish, with a deficit of 13 points now separating the two. In the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a> McLaren caught up two points on Ferrari; Ferrari led, eight points ahead of McLaren, with BMW Sauber a further 17 points behind.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_European_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_European_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Grand Prix was contested by 20&#160;drivers, in ten teams of two.<sup id="cite_ref-classification_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-classification-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> The teams, also known as "<a href="/wiki/Formula_One#Constructors" title="Formula One">constructors</a>", were <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>-<a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_HighPerformanceEngines" class="mw-redirect" title="Mercedes-Benz HighPerformanceEngines">Mercedes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a>, <a href="/wiki/Honda_Racing_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Honda Racing F1">Honda</a>, <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a>, <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a>, <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyota</a>, <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a>, <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-classification_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-classification-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyres" title="Formula One tyres">Tyre</a> supplier <a href="/wiki/Bridgestone" title="Bridgestone">Bridgestone</a> brought two different tyre compounds to the race; the softer of the two marked by a single white stripe down one of the grooves.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Prior to the race, McLaren driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> with 62&#160;<a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Championship_points_scoring_systems" title="List of Formula One World Championship points scoring systems">points</a>, and Ferrari driver <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> was second with 57&#160;points. Behind them in the Drivers' Championship, <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> was third with 54&#160;points also in a Ferrari, and the two BMW Sauber cars of <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> were fourth and fifth, with 49 and 41&#160;points respectively. In the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a>, Ferrari were leading with 111&#160;points, 11&#160;points ahead of their rivals McLaren. BMW Sauber were third with 90&#160;points. Toyota were fourth with 35&#160;points, and Renault were fifth with 31&#160;points.
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:S.Vettel_Europe_2008.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/73/S.Vettel_Europe_2008.jpg/220px-S.Vettel_Europe_2008.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="165" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/73/S.Vettel_Europe_2008.jpg/330px-S.Vettel_Europe_2008.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/73/S.Vettel_Europe_2008.jpg/440px-S.Vettel_Europe_2008.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2560" data-file-height="1920" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:S.Vettel_Europe_2008.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Bridgestone" title="Bridgestone">Bridgestone</a> celebrated its 200th Grand Prix since 1997.</div></div></div>
<p>The Grand Prix was the first to be held at the new Valencia street circuit. After seeing the track for the first time, four days before the race, the drivers were positive about the track.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Formula One tyre supplier, Bridgestone, celebrated their 200th Formula One grand prix at the race, having now supplied Formula One tyres since 1997.<sup id="cite_ref-B200_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-B200-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Celebrations included a special "golden tyre" which was displayed at the track, as well as two hoardings, inscribed with "Bridgestone 200".<sup id="cite_ref-B200_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-B200-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In technical developments, McLaren and Ferrari modified their front winglets, to make sure that the maximum amount of air flow was diverted.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Ferrari also revised their sidepod chimneys and winglets, with the aim of making sure the engine remained cool in hot conditions.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice_and_qualifying">Practice and qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_European_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Practice and qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Three practice sessions were held before the Sunday race—two on Friday, and a third on Saturday. The Friday morning and afternoon sessions each lasted 90&#160;minutes. The third session was held on Saturday morning and lasted an hour.<sup id="cite_ref-sporting_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sporting-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> The very first practice session on the newly opened Valencia Street Circuit was held on a dusty and slippery track, but despite the drivers' relative unfamiliarity with the circuit there were no major incidents (though <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> did hit the barriers late in the session).<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Formula1.com_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Formula1.com-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A9bastien_Bourdais" title="Sébastien Bourdais">Sébastien Bourdais</a> went fastest early on, but it was his Toro Rosso teammate <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> who posted the quickest time of the session. Massa and Hamilton finished second and third, with Bourdais just behind them.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Following the session, the drivers and personnel observed a minute's silence for those killed in the <a href="/wiki/Spanair_Flight_5022" title="Spanair Flight 5022">plane crash in Madrid</a> two days previously.<sup id="cite_ref-Formula1.com_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Formula1.com-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> topped the timesheets in second practice, having traded fastest times with teammate Massa for the majority of the session.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> had the set the quickest time late, but was surpassed by Räikkönen in his final flying lap.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> A notable incident in the session involved <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> spinning under brakes in the final corners, and narrowly missing <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> on the inside, before rejoining the track with no damage done.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Surprsingly, usual back-runner <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a>, driving for Honda, finished third.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> After the session, <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>, driving for Renault, was reprimanded and fined <a href="/wiki/Euro" title="Euro">€</a>10,000, after crossing the pitlane entry line early on in the second session.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Rain overnight meant that the Valencia track began with almost no rubber on the surface, but <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> was still able to carry enough speed to become the first man of the weekend to break into the 1:38s, with Rosberg and Bourdais close behind.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Simon_Strang_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Simon_Strang-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> There were minimal mishaps, with only Hamilton and <a href="/wiki/Timo_Glock" title="Timo Glock">Timo Glock</a> spinning.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a> spent the majority of the session in the pits with gearbox problems, completing only two laps.<sup id="cite_ref-Simon_Strang_21-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Simon_Strang-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Sebastian_Vettel_2008_Europe.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/48/Sebastian_Vettel_2008_Europe.jpg/220px-Sebastian_Vettel_2008_Europe.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="66" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/48/Sebastian_Vettel_2008_Europe.jpg/330px-Sebastian_Vettel_2008_Europe.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/48/Sebastian_Vettel_2008_Europe.jpg/440px-Sebastian_Vettel_2008_Europe.jpg 2x" data-file-width="511" data-file-height="153" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Sebastian_Vettel_2008_Europe.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> qualified sixth, which at that time was Toro Rosso's best ever qualifying position.<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup></div></div></div>
<p>After the third practice, but before the qualifying session, the <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_l%27Automobile" title="Fédération Internationale de l&#39;Automobile">Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile</a> safety delegate, <a href="/wiki/Charlie_Whiting" title="Charlie Whiting">Charlie Whiting</a>, announced that he had moved the pole position slot on the grid to the right side, after observing the cars' behaviour during the practice sessions.<sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Saturday afternoon's qualifying session was divided into three&#160;parts. In the first 20-minute period, cars finishing 16th or lower were eliminated. The second qualifying period lasted for 15&#160;minutes, at the end of which the fastest ten cars went into the final period, to determine their grid positions for the race. Cars failing to make the final period were allowed to be refuelled before the race but those competing in it were not, and so carried more fuel than they had done in the earlier qualifying sessions.<sup id="cite_ref-sporting_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sporting-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Massa clinched <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> with a time of 1:38.989, and was joined on the front row by championship leader, Hamilton.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> Kubica took third place on the grid, with Massa's teammate Räikkönen taking fourth.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_26-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton's teammate, Kovalainen, took fifth; Vettel, Trulli and <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> occupied the next three spots.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_26-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> The two Williams cars of <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kazuki_Nakajima" title="Kazuki Nakajima">Kazuki Nakajima</a> finished ninth and 11th, with Bourdais in between.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_26-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> Alonso qualified 12th, with <a href="/wiki/Timo_Glock" title="Timo Glock">Timo Glock</a> of Toyota behind him.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_26-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> of Red Bull was 14th, with <a href="/wiki/Nelson_Piquet,_Jr." class="mw-redirect" title="Nelson Piquet, Jr.">Nelson Piquet, Jr.</a> of Renault 15th.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_26-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> of Honda qualified 16th, and the other Red Bull car, driven by Coulthard, qualified 17th.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_26-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> The two Force India cars driven by <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a> and <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> finished 18th and 20th, with the other Honda car driven by <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a> qualifying 19th.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_26-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> Later, both Barrichello and Sutil were forced to change their gearboxes; as a result, both drivers started from the pit lane.<sup id="cite_ref-Pen_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pen-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_European_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The conditions on the grid were dry before the race. The <a href="/wiki/Air_temperature" class="mw-redirect" title="Air temperature">air temperature</a> was 28&#160;°C (82&#160;°F) and the track temperature was 44&#160;°C (111&#160;°F).<sup id="cite_ref-autocom_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocom-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The front-runners of the race started the race cleanly, but at turn four Alonso and Nakajima collided; Alonso retired from, what was in effect, his home race, while Nakajima had a new nose, and rejoined.<sup id="cite_ref-autocom_28-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocom-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FIA_29-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FIA-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-commbbc_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-commbbc-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At his final stop, <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> was released from its <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit stop</a> into the path of <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a>'s oncoming <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> car. There was no collision as Massa backed off as soon as he realised what had happened. This incident was deemed "unsafe" and was being investigated by the stewards. Shortly afterwards it was announced the incident would be investigated fully after the race. Ferrari was subsequently reprimanded by the stewards and fined €10,000, but the Brazilian kept his victory.<sup id="cite_ref-31" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>To add to Ferrari's pit stop blunders, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> left his pit box with his car's fuel hose still attached. The incident left a Ferrari mechanic with a minor fractures. Räikkönen also lost a place.<sup id="cite_ref-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> scored a "rare" point for his <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a> Team by finishing eighth.<sup id="cite_ref-formula1.com-officialresult_33-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formula1.com-officialresult-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
