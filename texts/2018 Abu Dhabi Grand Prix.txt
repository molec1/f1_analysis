<p>The <b>2018 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 2018 Etihad Airways Abu Dhabi Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 25 November 2018 at the <a href="/wiki/Yas_Marina_Circuit" title="Yas Marina Circuit">Yas Marina Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Abu_Dhabi" title="Abu Dhabi">Abu Dhabi</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_Arab_Emirates" title="United Arab Emirates">United Arab Emirates</a>. The race was the twenty first and final round of the <a href="/wiki/2018_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2018 Formula One World Championship">2018 Formula One World Championship</a> and marked the tenth running of the <a href="/wiki/Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix" title="Abu Dhabi Grand Prix">Abu Dhabi Grand Prix</a> and the tenth time that the race has been run as a World Championship event since the inaugural season in <a href="/wiki/1950_Formula_One_season" title="1950 Formula One season">1950</a>.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> announced they would be using a special one-off livery to celebrate the last race of <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>. This was the first time McLaren had run a special livery since the <a href="/wiki/1986_Portuguese_Grand_Prix" title="1986 Portuguese Grand Prix">1986 Portuguese Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>  McLaren also invited the family of seven time <a href="/wiki/Monster_Energy_NASCAR_Cup_Series" class="mw-redirect" title="Monster Energy NASCAR Cup Series">Monster Energy NASCAR Cup Series</a> Champion <a href="/wiki/Jimmie_Johnson" title="Jimmie Johnson">Jimmie Johnson</a> to the race.  Johnson and his family watched the race from the McLaren garage.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Title_standings_before_the_race">Title standings before the race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Title standings before the race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Prior to the race, <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> had already secured the 2018 World Drivers' Championship title, while his team, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a>, had secured the 2018 World Constructors' Championship title. Lewis Hamilton claimed his fifth World Drivers' title at the <a href="/wiki/2018_Mexican_Grand_Prix" title="2018 Mexican Grand Prix">2018 Mexican Grand Prix</a>; following the <a href="/wiki/2018_Brazilian_Grand_Prix" title="2018 Brazilian Grand Prix">Brazilian Grand Prix</a> he led <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> by 81 points with <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> being third, a further 51 points behind. In the World Constructors' Championship, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> led <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> by 67 points with only one race remaining in the season, with <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a> being third in the standings.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> was fastest in first practice, <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> was fastest in second practice and <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> was fastest in third practice.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> set the fastest time in qualifying followed by <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> teammate <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Hamilton</a> won the race from pole position with <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Vettel</a> and <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> completing the podium.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>. On the first lap at turn 9 <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> overtook <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> at turn 8 at the end of the back straight.  Grosjean was pushed off the track by the move but tried to still make turn 9 alongside Hülkenberg.  Not realizing that Grosjean was still on his inside, Hülkenberg attempted to take the normal line through turn 9 and his rear right tire ran over Grosjean's front left.  This caused the <a href="/wiki/Renault_R.S.18" title="Renault R.S.18">Renault R.S.18</a> to flip and barrel roll into the barriers to the left of turn 9, thus sending the race into a <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a> situation, the first such instance in the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix since 2012.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post_race">Post race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Post race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After the race had been completed <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Alonso</a> celebrated the end of his career by performing doughnuts on the pit straight alongside Lewis Hamilton and Sebastian Vettel, the other two World Champions who completed the race (<a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> had retired from the race.) <sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>  <a href="/wiki/Jimmie_Johnson" title="Jimmie Johnson">Jimmie Johnson</a> congratulated Alonso on his career.  The two would later exchange their respective cars in a car swap at <a href="/wiki/Bahrain_International_Circuit" title="Bahrain International Circuit">Bahrain International Circuit</a>.
</p>
