<p>The <b>2016 Italian Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 Gran Premio Heineken d'Italia 2016</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> held on 4 September 2016 at the <a href="/wiki/Autodromo_Nazionale_di_Monza" title="Autodromo Nazionale di Monza">Autodromo Nazionale di Monza</a> in <a href="/wiki/Monza" title="Monza">Monza</a>, <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a>. It was the fourteenth round of the <a href="/wiki/2016_FIA_Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="2016 FIA Formula One World Championship">2016 FIA Formula One World Championship</a>, and marked the 86th running of the <a href="/wiki/Italian_Grand_Prix" title="Italian Grand Prix">Italian Grand Prix</a> and the 81st time the race was held at Monza.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver and winner of <a href="/wiki/2015_Italian_Grand_Prix" title="2015 Italian Grand Prix">the previous year's race</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>, entered the race leading the World Drivers' Championship by nine points ahead of teammate <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a>. In the World Constructors' Championship, Mercedes held a lead of 181 points. <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a> was placed second and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> third.
</p>
