<p>The <b>2018 Belgian Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>Formula 1 2018 Johnnie Walker Belgian Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_racing" title="Formula One racing">Formula One motor race</a> held on 26 August 2018 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps" title="Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps">Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps</a> in <a href="/wiki/Stavelot" title="Stavelot">Stavelot</a>, <a href="/wiki/Belgium" title="Belgium">Belgium</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The race was the thirteenth round of the <a href="/wiki/2018_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2018 Formula One World Championship">2018 Formula One World Championship</a> and marked the 74th running of the <a href="/wiki/Belgian_Grand_Prix" title="Belgian Grand Prix">Belgian Grand Prix</a> and the 61st time the race was held at Spa-Francorchamps and 51st time as a round of the World Championship.
</p><p>The race was won by <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Scuderia Ferrari</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> ahead of <a href="/wiki/Mercedes_in_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Mercedes in F1">Mercedes</a>' <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> and <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a>. The victory moved Vettel ahead of <a href="/wiki/Alain_Prost" title="Alain Prost">Alain Prost</a> in <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_Grand_Prix_winners" title="List of Formula One Grand Prix winners">all-time career Formula One victories</a>.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After going into <a href="/wiki/Administration_(law)" title="Administration (law)">administration</a> during the <a href="/wiki/2018_Hungarian_Grand_Prix" title="2018 Hungarian Grand Prix">Hungarian Grand Prix</a> weekend, <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> arrived in Belgium under new ownership. A consortium led by <a href="/wiki/Lawrence_Stroll" title="Lawrence Stroll">Lawrence Stroll</a> purchased the racing assets and operations of Force India through a company named <a href="/wiki/Racing_Point_UK" title="Racing Point UK">Racing Point UK Ltd</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The original team, Sahara Force India, was excluded from the Constructors' Championship due to their inability to participate in the remaining races, allowing the new team known as <a href="/wiki/Racing_Point_Force_India" title="Racing Point Force India">Racing Point Force India</a> to apply for a late entry and start their participation in the championship in Belgium.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Championship_standings_before_the_race">Championship standings before the race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Championship standings before the race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> entered the round with a 24-point lead over <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> in the Drivers' Championship. In the World Constructors' Championship, Mercedes led <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> by 10 points.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2020)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> reserve driver <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">Lando Norris</a> made his first appearance at a Grand Prix weekend, replacing <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> during the first practice session.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The race was won by <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Scuderia Ferrari</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> ahead of <a href="/wiki/Mercedes_in_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Mercedes in F1">Mercedes</a>' <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> and <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a>. With the win Vettel took his 52nd and overtook <a href="/wiki/Alain_Prost" title="Alain Prost">Alain Prost</a> for third in the ranking of <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_race_winners" class="mw-redirect" title="List of Formula One race winners">drivers with the most Formula One wins</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2020)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>
