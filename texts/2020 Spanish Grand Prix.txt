<p>The <b>2020 Spanish Grand Prix</b> (officially known as the <b>Formula 1 Aramco Gran Premio de España 2020</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> held on 16 August 2020 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Barcelona-Catalunya" title="Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya">Circuit de Barcelona-Catalunya</a> in <a href="/wiki/Montmel%C3%B3" title="Montmeló">Montmeló</a>, <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The race was the sixth round in the <a href="/wiki/2020_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2020 Formula One World Championship">2020 Formula One World Championship</a>, and took place one week after the previous round, the <a href="/wiki/70th_Anniversary_Grand_Prix" title="70th Anniversary Grand Prix">70th Anniversary Grand Prix</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Spanish_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Impact_of_the_COVID-19_pandemic">Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Spanish_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">See also: <a href="/wiki/2020_Formula_One_World_Championship#Impact_of_the_COVID-19_pandemic" title="2020 Formula One World Championship">2020 Formula One World Championship §&#160;Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic</a>, and <a href="/wiki/COVID-19_pandemic_in_Spain" title="COVID-19 pandemic in Spain">COVID-19 pandemic in Spain</a></div>
<p>The opening rounds of the <a href="/wiki/2020_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2020 Formula One World Championship">2020</a> championship were heavily affected by the <a href="/wiki/COVID-19_pandemic" title="COVID-19 pandemic">COVID-19 pandemic</a>. Several Grands Prix were cancelled or postponed after the aborted <a href="/wiki/2020_Australian_Grand_Prix" title="2020 Australian Grand Prix">opening round</a> in <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>, prompting the <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_l%27Automobile" title="Fédération Internationale de l&#39;Automobile">FIA</a> to draft a new calendar. The Spanish Grand Prix was originally intended to be held on 10 May.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Championship_standings_before_the_race">Championship standings before the race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Spanish_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Championship standings before the race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> led the Drivers' Championship by 30 points from <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>-<a href="/wiki/Honda_in_Formula_One" title="Honda in Formula One">Honda</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a>. Four points behind Max Verstappen was Hamilton's teammate <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a>. In the Constructors' Championship, Mercedes led by 67 points from Red Bull Racing. In third place was <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, who were 58 points behind Red Bull and 2 points ahead of fourth-placed <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Entrants">Entrants</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Spanish_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Entrants">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">Further information: <a href="/wiki/2020_Formula_One_World_Championship#Entries" title="2020 Formula One World Championship">2020 Formula One World Championship §&#160;Entries</a></div>
<p>The drivers and teams were the same as those on the season entry list with no additional stand-in drivers for the race.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Roy_Nissany" title="Roy Nissany">Roy Nissany</a> took part in the first practice session for <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> in place of <a href="/wiki/George_Russell_(racing_driver)" title="George Russell (racing driver)">George Russell</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Tyres">Tyres</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Spanish_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Tyres">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">See also: <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyres" title="Formula One tyres">Formula One tyres</a></div>
<p>The C1, C2 and C3 tyre compounds were made available for teams to use by <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a>, the same compounds used at the <a href="/wiki/2019_Spanish_Grand_Prix" title="2019 Spanish Grand Prix">2019 event</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
