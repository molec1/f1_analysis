<p>The <b>2007 Monaco Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 27 May 2007 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Monaco" title="Circuit de Monaco">Circuit de Monaco</a>. It was the fifth round of the <a href="/wiki/2007_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2007 Formula One season">2007 Formula One season</a>. The 78-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> for the <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> team, from a <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> start. <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> finished second in the other McLaren car, with <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> third in a <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> car.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Grand Prix was contested by 22 drivers, in eleven teams of two. The teams, also known as "<a href="/wiki/Formula_One#Constructors" title="Formula One">constructors</a>", were <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a>, <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>-<a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_HighPerformanceEngines" class="mw-redirect" title="Mercedes-Benz HighPerformanceEngines">Mercedes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, <a href="/wiki/Honda_Racing_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Honda Racing F1">Honda</a>, <a href="/wiki/Spyker_F1" title="Spyker F1">Spyker</a>-Ferrari, <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a>, <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyota</a>, <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>-Renault, <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a>-Toyota, <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>-Ferrari and <a href="/wiki/Super_Aguri_F1" title="Super Aguri F1">Super Aguri</a>
</p><p>Before the race, McLaren driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a>, with 30&#160;<a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Championship_points_scoring_systems" title="List of Formula One World Championship points scoring systems">points</a>, ahead of team-mate <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> on 28 and Ferrari driver <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> on 27 points. Massa's team-mate <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> was fourth, ahead of BMW Sauber's <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> in fifth. In the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a>, McLaren were leading with 58 points, nine points ahead of Ferrari with 49&#160;points; BMW Sauber were a further 26&#160;points in third.
</p><p>Testing started a week and a half before the race at <a href="/wiki/Circuit_Paul_Ricard" title="Circuit Paul Ricard">Circuit Paul Ricard</a> using the circuit's 2D SC configuration to replicate the Circuit de Monaco.<sup id="cite_ref-Paul_Ricard_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Paul_Ricard-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> went on to score the fastest times on the days of testing whilst the circuit was in the Monaco configuration.
</p><p>Hamilton, who had never lost a race on the <a href="/wiki/Monte_Carlo" title="Monte Carlo">Monte Carlo</a> track (having won there in both <a href="/wiki/GP2_Series" title="GP2 Series">GP2</a> and <a href="/wiki/Formula_3" class="mw-redirect" title="Formula 3">F3</a>)<sup id="cite_ref-Hamilton_Plays_Down_Chances._2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hamilton_Plays_Down_Chances.-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> played down his chances of winning the race, although he still remained "realistic" and optimistic, looking to fight for a win as is "the case with everything (he) competes in."<sup id="cite_ref-Hamilton_Plays_Down_Chances._2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Hamilton_Plays_Down_Chances.-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The final practice session on Saturday morning was heavily affected by rain. <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> of Spyker topped the timesheets for this session due to the conditions.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:302px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Kovalainen_in_Monaco.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/86/Kovalainen_in_Monaco.jpg/300px-Kovalainen_in_Monaco.jpg" decoding="async" width="300" height="166" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/86/Kovalainen_in_Monaco.jpg/450px-Kovalainen_in_Monaco.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/86/Kovalainen_in_Monaco.jpg/600px-Kovalainen_in_Monaco.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1755" data-file-height="972" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Kovalainen_in_Monaco.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> in Monaco</div></div></div>
<p>Qualifying was dominated throughout by the two McLarens of Hamilton and <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>. Despite Hamilton setting fastest times for the first session, Alonso outpaced him in the final stages to take pole position, with Hamilton being held up slightly by <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a>. <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> of <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> qualified third fastest, posting a time after the clock stopped. <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> made a mistake and hit the barriers coming out of the Swimming Pool Complex during the second qualifying stage, breaking his front-right suspension. The damage inflicted could not be repaired and he qualified sixteenth. Despite provisionally making the top ten, <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> was penalised for impeding <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> and demoted to thirteenth.<sup id="cite_ref-coulthardpen_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-coulthardpen-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Coulthard's demotion allowed <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> to participate in the final qualifying stage, but he failed to move up the field and finished the qualifying session in 10th place.
</p><p>Of those whose qualifying went well, former Monaco resident <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a> fared best, putting his <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a> on the second row in fourth place. <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a>'s performance marked a return to form for his <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a> team; his fifth place was the British constructor's best qualifying position since Mark Webber's second place at the same track one year before in <a href="/wiki/2006_Formula_One_Season" class="mw-redirect" title="2006 Formula One Season">2006</a>. Webber himself also fared well, filling the second slot on row three, alongside Rosberg, in sixth place.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The race started at 14:00 <a href="/wiki/Central_European_Time" title="Central European Time">CET</a>. From the starting line everyone got away for the first laps, with <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Hamilton</a> diving in behind <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Alonso</a> to defend any of the Ferrari's advances. After the race, FIA launched an investigation of the McLaren team for giving out team orders, to the effect of asking Hamilton not to attempt to race or overtake Alonso.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Ecclestone said McLaren "could be excluded from the championship or they could have points deducted".<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Reliable_sources/Perennial_sources" title="Wikipedia:Reliable sources/Perennial sources"><span title="The material near this tag may rely on an unreliable source (October 2019)">deprecated source</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> McLaren were later cleared of any wrongdoing by the FIA.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>No other car in the field could keep up with the sheer pace of both of the McLaren-Mercedes, with <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Massa</a>, after a competitive first stint in third place, dropping to sixty seconds behind Hamilton. Notably, he was the only car the two McLaren drivers did not lap during the afternoon.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> came back up the field to eighth place to gain one championship point after his poor qualifying performance.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Vitantonio_Liuzzi" title="Vitantonio Liuzzi">Vitantonio Liuzzi</a> crashed out of the race on the second lap, a fate that befell <a href="/wiki/Spyker_F1" title="Spyker F1">Spyker</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> in the second half of the Grand Prix.  The other Spyker car, driven by <a href="/wiki/Christijan_Albers" title="Christijan Albers">Christijan Albers</a> failed to finish the race due to a mechanical problem.  The only other retirement was <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a>, whose <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull's</a> gearbox failed in the early stages of the race.
</p>
