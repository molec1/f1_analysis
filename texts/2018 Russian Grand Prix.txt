<p>The <b>2018 Russian Grand Prix</b> (officially the <b>Formula 1 2018 VTB Russian Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_racing" title="Formula One racing">Formula One motor race</a> held on 30 September 2018 at the <a href="/wiki/Sochi_Autodrom" title="Sochi Autodrom">Sochi Autodrom</a> in <a href="/wiki/Sochi" title="Sochi">Sochi</a>, <a href="/wiki/Russia" title="Russia">Russia</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The race was the 16th round of the <a href="/wiki/2018_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2018 Formula One World Championship">2018 Formula One World Championship</a> and marked the 7th running of the <a href="/wiki/Russian_Grand_Prix" title="Russian Grand Prix">Russian Grand Prix</a> and the 5th time the race had been held in Sochi.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:6_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> entered the round with a 40-point lead over <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> in the Drivers' Championship. In the World Constructors' Championship, Mercedes led <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> by 37 points.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The race was one of controversy, as Mercedes enacted team orders to swap their two drivers, handing Lewis Hamilton the win and <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> would move to 2nd place for the race finish.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The race result sparked discussion regarding the future of team orders in the sport, and some even going as to call for an outright ban on the practice.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Russian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The race was moved from its April date to September to fill the vacancy of the <a href="/wiki/Malaysian_Grand_Prix" title="Malaysian Grand Prix">Malaysian Grand Prix</a>, which was discontinued at the end of the <a href="/wiki/2017_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2017 Formula One World Championship">2017 season</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the weekend, the first DRS zone was extended by 100 meters so that the beginning of the DRS zone started 95 meters before turn 1.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Russian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At the start, Sebastian Vettel got a better start than Lewis Hamilton, but there was no room to overtake heading into turn one.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> On lap 4, both <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Scuderia Toro Rosso</a> drivers <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a> and <a href="/wiki/Brendon_Hartley" title="Brendon Hartley">Brendon Hartley</a> suffered brake failures which forced both to retire, they were the only retirements of the race.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Later on lap 14, Hamilton made his one and only pitstop of the race, putting him behind Vettel.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Two laps later, Hamilton made a move on Sebastian Vettel at turn 3 and passed him into fourth place into turn 4.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_11-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The most notable moment of the race came on lap 26, when Mercedes asked Valtteri Bottas to let Lewis Hamilton through into second at turn 13.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_11-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a>, who led the race since lap 19, came in for his pitstop on lap 42.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_11-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hamilton won the race for the third time in his career, with Valtteri Bottas second and Sebastian Vettel completing the podium in third place.<sup id="cite_ref-:6_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> As a consequence, Hamilton extended his lead over Vettel in the championship to 50 points.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
