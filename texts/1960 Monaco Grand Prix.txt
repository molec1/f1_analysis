<p>The <b>1960 Monaco Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Motor_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Motor race">motor race</a> held at <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Monaco" title="Circuit de Monaco">Monaco</a> on 29 May 1960. It was race 2 of 10 in the <a href="/wiki/1960_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1960 World Championship of Drivers">1960 World Championship of Drivers</a> and race 2 of 9 in the <a href="/wiki/1960_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1960 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1960 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>.
</p><p>The race was won by <a href="/wiki/Stirling_Moss" title="Stirling Moss">Stirling Moss</a> in a <a href="/wiki/Lotus_18" title="Lotus 18">Lotus 18</a> entered by the <a href="/wiki/Rob_Walker_Racing_Team" title="Rob Walker Racing Team">R.R.C Walker Racing Team</a>. It was the first World Championship F1 victory for the marque.
</p>
