<p>The <b>2009 Chinese Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 19 April 2009 at the <a href="/wiki/Shanghai_International_Circuit" title="Shanghai International Circuit">Shanghai International Circuit</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shanghai" title="Shanghai">Shanghai</a>, <a href="/wiki/People%27s_Republic_of_China" class="mw-redirect" title="People&#39;s Republic of China">People's Republic of China</a>. It was the third race of the <a href="/wiki/2009_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2009 Formula One season">2009 Formula One season</a>, a change from previous years, when it took place towards the end of the season.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> of <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a> won the race after starting from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>. Both the race win and pole position were the first for Red Bull team.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
<a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a>, also of Red Bull, finished second followed by the two <a href="/wiki/Brawn_GP" title="Brawn GP">Brawn GP</a>'s of <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a>. <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> finished fifth and sixth, ahead of <a href="/wiki/Toyota_Racing_(Formula_One_team)" title="Toyota Racing (Formula One team)">Toyota</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Timo_Glock" title="Timo Glock">Timo Glock</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Scuderia Toro Rosso</a>'s <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A9bastien_Buemi" title="Sébastien Buemi">Sébastien Buemi</a> in seventh and eighth.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2009_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Heading into the event, <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> of <a href="/wiki/Brawn_GP" title="Brawn GP">Brawn GP</a> led the Drivers' Championship by 5 points from team-mate <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a> and by a further 1.5 points from <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a> of <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyota</a>. <a href="/wiki/Brawn_GP" title="Brawn GP">Brawn GP</a> led <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyota</a> by 8.5 points in the Constructors' Championship, with <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a> and <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a> a further 12.5 points behind.
</p><p>Prior to the race, the <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_l%27Automobile" title="Fédération Internationale de l&#39;Automobile">Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile</a> (FIA) International Court of Appeal confirmed the legality of the <a href="/wiki/Diffuser_(automotive)" title="Diffuser (automotive)">diffuser</a> designs on the Brawn GP, <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a> and Toyota team cars.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Following this ruling, Renault and <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a> decided to run an updated diffuser design on their cars during the race.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> It was a hurried move by both teams, with Renault fitting it to Alonso's car for the first time on the Saturday before the race.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> announced that its team manager <a href="/wiki/Luca_Baldisserri" title="Luca Baldisserri">Luca Baldisserri</a> was taken off the track operations to be replaced by race engineer <a href="/wiki/Chris_Dyer" title="Chris Dyer">Chris Dyer</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> The reshuffle was undertaken after Ferrari failed to score a point in the first two races of the season.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Ferrari also announced that both its drivers, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>, would not use the <a href="/wiki/Regenerative_brake#Kinetic_energy_recovery_systems" title="Regenerative brake">Kinetic Energy Recovery System</a> (KERS) in the race, citing reliability and safety issues.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a>, on the other hand, declared that <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> would use KERS for the first time during the Friday practice session. Kubica had not used the system in the first two races as there were concerns that the additional weight would put him at a disadvantage.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> However, Kubica opted not to run the device any further after Friday practice, as did the Renault team, reducing the number of KERS-equipped cars to three.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Italian teams and drivers indicated their support for the victims of <a href="/wiki/2009_L%27Aquila_earthquake" title="2009 L&#39;Aquila earthquake">2009 L'Aquila earthquake</a> through charity initiative <i>"<a href="/wiki/Abruzzo" title="Abruzzo">Abruzzo</a> nel cuore"</i> (<i>Abruzzo in my heart</i>) led by Italian drivers Jarno Trulli and <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-abruzzo_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-abruzzo-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Ferrari displayed the writing <i>"Abruzzo nel cuore"</i> on their cars while Trulli wore the logo on his overalls.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a> cars had the message <i>"Vicini All'Abruzzo"</i> (<i>All neighbouring Abruzzo</i>) on their cars.<sup id="cite_ref-abruzzo_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-abruzzo-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Event_report">Event report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2009_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Event report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice_and_qualifying">Practice and qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2009_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Practice and qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quotebox pullquote floatright" style="width:35%;&#32;;"><style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r887775652">.mw-parser-output .quotebox{background-color:#F9F9F9;border:1px solid #aaa;box-sizing:border-box;padding:10px;font-size:88%;max-width:100%}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatleft{margin:0.5em 1.4em 0.8em 0}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatright{margin:0.5em 0 0.8em 1.4em}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.centered{margin:0.5em auto 0.8em auto}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatleft p,.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatright p{font-style:inherit}.mw-parser-output .quotebox-title{background-color:#F9F9F9;text-align:center;font-size:larger;font-weight:bold}.mw-parser-output .quotebox-quote.quoted:before{font-family:"Times New Roman",serif;font-weight:bold;font-size:large;color:gray;content:" “ ";vertical-align:-45%;line-height:0}.mw-parser-output .quotebox-quote.quoted:after{font-family:"Times New Roman",serif;font-weight:bold;font-size:large;color:gray;content:" ” ";line-height:0}.mw-parser-output .quotebox .left-aligned{text-align:left}.mw-parser-output .quotebox .right-aligned{text-align:right}.mw-parser-output .quotebox .center-aligned{text-align:center}.mw-parser-output .quotebox cite{display:block;font-style:normal}@media screen and (max-width:360px){.mw-parser-output .quotebox{min-width:100%;margin:0 0 0.8em!important;float:none!important}}</style>
<div class="quotebox-quote left-aligned" style="">"The car instantly felt a bit better. I can feel more stability and downforce from the front. We've definitely made a step forward for this race. The car feels much stronger through the corners and I think we've got a very positive baseline for the rest of the weekend. My first run on the options felt quite consistent".</div>
<p><cite class="left-aligned" style=""><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>, on the performance his car during Friday's practice sessions<sup id="cite_ref-friquotes_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-friquotes-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup></cite>
</p>
</div>
<p>Three practice sessions were held prior to the race; two sessions on Friday April 17, 2009, each lasting 90 minutes, and one 60 minute session on Saturday April 18, 2009. World Champion <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>'s McLaren was fastest at the end of the first practice session on Friday morning ahead of the Brawn GP cars of Jenson Button and Rubens Barrichello. Hamilton's McLaren team-mate <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> was fourth fastest.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton, whose <a href="/wiki/McLaren_MP4-24" title="McLaren MP4-24">McLaren MP4-24</a> car was fitted with a new interim diffuser and front wing, clocked his fastest lap of 1:37.334, around a tenth of a second clear of Button, around sixty minutes into the session.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> was fifth in the session, with Toyota's Trulli and <a href="/wiki/Timo_Glock" title="Timo Glock">Timo Glock</a> sixth and eighth. <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> and <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A9bastien_Bourdais" title="Sébastien Bourdais">Sébastien Bourdais</a> completed the top ten lap times of the first session.<sup id="cite_ref-practice_results_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-practice_results-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Button was the fastest in Friday afternoon's second practice session, followed by Rosberg and Barrichello. Red Bull drivers Webber and <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> who headed the time-sheets during the initial phases of the session were eventually classified fourth and fifth fastest respectively.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Toyota cars once again managed a competitive result as Trulli and Glock finished sixth and eighth with Williams' <a href="/wiki/Kazuki_Nakajima" title="Kazuki Nakajima">Kazuki Nakajima</a> splitting them. The two McLarens could not recreate their results from the morning session with Kovalainen finishing ninth fastest and Hamilton 13th.<sup id="cite_ref-practice_results_19-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-practice_results-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Williams' Rosberg was quickest in the practice session on Saturday morning. Rosberg, who slid off the track onto the gravel at the start of the session, timed 1:36.133 to top the timesheets, followed by Trulli and Hamilton.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Button finished fourth with Renault's <a href="/wiki/Nelson_Piquet,_Jr." class="mw-redirect" title="Nelson Piquet, Jr.">Nelson Piquet, Jr.</a> clocking the fifth fastest lap time for the session. Piquet's team-mate Fernando Alonso on the other hand, whose <a href="/wiki/Renault_R29" title="Renault R29">Renault R29</a> car was fitted with new diffuser and without the KERS, could only manage a time good enough for 19th fastest.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> BMW Sauber's <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> who also removed his KERS for this session, improved significantly from his results in the earlier two sessions.<sup id="cite_ref-practice_results_19-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-practice_results-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Timo Glock's <a href="/wiki/Toyota_TF109" title="Toyota TF109">Toyota TF109</a> car faced gearbox problems early in the session and had to change the gearbox for the upcoming qualifying session. Glock received a five-place grid penalty for this gearbox change.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<div class="quotebox-quote left-aligned" style="">"...if you only have one lap and if you have [<i><a href="/wiki/Sic" title="Sic">sic</a></i>] any mistake, you go wide or whatever, there is no second chance and it means it is the end. It was not so easy but I am very, very happy. Unbelievable we made it to pole position in the end."</div>
<p><cite class="left-aligned" style=""><a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>, in post-qualifying press conference.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_conf_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali_conf-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup></cite>
</p>
</div>
<p>The qualifying session on Saturday afternoon was split into three parts. Sebastian Vettel achieved his second career <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> – and the first for his Red Bull Racing team – at the end of the session.<sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Vettel, who ran only one <a href="/wiki/List_of_motorsport_terminology#F" class="mw-redirect" title="List of motorsport terminology">flying lap</a> each in the final two parts of qualifying, due to problems with the driveshafts of his car, edged out Fernando Alonso by two-tenths of a second in the final part of qualifying.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> Red Bull's other driver Mark Webber finished third, immediately ahead of Brawn GP cars of Barrichello and Button. Kimi Räikkönen, after a disappointing performance during practice, secured eighth position behind Trulli in sixth and Rosberg in seventh place. Hamilton and Toro Rosso's <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A9bastien_Buemi" title="Sébastien Buemi">Sébastien Buemi</a> completed the top ten on the grid.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_results_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali_results-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a>, Heikki Kovalainen, Felipe Massa, Timo Glock and Kazuki Nakajima made up 11th to 15th on the grid. Drivers eliminated in the first qualifying session and classified 16th to 20th were Sébastien Bourdais, Nelson Piquet, Jr., Robert Kubica, <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> and Giancarlo Fisichella.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_results_27-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali_results-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Due to his five-place penalty, Glock was placed 19th and Nakajima, Bourdais, Piquet, Kubica and Sutil were each promoted by one position in the revised starting grid.<sup id="cite_ref-starting_grid_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-starting_grid-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2009_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Red_Bull_3_China_09.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/af/Red_Bull_3_China_09.jpg/220px-Red_Bull_3_China_09.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/af/Red_Bull_3_China_09.jpg/330px-Red_Bull_3_China_09.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/af/Red_Bull_3_China_09.jpg/440px-Red_Bull_3_China_09.jpg 2x" data-file-width="4752" data-file-height="3168" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Red_Bull_3_China_09.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Sebastian Vettel took Red Bull Racing's first victory in Formula One.</div></div></div>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Mark_Webber_2009_China_2.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ed/Mark_Webber_2009_China_2.jpg/220px-Mark_Webber_2009_China_2.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="146" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ed/Mark_Webber_2009_China_2.jpg/330px-Mark_Webber_2009_China_2.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/ed/Mark_Webber_2009_China_2.jpg/440px-Mark_Webber_2009_China_2.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3017" data-file-height="2003" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Mark_Webber_2009_China_2.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Mark Webber completed Red Bull's perfect race by finishing second.</div></div></div>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Jenson_Button_2009_China.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8e/Jenson_Button_2009_China.jpg/220px-Jenson_Button_2009_China.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="146" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8e/Jenson_Button_2009_China.jpg/330px-Jenson_Button_2009_China.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8e/Jenson_Button_2009_China.jpg/440px-Jenson_Button_2009_China.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3901" data-file-height="2590" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Jenson_Button_2009_China.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Jenson Button maintained his championship lead by finishing in third place.</div></div></div>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Rubens_Barrichello_2009_China.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6b/Rubens_Barrichello_2009_China.jpg/220px-Rubens_Barrichello_2009_China.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="146" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6b/Rubens_Barrichello_2009_China.jpg/330px-Rubens_Barrichello_2009_China.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6b/Rubens_Barrichello_2009_China.jpg/440px-Rubens_Barrichello_2009_China.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2834" data-file-height="1881" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Rubens_Barrichello_2009_China.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Rubens Barrichello clocked in the fastest lap time even though only coming fourth.</div></div></div>
<p>Robert Kubica and Timo Glock started from the pit lane. Due to heavy rain, the first eight laps of the race were run under the <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a>. Sutil, Rosberg and Alonso made their first <a href="/wiki/Pit_stops" class="mw-redirect" title="Pit stops">pit stops</a> during the safety car period so they were at the back of the pack when the green flag was shown. Lewis Hamilton made early progress and overtook Jarno Trulli and Kimi Räikkönen, before he spun on lap 12 and dropped back to 10th. Meanwhile, Jenson Button overtook Rubens Barrichello for third after the Brazilian ran wide.
</p><p>Trulli and Räikkönen were quite slow in the early laps and they dropped back. Hamilton made progress once again and was 7th on lap 11 before another mistake which dropped him one place down. Webber made his first scheduled stop on lap 14 and Vettel on the following lap. At this point, Button led from Barrichello, Vettel, Buemi, Massa and Webber.
</p><p>On lap 17, Robert Kubica crashed into Jarno Trulli on the run to the last corner. Kubica damaged his front wing and Trulli his rear wing, which caused him to crawl one lap slowly into the pits to retire. The safety car was deployed due to debris left on the track. While avoiding the slowing Trulli, Vettel suffered a minor collision with Buemi, who damaged his front wing in the process. Buemi made his pit stop at that point, along with both Brawn GP drivers and Sutil, who had his second stop at this point and was set to finish the race from this point.
</p><p>Felipe Massa suffered an electrical problem during the safety car period and he stopped on the back straight. At the restart, Sébastien Bourdais spun before the green flag and dropped a number of places. The race continued with Vettel leading from Button while Webber, Räikkönen, Hamilton, Barrichello, Kovalainen and Buemi rounded out the top eight.
</p><p>Piquet crashed on lap 28 and damaged his front wing but was able to continue. Button missed his braking point at the hairpin on the following lap, allowing Webber through. Webber ran wide two laps later and Button was ahead again, until Webber passed him from the outside in turn 7.
</p><p>Hamilton made his only scheduled stop on lap 33, and his team-mate Kovalainen followed a lap later. Vettel stopped on lap 37 and Webber on lap 39, while two Brawn cars ran until laps 42 (Button) and 43 (Barrichello). Buemi was the last driver to make a scheduled stop, on lap 45 and after that race order was: Vettel, Webber, Button, Barrichello, Hamilton, Kovalainen, Sutil and Heidfeld. Nico Rosberg had tried intermediate tyres on his second stop but he spun soon after and pitted again for full wets.
</p><p>Hamilton continued with a lot of mistakes so Kovalainen and Sutil were able to pass him. Then on lap 51, Sutil crashed heavily before turn 5, ending his race. Heidfeld had to avoid Sutil's tyre which had bounced back to the track and Glock and Buemi went past him.
</p><p>Vettel stayed in the lead until the end, taking his second Grand Prix victory and his first for Red Bull Racing. His team-mate Webber achieved his best career result with second place while Button got his third successive podium with third place. Barrichello finished fourth, Kovalainen fifth, Hamilton sixth, Glock seventh and Buemi eighth. A total of 17 drivers were classified, including Sutil who had retired.
</p><p>On the podium, there was confusion when "<a href="/wiki/God_Save_the_Queen" title="God Save the Queen">God Save the Queen</a>", the British national anthem was played for winning constructor Red Bull instead of "<a href="/wiki/Land_der_Berge,_Land_am_Strome" class="mw-redirect" title="Land der Berge, Land am Strome">Land der Berge, Land am Strome</a>", the Austrian national anthem. Red Bull Racing is based in the UK, but is registered with the Austrian national racing authority, reflecting its Austrian ownership. When Vettel recorded Red Bull's second win at the <a href="/wiki/2009_British_Grand_Prix" title="2009 British Grand Prix">2009 British Grand Prix</a>, the Austrian anthem was played.
</p>
