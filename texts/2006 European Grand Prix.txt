<p>The <b>2006 European Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held at the <a href="/wiki/N%C3%BCrburgring" title="Nürburgring">Nürburgring</a> in <a href="/wiki/N%C3%BCrburg" title="Nürburg">Nürburg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a> on 7 May 2006.  
The 60-lap race was the fifth round of the <a href="/wiki/2006_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2006 Formula One season">2006 Formula One season</a>. It was won by <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> who took his second victory of the season. Polesitter <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> finished in second position for the <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a> team, whilst the second Ferrari of <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> achieved his first podium finish of the season with third place. 
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Franck_Montagny" title="Franck Montagny">Franck Montagny</a> made his Formula One début, becoming the first <a href="/wiki/France" title="France">French</a> F1 driver since <a href="/wiki/Olivier_Panis" title="Olivier Panis">Olivier Panis</a> at the <a href="/wiki/2004_Japanese_Grand_Prix" title="2004 Japanese Grand Prix">2004 Japanese Grand Prix</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2006_European_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Friday_drivers">Friday drivers</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2006_European_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Friday drivers">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The bottom 6 teams in the <a href="/wiki/2005_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2005 Formula One season">2005 Constructors' Championship</a> and <a href="/wiki/Super_Aguri_F1" title="Super Aguri F1">Super Aguri</a> were entitled to run a third car in free practice on Friday. These drivers drove on Friday but did not compete in qualifying or the race.
</p><p>Franck Montagny was originally scheduled to act as the third driver for Super Aguri,<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> but was promoted to a race seat<sup id="cite_ref-pitpass.com_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pitpass.com-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> after team driver <a href="/wiki/Yuji_Ide" title="Yuji Ide">Yuji Ide</a> had his <a href="/wiki/FIA_Super_License" class="mw-redirect" title="FIA Super License">FIA Super License</a> revoked<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> for erratic driving in prior races that season. Ide was set to act as Super Aguri's third driver for this race, according to the team, until the revocation came down.<sup id="cite_ref-pitpass.com_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pitpass.com-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The license revocation meant Ide could not participate in Formula One.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2006_European_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The qualifying session was affected by a red flag situation appearing on the timing monitors with 3½ minutes left. Unfortunately, this was due to a software glitch in the timing system, and the session was quickly restarted. <a href="/wiki/Christian_Klien" title="Christian Klien">Christian Klien</a> felt that he lost out through it, having abandoned a lap thinking it would not count. Initially <a href="/wiki/Jacques_Villeneuve" title="Jacques Villeneuve">Jacques Villeneuve</a> was outside the top 16, however the time for his lap (which he continued on during the red flag) was later recalled and placed him 7th in the session.
</p>
