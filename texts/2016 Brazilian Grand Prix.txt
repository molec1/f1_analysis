<p>The <b>2016 Brazilian Grand Prix</b> (officially known as the <b>Formula One Grande Prêmio do Brasil 2016</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 13 November 2016 at the <a href="/wiki/Aut%C3%B3dromo_Jos%C3%A9_Carlos_Pace" title="Autódromo José Carlos Pace">Autódromo José Carlos Pace</a> in São Paulo, Brazil. The race was the 20th and penultimate round of the <a href="/wiki/2016_FIA_Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="2016 FIA Formula One World Championship">2016 FIA Formula One World Championship</a> and marked the 45th running of the <a href="/wiki/Brazilian_Grand_Prix" title="Brazilian Grand Prix">Brazilian Grand Prix</a> and the 44th time that the race had been run as a World Championship event since the inaugural season in <a href="/wiki/1950_Formula_One_season" title="1950 Formula One season">1950</a>. At 3h 1m 1.335s, the race was the longest Brazilian race in history.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Winner of the <a href="/wiki/2015_Brazilian_Grand_Prix" title="2015 Brazilian Grand Prix">previous year's</a> race <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> entered the round with a 19-point lead over his teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> in the World Drivers' Championship. Their team, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a>, had already clinched the World Constructors' Championship, holding a lead of two hundred and fifty-two points over <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a>, with third place <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Scuderia Ferrari</a> another sixty-two points behind. Hamilton took pole position and won the race from Rosberg to take the championship to the final round in <a href="/wiki/2016_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix" title="2016 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix">Abu Dhabi</a>. <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> finished third, having dropped to 16th after a pit stop with 16 laps to go. His drive was widely acclaimed as one of Formula One's finest wet-weather drives.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
