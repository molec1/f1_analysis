<p>The <b>2014 Singapore Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>2014 Formula 1 Singapore Airlines Singapore Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 21 September at the <a href="/wiki/Marina_Bay_Street_Circuit" title="Marina Bay Street Circuit">Marina Bay Street Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Marina_Bay,_Singapore" title="Marina Bay, Singapore">Marina Bay, Singapore</a>. It was the 14th round of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2014 Formula One World Championship">2014 Formula One World Championship</a> and the seventh running of the <a href="/wiki/Singapore_Grand_Prix" title="Singapore Grand Prix">event</a> as part of the series. The 60-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> duo of <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> and <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> finished second and third. It was Hamilton's seventh victory of the season, his second in Singapore, and the 29th of his career.
</p><p>Hamilton won the pole position by posting the fastest lap in qualifying and maintained the lead into the first corner. His teammate <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> had a wiring loom steering problem and began from the <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit lane</a>. Hamilton remained in the lead after the first round of pit stops before ceding it to Ricciardo for one lap in the second pit stop phase. In the final laps, Hamilton made his final pit stop to switch tyres and rejoined just ahead of Ricciardo. Vettel led for one lap until Hamilton passed him on lap 54 to achieve victory. There were four lead changes among three different drivers during the course of the race.
</p><p>The result moved Hamilton back into the lead of the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">World Drivers' Championship</a> with 241 <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Championship_points_scoring_systems" title="List of Formula One World Championship points scoring systems">points</a>, three ahead of teammate Rosberg who retired after 14 laps after his problem could not be rectified. Ricciardo maintained third place, with <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> and Vettel passing the non-scoring <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> to move into fourth and fifth. Mercedes further extended their lead in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a> to 182 points over Red Bull in second. <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> maintained third and fourth and <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> overtook <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> for fifth with five races left in the season.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Singapore_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Pit_building_2014_Singapore.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5d/Pit_building_2014_Singapore.jpg/220px-Pit_building_2014_Singapore.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5d/Pit_building_2014_Singapore.jpg/330px-Pit_building_2014_Singapore.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5d/Pit_building_2014_Singapore.jpg/440px-Pit_building_2014_Singapore.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1920" data-file-height="1280" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Pit_building_2014_Singapore.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Marina_Bay_Street_Circuit" title="Marina Bay Street Circuit">Marina Bay Street Circuit</a>, where the race was held</div></div></div>
<p>The 2014 <a href="/wiki/Singapore_Grand_Prix" title="Singapore Grand Prix">Singapore Grand Prix</a> was the 14th of the 19 rounds in the <a href="/wiki/2014_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2014 Formula One World Championship">2014 Formula One World Championship</a>, and the seventh Formula One Singapore race. It was held on 21 September at the 23-turn 5.065&#160;km (3.147&#160;mi) <a href="/wiki/Marina_Bay_Street_Circuit" title="Marina Bay Street Circuit">Marina Bay Street Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Marina_Bay,_Singapore" title="Marina Bay, Singapore">Marina Bay</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FIApreview_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FIApreview-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The official name was the <i>2014 Formula 1 Singapore Airlines Singapore Grand Prix</i>,<sup id="cite_ref-formal_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> and was scheduled to run for 61 laps.<sup id="cite_ref-FIApreview_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FIApreview-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyres" title="Formula One tyres">Tyre</a> supplier <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> brought the red-banded super-soft "options" and yellow-marked soft "primes" dry tyre compounds to the race.<sup id="cite_ref-:10_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:10-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Drag_reduction_system" title="Drag reduction system">drag reduction system</a> (DRS) had two activation zones for the race; one was from turns five and seven, and the second was on the start/finish <a href="/wiki/Straight_(racing)" title="Straight (racing)">straight</a> from the final to first corners.<sup id="cite_ref-FIApreview_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FIApreview-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Parts of the track parts of the circuit were resurfaced with a mandatory track jet sweeper used to ensure the <a href="/wiki/Grip_(auto_racing)" title="Grip (auto racing)">grip</a> on the new asphalt equalled that from the old surface following the <a href="/wiki/2013_Singapore_Grand_Prix" title="2013 Singapore Grand Prix">2013 race</a>. The outside turn seven barrier on the <a href="/wiki/Run-off_area" title="Run-off area">run-off area</a> was realigned to allow traffic to drive behind it and the <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit lane</a>'s fast section was resurfaced.<sup id="cite_ref-FIApreview_2-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FIApreview-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Before the race <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> with 238 <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Championship_points_scoring_systems" title="List of Formula One World Championship points scoring systems">points</a>, 22 ahead of teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> in second. <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> was a further 50 points behind in third place. <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> was fourth on 122 points, one ahead of <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> in fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-points_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Mercedes led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a> with 454 points, and <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> were second on 272 points. <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> in third had 172 points, <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> in fourth had 162 and <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> were fifth with 110 points.<sup id="cite_ref-points_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At the <a href="/wiki/2014_Italian_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Italian Grand Prix">previous race in Italy</a>, the gap between Rosberg and Hamilton closed to seven points. Hamilton started from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> ahead of Rosberg, but a poor start dropped him to fourth. He retook the lead after Rosberg braked late and went straight on at the first turn and used the escape road to rejoin the track. Hamilton remained in the lead for the rest of the race to win.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> With six races left in the season, Hamilton said he was aware he could not lose any further ground to Rosberg in the championship and was pleased to have improved after winning at Monza: "Despite the highs and lows, I'm still well and truly in the hunt for the championship and that is something that gives me huge motivation for the final six races – starting in Singapore."<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg admitted Hamilton's form and past experience in championship battles could possibly be advantageous but was unconcerned about it and focused on himself: "At the moment, I am enjoying the moment more than I ever have in the sport because I get to a race and I have a car where I know I can be on pole and I can win,"<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo stated that while he was aware he had an unrealistic chance of winning the championship, he would take a race-by-race approach, and hoped to win one of the next two races to draw closer in the title battle: "All it takes is a couple of wins and a couple of bad races for the championship leader, and all of a sudden everything's possible again. So let's see what happens."<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the weeks before the race, the <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_l%27Automobile" title="Fédération Internationale de l&#39;Automobile">Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile</a> (FIA; Formula One's governing body) race director <a href="/wiki/Charlie_Whiting" title="Charlie Whiting">Charlie Whiting</a>, introduced a ban on certain pit-to-car communications, with a particular emphasis on banning driver "coaching"—such as describing their sector times relative to another competitor—under the sporting regulations stating a driver must drive the car "alone and unaided".<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Additionally, in the week of the race, the FIA banned teams from sending drivers coded messages either by radio or pit board, and decided that at the following round in <a href="/wiki/2014_Japanese_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Japanese Grand Prix">Japan</a>, they would be prohibited from sending information concerning tyres and brake wear.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> After team principals raised concerns about the extent of the ban three days before the race, the FIA revised what teams could tell their drivers by radio, including car performance and safety concerns.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> After persistent media speculation about his future at <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a>, the team confirmed that <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a> would enter the race.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Singapore_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Pastor_Maldonado_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/21/Pastor_Maldonado_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg/220px-Pastor_Maldonado_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/21/Pastor_Maldonado_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg/330px-Pastor_Maldonado_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/21/Pastor_Maldonado_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg/440px-Pastor_Maldonado_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1920" data-file-height="1280" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Pastor_Maldonado_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> caused the second practice session to be stopped after crashing at turn ten.</div></div></div>
<p>Three practice sessions—two on Friday and a third on Saturday—were held before the Sunday race. The Friday evening and night sessions lasted ninety minutes each; the third, one-hour session was held on Saturday evening.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> In the first practice session, Alonso set the fastest lap of 1 minute, 49.056 seconds, one-tenth of a second faster than Hamilton in second. Rosberg, Vettel, Ricciardo, <a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a>, <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> rounded out the session's top ten drivers.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> Although several drivers narrowly avoided damaging their cars, the session passed relatively smoothly.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> Vergne's <a href="/wiki/Automotive_battery" title="Automotive battery">battery</a> was changed, causing him to miss the first hour. After setting his fastest lap, he stopped with an <a href="/wiki/Kinetic_energy_recovery_system" title="Kinetic energy recovery system">energy recovery system</a> failure, ending his session early.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> Räikkonen's session also ended early with brake fire. Vettel stopped at the pit lane exit with a sudden loss in <a href="/wiki/Oil_pressure" title="Oil pressure">oil pressure</a>; he avoided a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_regulations#Penalties" title="Formula One regulations">ten-place grid penalty</a> because he used an older engine.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_15-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:1_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hamilton, who had brake vibrations, set the day's fastest lap of 1 minute, 47.490 seconds on the super soft compound tyres in the second practice session; Alonso, Ricciardo, Räikkönen, Vettel, the McLaren pair of <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a> and Button, Pérez, <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> and Kvyat followed in the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> The session was disrupted halfway through when <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> overdrove through turn 10, lost control of his car's rear, and slid into the right-hand exit barriers, damaging his vehicle's front-right hand corner.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:2_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> After the session, <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a> constructed a new <a href="/wiki/Lotus_E22" title="Lotus E22">E22</a> chassis overnight for Maldonado for the remainder of the event.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_20-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> In the final practice session, held in cloudy weather, Alonso set the fastest lap of the weekend so far at 1 minute, 47.299 seconds, 0.051 seconds faster than Ricciardo in second. Rosberg was third after a slow first third of the lap and went over the turn 11 <a href="/wiki/Curb" title="Curb">kerbs</a>. Vergne, Vettel, Hamilton, <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>, Bottas, Räikkönen and <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban Gutiérrez">Esteban Gutiérrez</a> completed the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Singapore_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9e/Lewis_Hamilton_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg/220px-Lewis_Hamilton_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9e/Lewis_Hamilton_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg/330px-Lewis_Hamilton_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9e/Lewis_Hamilton_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg/440px-Lewis_Hamilton_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1920" data-file-height="1280" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> took his sixth pole position of the season and the 37th of his career.</div></div></div>
<p>Saturday afternoon's <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#Q" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">qualifying session</a> was divided into three parts. The first session ran for 18 minutes, eliminating cars that finished 17th or below. The <a href="/wiki/107%25_rule" title="107% rule">107% rule</a> was in effect, requiring drivers to reach a time within 107 per cent of the quickest lap to qualify. The second part lasted 15 minutes, eliminating cars that finished 11th to 16th. The final session ran for 12 minutes determined <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> to tenth. Cars in the final session were not allowed to change tyres, using the tyres with which they set their quickest lap times in the second session.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Although Hamilton lost two-tenths of a second locking his tyres entering turn one and subsequently missing its <a href="/wiki/Racing_line" title="Racing line">apex</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-:4_23-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:3_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> he set the fastest time in the final session to achieve his sixth pole position of the season, his third in Singapore,<sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> and the 37th of his career with a lap of 1 minute, 45.681 seconds.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_24-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> He was joined on the <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#G" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">grid</a>'s front row by Rosberg,<sup id="cite_ref-:3_24-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> who called it "the most difficult qualifying of my career so far" because he had difficulty finding a rhythm and changed his brakes for qualifying.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo took third and had the pole position until both Mercedes drivers went faster in the session's closing seconds.<sup id="cite_ref-:6_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> He was ahead of teammate Vettel in fourth who believed pole position could have been his after feeling he overdrove.<sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Alonso made a minor set-up change, giving him confidence and qualified fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> Massa took sixth, ahead of Räikkönen, who was quickest in the first session, and was satisfied with his car's handling, but his engine shut down while preparing for a second timed lap in the final session.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_23-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:5_26-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:6_27-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Jenson_Button_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/76/Jenson_Button_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg/220px-Jenson_Button_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/76/Jenson_Button_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg/330px-Jenson_Button_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/76/Jenson_Button_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg/440px-Jenson_Button_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1920" data-file-height="1280" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Jenson_Button_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> struggled with front-end grip and was the fastest driver not to advance into the final qualifying session.</div></div></div>
<p>Bottas's preparation for his final timed lap was slightly compromised, and an error in the first turn left him eighth. Magnussen took ninth and Kvyat was tenth after only having the super soft compound tyre available.<sup id="cite_ref-:7_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:7-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Button was the fastest driver not to qualify for the final session;<sup id="cite_ref-:8_31-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> he locked his front tyres at the turn 13 <a href="/wiki/Hairpin_turn" title="Hairpin turn">hairpin</a> on his final timed lap and went wide, losing him time, and struggled with front grip despite a change of engine and <a href="/wiki/Differential_(mechanical_device)" title="Differential (mechanical device)">differential</a> settings.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_26-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> Vergne had front grip issues, and locked his tyres, putting him 12th. The <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> duo of Hülkenberg and Pérez qualified in 13th and 15th after being unable to go improve on their second attempts and were separated by Gutiérrez in the faster <a href="/wiki/Sauber_Motorsport" title="Sauber Motorsport">Sauber</a> car.<sup id="cite_ref-:7_30-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:7-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> was the slowest driver in the second session; a turbocharger <a href="/wiki/Wastegate" title="Wastegate">wastegate</a> problem slowed him and he radioed Lotus his frustration after his elimination.<sup id="cite_ref-:9_32-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:9-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> failed to advance beyond the first session due to energy management and engine problems.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_26-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:8_31-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Maldonado had the same turbocharger issue as his teammate and qualified 18th.<sup id="cite_ref-:9_32-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:9-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Minor errors on <a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a>'s first timed lap left him 19th and he was ahead of <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a> in 20th. <a href="/wiki/Max_Chilton" title="Max Chilton">Max Chilton</a> could not extract full engine power on his first attempt because of mapping problems and had to prepare for another lap and took 21st.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_26-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Ericsson" title="Marcus Ericsson">Marcus Ericsson</a> ventured onto the track late in the first session after his team worked quickly to rectify an electrical issue and completed the field in 22nd.<sup id="cite_ref-:7_30-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:7-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Singapore_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Kamui_Kobayashi_engine_failure_2014_Singapore_Race(L00).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d1/Kamui_Kobayashi_engine_failure_2014_Singapore_Race%28L00%29.jpg/220px-Kamui_Kobayashi_engine_failure_2014_Singapore_Race%28L00%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="110" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d1/Kamui_Kobayashi_engine_failure_2014_Singapore_Race%28L00%29.jpg/330px-Kamui_Kobayashi_engine_failure_2014_Singapore_Race%28L00%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d1/Kamui_Kobayashi_engine_failure_2014_Singapore_Race%28L00%29.jpg/440px-Kamui_Kobayashi_engine_failure_2014_Singapore_Race%28L00%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1920" data-file-height="960" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Kamui_Kobayashi_engine_failure_2014_Singapore_Race(L00).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a> failed to start the race due to an oil pressure problem.</div></div></div>
<p>The weather at the start was dry and clear, with the air temperature between 28 to 30&#160;°C (82 to 86&#160;°F) and a track temperature ranging from 33 to 37&#160;°C (91 to 99&#160;°F).<sup id="cite_ref-:10_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:10-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:11_33-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:11-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> All drivers started on the super soft compound tyre.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg's car had a faulty <a href="/wiki/Steering_wheel" title="Steering wheel">steering wheel</a> control due to a <a href="/wiki/Cable_harness" title="Cable harness">wiring loom</a> problem. Although he made the starting grid after a steering wheel change and computer reset, the problem persisted and he began from the pit lane.<sup id="cite_ref-wiringloom_35-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wiringloom-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> Kobayashi's car developed an oil pressure issue on the <a href="/wiki/Parade_lap" title="Parade lap">formation lap</a> and pulled off the circuit, rendering him unable to start.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_36-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> When the race began at 20:00 <a href="/wiki/Singapore_Standard_Time" title="Singapore Standard Time">Singapore Standard Time</a> (<a href="/wiki/UTC%2B08:00" title="UTC+08:00">UTC+08:00</a>),<sup id="cite_ref-:11_33-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:11-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton led the field into the first corner.<sup id="cite_ref-guardianrace_37-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-guardianrace-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo's engine briefly lost power, preventing him from passing his teammate Vettel. Alonso attempted to pass the Red Bull cars to their right but entered turn one too quickly, locked his front-left tyre, and drove onto the run-off area. He fell to third place.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Magnussen passed Massa for sixth outside of track limits at turn five, but slid sideways at turn seven while holding off teammate Button, dropping to ninth.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_39-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Button gained four positions by the end of the first lap, while Rosberg fell to 22nd over the same distance. At the end of the first lap, Hamilton led Vettel, Alonso, Ricciardo, Räikkönen, Massa, Button, Bottas, Magnussen and Kvyat.<sup id="cite_ref-40" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-40">&#91;n 1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_36-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> The stewards investigated Alonso's start but took no further action.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_39-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg left the pit lane on the second lap but was initially unable to keep up with Chilton's <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> DRS was enabled on the third lap as the field spread out. The stewards investigated Magnussen for violating track limits.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_39-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> McLaren teammates Magnussen and Button avoided a collision on lap four and the Williams duo passed them.<sup id="cite_ref-guardianrace_37-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-guardianrace-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Vettel kept Hamilton's lead constant, causing the latter to go faster by &#x200b;<span class="frac nowrap"><sup>1</sup>&#8260;<sub>2</sub></span> a second while conserving fuel.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Nico_Rosberg_2014_Singapore_FP3.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5c/Nico_Rosberg_2014_Singapore_FP3.jpg/220px-Nico_Rosberg_2014_Singapore_FP3.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5c/Nico_Rosberg_2014_Singapore_FP3.jpg/330px-Nico_Rosberg_2014_Singapore_FP3.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5c/Nico_Rosberg_2014_Singapore_FP3.jpg/440px-Nico_Rosberg_2014_Singapore_FP3.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1920" data-file-height="1280" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Nico_Rosberg_2014_Singapore_FP3.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> started from the pit lane because of a steering wiring loom fault and retired after 13 laps.</div></div></div>
<p>Rosberg overtook Chilton to move into 20th on lap five,<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> while Kvyat was told by radio to battle Magnussen after lapping 0.3 seconds faster than Magnussen in the first sector on the next lap.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_36-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg could not pass Ericsson and was more than 40 seconds behind Hamilton by that lap.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> It was announced on lap seven that there would be no further action against Magnussen. Magnussen, Kvyat and Vergne were within one second of each other and battled for ninth place.<sup id="cite_ref-guardianrace_37-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-guardianrace-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Vergne encouraged his teammate Kvyat to cede a position to him but Kvyat insisted he could pass Magnussen.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_36-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Vergne overtook teammate Kvyat for tenth on lap eight and began gaining on Magnussen. Rosberg was limited to two gears and was five seconds slower than teammate Hamilton.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton led by five seconds by the start of lap ten despite car balance problems over a single lap. Massa ran close behind Räikkönen until his first pit stop at the end of the lap as part of the Williams' team plan to pass the latter through strategy. Räikkönen made his stop on the following lap and emerged behind Massa.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Bottas entered the pit lane on lap eleven with Vettel, Alonso and Ricciardo stopping on the next lap.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> After Kvyat made his stop, he rejoined behind Rosberg but overtook him shortly after. Hamilton made his pit stop from the lead on lap 14, and retained it, narrowly in front of Button.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg was informed by radio that he could not go past 6,500 <a href="/wiki/Revolutions_per_minute" title="Revolutions per minute">revolutions per minute</a> in first gear because of a faulty pit lane limiter. He slowly entered the pit lane on lap 14 and purposely <a href="/wiki/Stall_(engine)" title="Stall (engine)">stalled</a> his car as his tyres and steering wheel were changed. Mechanics switched Rosberg's car off and he selected multiple buttons on his steering wheel to try and change gear. After almost two minutes, Rosberg's car was pushed into his garage to be retired.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_36-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_39-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Massa overtook Pérez for sixth place on the 15th lap.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Vergne incurred a five-second stop-and-go penalty on the following lap after he was deemed to have exceeded track limits.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_39-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> On lap 19 Gutiérrez retired in his garage with an engine electrical problem.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Two laps later, Chilton made an unscheduled pit stop after a wheel value punctured his front-right tyre.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-41" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-41">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Adrian_Sutil_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c0/Adrian_Sutil_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg/220px-Adrian_Sutil_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c0/Adrian_Sutil_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg/330px-Adrian_Sutil_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c0/Adrian_Sutil_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg/440px-Adrian_Sutil_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1920" data-file-height="1280" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Adrian_Sutil_2014_Singapore_FP2.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> prompted the <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a>'s deployment after colliding with <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>On lap 23, Räikkönen drew closer to Massa and the Williams team responded by bringing the latter into the pit lane for soft compound tyres for a potential strategic overtake. He rejoined in ninth behind teammate Bottas.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_39-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton pulled away from Vettel who was caught by Alonso. By the 24th lap, Alonso had the potential to pass Vettel after the pit stops. That lap, Ferrari brought Alonso into the pit lane for super soft compound tyres.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Vettel made his pit stop on lap 25 for soft compound tyres and emerged in third behind Alonso's faster Ferrari.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Massa overtook Hülkenberg on the main straight for eighth on the same lap.<sup id="cite_ref-guardianrace_37-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-guardianrace-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton made his stop for super soft compound tyres on the following lap, giving the lead to Ricciardo; his team used a large time gap to remove rubber debris on his front wing.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo took his pit stop on lap 27 returning the lead to Hamilton. Grosjean was close behind Pérez but he could not pass him.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_36-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Pérez attempted an overtake Sutil on the straight on the 30th lap,<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> and Sutil squeezed Pérez into a wall, clipping the left-front nose cone section. It slid under Pérez's wheel, littering the track with debris, and prompting the <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a>'s deployment,<sup id="cite_ref-guardianrace_37-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-guardianrace-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> to allow marshals to remove debris from the track.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Some drivers elected to make pit stops to switch onto the soft compound tyres;<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_39-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Ferrari brought their drivers in to enable them to reach the end of the race without the need for another pit stop.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton's race engineer Peter Bonnington cautioned him about the debris but Hamilton did not receive the warning in time and drove over shards of wing.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> He inquired about tyre pressures and was told no issues had been discovered.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_36-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Sutil incurred a five-second stop-and-go penalty for the collision with Pérez.<sup id="cite_ref-guardianrace_37-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-guardianrace-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> The safety car was withdrawn at the end of the 37th lap and racing resumed with Hamilton in first.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton set the race's fastest lap of 1 minute and 50.417 seconds on lap 39 to lead Vettel by 5.8 seconds.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_36-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Sutil's car developed a water leak and retired two laps later to prevent the risk of an engine failure.<sup id="cite_ref-42" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-42">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Valtteri_Bottas_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/89/Valtteri_Bottas_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg/220px-Valtteri_Bottas_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/89/Valtteri_Bottas_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg/330px-Valtteri_Bottas_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/89/Valtteri_Bottas_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg/440px-Valtteri_Bottas_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1920" data-file-height="1280" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Valtteri_Bottas_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> fell to eleventh when his tyres lost grip in the race's closing period.</div></div></div>
<p>Hamilton led Vettel by 24 seconds by the next lap and wanted to make a pit stop because he was worried about tyre wear but was advised to further grow his advantage over the next three laps.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton made his final pit stop for the soft compound tyres on lap 52. He rejoined in second behind Vettel and narrowly ahead of Ricciardo.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton quickly caught Vettel, used DRS leaving turn five, and turned right to reclaim the lead two corners later on lap 54.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Button stopped at the side of the track to retire with a <a href="/wiki/Power_box" title="Power box">power box</a> failure on the same lap.<sup id="cite_ref-43" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-43">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> His retirement promoted Räikkönen to seventh and Bottas' worn tyres slowed him, enabling Hülkenberg, Vergne and Pérez to run close behind him.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_39-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Maldonado's aspiration for his first point of 2014 was not realised when Magnussen passed him for tenth place on lap 56. Three laps later, Vergne passed Hülkenberg for eighth place.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Vergne overtook Räikkönen for seventh on the left into the first corner and then Bottas for sixth. Pérez had better traction than teammate Hülkenberg and passed him between turns 17 and 18.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Pérez passed Räikkönen into turn 10 and took seventh when Bottas lost all tyre grip and fell from the top ten quickly. Because of the safety car period, the race ended under a two-hour time limit.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_34-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> In the remaining eight laps, Hamilton distanced himself from Vettel and crossed the start/finish line after 60 laps for his seventh victory of the season, his second in Singapore, and the 29th of his career.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_38-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-44" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-44">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> Vettel finished second in his best result of the season, 13.534 seconds behind,<sup id="cite_ref-guardianrace_37-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-guardianrace-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> and successfully held off teammate Ricciardo and Alonso in the final laps. Massa finished fifth. Vergne's five-second time penalty did not affect his sixth-place finish. Pérez, Räikkönen, Hülkenberg and Magnussen rounded out the top-ten. Bottas, Maldonado, Grosjean, Kvyat and Ericsson, Bianchi and Chilton were the final finishers.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_36-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> There were four lead changes in the race; three drivers reached the front of the field. Hamilton led three times for a total of 58 laps, more than any other driver.<sup id="cite_ref-:12_45-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:12-45">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Singapore_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_won_2014_Singapore_GP.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e6/Lewis_Hamilton_won_2014_Singapore_GP.jpg/220px-Lewis_Hamilton_won_2014_Singapore_GP.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e6/Lewis_Hamilton_won_2014_Singapore_GP.jpg/330px-Lewis_Hamilton_won_2014_Singapore_GP.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e6/Lewis_Hamilton_won_2014_Singapore_GP.jpg/440px-Lewis_Hamilton_won_2014_Singapore_GP.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1920" data-file-height="1280" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_won_2014_Singapore_GP.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> celebrating his seventh victory of the season and the 29th of his career.</div></div></div>
<p>At the podium interviews, conducted by former team owner <a href="/wiki/Eddie_Jordan" title="Eddie Jordan">Eddie Jordan</a>, Hamilton said he did not feel any pressure at his final pit stop because he felt comfortable with his large lead. Vettel said his team altered their strategy before the safety car and the final laps on worn tyres were "very, very much borderline" and emphasised the challenge of the track in hot and humid weather. Ricciardo said the event felt like a "home race" and that he would watch Alonso's first lap to see whether he should have ceded another position to Vettel.<sup id="cite_ref-:13_46-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:13-46">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup> In the later press conference, Hamilton felt the race would have been "hardcore" had Rosberg not retired and knew Mercedes would not be "100 percent happy" because they aspired to secure victories collectively and be the series' most dominant team. Vettel revealed how uncomfortable he felt that he could not reach the end of the race on worn tyres but stated second was the best possible result for Red Bull. Ricciardo was satisfied to pressure his teammate Vettel but felt it was a processional event for the top three.<sup id="cite_ref-:13_46-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:13-46">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Rosberg called his race "the toughest of the year" and deemed it worse than the <a href="/wiki/2014_British_Grand_Prix" title="2014 British Grand Prix">British Grand Prix</a> where he retired with a gearbox failure. He stated that his car's reliability needed the most attention and wanted to understand the problem: "It’s tough not even leaving the grid. I was hoping the team could fix it [after the race had started] because it was going on and off, sometimes working, sometimes not. There’s no point in shouting, it’s a reliability issue, and it’s happened again."<sup id="cite_ref-47" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-47">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup> Mercedes team principal <a href="/wiki/Toto_Wolff" title="Toto Wolff">Toto Wolff</a> apologised to Rosberg immediately following the driver's retirement, saying it was "a bitter moment" to lose ground in the Drivers' Championship.<sup id="cite_ref-48" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-48">&#91;47&#93;</a></sup> He stated he did not want to see the championship decided on reliability and Mercedes needed to discover the origin of their problems.<sup id="cite_ref-:14_49-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:14-49">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup> The team's technical director <a href="/wiki/Paddy_Lowe" title="Paddy Lowe">Paddy Lowe</a> added reliability concerns worried him throughout the season and hoped it would not be repeated in the future.<sup id="cite_ref-:14_49-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:14-49">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Kevin_Magnussen_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c1/Kevin_Magnussen_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg/220px-Kevin_Magnussen_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c1/Kevin_Magnussen_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg/330px-Kevin_Magnussen_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c1/Kevin_Magnussen_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg/440px-Kevin_Magnussen_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1920" data-file-height="1280" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Kevin_Magnussen_2014_Singapore_FP1.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a> suffered from back burns and dehydration after his drinks bottle became hot because of a broken <a href="/wiki/Radiator_(engine_cooling)" title="Radiator (engine cooling)">radiator</a> seal.</div></div></div>
<p>Post-race, trainer Antti Vierula treated Magnussen for dehydration and minor burns on his lower back with an iced towel and McLaren sought a doctor who tended to Magnussen in its hospitality building.<sup id="cite_ref-:15_50-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:15-50">&#91;49&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:16_51-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:16-51">&#91;50&#93;</a></sup> His condition was caused by a broken <a href="/wiki/Radiator_(engine_cooling)" title="Radiator (engine cooling)">radiator</a> seal possibly loosened after mounting the turn seven kerbs early in the race, causing hot air to be directed into his cockpit, warming his seat and drinks bottle. He found the water in his drink bottle very hot to consume and radioed his team it burned his mouth. Magnussen raised his arms out of the cockpit to direct cool air down his sleeves and onto his back to alleviate the pain.<sup id="cite_ref-:15_50-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:15-50">&#91;49&#93;</a></sup> He called his tenth-place finish "the hardest-earnt point I've ever had" and the McLaren team principal <a href="/wiki/%C3%89ric_Boullier" title="Éric Boullier">Éric Boullier</a> said his performance under the circumstances was a "scant consolidation" and praised Magnussen for being "indicative of his tremendous fighting spirit".<sup id="cite_ref-52" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-52">&#91;51&#93;</a></sup> Magnussen posted on <a href="/wiki/Twitter" title="Twitter">Twitter</a> reports of his condition were exaggerated and said he felt "just hot and sweaty."<sup id="cite_ref-53" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-53">&#91;52&#93;</a></sup> Kvyat also suffered from dehydration when his water supply malfunctioned after &#x200b;<span class="frac nowrap"><sup>2</sup>&#8260;<sub>3</sub></span> race distance.<sup id="cite_ref-:15_50-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:15-50">&#91;49&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:16_51-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:16-51">&#91;50&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Alonso said he believed the timing of the safety car and not a strategy error prevented him from finishing on the podium: "The moment of the safety car was probably not good, but sometimes it helps and sometimes it doesn't help. Today it didn't help."<sup id="cite_ref-:17_54-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:17-54">&#91;53&#93;</a></sup> Ferrari team principal <a href="/wiki/Marco_Mattiacci" title="Marco Mattiacci">Marco Mattiacci</a> defended his team's strategy and did not believe anything specific prevented them from finishing on the podium: "Definitely the safety car didn't do any better for us, but the race is made of 60 laps. We squeezed the car as much as we could; the strategy from the pits was excellent. There were other variables we know we cannot control. We did our best."<sup id="cite_ref-:17_54-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:17-54">&#91;53&#93;</a></sup> Boullier suggested Red Bull used coded messages to assist Ricciardo when they told Ricciardo to stay off the kerbs but stressed it was the FIA's task to investigate. Red Bull team principal <a href="/wiki/Christian_Horner" title="Christian Horner">Christian Horner</a> asked Whiting for clarification because Ricciardo had battery damage from mounting the kerbs.<sup id="cite_ref-55" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-55">&#91;54&#93;</a></sup> The governing body confirmed it were satisfied with Red Bull's radio messages and said the team abided by the rules.<sup id="cite_ref-56" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-56">&#91;55&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The result regained Hamilton the lead of the World Drivers' Championship with 241 points. Rosberg's retirement dropped him to second, three points behind Hamilton. Ricciardo consolidated third place with 181 points, and Alonso and Vettel moved ahead of Bottas for fourth and fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-points_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Mercedes further extended their lead in the World Constructors' Championship to 182 points over of Red Bull in second place. Williams and Ferrari remained in third and fourth places and Force India moved in front of McLaren to move into fifth position with five races left in the season.<sup id="cite_ref-points_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
