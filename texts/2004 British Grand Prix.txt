<p>The <b>2004 British Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>LVII Foster's British Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held at the <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone Circuit</a> on 11 July 2004. It was the eleventh race of the <a href="/wiki/2004_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2004 Formula One season">2004 Formula One season</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2004_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The British Grand Prix was officially confirmed as the first of eighteen races of the <a href="/wiki/2004_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2004 Formula One World Championship">2004 Formula One World Championship</a> at an <a href="/wiki/FIA_World_Motor_Sport_Council" title="FIA World Motor Sport Council">FIA World Motor Sport Council</a> meeting in Paris on 12 December 2003.
</p><p>The race was preceded by a demonstration of contemporary Formula One cars on <a href="/wiki/Regent_Street" title="Regent Street">Regent Street</a><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> in <a href="/wiki/London" title="London">London</a>, including former British Formula One World Champion <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Mansell" title="Nigel Mansell">Nigel Mansell</a> driving the <a href="/wiki/Jordan_EJ14" title="Jordan EJ14">Jordan EJ14</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> The event attracted an estimated 500,000 spectators.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The event was also notable for the death of <a href="/wiki/Minardi" title="Minardi">Minardi</a> Sporting Director <a href="/wiki/John_Walton_(Formula_One)" title="John Walton (Formula One)">John Walton</a>, who died of a heart attack following the street demonstration, causing the Minardi team to withdraw its cars from Saturday's early practice session.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notes">Notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2004_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Notes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>This was <a href="/wiki/Marc_Gen%C3%A9" title="Marc Gené">Marc Gené</a>'s last race to date. From the <a href="/wiki/2004_German_Grand_Prix" title="2004 German Grand Prix">German Grand Prix</a> to the <a href="/wiki/2004_Italian_Grand_Prix" title="2004 Italian Grand Prix">Italian Grand Prix</a>, he was replaced by <a href="/wiki/Ant%C3%B4nio_Pizzonia" title="Antônio Pizzonia">Antônio Pizzonia</a>.</li>
<li>Kimi Raikkonen's second place was the first podium finish of the season for a <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> driver.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a> lost control of his Renault through the high-speed Bridge Corner, and hit the tyres on the inside retaining wall.  The car then barrel-rolled through the gravel before coming to a rest just after Priory. Trulli was unhurt, later saying he was "a little crazier, maybe".<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (December 2019)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup></li>
<li>Both <a href="/wiki/Minardi" title="Minardi">Minardi</a> cars raced with sponsor-free liveries, showing only the dedication "John Boy" to team manager <a href="/wiki/John_Walton_(Formula_One)" title="John Walton (Formula One)">John Walton</a>, who had died days before the race.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (December 2019)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup></li></ul>
