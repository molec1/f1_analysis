<p>The <b>2014 United States Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>2014 Formula 1 United States Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 2 November at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_of_the_Americas" title="Circuit of the Americas">Circuit of the Americas</a> in <a href="/wiki/Travis_County,_Texas" title="Travis County, Texas">Travis County</a> near <a href="/wiki/Austin,_Texas" title="Austin, Texas">Austin, Texas</a>. It was the 17th round of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2014 Formula One World Championship">2014 Formula One World Championship</a> and the 36th running of the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Grand_Prix" title="United States Grand Prix">event</a> held as part of the series. The 56-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> after starting from second position. His teammate <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> finished second and <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> was third. It was Hamilton's tenth victory of the season, his second at Austin, and the 32nd of his career.
</p><p>Rosberg won the <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> by recording the fastest lap in qualifying and kept the lead on the first lap. The race was neutralised by a four-lap <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a> period when <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a>, <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> made contact. During the first half of the race, Hamilton ran close behind teammate Rosberg to conserve fuel but braked heavily through parts of the track to prevent <a href="/wiki/Brake_fade" title="Brake fade">brake glazing</a>. After the two Mercedes cars made their <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit stops</a> for tyre changes, Hamilton passed his teammate Rosberg for the lead on lap 24, and pulled away to achieve victory and overtook <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Mansell" title="Nigel Mansell">Nigel Mansell</a> for the highest number of wins for a British driver in the history of Formula One.
</p><p>In the week before the event, the <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a> and <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia</a> teams went into administration and were granted dispensation to miss the race, leaving the sport with nine entered teams, the lowest number since the <a href="/wiki/2005_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="2005 Monaco Grand Prix">2005 Monaco Grand Prix</a>. Hamilton's victory, his fifth in a row, moved him further clear from Rosberg atop the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">World Drivers' Championship</a>, as Ricciardo's third-place finish eliminated him from title contention. <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> moved further ahead of <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> in fifth. Mercedes increased their unassailable lead in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">World Constructors' Championship</a> to 245 points over Red Bull while <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> moved further away from <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> in fourth with two races left in the season.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:CircuitOfTheAmericasAerial-July2015.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/73/CircuitOfTheAmericasAerial-July2015.jpg/220px-CircuitOfTheAmericasAerial-July2015.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/73/CircuitOfTheAmericasAerial-July2015.jpg/330px-CircuitOfTheAmericasAerial-July2015.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/73/CircuitOfTheAmericasAerial-July2015.jpg/440px-CircuitOfTheAmericasAerial-July2015.jpg 2x" data-file-width="4272" data-file-height="2848" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:CircuitOfTheAmericasAerial-July2015.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The <a href="/wiki/Circuit_of_the_Americas" title="Circuit of the Americas">Circuit of the Americas</a> <i>(pictured in 2015)</i>, where the race was held.</div></div></div>
<p>The 2014 <a href="/wiki/United_States_Grand_Prix" title="United States Grand Prix">United States Grand Prix</a> was the 17th of the 19 rounds of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2014 Formula One World Championship">2014 Formula One World Championship</a>, and the 36th running of the event in Formula One. It was held on 2 November at the 5.513-kilometre (3.426&#160;mi) 20-turn <a href="/wiki/Circuit_of_the_Americas" title="Circuit of the Americas">Circuit of the Americas</a> in <a href="/wiki/Travis_County,_Texas" title="Travis County, Texas">Travis County</a>, <a href="/wiki/Austin,_Texas" title="Austin, Texas">Austin</a>, Texas.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;n 1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-preview_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-preview-4">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The sport's governing body, the <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_l%27Automobile" title="Fédération Internationale de l&#39;Automobile">Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile</a> (FIA), elected to retain two <a href="/wiki/Drag_reduction_system" title="Drag reduction system">drag reduction system</a> (DRS) activation zones from 2013: one was on the <a href="/wiki/Straight_(racing)" title="Straight (racing)">straight</a> between turns 10 and 11, and the second was on the straight linking the final and first corners.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The circuit was changed following the 2013 race. At the request of motorcycling's governing body, the <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_Motocyclisme" title="Fédération Internationale de Motocyclisme">Fédération Internationale de Motocyclisme</a>, some sections of turn ten's asphalt <a href="/wiki/Run-off_area" title="Run-off area">run-off area</a> were replaced by gravel and some light panels were mounted closer to the ground for better visibility.<sup id="cite_ref-preview_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-preview-4">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyres" title="Formula One tyres">Tyre</a> supplier <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> brought the yellow-banded soft and white-banded medium dry compound tyres to the race,<sup id="cite_ref-stats_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-stats-6">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> as opposed to the Hard and Medium tyres in 2012 and 2013.<sup id="cite_ref-preview_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-preview-4">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The event's official name was the <i>2014 Formula 1 United States Grand Prix</i>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Before the race, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">World Drivers' Championship</a> with 291 <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Championship_points_scoring_systems" title="List of Formula One World Championship points scoring systems">points</a>, ahead of teammate <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> in second and <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> third. <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> was fourth with 145 points, with <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> two points behind in fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-points_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-8">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> While the Drivers' Championship had not been won, Mercedes had taken the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">World Constructors' Championship</a> in the preceding <a href="/wiki/2014_Russian_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Russian Grand Prix">Russian Grand Prix</a>. <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> were in second place on 342 points; <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> (216 points) and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> (188) were third and fourth and <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> were fifth with 143 points.<sup id="cite_ref-points_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-8">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After spending most the season behind Rosberg, Hamilton had won the previous four races, converting a 29-point deficit into a 17-point lead over his teammate.<sup id="cite_ref-:17_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:17-9">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> With three races left, Hamilton said he would prioritise not allowing any negative thoughts consume him and would take any opportunities presented to him: "We've just got to do our due diligence and work as hard as we can to make sure we don't have any problems."<sup id="cite_ref-:17_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:17-9">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton hoped to achieve his fifth consecutive victory of the year: "A lot of the American side of my family come to the race, plus I won the first F1 GP at the circuit back in 2012 – it's a special one for me. I really enjoy going there and I'm looking forward to another great race."<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg aimed to improve on his best finish in Austin and lower Hamilton's points lead. He said Mercedes targeted "a big finish" to the season, declaring he would not concede defeat until the final round, and hoped to maintain the level of entertainment for the fan base.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>After both Friday practice sessions, the FIA tested for the first time a new system called the <a href="/wiki/Safety_car#Virtual_Safety_Car_(VSC)" title="Safety car">Virtual Safery Car</a>, which is in response to minimising the risk of serious accidents and injuries, such as those suffered by <a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a> at the <a href="/wiki/2014_Japanese_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Japanese Grand Prix">Japanese Grand Prix</a>. Its aim is to force drivers to slow by about 35 per cent in an accident zone, with the aid of a dashboard display.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Drivers agreed it was a worthwhile system that, however, required refinement for further testing at subsequent races before it was introduced for the <a href="/wiki/2015_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2015 Formula One World Championship">2015</a> season.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a> and <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia</a> were each granted permission by Formula One's commercial rights owner <a href="/wiki/Bernie_Ecclestone" title="Bernie Ecclestone">Bernie Ecclestone</a> to miss the race due to their long-term financial problems and entering <a href="/wiki/Administration_(law)" title="Administration (law)">administration</a>. This was the smallest field entered into a Formula One race since the <a href="/wiki/2005_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="2005 Monaco Grand Prix">2005 Monaco Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> There were driver changes for the first free practice session. <a href="/wiki/2014_FIA_Formula_3_European_Championship" title="2014 FIA Formula 3 European Championship">European Formula Three Championship</a> third-place finisher <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> replaced <a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a> at <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a> for the second time in 2014, and Williams reserve driver <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Nasr" title="Felipe Nasr">Felipe Nasr</a> drove Bottas's car.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportfp1_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportfp1-15">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The press reported that the <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a> and <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> teams had considered withdrawing from the race to signal to Formula One's commercial leaders about its financial state following Caterham and Marussia's non-participation in the race.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-16">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> Ecclestone said if two of those squads left the series then "that is what will happen" and teams should only participate if they were competitive.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-17">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> Force India team principal Bob Fernley stated discussions about a boycott were as a final recourse.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-18">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Lotus owner <a href="/wiki/G%C3%A9rard_Lopez_(businessman)" title="Gérard Lopez (businessman)">Gérard Lopez</a> said he had not heard about the proposed boycott and the team insisted that they would race.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-17">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Vijay_Mallya" title="Vijay Mallya">Vijay Mallya</a>, the owner of Force India, severely criticised suggestions of a boycott six days after the race: "Who said that we wanted to boycott the race? Please tell me. If I am wrong, you educate me. I speak on behalf of Force India. I have never said that we are going to boycott, and I have clarified this in Austin. Why would we? We have come to go racing."<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;n 2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Three practice sessions—two on Friday and a third on Saturday—were held before the Sunday race. The Friday morning and afternoon sessions lasted ninety minutes each; the third, one-hour session was held on Saturday morning.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-21">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton set the fastest time in the first practice session—which took place on a damp track that later dried—of 1 minute, 39.941 seconds;<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> his session 18 minutes ended early due to a gear shift problem.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> His teammate Rosberg, <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a>, <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>, Vettel, Nasr, <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> and Verstappen occupied positions two to ten.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportfp1_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportfp1-15">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo was only able to participate in the session's first &#x200b;<span class="frac nowrap"><sup>3</sup>&#8260;<sub>4</sub></span> of an hour because his <a href="/wiki/Kinetic_energy_recovery_system" title="Kinetic energy recovery system">energy recovery system</a> failed and was restricted to five timed laps.<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the second session, where it was difficult for teams to alter their car's race set-ups because of strong <a href="/wiki/Crosswind" title="Crosswind">crosswinds</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-:3_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-26">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton set the day's fastest lap at 1 minute, 39.085 seconds on super-soft compound tyres; Rosberg was three-thousands of a second slower in second and had the fastest time until Hamilton's lap.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-27">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Both Mercedes cars had reliability concerns with Hamilton reporting another <a href="/wiki/Transmission_(mechanics)" title="Transmission (mechanics)">gearbox</a> problem and his session ended early with a minor hydraulic issue. Rosberg had a downshifting concern which he attributed to hitting the <a href="/wiki/Car_controls" title="Car controls">clutch pedal</a> after lifting his leg.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_26-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-26">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> Alonso was third-fastest, followed by Ricciardo, <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>—who locked his brakes lightly and later spun his car—and <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>. Kvyat, Magnussen, Button and Hülkenberg followed in the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_27-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-27">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> and his teammate <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> both lost control of their tight-handling Lotus cars and went off onto the <a href="/wiki/Run-off_area" title="Run-off area">run-off area</a> several times.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_26-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-26">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hamilton was fastest in the third session, which was held in chilly but bright weather,<sup id="cite_ref-:5_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-28">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> with a time of 1 minute, 37.107 seconds; Rosberg had a brake issue until the final minutes and went faster in the first and second sectors but an untidy final sector caused by a <a href="/wiki/Brake_fade" title="Brake fade">glazed left rear brake disc</a> only allowed him to improve his best time by one-thousands of a second and was second-fastest.<sup id="cite_ref-:6_29-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-29">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:7_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:7-30">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Massa, Bottas, Alonso, Ricciardo, Hülkenberg, <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a>, Räikkönen and Button completed the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-:6_29-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-29">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Upon switching settings on his steering wheel, Hamilton spun under braking at turn 12,<sup id="cite_ref-:7_30-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:7-30">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> and Rosberg ran wide at turns 11 and 12.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_28-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-28">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> Vergne's session ended early when his engine cut out.<sup id="cite_ref-:6_29-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-29">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_(cropped).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/51/Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg/170px-Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="206" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/51/Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg/255px-Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/51/Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg/340px-Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1890" data-file-height="2285" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_(cropped).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> had the 13th <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> of his career.</div></div></div>
<p>Following the withdrawal of the Caterham and Marussia team's two entries, minor changes were made to Saturday afternoon's three-part <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#Q" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">qualifying</a> session.<sup id="cite_ref-:8_31-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-31">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> The first part ran for 18 minutes, eliminating cars that were 15th or below. The <a href="/wiki/107%25_rule" title="107% rule">107% rule</a> was in effect, requiring drivers to reach a time within 107 per cent of the quickest lap to qualify. The second part lasted 15 minutes, eliminating cars in 11th to 14th. The final 12-minute session determined <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> to tenth.<sup id="cite_ref-:8_31-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-31">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> Cars who progressed to the final session were not allowed to change tyres for the race's start, using the tyres with which they set their quickest lap times in the second session.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_21-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-21">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg was fastest in the second and final sessions and achieved his ninth pole position of 2014, his first in the United States, and the 13th of his career, with a lap of 1 minute, 36.067 seconds.<sup id="cite_ref-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-32">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> He praised the balance of his car but said it was challenging due to track and wind direction changes.<sup id="cite_ref-:9_33-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:9-33">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg was joined on the <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#G" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">grid</a>'s front row by teammate Hamilton,<sup id="cite_ref-:10_34-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:10-34">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> who had a left-front brake 100&#160;°C (212&#160;°F) cooler than the right-front, slowing him,<sup id="cite_ref-:9_33-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:9-33">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> which was observed by him constant locking them in the second session.<sup id="cite_ref-:11_35-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:11-35">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> Bottas and Massa were third and fourth, and RIcciardo improved greatly on his final timed lap for fifth, ahead Alonso in sixth.<sup id="cite_ref-:10_34-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:10-34">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Button qualified seventh,<sup id="cite_ref-:10_34-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:10-34">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> but incurred a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_regulations#Penalties" title="Formula One regulations">five-place grid penalty</a> for changing his gearbox,<sup id="cite_ref-36" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-36">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> promoting his teammate Magnussen to seventh.<sup id="cite_ref-:10_34-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:10-34">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Although Räikkönen in eighth improved his car's balance, he slid multiple times after a unrectifiable frontal problem, creating <a href="/wiki/Understeer_and_oversteer" title="Understeer and oversteer">understeer</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:12_37-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:12-37">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> Sutil took ninth and Maldonado, who was the fastest driver not to advance into the final session, was tenth,<sup id="cite_ref-qual_38-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qual-38">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> after sliding in turn 19, losing about two-tenths of a second, preventing an improvement.<sup id="cite_ref-:10_34-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:10-34">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a>, 11th, used two sets of soft tyres in the first session to get into the second.<sup id="cite_ref-:12_37-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:12-37">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> Hülkenberg, 13th, was forecast to qualify in the top ten until a tear-off visor wrapped itself around a front tyre into turn 12, losing <a href="/wiki/Grip_(auto_racing)" title="Grip (auto racing)">grip</a>, ran wide, and lost time.<sup id="cite_ref-:12_37-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:12-37">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> Kvyat complained of tyre temperature issues in slower traffic on his <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#O" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">out-lap</a>. However, he incurred a ten-place grid penalty for changing an engine component.<sup id="cite_ref-:10_34-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:10-34">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> His teammate Vergne started 14th and was the fastest driver not to enter the second session,<sup id="cite_ref-qual_38-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qual-38">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> attributing the result to switching to an older, less powerful engine and excess oversteer.<sup id="cite_ref-:12_37-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:12-37">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban Gutiérrez">Esteban Gutiérrez</a> took 15th ahead of Grosjean.<sup id="cite_ref-:12_37-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:12-37">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> Vettel in 17th opted to do one lap on soft tyres to comply with the 107 per cent rule.<sup id="cite_ref-:11_35-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:11-35">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> He began from the pit lane after changing his engine, his sixth of the season.<sup id="cite_ref-:10_34-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:10-34">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Sergio_Perez_2011_Malaysia.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/4b/Sergio_Perez_2011_Malaysia.jpg/170px-Sergio_Perez_2011_Malaysia.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="263" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/4b/Sergio_Perez_2011_Malaysia.jpg/255px-Sergio_Perez_2011_Malaysia.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/4b/Sergio_Perez_2011_Malaysia.jpg/340px-Sergio_Perez_2011_Malaysia.jpg 2x" data-file-width="800" data-file-height="1238" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Sergio_Perez_2011_Malaysia.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> <i>(pictured in 2011)</i> retired on the first lap following a collision with <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>The race began before 107,778 people at 14:00 <a href="/wiki/Central_Time_Zone" title="Central Time Zone">Central Time Zone</a> (<a href="/wiki/UTC%E2%88%9206:00" title="UTC−06:00">UTC−06:00</a>), in dry and sunny weather with an air temperature of 23&#160;°C (73&#160;°F) and a track temperature between 31 to 34&#160;°C (88 to 93&#160;°F).<sup id="cite_ref-39" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-39">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-40" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-40">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Every driver, bar Vettel, Kvyat and Hülkenberg, started on the soft compound tyres. The trio began on the medium compound tyres. A strong cross wind cutting through turns three and five caused cars to oversteer. When the race began, Rosberg maintained the lead into the first corner. Hamilton. who put his car at an angle towards Rosberg, stayed in second. <a href="/wiki/Wheelspin" title="Wheelspin">Wheelspin</a> dropped Bottas behind his teammate Massa.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_41-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-41">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Alonso attempted to pass Bottas into turn two for fourth but slowed in doing so. Ricciardo also had wheelspin,<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> and fell behind the Ferrari and McLaren cars. He remained on the <a href="/wiki/Racing_line" title="Racing line">outside</a> and overtook Button, Räikkönen and Magnussen before turn 12.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_41-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-41">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Pérez glimpsed space to Sutil's left but the extra speed needed to drive alongside caused him to slide into the rear of Räikkönen's car. Räikkonen was able to continue but Pérez ploughed into Sutil. As Sutil spin, he hit Pérez again; both their front wheels interlocked and damaged their <a href="/wiki/Suspension_(vehicle)" class="mw-redirect" title="Suspension (vehicle)">suspensions</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_41-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-41">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sutil's race was over but Pérez drove his damaged car into the pit lane to retire.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_41-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-41">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> The incident necessitated the safety car's deployment to allow track <a href="/wiki/Motorsport_marshal" title="Motorsport marshal">marshals</a> to remove debris.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Both McLaren cars, Hülkenberg, Gutiérrez and Vettel made pit stops for the medium tyres.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_41-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-41">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> The safety car was withdrawn at the end of lap four and racing resumed with Rosberg leading Hamilton.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> The duo pulled away from Massa in third who held off his teammate Bottas.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Alonso locked his rear wheels,<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_44-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-44">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> and Ricciardo switched lines to pass him for fifth as he narrowly avoided hitting the rear of Bottas's car. Alonso and Ricciardo were alongside through turn two with Ricciardo keeping fifth by driving onto the <a href="/wiki/Artificial_turf" title="Artificial turf">artificial grass</a>, causing Alonso to slow through the turn three and four switchback.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_41-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-41">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Räikkönen held off Maldonado's attempts to pass as Vergne unsuccessfully tried to overtake Maldonado into turn one.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On the seventh lap, the stewards placed Vergne, Maldonado, Button and Gutiérrez under investigation for speeding behind the safety car.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_44-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-44">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> The Mercedes duo ran close behind each other with Hamilton remaining behind to conserve fuel, and observe the comparison between himself and Rosberg. The tight confines of the second half of the lap gave Hamilton difficulty entering the DRS activation zone before turn 11 and his left-front brake was 100&#160;°C (212&#160;°F) cooler because the safety car was slower. He braked heavily at certain corners to prevent glazing.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_41-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-41">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Grosjean steered right to try and pass Vergne for ninth on the tenth lap but the latter blocked him.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> It was announced on the following lap no further action would be taken against Button although Vergne, Gutiérrez and Maldonado were imposed five-second stop-and-go penalties to be taken at their next pit stops.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo ran close behind Bottas but was not fast enough to pass.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_41-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-41">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_(04).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/0b/Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2804%29.jpg/170px-Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2804%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="255" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/0b/Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2804%29.jpg/255px-Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2804%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/0b/Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2804%29.jpg/340px-Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2804%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2425" data-file-height="3637" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_(04).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> finished third after getting ahead of both Williams drivers following their <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit stops</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>Red Bull elected to bring Ricciardo into the pit lane for the medium compound tyres on lap 14.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Bottas was vulnerable to Ricciardo passing him and his pace on the lap allowed Ricciardo to draw alongside into turn one after his pit stop. Bottas gained momentum to pass Ricciardo on the outside for fourth place.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_41-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-41">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg made his pit stop from the lead on lap 15 and fell behind teammate Hamilton.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Bottas used DRS to attack Ricciardo into turn 12 but the latter defended.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton was slower than Rosberg and made his pit stop (lap 17) for a front wing adjustment and rejoined with a larger lead over his teammate.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_41-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-41">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_44-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-44">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo continued to hold off the faster Bottas who closed up to him into turn 12.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> On lap 18, Hülkenberg lost engine power and Sauber instructed him to stop the car at the side of the track on the backstraight to retire.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_44-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-44">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-45" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-45">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> Alonso overtook Vettel for ninth on the next lap.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hamilton gained on Rosberg enough to use DRS by lap 22. On the following lap Hamilton lined up a pass on his teammate on the back straight but did not get ahead.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_41-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-41">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> On lap 24, Rosberg selected a setting intended to give him extra power to defend against Hamilton of which he did not receive.<sup id="cite_ref-46" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-46">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton <a href="/wiki/Drafting_(aerodynamics)" title="Drafting (aerodynamics)">slipstreamed</a> Rosberg on the backstraight, used DRS, and drove alongside his teammate going into turn 12.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg attempted to defend his position but Hamilton steered left to overtake him for the lead.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton forced Rosberg onto the run-off area and both drivers narrowly avoided a collision as Hamilton began to pull away from his teammate.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_41-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-41">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Alonso batted Button in the past two laps and overtook him on the outside for seventh place on that lap. Vergne attacked Grosjean on lap 25 after the latter could not overtake the slower Vettel. Grosjean tried again on the next lap and passed Vettel for ninth at the first turn. Alonso overtook Magnussen for sixth with a switchback move at turn one.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Over the next two laps, Grosjean and Vergne demoted Button to tenth.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_44-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-44">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Massa kept reasonably between both Mercedes cars and was 2.4 seconds ahead of Ricciardo. Williams elected to make a pit stop for the medium compound tyres for Bottas at the end of lap 30 and Ricciardo responded by going seven-tenths of a second faster than Bottas in the final third of the lap.<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_41-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-41">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo made his pit stop on the following lap and emerged ahead of Bottas. He began matching Massa's pace before the latter made a problematic pit stop on lap 32:<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> his mechanics were delayed for one second in the installation of a front tyres,<sup id="cite_ref-motorsportmagrace_41-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-motorsportmagrace-41">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> promoting Ricciardo to third.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_44-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-44">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton entered the pit lane from the lead on the 33rd for his final stop and rejoined in second, behind teammate Rosberg, whom Mercedes told to go faster. Rosberg led lap 34 before making his pit stop, returning the lead to Hamilton.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Vettel overtook Grosjean for tenth on lap 37 and drew close to Magnussen soon after.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> He overtook Magnussen on the outside three laps later but was unable to slow sufficiently and the latter retook ninth.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c3/Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg/170px-Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="237" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c3/Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg/255px-Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c3/Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg/340px-Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg 2x" data-file-width="784" data-file-height="1094" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> took the 32nd victory of his career, becoming the most successful British driver in Formula One history.</div></div></div>
<p>On lap 41, Maldonado incurred a five-second stop-and-go penalty for speeding in the pit lane.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Vettel braked later than Magnussen and passed him for ninth on the next lap. Alonso made a pit stop for soft compound tyres on lap 43 and emerged in seventh behind Vettel.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_44-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-44">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> He immediately got involved in a three-way battle between Vettel and Magnussen for sixth to eighth.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_44-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-44">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> Vettel was unhappy with how his car handled on worn tyres and Alonso passed him with DRS for sixth on lap 46.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_44-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-44">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg went &#x200b;<span class="frac nowrap"><sup>1</sup>&#8260;<sub>2</sub></span> a second quicker than teammate Hamilton to be 1.9 seconds behind at the start of the next lap and Hamilton was radioed to be cautious about tearing up a loose piece of artificial grass exiting turn 19.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> As Rosberg sought to close up more, Hamilton went quicker to extend his lead by four-tenths of a second.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_44-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-44">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> Williams radioed Massa to close up to Ricciardo and went &#x200b;<span class="frac nowrap"><sup>1</sup>&#8260;<sub>2</sub></span> a second quicker on lap 49.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Vergne and Grosjean made contact at turn one with the former taking ninth on the inside on lap 50, as Maldonado passed his teammate Grosjean for tenth.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_44-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-44">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On lap 52, an error from Kvyat allowed Räikkönen to pass him. Button lost eighth to Vergne on the same lap and fell behind Vettel on lap 54. Vettel overtook Vergne for ninth on the next lap.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_43-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-43">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Slower traffic delayed Hamilton, reducing his lead to 2.1 seconds when the final lap began, but his teammate Rosberg was also baulked, allowing Hamilton to win by 4.3 seconds. Ricciardo finished third, 1.3 seconds ahead of Massa in fourth. Bottas, Alonso, Vettel, Magnussen, Maldonado finished in positions five to nine.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Vergne finished tenth after a five-second time penalty for the collision with Grosjean.<sup id="cite_ref-:13_47-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:13-47">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup> Grosjean, Button, Räikkönen, Gutiérrez and Kvyat were the final classified finishers.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_42-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-42">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> It was Hamilton's tenth win of 2014, his second in Austin, and the 32nd of his career, breaking <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Mansell" title="Nigel Mansell">Nigel Mansell</a>'s record for the highest number of race victories for a British driver.<sup id="cite_ref-49" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-49">&#91;n 3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-50" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-50">&#91;47&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-upiwin_48-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-upiwin-48">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton also became the first British driver since Mansell to win five races in a row.<sup id="cite_ref-:14_51-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:14-51">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup> There were five lead changes in the race; two drivers reached the front of the field. Hamilton led three times for a total of 33 laps, more than any other driver.<sup id="cite_ref-results_52-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-results-52">&#91;49&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At the podium interviews, conducted by former driver <a href="/wiki/Mario_Andretti" title="Mario Andretti">Mario Andretti</a>, Hamilton was grateful to be at the front of the field and said it was "such a privilege" to represent his nation. Rosberg said he found a rhythm he liked when Hamilton passed him. Ricciardo thanked his team for fiving him with a podium-contending car and was "really happy" to finish third. In the later press conference, Hamilton said upon passing Rosberg, he controlled the pace by managing tyre wear, and was unhindered by brake locking. Rosberg stated there were no lasting problems after driving over the kerbs but was fully committed to closing up to his teammate. Ricciardo said he felt his team's strategy allowed him to pass the Williams cars and believed he was at fault for his slow start, which he intended to correct before the <a href="/wiki/2014_Brazilian_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Brazilian Grand Prix">Brazilian Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-53" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-53">&#91;50&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Festival_automobile_international_2012_-_Photocall_-_Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne_-_006.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/22/Festival_automobile_international_2012_-_Photocall_-_Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne_-_006.jpg/170px-Festival_automobile_international_2012_-_Photocall_-_Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne_-_006.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="255" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/22/Festival_automobile_international_2012_-_Photocall_-_Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne_-_006.jpg/255px-Festival_automobile_international_2012_-_Photocall_-_Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne_-_006.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/22/Festival_automobile_international_2012_-_Photocall_-_Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne_-_006.jpg/340px-Festival_automobile_international_2012_-_Photocall_-_Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne_-_006.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3168" data-file-height="4752" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Festival_automobile_international_2012_-_Photocall_-_Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne_-_006.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a> <i>(pictured in 2012)</i> was demoted to tenth for colliding with <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> and was handed one <a href="/wiki/FIA_Super_Licence" title="FIA Super Licence">super licence</a> penalty point.</div></div></div>
<p>Along with his five-second time penalty for forcing Grosjean off the track, Vergne incurred one penalty point on his <a href="/wiki/FIA_Super_Licence" title="FIA Super Licence">super licence</a>, his third of the season.<sup id="cite_ref-:13_47-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:13-47">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup> Vergne said he wanted to move up the field late on, and perceived the contact with Grosjean as part of motor sport.<sup id="cite_ref-54" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-54">&#91;51&#93;</a></sup> Grosjean stated it was "really frustrating" not to finish in the top ten, and that the collision with Vergne damaged his front wing and floor, making it difficult to control for the rest of the race.<sup id="cite_ref-55" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-55">&#91;52&#93;</a></sup> For the contact with Sutil and Räikkonen, a seven-place grid penalty was imposed on Pérez for the Brazilian Grand Prix and two penalty points on his super licence.<sup id="cite_ref-:15_56-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:15-56">&#91;53&#93;</a></sup> Pérez described the situation as "unfortunate" and did not anticipate Räikkönen being in the place that he was.<sup id="cite_ref-:15_56-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:15-56">&#91;53&#93;</a></sup> Sutil was frustrated over the crash, which prevented him from scoring Sauber's first points of 2014, describing it "such a shame and so disappointing" and called the incident "unnecessary": "We were hoping for a great result today and because of a silly action like this we missed everything."<sup id="cite_ref-:15_56-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:15-56">&#91;53&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Räikkonen disliked the situation he found himself in during the race with a three pit stop strategy, complaining of heavy front tyre degradation and excess understeer. He told the press neither he nor his team identified the source of the problem but appreciated assistance: "To try and drive slowly in certain places didn't make any difference so I don't understand it really. Wherever we finished it's pretty disappointing. Not being able to solve certain issues is frustrating. Hopefully something comes up and we find what the issues are."<sup id="cite_ref-:16_57-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:16-57">&#91;54&#93;</a></sup> Similarly, Alonso complained his car had severe tyre vibrations in the final two laps, reducing his visibility: "I tried to control the pace. I was asking for a gap from behind to cross the line just in front of them. But then Vettel overtook [Kevin] Magnussen and there was no time to save any more so on the last lap I was flat out with huge vibrations."<sup id="cite_ref-:16_57-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:16-57">&#91;54&#93;</a></sup> The Williams performance chief <a href="/wiki/Rob_Smedley" title="Rob Smedley">Rob Smedley</a> suspected minor issues such as Massa's slow pit stop prevented his team from finishing on the podium.<sup id="cite_ref-58" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-58">&#91;55&#93;</a></sup> Red Bull team principal <a href="/wiki/Christian_Horner" title="Christian Horner">Christian Horner</a> said Vettel's first lap pit stop to switch to the medium tyres was done with the intention of not using soft tyres for the rest of the race.<sup id="cite_ref-59" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-59">&#91;56&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The result increased Hamilton's lead over Rosberg in the World Drivers' Championship to 24 points.<sup id="cite_ref-points_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-8">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo remained in third but by finishing third was excluded from title contention.<sup id="cite_ref-:14_51-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:14-51">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup> Bottas in fourth moved another four points clear of Vettel in fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-points_8-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-8">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Mercedes further extended its unassailable lead in the World Constructors' Championship to 245 points over Red Bull in second. Williams and Ferrari remained in third and fourth and McLaren were fifth on 147 points with two races left in the season.<sup id="cite_ref-points_8-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-8">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
