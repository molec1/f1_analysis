<p>The <b>2010 Chinese Grand Prix</b> (officially the <b>VII Chinese Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held at the Shanghai International Circuit in the <a href="/wiki/Jiading_District" title="Jiading District">Jiading District</a> of <a href="/wiki/Shanghai" title="Shanghai">Shanghai</a> on 18 April 2010. Approximately 85,000 people attended the event. It was the fourth race of the <a href="/wiki/2010_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2010 Formula One World Championship">2010 Formula One World Championship</a> and the seventh running of the <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Grand_Prix" title="Chinese Grand Prix">race</a>. The 56-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> from a fifth position start. His teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> finished in second and <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> team was third.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> won the eighth <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> of his career by posting the fastest lap in qualifying. <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> for <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, started from third, and passed both Red Bull cars into the first corner. However, the stewards deemed that he had <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#J" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">jumped the start</a> and incurred a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_regulations#Penalties" title="Formula One regulations">drive-through penalty</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a> was deployed on the first lap, after a three-car collision at turn six, and remained out for four laps. Rosberg led from laps five to 19 until Button overtook him. Intermittent rain made track conditions slippery though Button maintained the lead for the rest of the race to secure his second victory of the season and the ninth of his career.
</p><p>Button's win promoted him to the lead of the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> for the first time in the season from Ferrari driver <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>. Rosberg moved to second while Alonso dropped to third and tied on <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Championship_points_scoring_systems" title="List of Formula One World Championship points scoring systems">points</a> with Hamilton. Vettel fell from joint third to fifth in the standings. The lead of the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a> changed from Ferrari to McLaren as Red Bull maintained a hold on third place with 15 races remaining in the season.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2010_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Shanghai_International_Circuit,_April_7,_2018_SkySat_(rotated).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d6/Shanghai_International_Circuit%2C_April_7%2C_2018_SkySat_%28rotated%29.jpg/220px-Shanghai_International_Circuit%2C_April_7%2C_2018_SkySat_%28rotated%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="141" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d6/Shanghai_International_Circuit%2C_April_7%2C_2018_SkySat_%28rotated%29.jpg/330px-Shanghai_International_Circuit%2C_April_7%2C_2018_SkySat_%28rotated%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d6/Shanghai_International_Circuit%2C_April_7%2C_2018_SkySat_%28rotated%29.jpg/440px-Shanghai_International_Circuit%2C_April_7%2C_2018_SkySat_%28rotated%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2557" data-file-height="1637" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Shanghai_International_Circuit,_April_7,_2018_SkySat_(rotated).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The <a href="/wiki/Shanghai_International_Circuit" title="Shanghai International Circuit">Shanghai International Circuit</a> <i>(pictured in 2018)</i>, where the race was held.</div></div></div>
<p>The 2010 <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Grand_Prix" title="Chinese Grand Prix">Chinese Grand Prix</a> was the fourth of nineteen scheduled <a href="/wiki/Open-wheel_car" title="Open-wheel car">single seater</a> races of the <a href="/wiki/2010_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2010 Formula One World Championship">2010 Formula One World Championship</a> and the seventh running of the event. It was held at the 16-turn 5.451&#160;km (3.387&#160;mi) <a href="/wiki/Shanghai_International_Circuit" title="Shanghai International Circuit">Shanghai International Circuit</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Jiading_District" title="Jiading District">Jiading District</a> of <a href="/wiki/Shanghai" title="Shanghai">Shanghai</a> on 18 April.<sup id="cite_ref-CF1China10_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-CF1China10-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-FIAPreview_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FIAPreview-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyre" class="mw-redirect" title="Formula One tyre">Tyre</a> supplier <a href="/wiki/Bridgestone" title="Bridgestone">Bridgestone</a> brought four tyre types to the race: the soft "options" and hard "primes" dry compounds and the intermediate and full <a href="/wiki/Rain_tyre" title="Rain tyre">wet-weather compounds</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The soft compounds were denoted by a green stripe on their side-walls; the wet compound tyres were identified by a green line at the bottom of their central groove.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Going into the race, <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> with 39 <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Championship_points_scoring_systems" title="List of Formula One World Championship points scoring systems">points</a>, two ahead of his teammate <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> in second and jointly held the position with the leading <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>, who had won the <a href="/wiki/2010_Malaysian_Grand_Prix" title="2010 Malaysian Grand Prix">Malaysian Grand Prix</a> two weeks earlier. <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> of <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> were tied for fourth with 35 points.<sup id="cite_ref-points_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Ferrari led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a> with 76 points and McLaren were a further ten points behind in second. Red Bull stood in third place with 61 points, Mercedes were fourth with 44 points and <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a> rounded out the top five with 30 points.<sup id="cite_ref-points_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Button had won the <a href="/wiki/2010_Australian_Grand_Prix" title="2010 Australian Grand Prix">Australian Grand Prix</a> and his teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> finished sixth in the previous two races of the season. Despite this, the McLaren team principal <a href="/wiki/Martin_Whitmarsh" title="Martin Whitmarsh">Martin Whitmarsh</a> said that the team did not meet its targets but sought to establish themselves at the front of the field, "One of the greatest qualities of this team is its considerable strength in depth. After establishing a strong and solid start to the season, we're confident we'll become an even more formidable contender as we head into China and then the start of the European season."<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Others teams in the field predicted the team would have an advantage with the <a href="/wiki/McLaren_MP4-25" title="McLaren MP4-25">MP4-25s</a> F-duct (a device which increases the top speed of a car by stalling the <a href="/wiki/Spoiler_(car)" title="Spoiler (car)">rear wing</a>), though Hamilton said he expected Red Bull and Ferrari to challenge them,<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> while Button stated he was uncertain whether his car would be quick enough in qualifying trim but felt it was fast for the race, "I feel that when I get to Barcelona, I'll have everything that I want to be 100 per cent. Where I am at the moment is already very good, I'm very comfortable with the car and everyone in the team."<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Two weeks before the race, the governing body of motor racing, the <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_l%27Automobile" title="Fédération Internationale de l&#39;Automobile">Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile</a> (FIA), wrote to all of the teams that any system discovered to make adjustments to the <a href="/wiki/Suspension_(vehicle)" class="mw-redirect" title="Suspension (vehicle)">suspension</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ride_height" title="Ride height">ride height</a> of a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_car" title="Formula One car">car</a> to achieve an aerodynamic advantage while in <i><a href="/wiki/Parc_ferm%C3%A9" title="Parc fermé">parc fermé</a></i> conditions after the end of qualifying and before the start of a race would be outlawed under <i>Article 34.5 of the F1 Sporting Regulations</i> and <i>Article 3.15 of the Technical Regulations</i>. It came after McLaren suggested that Red Bull had used a ride height device on the <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_RB6" title="Red Bull RB6">RB6</a> at the preceding Malaysian Grand Prix, which the latter outfit denied.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The team's engineering director <a href="/wiki/Paddy_Lowe" title="Paddy Lowe">Paddy Lowe</a> stated that they had stopped development on its own suspension system after clarification was confirmed, "We were aware over the last few months of a different approach to it [the suspension system]; an approach which historically we hadn't thought to be the typical interpretation [of the regulations], and we were reacting to that."<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Several teams made modifications to their cars in preparation for the event. Red Bull installed a small vertical exhaust duct to the rear of the RB6 to stop hot air from hitting the <a href="/wiki/Exhaust_system" title="Exhaust system">exhaust</a> onto its rear wheels and better direct airflow towards the <a href="/wiki/Diffuser_(automotive)" title="Diffuser (automotive)">diffuser</a>'s side channel.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Mercedes introduced a modified rear wing controlled by pressure sensors to feed airflow through two small openings and onto the back of its flap.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Ferrari brought a revised aerodynamic package for the <a href="/wiki/Ferrari_F10" title="Ferrari F10">F10</a>, which included a new frontal and curved splitter, an altered diffuser, and an extra vane to straighten the flow of air beside the splitter.<sup id="cite_ref-ChinaREngineering_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ChinaREngineering-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> During the Friday practice sessions, Alonso tested a modified rear wing, which directed air onto its flap and onto the diffuser's leading edge via a curved pipe.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> and Renault teams changed the end plates on both of their car's front wings as the latter constructor opted to mount small vortex generators behind the <a href="/wiki/Rear-view_mirror" title="Rear-view mirror">rear-view mirrors</a> to the side of the <a href="/wiki/Cockpit" title="Cockpit">cockpit</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ChinaREngineering_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ChinaREngineering-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2010_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Buemi_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_(cropped).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/14/Buemi_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_%28cropped%29.jpg/220px-Buemi_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_%28cropped%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="130" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/14/Buemi_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_%28cropped%29.jpg/330px-Buemi_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_%28cropped%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/14/Buemi_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_%28cropped%29.jpg/440px-Buemi_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_%28cropped%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="814" data-file-height="482" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Buemi_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_(cropped).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/S%C3%A9bastien_Buemi" title="Sébastien Buemi">Sébastien Buemi</a> had the front wheels shear from his <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a> during the first practice session.</div></div></div>
<p>Three practice sessions – two on Friday and a third on Saturday – were held before the race on Sunday. The Friday morning and afternoon sessions lasted ninety minutes each; the third, one-hour session was held on Saturday morning.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> In the first practice session, held in hazy and cold weather conditions, Button was fastest with a lap of 1 minute and 36.677 seconds, with his teammate Hamilton third. They were separated by Rosberg in second and <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> in the other Mercedes was fourth. Vettel was fifth-fastest, ahead of the Renaults of <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> and <a href="/wiki/Vitaly_Petrov" title="Vitaly Petrov">Vitaly Petrov</a>. Red Bull's <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a>, <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> for <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> and Massa followed in eighth to tenth.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportFP1_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportFP1-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> Alonso stopped at the exit of turn six after 54 minutes with a right-hand exhaust bank bay fire that prevented him from recording a lap.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportFP1_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportFP1-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-ITVFP1_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ITVFP1-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>With ten minutes remaining, the front wheels on <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A9bastien_Buemi" title="Sébastien Buemi">Sébastien Buemi</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a> sheared under braking the turn 14 <a href="/wiki/Hairpin_turn" title="Hairpin turn">hairpin</a> due to the right-front upright failing, causing the front-right wheel to break free. The front-left wheel followed suit when its upright section was suddenly forced to bear the entire load of the front. He was pitched into a <a href="/wiki/Run-off_area" title="Run-off area">gravel trap</a> and a barrier at high speed. Although Buemi was unhurt, the FIA race director <a href="/wiki/Charlie_Whiting" title="Charlie Whiting">Charlie Whiting</a> stopped the session for debris clearing and his teammate <a href="/wiki/Jaime_Alguersuari" title="Jaime Alguersuari">Jaime Alguersuari</a> was kept in the garage while the team investigated the cause of the incident.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Virgin_Racing" title="Virgin Racing">Virgin</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Timo_Glock" title="Timo Glock">Timo Glock</a>'s front wing shattered and the <a href="/wiki/Team_Lotus_(2010%E2%80%9311)" title="Team Lotus (2010–11)">Lotus</a> of <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a> shed its diffuser, both through bottoming out on a bump approaching turn one.<sup id="cite_ref-ITVFP1_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ITVFP1-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Petrov_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_(cropped).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e7/Petrov_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_%28cropped%29.jpg/220px-Petrov_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_%28cropped%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="122" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e7/Petrov_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_%28cropped%29.jpg/330px-Petrov_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_%28cropped%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e7/Petrov_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_%28cropped%29.jpg/440px-Petrov_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_%28cropped%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="764" data-file-height="423" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Petrov_Chinese_GP_qualy_2010_(cropped).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Vitaly_Petrov" title="Vitaly Petrov">Vitaly Petrov</a> crashed his <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a> heavily at the final corner in the final practice session.</div></div></div>
<p>McLaren continued to run strong with Hamilton setting the fastest lap of the day in the second practice session of 1 minute and 35.217 seconds on the soft compound tyres and his teammate Button was third. The Mercedes of Rosberg and Schumacher duplicated their first practice session results in second and fourth. Vettel, Webber, Sutil, Alguersuari, Kubica and Alonso followed in the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> During the session, where several drivers pirouetted and ran off the circuit, Buemi was unable to set a lap time because his car was rebuilt by Toro Rosso and <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> stopped his Lotus at the side of the track towards the conclusion of practice with fading <a href="/wiki/Oil_pressure" title="Oil pressure">oil pressure</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The final practice session was held in dry weather conditions. Hamilton set the initial pace before Webber recorded the overall fastest lap on the soft compound tyres at 1 minute and 35.323 seconds. Hamilton was second and Vettel third. Button, Alonso, Rosberg, Schumacher, Kubica, Massa and <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a> completed the top ten ahead of qualifying.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportFP3_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportFP3-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Though the session passed relatively peacefully, Petrov ran wide onto the artificial grass leaving the final corner and he lost control of his Renault. He skidded across a gravel trap and crashed heavily into a tyre barrier with the front of his car. Although Petrov was unhurt, Whiting stopped the session for seven minutes to allow <a href="/wiki/Motorsport_marshal" title="Motorsport marshal">track marshals</a> to clear debris.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportFP3_20-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportFP3-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2010_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Sebastian_Vettel_2010_Japan.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8b/Sebastian_Vettel_2010_Japan.jpg/170px-Sebastian_Vettel_2010_Japan.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="247" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8b/Sebastian_Vettel_2010_Japan.jpg/255px-Sebastian_Vettel_2010_Japan.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8b/Sebastian_Vettel_2010_Japan.jpg/340px-Sebastian_Vettel_2010_Japan.jpg 2x" data-file-width="600" data-file-height="873" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Sebastian_Vettel_2010_Japan.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> <i>(pictured at the <a href="/wiki/2010_Japanese_Grand_Prix" title="2010 Japanese Grand Prix">Japanese Grand Prix</a> later in the year)</i> took his third <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> of the season and the eighth of his career.</div></div></div>
<p>Saturday afternoon's <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#Q" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">qualifying session</a> was split into three parts. The first part ran for 20 minutes, eliminating cars that finished the session 18th or lower. The second part lasted 15 minutes, eliminated cars that finished 11th to 17th. The final session ran for ten minutes and determined <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> to tenth. Cars in the final session were not allowed to change tyres, using the tyres with which they set their quickest lap times.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Conditions were warm and dry.<sup id="cite_ref-PAQuali_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PAQuali-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> Vettel altered the <a href="/wiki/Racing_setup" title="Racing setup">setup of his car</a> to be more aligned with his teammate Webber but he struggled with its handling.<sup id="cite_ref-QualiQuotes_23-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QualiQuotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Nonetheless, he went fastest in the third session to take his third pole position of the season and the eighth of his career with a time of 1 minute and 34.558 seconds on his final lap of qualifying. He was joined on the <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#G" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">grid</a>'s front row by Webber, who was 0.248 seconds slower and had the pole position until Vettel's lap.<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportQuali_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportQuali-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Alonso felt more comfortable driving on the hard compound tyres and took third while Rosberg in fourth worked with his race engineer <a href="/wiki/Jock_Clear" title="Jock Clear">Jock Clear</a> to find a setup he liked. Button used the soft compound tyres though an unbalanced car that required a change of <a href="/wiki/Limited-slip_differential" title="Limited-slip differential">differential</a> and front wing settings put him fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-QualiQuotes_23-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QualiQuotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton was fastest in the first and second sessions although car troubles in the final session restricted him to sixth.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportQuali_25-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportQuali-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> An untidy lap and a driver error in the final corner qualified Massa seventh and Kubica was the faster of the two Renaults in eighth.<sup id="cite_ref-QualiQuotes_23-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QualiQuotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Limited rear <a href="/wiki/Grip_(auto_racing)" title="Grip (auto racing)">grip</a> that slowed Schumacher in ninth and restricted his cornering ability at certain areas of the circuit.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCQuali_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCQuali-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> Sutil completed the top ten qualifiers after reporting <a href="/wiki/Understeer_and_oversteer" title="Understeer and oversteer">oversteer</a> in the middle sector.<sup id="cite_ref-QualiQuotes_23-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QualiQuotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Williams' <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a> had a new engine installed after practice though he was the fastest driver not to qualify in the top ten;<sup id="cite_ref-QualiQuotes_23-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QualiQuotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> his fastest lap was 0.820 seconds slower than Hamilton's best time in the second session.<sup id="cite_ref-QualiResults_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QualiResults-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> He was followed by the Toro Rossos of Alguersuari and Buemi in 12th and 13th.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCQuali_26-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCQuali-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> After Petrov's car was repaired for qualifying,<sup id="cite_ref-PAQuali_22-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PAQuali-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> he took 14th and spoke of his belief that he had improved his driving ability through car acclimatisation. An understeer and a lack of stability under braking put Kobayashi in 15th though he was not impeded by slower traffic. <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> in the second Williams ran wide at the final corner and qualified 16th. <a href="/wiki/Pedro_de_la_Rosa" title="Pedro de la Rosa">Pedro de la Rosa</a> of the Sauber team made a driving error by locking his rear wheels on the approach to turn 14 during his final timed lap and began from 17th.<sup id="cite_ref-QualiQuotes_23-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QualiQuotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Force India's <a href="/wiki/Vitantonio_Liuzzi" title="Vitantonio Liuzzi">Vitantonio Liuzzi</a> failed to advance beyond the first session,<sup id="cite_ref-BBCQuali_26-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCQuali-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> having been baulked by slower traffic and unable to set a lap that represented the speed of his car.<sup id="cite_ref-QualiQuotes_23-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QualiQuotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Glock was delayed by the slower Barrichello while on his first set of tyres and his sole timed lap qualified him 19th.<sup id="cite_ref-QualiQuotes_23-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QualiQuotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Trulli in 20th and his teammate Kovalainen in 21st were affected by a strong <a href="/wiki/Headwind_and_tailwind" title="Headwind and tailwind">tailwind</a> that unbalanced their cars in the braking zones and Kovalainen pirouetted under braking for turn six. <a href="/wiki/Lucas_di_Grassi" title="Lucas di Grassi">Lucas di Grassi</a> of the Virgin outfit made a driver error in the final sector that lost time and put him 22nd. The <a href="/wiki/HRT_Formula_1_Team" title="HRT Formula 1 Team">Hispanias</a> of <a href="/wiki/Bruno_Senna" title="Bruno Senna">Bruno Senna</a> and <a href="/wiki/Karun_Chandhok" title="Karun Chandhok">Karun Chandhok</a> completed the final row of the grid in 23rd and 24th, respectively.<sup id="cite_ref-QualiQuotes_23-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-QualiQuotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportQuali_25-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportQuali-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-qualifying">Post-qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2010_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Post-qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Chandhok received a five-place grid penalty as the Hispania team broke the FIA seal on his <a href="/wiki/Hispania_F110" title="Hispania F110">F110</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Transmission_(mechanics)" title="Transmission (mechanics)">gearbox</a> without a representative of the governing body being present;<sup id="cite_ref-ChandhokPenalty_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ChandhokPenalty-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> he was then further penalised for his mechanics changing a <a href="/wiki/Hydraulic_pump" title="Hydraulic pump">hydraulic pump</a> assembly with parts of a different specification to the originals overnight, and was made to start from the <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit lane</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ChandhokPits_29-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ChandhokPits-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2010_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:China_GP_2010_start.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/76/China_GP_2010_start.jpg/220px-China_GP_2010_start.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="128" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/76/China_GP_2010_start.jpg/330px-China_GP_2010_start.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/76/China_GP_2010_start.jpg/440px-China_GP_2010_start.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1024" data-file-height="595" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:China_GP_2010_start.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The start saw the pole position Sebastian Vettel be overtaken by teammate <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>The race took place in the afternoon from 15:00 <a href="/wiki/Time_in_China" title="Time in China">China Standard Time</a> (<a href="/wiki/UTC%2B08:00" title="UTC+08:00">UTC+08:00</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-FIAPreview_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FIAPreview-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Approximately 85,000 people attended the Grand Prix.<sup id="cite_ref-GuardianRace_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GuardianRace-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Weather conditions at the start were cloudy,<sup id="cite_ref-BBCWebRace_31-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCWebRace-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> with rain falling on the circuit with increasing intensity 30 minutes before the <a href="/wiki/Parade_lap" title="Parade lap">formation lap</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> The air temperature was between 18 to 21&#160;°C (64 to 70&#160;°F) and the track temperature ranged from 17 to 23&#160;°C (63 to 73&#160;°F).<sup id="cite_ref-MTotalOverview_33-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MTotalOverview-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Buemi modified his Toro Rosso's setup to optimise it for the wet conditions while Ferrari opted for a high-<a href="/wiki/Downforce" title="Downforce">downforce</a> setup for the anticipated rain shower.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCWebRace_31-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCWebRace-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Glock's car was stationary on its front <a href="/wiki/Jack_(device)" title="Jack (device)">jacks</a> due to a sudden loss of <a href="/wiki/Air_pressurized_water" title="Air pressurized water">air pressure</a> in the engine valve system, which was traced to a chassis fault. He was retired in the garage after it was determined that repairs were not feasible. Glock's teammate di Grassi had a malfunctioning <a href="/wiki/Clutch" title="Clutch">clutch</a> that necessitated its replacement and he began from the pit lane.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_34-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Alonso made a fast getaway and passed the Red Bulls to lead the field on the approach to the first corner as Webber moved past his teammate Vettel for second.<sup id="cite_ref-SkyRace_35-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SkyRace-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> At turn six, a three-car accident prompted the deployment of the <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a>. Liuzzi lost control of his car under braking and he slid backwards into the cars of Kobayashi and Buemi.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCWebRace_31-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCWebRace-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Several drivers, including Alonso, made pit stops for the intermediate compound tyres at the end of the second lap due to the intensifying rain.<sup id="cite_ref-SkyRace_35-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SkyRace-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg, Button and the Renaults of Kubica and Petrov chose to remain on the circuit and the former led the field back to racing speed when the safety car was withdrawn at the end of lap three. On the lap after, Kovalainen in seventh was passed by the eighth-placed Barrichello driving towards turn six. As Rosberg continued to pull away from Button on lap five, the stewards informed the Ferrari team that Alonso had <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#J" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">jumped the start</a>, for which he incurred a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_regulations#Penalties" title="Formula One regulations">drive-through penalty</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> He took the penalty on the next lap and rejoined the race in 14th.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCWebRace_31-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCWebRace-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Although the intermediate tyres initially provided an increase in speed, the rain did not intensify and every driver running on them made a second pit stop, providing Rosberg, Button, Kubica and Petrov with a significant lead over the rest of the field.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> During this period, Hamilton got <a href="/wiki/Wheelspin" title="Wheelspin">wheelspin</a> and slewed sideways as he exited his pit stall alongside Vettel. The two made contact as the latter turned right and Hamilton veered close to the Williams mechanics before going behind Vettel.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_36-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Webber damaged the front wing after he hit the front jack. The incident cost him nine seconds and dropped him to tenth.<sup id="cite_ref-37" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> On lap nine, Hamilton passed Barrichello for 12th at turn one.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> De La Rosa in fourth pulled off the side of the track on the same lap to retire with an engine failure.<sup id="cite_ref-SkyRace_35-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SkyRace-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Alguersuari_Petrov_China_GP_2010.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/91/Alguersuari_Petrov_China_GP_2010.jpg/220px-Alguersuari_Petrov_China_GP_2010.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="119" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/91/Alguersuari_Petrov_China_GP_2010.jpg/330px-Alguersuari_Petrov_China_GP_2010.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/91/Alguersuari_Petrov_China_GP_2010.jpg/440px-Alguersuari_Petrov_China_GP_2010.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1024" data-file-height="553" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Alguersuari_Petrov_China_GP_2010.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The race saw rain, and most of it was run on the <a href="/wiki/Rain_tyre" title="Rain tyre">intermediate compound tyres</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>Webber was overtaken by his teammate Vettel on the back straight into the turn 14 hairpin for eighth on lap 11 and lost a further position to Hamilton on the lap after. The two drivers got ahead of Kovalainen soon after. On lap 13, Vettel attempted to pass Sutil for seventh at the same corner though Hamilton got past the two on the inside line. Sutil delayed Vettel until the latter clung onto his <a href="/wiki/Drafting_(aerodynamics)" title="Drafting (aerodynamics)">slipstream</a> before making a pass on the inside line driving towards the turn 14 hairpin. On the 15th lap, Schumacher was caught by Hamilton at a rate of three seconds per lap, and Hamilton tried a pass on the inside at turn 14 but Schumacher defended the position. Hamilton tried again at the same turn on the next lap but Schumacher again kept fifth on the inside line. During lap 17, Hamilton made a third try by achieving a fast exit leaving turn 13 and slipstreamed up the back of Schumacher on the backstraight and withstood the Mercedes driver's attempt to keep fifth.  Further back, Alguersuari overtook Sutil around the outside at the turn 14 hairpin to take ninth as the two made contact leaving the turn. Upfront, Rosberg's lead of 4.5 seconds became nothing when Button closed up to him. He ran wide at turn 11 and Button made the pass for the lead at the turn 14 hairpin on lap 19.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCWebRace_31-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCWebRace-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Rain began to fall heavier on lap 20. The leading trio of Button, Rosberg and Kubica made pit stops and switched to the intermediate tyres on the following lap.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Their advantage  – Petrov gradually fell off the pace to be within striking distance of the more experienced competitors – was negated when Alguersuari had an encounter with one of the Hisapnias that damaged his front wing. As he made a pit stop for a new nose cone, the wing fell off, scattering debris across the circuit and triggering the second deployment of the safety car on lap 22.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCWebRace_31-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCWebRace-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> During the safety car period, Massa was slow exiting the turn 14 hairpin. His teammate Alonso passed him around the outside and caused Massa to run into the grass to avoid his teammate and enter the pit lane.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_38-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> When the safety car withdrew at the end of lap 25, Button controlled the field between the safety car's exit and the start/finish line. Button slowed to a near halt at the bottom hairpin, bunching the field up and causing controversy when Webber ran off the circuit at the final bend. As the lead driver has the right to drive as fast or as slow as he chooses when he gains control of the race, no action was taken. Webber fell from sixth to 11th. Hamilton overtook Schumacher at turn eight on lap 26 and then Petrov at the same corner on the next lap.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Jenson_Button_2010_Malaysia.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/ad/Jenson_Button_2010_Malaysia.jpg/170px-Jenson_Button_2010_Malaysia.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="231" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/ad/Jenson_Button_2010_Malaysia.jpg/255px-Jenson_Button_2010_Malaysia.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/ad/Jenson_Button_2010_Malaysia.jpg/340px-Jenson_Button_2010_Malaysia.jpg 2x" data-file-width="800" data-file-height="1088" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Jenson_Button_2010_Malaysia.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> <i>(pictured at the preceding <a href="/wiki/2010_Malaysian_Grand_Prix" title="2010 Malaysian Grand Prix">Malaysian Grand Prix</a>)</i> won his second race of the season and the ninth of his career.</div></div></div>
<p>On the 29th lap, Hamilton closed up to Kubica and overtook him around the outside going into the turn 14 hairpin for third. Alonso passed Sutil for sixth soon after at the same corner. Two laps later, Barrichello lost 11th to Webber on the inside of turn six.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg's tyres began to degrade on lap 32 as well as Hamilton's though his team told him to remain on the track because more rain was forecast later in the Grand Prix.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCWebRace_31-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCWebRace-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> On the following lap, Petrov had an oversteer that caused him to pirouette leaving turn eighth. He fell from fifth to seventh, behind Alonso and Vettel.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCWebRace_31-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCWebRace-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> The pace began to lower on the intermediate tyres by the 34th lap as they continued to degrade. Hamilton closed up to Rosberg and attempted a pass around the outside on the Mercedes driver at the turn 14 hairpin but Rosberg held the position.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> On lap 36, Hamilton tried again, this time going into turn eight,<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> and got ahead at turn nine before Rosberg came back to retain second at the exit of turn 10.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCWebRace_31-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCWebRace-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> The final round of pit stops for intermediate tyres took place from the same lap to the 38th lap,<sup id="cite_ref-BBCWebRace_31-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCWebRace-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> and Hamilton moved ahead of Rosberg. Similarly, Alonso passed Kubica.<sup id="cite_ref-SkyRace_35-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SkyRace-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> The final phase of the race was run under increasingly heavy rain across the back part of the circuit, as a dry line began to appear along the main straight.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Alguersuari attempted to keep Petrov and Massa behind him though Petrov made an overtake on Alguersuari at the turn 14 hairpin and Massa got ahead of the Spaniard at the final corner on lap 45. Upfront, Button increased his advantage over his teammate Hamilton to 9.5 seconds but it was reduced to 5.3 seconds when he ran deep at the turn 14 hairpin six laps later.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCWebRace_31-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCWebRace-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> On lap 52, Petrov overtook Schumacher around the outside for eighth at the turn 14 hairpin. Massa drew close to Schumacher on the next lap. He slipstreamed the Mercedes and moved to the outside on the approach to the turn 14 hairpin but Schumacher defended his line and kept ninth.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> On the 55th lap, Webber slid going into turn 12 and it allowed Petrov to pass him for seventh place.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCWebRace_31-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCWebRace-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Massa tried again to get past Schumacher and was successful that time round, withstanding Schumacher's attempt to retain his hold on ninth to get ahead leaving the turn 14 hairpin because of better traction from his car as Schumacher ran wide onto the artificial grass at the final corner.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Rain began to fall heavily on the final lap.<sup id="cite_ref-SkyRace_35-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SkyRace-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> Button and Hamilton closed up though they elected to be conservative and Button crossed the finish line after the 56th lap to take his second victory of the season and the ninth of his career.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportRace_32-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportRace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton was 1.530 seconds behind in second to claim the first <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#O" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">1–2</a> finish for British drivers since <a href="/wiki/Eddie_Irvine" title="Eddie Irvine">Eddie Irvine</a> and <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> at the <a href="/wiki/1999_Austrian_Grand_Prix" title="1999 Austrian Grand Prix">1999 Austrian Grand Prix</a> and the first for Englishmen since <a href="/wiki/Graham_Hill" title="Graham Hill">Graham Hill</a> and <a href="/wiki/Piers_Courage" title="Piers Courage">Piers Courage</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1969_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="1969 Monaco Grand Prix">1969 Monaco Grand Prix</a>. Rosberg completed the podium finishers in third to complete a sweep of the podium for Mercedes-powered cars for the first time since the <a href="/wiki/1955_British_Grand_Prix" title="1955 British Grand Prix">1955 British Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Autosport_race_39-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Autosport_race-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Off the podium, Alonso followed in fourth, Kubica fifth and Vettel sixth. Petrov took his first Formula One points finish in seventh and the first for a Russian driver in the championship.<sup id="cite_ref-Autosport_race_39-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Autosport_race-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Webber, Massa and Schumacher completed the top ten. Sutil, Barrichello followed close behind in 11th and 12th while Alguersuari, Kovalainen, Hülkenberg, Senna and Chandhok were the final classified finishers.<sup id="cite_ref-SkyRace_35-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SkyRace-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> Of the other retirements, di Grassi completed eight laps before retiring and Trulli stopped his Lotus in the garage with a hydraulics failure.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_34-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-40" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2010_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The top three drivers appeared on the podium to collect their trophies and spoke to the media in a later press conference.<sup id="cite_ref-FIAPreview_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FIAPreview-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-regulations_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Button hailed the win as "my best victory" and emphasised the importance about changing to the intermediate tyres during the safety car early in the Grand Prix, "That made the race. If we didn’t have that safety car later on for debris we would have been a long... well, I don’t know, I don’t know how quick Lewis was in the race. But the safety car definitely helped the situation for people that stopped for inters."<sup id="cite_ref-:2_41-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton called his race "quite eventful" and congratulated his teammate Button on taking the victory, "But it was very tough. Obviously at the beginning with the weather it wasn’t easy making the call or when to change the tyres. I chose very, very late, when I was half-way round the last corner and clearly it wasn’t the right choice."<sup id="cite_ref-:2_41-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg spoke of how comfortable he felt in the environment of the Mercedes team and how they aided in his integration, "We have had a good step forward again this weekend. We had some upgrades for this track, very small actually, so we have a lot more to come, so it is quite promising for us."<sup id="cite_ref-:2_41-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Ferrari team principal <a href="/wiki/Stefano_Domenicali" title="Stefano Domenicali">Stefano Domenicali</a> made the incident between Alonso and Massa entering the pit lane on lap 22 less important and called it "a racing incident", adding, "We only saw it afterwards on the television replay. There is absolutely no problem between the two drivers. When you are always racing to win, you can have moments like this."<sup id="cite_ref-:0_36-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Massa said he was unsure whether Alonso went beyond the sporting regulations, "He was inside so there was nothing I could do there. In terms of the rules, I don't know. I think he should be okay because we saw another car doing the same. You need to ask Charlie [Whiting] not me."<sup id="cite_ref-:3_42-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> Alonso stated he felt the manoeuvre was uncontroversial, "I knew that when two team-mates are fighting there are rumours. If it was two different cars there wouldn't be a problem. I think Felipe had too much wheel-spin on the final corner and I had a bit of an advantage in the pitlane entry."<sup id="cite_ref-:3_42-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> Both drivers later responded on the Ferrari website in a post called "media polemics" to reports carried in the press about a potential conflict. They said that no such dispute between the two existed, something that senior Ferrari personnel, including Domenciali and the president <a href="/wiki/Luca_Cordero_di_Montezemolo" title="Luca Cordero di Montezemolo">Luca Cordero di Montezemolo</a> confirmed.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_38-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-43" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Renault team principal <a href="/wiki/%C3%89ric_Boullier" title="Éric Boullier">Éric Boullier</a> praised Petrov's pace and remaining calm under pressure on the wet track, and spoke of his belief that the manufacturer's first double-points finish was indicative of their aggressive development plan.<sup id="cite_ref-44" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> Conversely, Webber commented that the pace of the Red Bulls was not enough to challenge McLaren, "We got blown away, it was a difficult grand prix. We weren't quick enough. We know [why the car falters in the wet conditions] and we have to improve it."<sup id="cite_ref-45" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup> Schumacher was philosophical to have finished in ninth, saying his tyre strategy caused him to lose pace in the final ten laps but he was pleased duel other drivers, "Today was one of those races that you do not want to remember, just like the whole weekend really. It was not good for me and it not good from me. You have to take it as another experience and accept it, even if it is frustrating that I was not able to get my tyres together better."<sup id="cite_ref-46" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup> Kubica felt he could have achieved a finish on the podium had the safety car not been deployed as it lost him a large amount of time, "So although I'm happy to finish fifth, I still feel a bit frustrated to have missed a podium."<sup id="cite_ref-47" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-47">&#91;47&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The race stewards summoned Hamilton and Vettel to discuss the clash of wheels in the pit lane on lap five and review television footage of the incident. Both drivers were issued with a reprimand for "dangerous driving".<sup id="cite_ref-48" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-48">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton said of the incident, "As far as I'm concerned I'm OK. The team waited for quite some time and released me when they thought it was right. I got quite a lot of wheelspin and struggled to get out of the box. As I came out I noticed Sebastian was there."<sup id="cite_ref-:0_36-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Vettel stated he did not understand why Hamilton was released alongside him, adding, "I don't know why he was keen to touch me."<sup id="cite_ref-:0_36-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Button's win promoted him to the lead of the Drivers' Championship for the first time in the season with 60 points, and Rosberg was ten points behind in second. Alonso fell to third and tied with Hamilton for the position. Vettel's sixth-place finish dropped him from second to fifth with 45 points.<sup id="cite_ref-points_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> In the Constructors' Championship, McLaren moved past Ferrari to become the new leaders. Red Bull retained third while Mercedes and Renault were four and five with 15 races remaining in the season.<sup id="cite_ref-points_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
