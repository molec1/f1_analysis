<p>The <b>2015 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>2015 Formula 1 Etihad Airways Abu Dhabi Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> held at the <a href="/wiki/Yas_Marina_Circuit" title="Yas Marina Circuit">Yas Marina Circuit</a> on 29 November 2015. The race was the nineteenth and final round of the <a href="/wiki/2015_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2015 Formula One season">2015 season</a>, and marked the seventh running of the <a href="/wiki/Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix" title="Abu Dhabi Grand Prix">Abu Dhabi Grand Prix</a> as a round of the World Championship since its inception in <a href="/wiki/1950_Formula_One_season" title="1950 Formula One season">1950</a>.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> was the defending race winner and had already secured his third <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> earlier in the season at the <a href="/wiki/2015_United_States_Grand_Prix" title="2015 United States Grand Prix">United States Grand Prix</a>. His team, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a>, had decided the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a> in their favour at the <a href="/wiki/2015_Russian_Grand_Prix" title="2015 Russian Grand Prix">Russian Grand Prix</a>.
</p><p>In Saturday's qualifying, <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> (Mercedes) secured his sixth consecutive pole position and went on to win the race ahead of teammate Hamilton. <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> finished third for Ferrari. It was the twelfth one-two finish for Mercedes, cementing a record-breaking season that saw them finish with the highest ever number of points scored by a constructor at 703, most front row lookouts and one-two finishes over the course of any Formula One season. This would also prove to be the last Grand Prix for <a href="/wiki/Will_Stevens" title="Will Stevens">Will Stevens</a>, <a href="/wiki/Roberto_Merhi" title="Roberto Merhi">Roberto Merhi</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2015_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Going into the race weekend, both Championships were already decided. <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> had already secured the drivers' title three races earlier at the <a href="/wiki/2015_United_States_Grand_Prix" title="2015 United States Grand Prix">2015 United States Grand Prix</a> and was leading the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> with 363 points, 66 points ahead of his teammate <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-driversbefore_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-driversbefore-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> In the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a>, their team <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> had also secured the constructors' title four races earlier at the <a href="/wiki/2015_Russian_Grand_Prix" title="2015 Russian Grand Prix">2015 Russian Grand Prix</a> and was leading <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> on 660 to 401 points.<sup id="cite_ref-constrbefore_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-constrbefore-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> However, several positions in the standings were still undecided. In the battle for fourth place in the Drivers' Championship, <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> was just one point ahead of compatriot <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> in sixth also still able to get the position, being 19 points behind his <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> teammate Bottas. <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> teammates <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a> and <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> were just ten points apart in the battle for seventh in the championship.<sup id="cite_ref-driversbefore_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-driversbefore-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> In the Constructors' standings, sixth placed <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a> was just nine points ahead of <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>, while <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> needed at least nine points as well to overcome <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a> for eighth, a points tally they had only managed once during the rest of the season.<sup id="cite_ref-constrbefore_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-constrbefore-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> supplied the teams with the yellow banded soft and the red banded super-soft tyres as the two dry weather options for the race.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> It was the 150th race for <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> under their current moniker.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Free_practice">Free practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2015_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Free practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Bottas_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/18/Bottas_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg/220px-Bottas_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="146" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/18/Bottas_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg/330px-Bottas_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/18/Bottas_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg/440px-Bottas_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1600" data-file-height="1064" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Bottas_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Due to local laws, <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> ran their car without the usual <a href="/wiki/Martini_Racing" title="Martini Racing">Martini Racing</a> livery.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup></div></div></div>
<p>Per the regulations for the 2015 season, three practice sessions were scheduled, two 1.5-hour sessions on Friday and another one-hour session before qualifying on Saturday.<sup id="cite_ref-pracreg_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pracreg-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Lewis Hamilton was fastest in the first free practice session, run in clear and sunny conditions, lapping in a time of 1m43.754s, over one-tenth of a second faster than teammate Rosberg in second. Rosberg had set new fastest sector times on his last fast run, but a mistake in the last part of the track lost him a chance at the best time. Kimi Räikkönen was third fastest for Ferrari, ahead of Daniil Kvyat. Kvyat's Red Bull teammate Ricciardo changed back to his older specification <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a> power unit, after being unsatisfied with the performance of his updated unit at the <a href="/wiki/2015_Brazilian_Grand_Prix" title="2015 Brazilian Grand Prix">previous race in Brazil</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> He finished the session seventh fastest. At Lotus, <a href="/wiki/Jolyon_Palmer" title="Jolyon Palmer">Jolyon Palmer</a> replaced <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a>, but had to sit out most of the session due to a water leak, managing just nine laps.<sup id="cite_ref-fp1_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fp1-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the second session of Friday afternoon, Nico Rosberg topped the timesheets, setting a time of 1m41.983s, finishing more than a tenth of a second ahead of Hamilton. Third fastest was <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> for Force India, although he was unable to run in the last twenty minutes of the session due to brake issues. The Red Bull drivers Kvyat and Ricciardo were fourth and sixth respectively, while <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> came within one second of Rosberg's time in ninth.<sup id="cite_ref-fp2_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fp2-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Rosberg was again fastest in the third session on Saturday with a time of 1m41.856s. Hamilton was second quickest, almost three-tenths of a second slower, unable to get a clean lap to match his teammate. On the slower soft tyres, one lap was impeded by traffic, another was disturbed by Hamilton spinning after hitting the kerb at turn five. He again failed in his attempt on a very fast lap on the super-soft tyres later in the session, being forced wide after repeating his mistake at turn five. Hamilton was just marginally ahead of <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>, who finished third ahead of Pérez. Daniil Kvyat was unable to set a timed lap as his team worked to solve an electrical problem.<sup id="cite_ref-fp3_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fp3-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2015_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Kvyat_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cd/Kvyat_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg/220px-Kvyat_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cd/Kvyat_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg/330px-Kvyat_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cd/Kvyat_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg/440px-Kvyat_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg 2x" data-file-width="5184" data-file-height="3456" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Kvyat_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a> qualified ninth.</div></div></div>
<p>Qualifying consisted of three parts, 18, 15 and 12 minutes in length respectively, with five drivers eliminated from competing after each of the first two sessions.<sup id="cite_ref-pracreg_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pracreg-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> At the beginning of the first part of qualifying (Q1), Mercedes sent both their drivers out on the faster super-soft compound, while Ferrari stayed on the slower soft tyres.<sup id="cite_ref-qualisky_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualisky-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> Even though Ferrari eventually decided to give both their drivers out on the faster tyres towards the end of the session, Sebastian Vettel aborted his last flying lap, believing his time to be sufficient for proceeding into the next round, only for <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> to set a time to relegate Vettel into 16th place on the grid, eliminating him from qualifying.<sup id="cite_ref-qualifan_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualifan-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Vettel would later describe his and the team's judgement as "a small mistake with a big consequence".<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Fernando Alonso in the other McLaren did not make the cut, after a puncture on his last lap left him without a chance at a faster time, while <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Ericsson" title="Marcus Ericsson">Marcus Ericsson</a> was held up by a technical issue, qualifying 18th.<sup id="cite_ref-qualifan_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualifan-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Both <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Manor Marussia</a> drivers were eliminated at the bottom of the standings, with <a href="/wiki/Will_Stevens" title="Will Stevens">Will Stevens</a> 1.137 seconds ahead of his teammate <a href="/wiki/Roberto_Merhi" title="Roberto Merhi">Roberto Merhi</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-qualiauto_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualiauto-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Lewis Hamilton was fastest in Q2, two-tenths of a second ahead of his teammate Rosberg. In his last qualifying session with Lotus, Romain Grosjean experienced technical difficulties that forced him to come back into the pits. Even though he was sent out again, he was unable to set a time, stopping his car at turn seven. His teammate <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> also did not proceed into Q3, as did Jenson Button who missed out by two-tenths of a second. The two other drivers eliminated were <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Nasr" title="Felipe Nasr">Felipe Nasr</a> for Sauber and <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a>, who was unable to match the time of his teammate <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz Jr.</a><sup id="cite_ref-qualifan_17-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualifan-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The top ten drivers competed for pole position in Q3 and it was a battle between the two Mercedes. Lewis Hamilton set his fastest time first with Rosberg right behind him. A much better time in the third sector of the track allowed Rosberg to take his sixth consecutive pole position almost four-tenths ahead of his teammate. Sergio Pérez held third spot for much of the session but was beaten to it by Kimi Räikkönen during the second turn of runs. The two were followed by Daniel Ricciardo and Valtteri Bottas on the third row of the grid.<sup id="cite_ref-qualifan_17-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualifan-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Following a gearbox change after qualifying, Romain Grosjean was demoted five places on the grid. Roberto Merhi and Will Stevens also received five place penalties: Stevens for a change of the Control Electronics unit, Merhi for changes made to his suspension. Both would start the race from the back row of the grid.<sup id="cite_ref-penalty_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-penalty-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2015_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Rosberg_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cc/Rosberg_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg/220px-Rosberg_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cc/Rosberg_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg/330px-Rosberg_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cc/Rosberg_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg/440px-Rosberg_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg 2x" data-file-width="5184" data-file-height="3456" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Rosberg_Abu_Dhabi_2015.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> won the race for <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>At the start of the race on Sunday, Hamilton got away slowly but was able to keep both Pérez and Räikkönen behind him while Rosberg retained the lead. Further back, Fernando Alonso made contact with Felipe Nasr and then crashed into Pastor Maldonado, who was forced to retire. Alonso needed to pit for a new front wing and was later given a drive-through penalty, dropping him to the end of the field. Meanwhile, <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> and Sebastian Vettel made up places, sitting in fifth and twelfth respectively after the first lap. Valtteri Bottas on the other hand lost positions, finding himself in tenth. Rosberg soon built a lead of over one second, giving Hamilton no chance to deploy <a href="/wiki/Drag_reduction_system" title="Drag reduction system">DRS</a> for an overtake manoeuvre. By lap 5, both Kvyat and Nasr had come into the pits for the harder compound tyre, followed by Ricciardo, Massa and Pérez one lap later. On lap seven, both Hülkenberg and Sainz Jr. made pit stops. While Hülkenberg was able to stay ahead of Massa, Sainz's stop was unfortunate and he lost time due to a problem with the tyre change. Another two laps later, Valtteri Bottas pitted and was sent out into the path of Button, damaging his front wing, which he needed to replace the following lap. He also received a five-second penalty to be served at his next scheduled stop. The leaders pitted for new tyres around laps ten and eleven, with the order now being Rosberg ahead of Vettel, who was yet to stop, Hamilton, and Räikkönen. Romain Grosjean, who started on the harder tyres just as Vettel, was up to sixth.<sup id="cite_ref-ticker_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ticker-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Button_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_Abu_Dhabi_2015.png" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d8/Button_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_Abu_Dhabi_2015.png/220px-Button_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_Abu_Dhabi_2015.png" decoding="async" width="220" height="116" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d8/Button_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_Abu_Dhabi_2015.png/330px-Button_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_Abu_Dhabi_2015.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d8/Button_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_Abu_Dhabi_2015.png/440px-Button_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_Abu_Dhabi_2015.png 2x" data-file-width="2148" data-file-height="1136" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Button_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_Abu_Dhabi_2015.png" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> (left) and <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a></div></div></div>
<p>On lap 13, Felipe Massa passed Marcus Ericsson for ninth place and one lap later, Hamilton capitalised on a mistake by Vettel and overtook him to retake second position. Vettel lost another position when he let Räikkönen pass him on lap 16, the two being on different strategies. Meanwhile, Rosberg had built up a lead of around six seconds to Hamilton. On lap 19, Carlos Sainz Jr. allowed teammate Max Verstappen to pass him, while Alonso came in for what was his third pit stop of the race, leaving him in last position. Two laps later, Verstappen created flatspots on his tyres when he locked up his brakes, forcing him to pit. He emerged in 15th position. Vettel came in for his first pit stop on lap 24, coming out in sixth place ahead of compatriot Nico Hülkenberg. At the same time, Hamilton started to reduce the gap to Rosberg in front, cutting it down to less than two seconds by lap 29. With his teammate closing in, Rosberg made pit stops on lap 32, with Räikkönen following suit, whose pit stop was delayed, losing him around four seconds. Sergio Pérez was starting to close the gap to both Ferrari drivers in front of him after pitting early. Vettel reacted on lap 41 and changed to the super-soft tyres, coming out behind both Pérez and Ricciardo in sixth. One lap later, Hamilton came in for his second and final stop, having asked his team before if it would be possible to finish the race without stopping again. He emerged twelve seconds behind Rosberg and started to cut into his advantage immediately.<sup id="cite_ref-ticker_24-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ticker-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Further back in the field, Max Verstappen overtook Nasr and Button to move into twelfth place on laps 44 and 46 respectively. However, he was deemed to have forced Button wide and was handed a five-second penalty to be added to his finishing time. Lap 53 saw Romain Grosjean move into the final point scoring position of tenth by passing Carlos Sainz Jr. into turn eight. Just one lap later, he took ninth place from Daniil Kvyat. At the front, Hamilton proved unable to reach his teammate, who took the checkered flag for his sixth win of the season. Kimi Räikkönen completed the podium, followed by Vettel, Pérez and Ricciardo.<sup id="cite_ref-ticker_24-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ticker-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2015_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Firework_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix_2015.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/ae/Firework_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix_2015.jpg/220px-Firework_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix_2015.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/ae/Firework_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix_2015.jpg/330px-Firework_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix_2015.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/ae/Firework_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix_2015.jpg/440px-Firework_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix_2015.jpg 2x" data-file-width="5184" data-file-height="3456" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Firework_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix_2015.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Fireworks after the race</div></div></div>
<p>In the podium interviews, conducted by former Formula One driver <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a>, Nico Rosberg expressed excitement over how the end of the season had worked out for him, describing the race in the United States as his "low point". Speaking about racing his teammate Hamilton, he called him "one of the best out there" and said that it was "an even better feeling to win, definitely against such opposition". Hamilton in turn praised his team for their performance over the whole season, saying that they had "surpassed their own expectations", albeit confessing that he was happy that the season was over. During the press conference, third-placed Kimi Räikkönen said: "It’s never nice to finish third but I’ll take it after previous races", pointing to a season that had been marred with small mistakes and misfortunes for him. He also stated that securing fourth place in the World Championship did not mean much to him, dismissing the idea of a "Finnish championship" between himself and Valtteri Bottas.<sup id="cite_ref-pressconf_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pressconf-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Fourth-placed Sebastian Vettel said after the race: "The best result one way or another would be P3 and P4 so we can be very happy with today", adding that he and Räikkönen had "a clean race for P3". He also described his 2015 campaign as "a miracle", after Ferrari had endured a winless <a href="/wiki/2014_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2014 Formula One World Championship">2014</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Speaking about the incident between Bottas and Button at the first round of pit stops, Williams' head of performance engineering <a href="/wiki/Rob_Smedley" title="Rob Smedley">Rob Smedley</a> said that the team had released their driver at the right moment, declaring the accident happened because Bottas saw Button too late.<sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Following two incidents during the race, Max Verstappen was given time penalties and three penalty points on his <a href="/wiki/FIA_Super_Licence" title="FIA Super Licence">FIA Super Licence</a> – one for overtaking Jenson Button outside track limits and two for ignoring blue flags while Lewis Hamilton tried to lap him. The penalties meant that he was just four points away from a one-race ban.<sup id="cite_ref-verstappen_29-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-verstappen-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Räikkönen taking fourth position from Bottas in the Drivers' Championship was the only change in both championship standings as a result of the Grand Prix.<sup id="cite_ref-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-31" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Following the Grand Prix, Renault confirmed their takeover of Lotus, meaning that it was the last Grand Prix for the team in their incarnation under the Lotus moniker that had started in <a href="/wiki/2012_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2012 Formula One World Championship">2012</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
