<p>The <b>2008 Monaco Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>LXVI Grand Prix de Monaco</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 25 May 2008 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Monaco" title="Circuit de Monaco">Circuit de Monaco</a>; contested over 76&#160;laps, it was the sixth race of the <a href="/wiki/2008_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2008 Formula One World Championship">2008 Formula One World Championship</a>. The race was won by the season's eventual <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Champion</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>, for the <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a> team. <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> finished second, and <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>, who started from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>, was third in a <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>.
</p><p>Conditions were wet at the start of the race. Massa maintained his lead into the first corner, but his teammate <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> was passed for second by Hamilton, who had started in third position on the <a href="/wiki/List_of_motorsport_terminology#G" class="mw-redirect" title="List of motorsport terminology">grid</a>. Hamilton suffered a punctured <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyres" title="Formula One tyres">tyre</a> on lap six, forcing him to make a <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit stop</a> from which he re-entered the race in fifth place. As the track dried and his rivals made their own pit stops Hamilton became the race leader, a position he held until the end of the race. Kubica's strategy allowed him to pass Massa during their second pit stops, after the latter's Ferrari was forced to change from wet to dry tyres. Räikkönen dropped back from fifth position to ninth after colliding with <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> late in the race. Sutil had started from 18th on the grid and was in fourth position before the incident, which allowed <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> to finish fourth, ahead of <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> in fifth.
</p><p>The race was Hamilton's second win of the season, his first in Monaco, and the result meant that he led the Drivers' Championship, three <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Championship_points_scoring_systems" title="List of Formula One World Championship points scoring systems">points</a> ahead of Räikkönen and four ahead of Massa. Ferrari maintained their lead in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a>, 16&#160;points ahead of McLaren and 17 ahead of BMW Sauber, with 12&#160;races of the season remaining.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Grand Prix was contested by 20&#160;drivers, in ten teams of two.<sup id="cite_ref-classification_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-classification-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The teams, also known as "<a href="/wiki/Formula_One#Constructors" title="Formula One">constructors</a>", were <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a>-<a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_HighPerformanceEngines" class="mw-redirect" title="Mercedes-Benz HighPerformanceEngines">Mercedes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a>, <a href="/wiki/Honda_Racing_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Honda Racing F1">Honda</a>, <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a>-Ferrari, <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a>, <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyota</a>, <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>-Renault, <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a>-Toyota and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>-Ferrari.<sup id="cite_ref-classification_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-classification-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyres" title="Formula One tyres">Tyre</a> supplier <a href="/wiki/Bridgestone" title="Bridgestone">Bridgestone</a> brought four different tyre types to the race: two dry-weather tyre compounds, the softer marked by a single white stripe down one of the grooves, and two wet-weather compounds, the extreme wet also marked by a single white stripe.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg151_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg151-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Kimi_Raikkonen_2008_(square).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/40/Kimi_Raikkonen_2008_%28square%29.jpg/220px-Kimi_Raikkonen_2008_%28square%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="220" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/40/Kimi_Raikkonen_2008_%28square%29.jpg/330px-Kimi_Raikkonen_2008_%28square%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/40/Kimi_Raikkonen_2008_%28square%29.jpg/440px-Kimi_Raikkonen_2008_%28square%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="964" data-file-height="964" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Kimi_Raikkonen_2008_(square).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> said his team would have a competitive car for the Monaco circuit.</div></div></div>
<p>Before the race, Ferrari driver <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> with 35&#160;<a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Championship_points_scoring_systems" title="List of Formula One World Championship points scoring systems">points</a>, and his teammate <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> was second with 28&#160;points. McLaren driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> was third, also on 28&#160;points, with one win fewer than Massa; BMW driver <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> was fourth on 24&#160;points, ahead of his teammate <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> on 20&#160;points.<sup id="cite_ref-pointsFIA_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pointsFIA-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> In the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a>, Ferrari were leading with 63&#160;points, 19&#160;points ahead of BMW Sauber, and 21&#160;points ahead of McLaren-Mercedes.<sup id="cite_ref-pointsFIA_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pointsFIA-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ferrari had responded to Hamilton's win in the opening round of the season <a href="/wiki/2008_Australian_Grand_Prix" title="2008 Australian Grand Prix">in Australia</a> by winning each of the subsequent four races, giving them a commanding lead in the Constructors' Championship. Ferrari's dominance had been highlighted by two <a href="/wiki/List_of_motorsport_terminology#O" class="mw-redirect" title="List of motorsport terminology">one-two</a> finishes: Massa ahead of Räikkönen <a href="/wiki/2008_Bahrain_Grand_Prix" title="2008 Bahrain Grand Prix">in Bahrain</a> and Räikkönen's win over Massa at the <a href="/wiki/2008_Spanish_Grand_Prix" title="2008 Spanish Grand Prix">following round in Spain</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-bahgpreport_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bahgpreport-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-espgpreport_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-espgpreport-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> A strong drive despite an unfavourable strategy had helped Hamilton to split the Ferrari drivers on the <a href="/wiki/Podium" title="Podium">podium</a> <a href="/wiki/2008_Turkish_Grand_Prix" title="2008 Turkish Grand Prix">in Turkey</a>, coming second, behind Massa but ahead of Räikkönen.<sup id="cite_ref-turgpreport_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-turgpreport-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Ferrari had not won in Monaco <a href="/wiki/2001_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="2001 Monaco Grand Prix">since 2001</a>, and had been unable to match McLaren's pace <a href="/wiki/2007_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="2007 Monaco Grand Prix">in 2007</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Elizalde_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Elizalde-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> After tests at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_Paul_Ricard" title="Circuit Paul Ricard">Circuit Paul Ricard</a> in mid-May, Räikkönen said his team had developed a strong car for the tight, low-speed Monaco circuit, but they still expected a challenge from McLaren and BMW Sauber.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton claimed that McLaren would be competitive at Monaco, but Kubica played down his team's chances, saying "I think a win will be very difficult."<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Toro Rosso's main 2008 car, the <a href="/wiki/Toro_Rosso_STR3" title="Toro Rosso STR3">STR3</a>, was also introduced that weekend; the team had used a modified version of their 2007 car, the <a href="/wiki/Toro_Rosso_STR2" title="Toro Rosso STR2">STR2</a>, for the opening five races.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Originally due to be introduced at the previous race in Turkey, a crash in testing had left the team short of spare parts, delaying the car's race debut.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> As the STR3 used a different <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_car#Transmission" title="Formula One car">transmission</a> than that used in the STR2, Toro Rosso driver <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> was forced to take a five-place penalty on the <a href="/wiki/List_of_motorsport_terminology#G" class="mw-redirect" title="List of motorsport terminology">grid</a> for an unscheduled change of gearbox.<sup id="cite_ref-trpenalty_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-trpenalty-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> His teammate <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A9bastien_Bourdais" title="Sébastien Bourdais">Sébastien Bourdais</a> escaped a similar penalty because he had failed to finish the race in Turkey, allowing him a free gearbox change.<sup id="cite_ref-trpenalty_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-trpenalty-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_2008_Monaco.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/11/Lewis_Hamilton_2008_Monaco.jpg/220px-Lewis_Hamilton_2008_Monaco.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="140" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/11/Lewis_Hamilton_2008_Monaco.jpg/330px-Lewis_Hamilton_2008_Monaco.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/11/Lewis_Hamilton_2008_Monaco.jpg/440px-Lewis_Hamilton_2008_Monaco.jpg 2x" data-file-width="606" data-file-height="385" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_2008_Monaco.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> during Thursday practice</div></div></div>
<p>Three practice sessions were held before the Sunday race&#160;– two on Thursday, and one on Saturday. The Thursday morning and afternoon sessions each lasted 90&#160;minutes; the third session, on Saturday morning, lasted for an hour.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg151_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg151-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-sporting_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sporting-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> One hour into the first session, officials noticed a loose drain cover in the run through Beau Rivage, and suspended practice. Ferrari and McLaren took the top four spots after the resumption&#160;– Räikkönen ahead of Hamilton, McLaren driver <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a>, and Massa.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Behind Williams driver <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a>, Kubica was the best of the BMW Saubers in sixth; his teammate Heidfeld was forced to retire because of an engine failure, and stopped his car in Casino Square after just 13&#160;laps.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Despite further delays during the second session&#160;– both Renault drivers crashed in separate incidents at Sainte Devote, requiring the <a href="/wiki/Motorsport_marshal" title="Motorsport marshal">marshals</a> to sweep the track of debris&#160;– Hamilton again proved strong, fastest ahead of Rosberg, Räikkönen, Massa, Kovalainen and Kubica.<sup id="cite_ref-prac2_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-prac2-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Apart from the Renault drivers, two more cars struck the barriers: Toyota driver <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a> scraped the wall out of Piscine; <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a>'s Force India lost its <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_car#Aerodynamics" title="Formula One car">front wing</a> after tagging the barrier at Rascasse.<sup id="cite_ref-prac2_20-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-prac2-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> While light rain fell on Saturday morning, Kovalainen set the fastest time in the final session, before losing control at Piscine and damaging the rear of his car.<sup id="cite_ref-f1.comprac3_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-f1.comprac3-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> The rain increased as the marshals cleared the debris, and in the ensuing poor track conditions Kovalainen's time remained unbeaten.<sup id="cite_ref-f1.comprac3_21-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-f1.comprac3-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton managed second fastest, ahead of Räikkönen, Rosberg, Kubica and Massa.<sup id="cite_ref-prac3_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-prac3-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Saturday afternoon's qualifying session was divided into three&#160;parts. In the first 20-minute period, cars finishing 16th or lower were eliminated. The second qualifying period lasted for 15&#160;minutes, at the end of which the fastest ten cars went into the final period, to determine their grid positions for the race. Cars failing to make the final period were allowed to be refuelled before the race but those competing in it were not, and so carried more fuel than they had done in the earlier qualifying sessions.<sup id="cite_ref-sporting_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sporting-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quotebox pullquote floatleft" style="width:28%;&#32;;"><style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r887775652">.mw-parser-output .quotebox{background-color:#F9F9F9;border:1px solid #aaa;box-sizing:border-box;padding:10px;font-size:88%;max-width:100%}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatleft{margin:0.5em 1.4em 0.8em 0}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatright{margin:0.5em 0 0.8em 1.4em}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.centered{margin:0.5em auto 0.8em auto}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatleft p,.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatright p{font-style:inherit}.mw-parser-output .quotebox-title{background-color:#F9F9F9;text-align:center;font-size:larger;font-weight:bold}.mw-parser-output .quotebox-quote.quoted:before{font-family:"Times New Roman",serif;font-weight:bold;font-size:large;color:gray;content:" “ ";vertical-align:-45%;line-height:0}.mw-parser-output .quotebox-quote.quoted:after{font-family:"Times New Roman",serif;font-weight:bold;font-size:large;color:gray;content:" ” ";line-height:0}.mw-parser-output .quotebox .left-aligned{text-align:left}.mw-parser-output .quotebox .right-aligned{text-align:right}.mw-parser-output .quotebox .center-aligned{text-align:center}.mw-parser-output .quotebox cite{display:block;font-style:normal}@media screen and (max-width:360px){.mw-parser-output .quotebox{min-width:100%;margin:0 0 0.8em!important;float:none!important}}</style>
<div class="quotebox-quote left-aligned" style="">Incredible! I have got pole on a track where I have always struggled. Now I am beginning to like it a bit more&#160;... I managed to do a perfect lap with a great car: this result shows that, if you work hard and with attention to detail, you can do it everywhere&#160;... Starting at the front means I have the best possible conditions, but we will have to do everything perfectly. We worked a lot on the set-up to improve on the situation compared to past years and I think this decision has paid off.</div>
<p><cite class="left-aligned" style=""><a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>, following the third qualifying session.<sup id="cite_ref-qualquotes_23-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualquotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup></cite>
</p>
</div>
<p>Massa clinched his third <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> of the season with a time of 1:15.787, and was joined on the front row by teammate Räikkönen.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportqual_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportqual-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton took third place on the grid, with a qualifying time just 0.052&#160;seconds slower than Massa's.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg144_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg144-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Kovalainen edged out Kubica to take fourth, the latter struggling to get heat into his tyres for his final run.<sup id="cite_ref-qualquotes_23-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualquotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg's attacking style took him to sixth; Renault driver <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>, Trulli and Red Bull driver <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> occupied the next three places.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg144_25-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg144-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-bbcqual_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcqual-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> Webber's teammate <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> ended his second session in the barriers after the tunnel, after his car jerked sideways at the crest under braking.<sup id="cite_ref-gp.comqual_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gp.comqual-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Although Coulthard was unhurt, the position of his stricken car and the subsequent <a href="/wiki/Racing_flags#Yellow_flag" title="Racing flags">caution flags</a> surrounding it denied many drivers the opportunity to make their final <a href="/wiki/List_of_motorsport_terminology#F" class="mw-redirect" title="List of motorsport terminology">flying laps</a> in the session.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg144_25-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg144-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Honda driver <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a>, who took 12th behind <a href="/wiki/Timo_Glock" title="Timo Glock">Timo Glock</a> of Toyota, blamed the disruption for his performance, having prepared his car specifically for the final run.<sup id="cite_ref-qualquotes_23-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualquotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Heidfeld suffered from similar tyre problems to his teammate and managed 13th; Williams driver <a href="/wiki/Kazuki_Nakajima" title="Kazuki Nakajima">Kazuki Nakajima</a> and Honda driver <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a> took the next two places ahead of Bourdais in the new STR3.<sup id="cite_ref-qualquotes_23-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualquotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Renault driver <a href="/wiki/Nelson_Piquet,_Jr." class="mw-redirect" title="Nelson Piquet, Jr.">Nelson Piquet</a> lined up from 17th and Vettel managed 18th before his gearbox penalty demoted him to 19th.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportqual_24-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportqual-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> The Force Indias of Sutil and <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a> qualified slowest; Fisichella's penalty for a change of gearbox after morning practice meant he started from 20th on the grid.<sup id="cite_ref-gp.comqual_27-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gp.comqual-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Massa_Monaco_2008.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/31/Massa_Monaco_2008.jpg/220px-Massa_Monaco_2008.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="165" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/31/Massa_Monaco_2008.jpg/330px-Massa_Monaco_2008.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/31/Massa_Monaco_2008.jpg/440px-Massa_Monaco_2008.jpg 2x" data-file-width="800" data-file-height="600" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Massa_Monaco_2008.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> drives through Sainte-Dévote</div></div></div>
<p>On Sunday morning Coulthard became the third driver to incur a gearbox penalty, after changing his transmission following his crash during qualifying.<sup id="cite_ref-coulthardpenalty_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-coulthardpenalty-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> The penalty moved him from 10th to 15th on the grid.<sup id="cite_ref-coulthardpenalty_28-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-coulthardpenalty-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> In contrast to Saturday's dry qualifying session, frequent showers soaked the track on Sunday morning, making racing slippery and potentially hazardous.<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> Although the showers subsided by early afternoon, they resumed 20&#160;minutes before the start, the changeable conditions forcing teams to delay tyre choices for as long as possible.<sup id="cite_ref-commautosport_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-commautosport-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> By the time of the three-minute warning most drivers had opted for the standard wet tyre; only Piquet started the race on the extreme wet.<sup id="cite_ref-tyrestrategies_31-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-tyrestrategies-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Kovalainen's engine <a href="/wiki/Stall_(engine)" title="Stall (engine)">stalled</a> as the cars set off on their <a href="/wiki/List_of_motorsport_terminology#F" class="mw-redirect" title="List of motorsport terminology">formation lap</a>; his car was pushed back to the pit lane by his mechanics, where it was restarted and a new steering wheel fitted to solve the problem.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg146-149_32-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg146-149-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Massa held his lead into the first corner at Sainte Devote, while behind him Hamilton used the pit lane exit to pass Räikkönen down the inside.<sup id="cite_ref-commautosport_30-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-commautosport-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Kovalainen's vacated fourth position was filled by Kubica, as Alonso moved into fifth, passing Rosberg.<sup id="cite_ref-fiaracefacts_33-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiaracefacts-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> The latter made a <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit stop</a> soon after for a new front wing after making contact with Alonso at the <a href="/wiki/Hairpin_turn" title="Hairpin turn">hairpin</a>, promoting Trulli to sixth.<sup id="cite_ref-gpreport_34-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gpreport-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> These positions were maintained for several laps, but the distances between the cars increased, in part because the spray thrown up by their wheels made close racing difficult.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg146-149_32-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg146-149-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> The conditions proved to be crucial when Hamilton made contact with barriers on the outside of Tabac on lap six, necessitating his return to the pit lane for a new set of tyres.<sup id="cite_ref-commbbc_35-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-commbbc-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> The McLaren mechanics fuelled the car for a longer second <a href="/wiki/List_of_motorsport_terminology#S" class="mw-redirect" title="List of motorsport terminology">stint</a> and Hamilton emerged in fifth, the distances between his competitors' cars resulting in him losing only three places during the pit stop.<sup id="cite_ref-gpreport_34-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gpreport-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Alonso had a similar accident to Hamilton's at Massenet two laps later, and emerged in seventh after pitting and taking on extreme wets.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg146-149_32-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg146-149-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Massa's lead&#160;– 12&#160;seconds over the second-placed Räikkönen by lap six&#160;– was reduced to nothing when the <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a> was deployed on the eighth lap.<sup id="cite_ref-fiaracefacts_33-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiaracefacts-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> Coulthard and Bourdais had crashed into the barriers at Massenet just seconds apart, requiring the marshals to separate the cars and lift them off the track.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg146-149_32-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg146-149-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> When the safety car pulled off after three laps, Massa consolidated his lead over his teammate. Räikkönen, however, was called into the pit lane for a <a href="/wiki/List_of_motorsport_terminology#D" class="mw-redirect" title="List of motorsport terminology">drive-through penalty</a> after to failing to have his tyres attached by the three-minute warning before the race, dropping him back to fourth.<sup id="cite_ref-commautosport_30-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-commautosport-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Kubica, now second, took the lead when Massa slid off down the escape road at Sainte Devote, the undamaged Ferrari rejoining in second as the rain eased.<sup id="cite_ref-commautosport_30-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-commautosport-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> The two swapped positions again after their pit stops&#160;– Kubica on lap&#160;26 and Massa on lap&#160;33&#160;– but Hamilton, carrying more fuel than his rivals, took the lead.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg146-149_32-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg146-149-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Sutil_Monaco_2008_crop.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/02/Sutil_Monaco_2008_crop.jpg/220px-Sutil_Monaco_2008_crop.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="165" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/0/02/Sutil_Monaco_2008_crop.jpg 1.5x" data-file-width="263" data-file-height="197" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Sutil_Monaco_2008_crop.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> early in the race</div></div></div>
<p>As a <a href="/wiki/List_of_motorsport_terminology#D" class="mw-redirect" title="List of motorsport terminology">dry line</a> appeared on the track, Hamilton extended his lead over Massa, from 13 to 37&#160;seconds, by the time he made a pit stop for the second time on lap&#160;54.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg146-149_32-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg146-149-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> His timing proved fortunate, as he changed to dry tyres just as such a strategy emerged as the strongest, and he emerged 13&#160;seconds ahead of Massa.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg146-149_32-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg146-149-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> The Ferrari's own pit stop two laps later dropped Massa behind Kubica.<sup id="cite_ref-fialapchart_36-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fialapchart-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton's lead was reduced when the safety car once again deployed on lap&#160;62 after Rosberg crashed at Piscine, hitting both sides of the track and scattering debris.<sup id="cite_ref-fiaracefacts_33-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiaracefacts-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-commbbc_35-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-commbbc-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg was unhurt.<sup id="cite_ref-37" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Of the 20&#160;cars which started the race, Sutil had gained the most places by the second safety car period.<sup id="cite_ref-fialapchart_36-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fialapchart-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> The Force India started from 18th, rose one place after Kovalainen was pushed back to the pit lane, passed Piquet on the second lap and Bourdais a lap later.<sup id="cite_ref-fiaracefacts_33-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiaracefacts-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> Sutil benefited as Button, Rosberg, Glock and Trulli made pit stops for repairs over the following laps, to sit in 11th by lap&#160;14.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg146-149_32-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg146-149-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Alonso, on extreme wet tyres behind Heidfeld, attempted to pass the BMW at the hairpin, but succeeded only in damaging his front wing and forming a stationary queue behind him.<sup id="cite_ref-commautosport_30-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-commautosport-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> He also benefitted by overtaking three cars under yellow flags and would have been penalised if he had finished the race.<sup id="cite_ref-38" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-f1complete.com_39-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-f1complete.com-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Sutil took advantage of the situation and passed four cars to take seventh, behind Heidfeld and Webber.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg146-149_32-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg146-149-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Running a long first stint until lap&#160;53, he moved to fourth as the cars ahead of him made pit stops.<sup id="cite_ref-fialapchart_36-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fialapchart-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Vettel gained 12&#160;places from the start, battling with Barrichello and Nakajima early on before jumping several places on his first stint as his rivals made pit stops, eventually switching to dry tyres when he stopped on lap&#160;52.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg151_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg151-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-gpreport_34-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gpreport-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Webber improved three places from the start to sit in sixth, ahead of Vettel, Barrichello and Nakajima.<sup id="cite_ref-fialapchart_36-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fialapchart-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The safety car was withdrawn on lap&#160;68.<sup id="cite_ref-fialapchart_36-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fialapchart-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Later on the same lap, Räikkönen lost control of his car under braking out of the tunnel, and by the time he regained control his speed was too great to avoid a collision with Sutil.<sup id="cite_ref-gpreport_34-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gpreport-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> The Ferrari damaged its front wing and made a pit stops for a replacement before resuming, but the damage to Sutil's rear <a href="/wiki/Suspension_(vehicle)" class="mw-redirect" title="Suspension (vehicle)">suspension</a> led to his retirement.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg146-149_32-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg146-149-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Webber benefited from the incident and moved to fourth place, while Räikkönen dropped back to ninth.<sup id="cite_ref-fialapchart_36-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fialapchart-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>The slow pace during the opening laps meant the race ended after two hours, or 76&#160;laps, rather than the 78&#160;laps originally scheduled.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg146-149_32-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg146-149-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton, despite suffering a slow puncture on the final lap, crossed the finish line to take his first win in Monaco.<sup id="cite_ref-gpreport_34-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gpreport-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Kubica followed in second, ahead of Massa and Webber.<sup id="cite_ref-classification_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-classification-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Vettel took the STR3 to its first points in its maiden race, coming fifth, ahead of Barrichello and Nakajima.<sup id="cite_ref-classification_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-classification-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Kovalainen recovered from his stall to finish eighth, one place ahead of Räikkönen, who set the <a href="/wiki/Fastest_lap" title="Fastest lap">fastest lap</a> of the race on lap&#160;74, a 1:16.689.<sup id="cite_ref-classification_3-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-classification-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He was followed by Alonso, Button, Glock and Trulli.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg146-149_32-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg146-149-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Heidfeld was the last of the classified finishers, in 14th position, four laps behind Hamilton.<sup id="cite_ref-gpreport_34-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gpreport-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Fisichella retired with a gearbox failure after 36&#160;laps; Piquet&#160;– two laps after he switched to dry tyres on lap&#160;46&#160;– finished his race in the barriers at Sainte Devote.<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg146-149_32-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg146-149-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Sutil, Rosberg, Coulthard and Bourdais were the four other retirements.<sup id="cite_ref-classification_3-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-classification-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div style="clear:both;"></div>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quotebox pullquote floatright" style="width:29%;&#32;;"><link rel="mw-deduplicated-inline-style" href="mw-data:TemplateStyles:r887775652"/>
<div class="quotebox-quote left-aligned" style="">This has got to be the highlight of my career and I am sure it will continue to be the highlight for the rest of my life. I remember on the last few laps I was just thinking that <a href="/wiki/Ayrton_Senna" title="Ayrton Senna">Ayrton Senna</a> won here a lot of times and to win here would be amazing&#160;... [From] the last corner onwards, I was screaming my head off basically, making sure the radio was off, but just so happy that I was able to pull it off.</div>
<p><cite class="left-aligned" style=""><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>, speaking after the race.<sup id="cite_ref-pressconference_40-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pressconference-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup></cite>
</p>
</div>
<p>The top three finishers appeared in <a href="/wiki/Albert_II,_Prince_of_Monaco" title="Albert II, Prince of Monaco">Prince Albert II of Monaco's</a> Royal box to collect their trophies.<sup id="cite_ref-commautosport_30-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-commautosport-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> In the subsequent <a href="/wiki/Press_conference" title="Press conference">press conference</a> Hamilton said that conditions early in the race made driving difficult: "When the weather is like this, when it starts to rain and we had an idea it was going to start to dry, the important thing is to keep it on the track but I can't explain how difficult it was for all of us. You were <a href="/wiki/Hydroplaning_(tires)" class="mw-redirect" title="Hydroplaning (tires)">aquaplaning</a> all the time and you were tip-toeing almost."<sup id="cite_ref-pressconference_40-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pressconference-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton also said that his crash at Tabac had been the result of a stream of water running over the track, causing his car to <a href="/wiki/Oversteer" class="mw-redirect" title="Oversteer">oversteer</a> and resulting in a puncture to his right rear tyre.<sup id="cite_ref-pressconference_40-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pressconference-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Nevertheless, he praised his team and strategy for helping him take the victory.<sup id="cite_ref-pressconference_40-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pressconference-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Kubica said that tyre problems in his middle stint meant he was unable to overtake Massa, who was driving a heavier car. Noticing Glock's success on dry tyres, Kubica asked his team to make a similar change, resulting in his pass on Massa.<sup id="cite_ref-pressconference_40-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pressconference-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> During the post-race interview Massa said that although he was fuelled to the end of the race after his first pit stop, the drying track forced him to pit again for new tyres: "It was a shame that we made a little mistake on the strategy but it is good to be on the podium."<sup id="cite_ref-pressconference_40-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pressconference-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sutil expressed his disappointment after being knocked out of the race when he was on course to record his team's first points:
</p>
<style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r960796168">.mw-parser-output .templatequote{overflow:hidden;margin:1em 0;padding:0 40px}.mw-parser-output .templatequote .templatequotecite{line-height:1.5em;text-align:left;padding-left:1.6em;margin-top:0}</style><blockquote class="templatequote"><p>I can't believe it, it was so close. It feels like a pain in my heart. It is like a dream gone to a nightmare&#160;– suddenly you are in the car and it looks all fantastic, then you have to accept it is not going to happen&#160;... It was after the restart after the final safety car that Kimi had a problem under braking and crashed into the back of my car. The race was over and it was a real shock. A few tears came out as the adrenaline was high&#160;– I just can't explain it.<sup id="cite_ref-41" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup></p></blockquote>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Robert_Kubica_2009_Australia.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/47/Robert_Kubica_2009_Australia.jpg/220px-Robert_Kubica_2009_Australia.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="173" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/47/Robert_Kubica_2009_Australia.jpg/330px-Robert_Kubica_2009_Australia.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/47/Robert_Kubica_2009_Australia.jpg/440px-Robert_Kubica_2009_Australia.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2282" data-file-height="1797" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Robert_Kubica_2009_Australia.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> finished second and credited his strategy for his performance.</div></div></div>
<p>Räikkönen apologised to Sutil for their collision, blaming cold brakes for his loss of control.<sup id="cite_ref-42" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Mike_Gascoyne" title="Mike Gascoyne">Mike Gascoyne</a>, Force India's technical chief, called for the <a href="/wiki/List_of_motorsport_terminology#S" class="mw-redirect" title="List of motorsport terminology">stewards</a> to investigate the incident, but after deliberation no action was taken.<sup id="cite_ref-gascoyne_43-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gascoyne-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-44" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> "The frustration is that if that was a Force India driver hitting a world champion we'd expect to get a one or two-race ban, but the other way round nothing ever seems to happen" said Gascoyne.<sup id="cite_ref-gascoyne_43-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gascoyne-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> Sutil was called to the stewards' room and reprimanded for passing three cars under yellow flags on lap&#160;13. Because he had retired, he was issued with merely a warning, but had he finished the race he would have been given a 25-second time penalty, which would have dropped him out of the point-scoring positions.<sup id="cite_ref-f1complete.com_39-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-f1complete.com-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-f1complete.com_39-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-f1complete.com-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-45" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hamilton's win was praised by <a href="/wiki/Jackie_Stewart" title="Jackie Stewart">Jackie Stewart</a>, a three-time winner of the <a href="/wiki/Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="Monaco Grand Prix">Monaco Grand Prix</a>. "At his age, Lewis can win this race many times," he said. "This is the first, I hope, of many victories for him in Monaco so that he can join the greats of Formula One."<sup id="cite_ref-46" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Damon_Hill" title="Damon Hill">Damon Hill</a>, the <a href="/wiki/1996_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="1996 Formula One season">1996 World Champion</a>, said Hamilton "did very well indeed. I was most impressed, and the race as a whole was also a great advertisement for what F1 should be about."<sup id="cite_ref-autocourse2008-pg148_47-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autocourse2008-pg148-47">&#91;47&#93;</a></sup> The race result left Hamilton leading the Drivers' Championship with 38&#160;points.<sup id="cite_ref-pointsFIA_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pointsFIA-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Räikkönen, who failed to score in Monaco, was second on 35&#160;points, one point ahead of Massa and three ahead of Kubica. Heidfeld was sixth on 20&#160;points.<sup id="cite_ref-pointsFIA_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pointsFIA-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> In the Constructors' Championship, Ferrari maintained their lead with 69&#160;points, McLaren jumped to second on 53&#160;points, and BMW Sauber dropped to third on 52&#160;points, with 12&#160;races of the season remaining.<sup id="cite_ref-pointsFIA_5-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pointsFIA-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
