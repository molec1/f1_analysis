<p>The <b>1974 British Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>John Player Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Motor_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Motor race">motor race</a> held at <a href="/wiki/Brands_Hatch" title="Brands Hatch">Brands Hatch</a> on 20 July 1974. It was race 10 of 15 in both the <a href="/wiki/1974_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1974 World Championship of Drivers">1974 World Championship of Drivers</a> and the <a href="/wiki/1974_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1974 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1974 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>. The 75-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Jody_Scheckter" title="Jody Scheckter">Jody Scheckter</a>, driving a <a href="/wiki/Tyrrell_007" title="Tyrrell 007">Tyrrell</a>-<a href="/wiki/Ford_Motor_Company" title="Ford Motor Company">Ford</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Emerson_Fittipaldi" title="Emerson Fittipaldi">Emerson Fittipaldi</a> second in a <a href="/wiki/McLaren_M23" title="McLaren M23">McLaren</a>-Ford and <a href="/wiki/Jacky_Ickx" title="Jacky Ickx">Jacky Ickx</a> third in a <a href="/wiki/Lotus_72" title="Lotus 72">Lotus</a>-Ford.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notes">Notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1974_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Notes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Lauda completed just 73 laps but was allowed an extra lap after the team protested his exit from the pit lane was blocked after a late wheel change. He initially classified ninth, but was awarded fifth place after appeal.</li>
<li>Lombardi used number 208 because her car was sponsored by <a href="/wiki/Radio_Luxembourg_(English)" class="mw-redirect" title="Radio Luxembourg (English)">Radio Luxembourg</a>, whose wavelength was 208 metres (equivalent to a frequency of 1439 kHz).<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup></li></ul>
