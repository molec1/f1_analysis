<p>The <b>2014 Chinese Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>2014 Formula 1 UBS Chinese Grand Prix</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> that was held on 20 April 2014 at the <a href="/wiki/Shanghai_International_Circuit" title="Shanghai International Circuit">Shanghai International Circuit</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shanghai" title="Shanghai">Shanghai</a>, <a href="/wiki/China" title="China">China</a>. The race was the fourth round of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2014 Formula One season">2014 Formula One season</a>, and marked the eleventh time that the <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Grand_Prix" title="Chinese Grand Prix">Chinese Grand Prix</a> was held as a round for the Formula One World Championship.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> won the race comfortably after starting from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>, leading from start to finish driving for the <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> team. <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> finished second in the other Mercedes with <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> third in a <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>. Although contested over 56 laps, the race result was declared after 54 laps, as the chequered flag was shown one lap early through a marshalling error. The leading positions were not affected by this error.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>	
</p><p>As a consequence of the race, Rosberg's lead in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> was reduced to four points, with Alonso a further thirty-four behind. In the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">World Constructors Championship</a>, <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a> passed <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> for second position, ninety-six points behind Mercedes.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Heading into the fourth round of the season, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> was leading the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> with 61 points; Rosberg's teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> was second on 50 points, 11 points behind Rosberg. Behind Rosberg and Hamilton in the Drivers' Championship, <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> was third on 28 points, with <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> on 26 and 23 points respectively.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> In the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors Championship</a>, Mercedes were leading with 111 points and <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> were second on 44 points, with <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> third on 43 points.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Prior to the race weekend, <a href="/wiki/Stefano_Domenicali" title="Stefano Domenicali">Stefano Domenicali</a> resigned from position as <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> team principal citing the team's poor start to the season and their longest winless streak for 18 years.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Domenicali was replaced by the President and <a href="/wiki/Chief_Operating_Officer" class="mw-redirect" title="Chief Operating Officer">CEO</a> of Ferrari North America <a href="/wiki/Marco_Mattiacci" title="Marco Mattiacci">Marco Mattiacci</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Like the <a href="/wiki/2013_Chinese_Grand_Prix" title="2013 Chinese Grand Prix">2013 Chinese Grand Prix</a>, tyre supplier <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> brought its white-banded medium compound tyre as the harder "prime" tyre and the yellow-banded soft compound tyre as the softer "option" tyre.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice_and_qualifying">Practice and qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Practice and qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Three practice sessions were held before the race; the first on Friday morning and the second on Friday afternoon. Both sessions lasted one and a half hours. The third session was held on Saturday morning and lasted an hour.<sup id="cite_ref-f1regulations_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-f1regulations-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The qualifying session held on Saturday afternoon was split into three parts. The first part ran for 18 minutes and eliminated the cars from qualifying that finished the session 16th or lower. The second part of qualifying lasted 15 minutes and eliminated cars that finished in positions 11 to 16. The final part of qualifying determined the positions from first to tenth, and decided <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>.
</p><p>Qualifying took place under wet conditions, with all drivers using either intermediate or full wet tyres. Hamilton clinched his third pole position for the season, with a time of 1:53.860. As a result, Hamilton became the British driver with the highest number of pole positions, surpassing <a href="/wiki/Jim_Clark" title="Jim Clark">Jim Clark</a>'s record of 33.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Reliable_sources/Perennial_sources" title="Wikipedia:Reliable sources/Perennial sources"><span title="The material near this tag may rely on an unreliable source (November 2019)">deprecated source</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> He was joined on the front row of the grid by Riccardo with Vettel qualifying third. Rosberg qualified fourth, 1.2 seconds behind Hamilton and Alonso qualified fifth. Massa, Bottas, Hulkenberg, <a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a> and <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> rounded out the top ten. Räikkönen only managed 11th&#160;— missing out on the top ten for the second time in <a href="/wiki/2014_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2014 Formula One World Championship">2014</a> having struggled with the balance and handling of his car during the session.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Jenson Button was the best placed McLaren in 12th, ahead of <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a>, <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a>, Button's teammate <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> qualifying 16th. <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban Gutiérrez">Esteban Gutiérrez</a> was 17th, with the rest of the field consisting of the <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a> and <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia</a> cars. <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> did not take part in the qualifying sessions,<sup id="cite_ref-quali_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> but was allowed to start the race.<sup id="cite_ref-Maldonado_starts_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Maldonado_starts-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The race was raced over 56 laps, however the chequered flag was shown to <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> at end of lap 55 by mistake. According to Article 43.2 of the FIA Sporting Regulations this meant that the race officially finished at the end of lap 54.<sup id="cite_ref-f1regulations_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-f1regulations-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> This meant that <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a>'s overtake of <a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a> on the final lap did not stand.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As a consequence of the race, Hamilton reduced Rosberg's lead over him in the Drivers' Championship to four points. Alonso moved up to third position passing Hülkenberg with Vettel maintaining fifth place and Ricciardo moved from 10th to 6th.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> In the Constructors' Championship, Mercedes increased their lead, with Red Bull jumping ahead of Force India for second position and Ferrari moving to fourth, ahead of McLaren who fell from third to fifth position.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
