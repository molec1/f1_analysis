<p>The <b>2008 Australian Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>LXXIII ING Australian Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 16 March 2008 at the <a href="/wiki/Melbourne_Grand_Prix_Circuit" class="mw-redirect" title="Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit">Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit</a>, <a href="/wiki/Melbourne" title="Melbourne">Melbourne</a>, <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>. It was the first&#160;race of the <a href="/wiki/2008_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2008 Formula One season">2008 Formula One season</a>. The 58&#160;lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> for the <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a> team after starting from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>. <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> finished second in a <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a> car, with <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> third in a <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a>, his first ever F1 podium.<sup id="cite_ref-formula1.com-officialresult_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formula1.com-officialresult-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
This was the last race until the <a href="/wiki/2010_Korean_Grand_Prix" title="2010 Korean Grand Prix">2010 Korean Grand Prix</a> that neither Red Bull was classified.
</p><p>The race saw a very high rate of attrition, with only seven cars out of the 22-car grid running at the chequered flag â which reduced from seven to six after <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a>'s disqualification â with two other drivers being classified as they had completed at least 90% of the 307.574&#160;km race distance in order for classification.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_Australian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Grand Prix was contested by 22&#160;drivers, in 11 teams of two.<sup id="cite_ref-race_classification_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-race_classification-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> The teams, also known as "<a href="/wiki/Formula_One#Constructors" title="Formula One">constructors</a>", were <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>-<a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_HighPerformanceEngines" class="mw-redirect" title="Mercedes-Benz HighPerformanceEngines">Mercedes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a>, <a href="/wiki/Honda_Racing_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Honda Racing F1">Honda</a>, <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a>, <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a>, <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyota</a>, <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a>, <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a> and <a href="/wiki/Super_Aguri" class="mw-redirect" title="Super Aguri">Super Aguri</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-race_classification_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-race_classification-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Heading into the <a href="/wiki/2008_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2008 Formula One season">new season</a>, several teams opted to keep the same line-up as they had in <a href="/wiki/2007_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2007 Formula One World Championship">2007</a>, however some teams changed drivers for 2008. One of the main driver changes involved <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>, who chose to move back to <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a> for the 2008 season, with <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> moving in the opposite direction to McLaren.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Defending <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Champion</a> <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi RÃ¤ikkÃ¶nen">Kimi RÃ¤ikkÃ¶nen</a> stayed at <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> after his title-winning campaign, with the Finnish driver again being joined by <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>. As in 2007, eleven teams took to the grid for the start of the new season.
</p><p>The final mass test session before the new season took place at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Catalunya" class="mw-redirect" title="Circuit de Catalunya">Circuit de Catalunya</a> in <a href="/wiki/Montmel%C3%B3" title="MontmelÃ³">MontmelÃ³</a>, just north of <a href="/wiki/Barcelona" title="Barcelona">Barcelona</a> over 25 Februaryâ27. The Ferraris were third and sixth; Massa in 3rd and <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi RÃ¤ikkÃ¶nen">Kimi RÃ¤ikkÃ¶nen</a> in sixth. <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Toyota_Racing_(Formula_One_team)" title="Toyota Racing (Formula One team)">Toyota</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> in the <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a> completed the top eight positions.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> In preparation for the qualifying session, many teams put their cars on <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyres" title="Formula One tyres">soft tyres</a> for the third practice session. The soft tyre gives better performance than the harder type of tyre but is less durable than the harder compound.<sup id="cite_ref-formula1.com-tyres_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formula1.com-tyres-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a> team topped the third practice session with first and second positionsâ<a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> finishing the session ahead of teammate <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a>. Alonso was third in the Renault, with Rosberg rounding off the top four positions.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_Australian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quotebox pullquote floatright" style="width:40%;&#32;;"><link rel="mw-deduplicated-inline-style" href="mw-data:TemplateStyles:r887775652"/>
<div class="quotebox-quote left-aligned" style="">"I am pleased to qualify third, but there is still room for improvement. It was a fairly smooth lap, and the balance of the car felt good. At the end I didnât nail a perfect lap, but throughout the weekend I have just been taking things steady, not making any mistakes and keep pushing harder and harder. Last year I didnât make the best start to the season, so todayâs result is a great beginning to my career as a Vodafone McLaren Mercedes driver."</div>
<p><cite class="left-aligned" style=""><a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a>, commenting on his qualifying performance.<sup id="cite_ref-formula1.com-satquotes_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formula1.com-satquotes-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup></cite>
</p>
</div>
<p>The qualifying session was split into three parts. The first part of qualifying runs for 20&#160;minutes and eliminates the cars that finished the session 17th or lower. The second part of qualifying lasts for 15&#160;minutes and eliminates cars that finished in positions 11 to 16. The final part of qualifying determines the positions from first to tenth, and decides who starts the Grand Prix in <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-sporting_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sporting-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton clinched his seventh career pole position of his career, with a time of 1:26.714 in the third session. He was joined on the front-row by Kubica, who was a tenth of a second behind Hamilton.<sup id="cite_ref-autosport-quali_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosport-quali-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> Kovalainen was third, with Massa fourth, four-tenths slower than Hamilton in the third part. In contrast to Massa's fourth place, his teammate, RÃ¤ikkÃ¶nen finished qualifying in 16th place. His Ferrari car suffered a <a href="/wiki/Fuel_pump_(engine)" class="mw-redirect" title="Fuel pump (engine)">fuel pump</a> problem at the end of part one, with the Ferrari team unable to fix the problem before the second session.<sup id="cite_ref-kimi-quali_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-kimi-quali-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> RÃ¤ikkÃ¶nen was not the only driver to suffer problems. Webber in the Red Bull and <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a> also suffered problems, with Webber spinning off in session two due to <a href="/wiki/Brake_fade" title="Brake fade">brake failure</a>, leaving him down in 15th. Vettel was scheduled to be in the third session, but his Toro Rosso suffered <a href="/wiki/Hydraulic_pump" title="Hydraulic pump">oil pump failure</a>, preventing him from setting a time in the session. Heidfeld, Trulli, Rosberg and Coulthard completed the top eight positions.<sup id="cite_ref-autosport-quali_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosport-quali-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Timo_Glock" title="Timo Glock">Timo Glock</a> in a Toyota finished qualifying in ninth position, however, he was demoted to 19th after being given two five-place grid penalties. His first five-place penalty was a result of a gearbox change, while his second five-place penalty was for impeding Webber during the session.<sup id="cite_ref-timo-quali_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-timo-quali-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_Australian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The conditions for the race were dry with the <a href="/wiki/Air_temperature" class="mw-redirect" title="Air temperature">air temperature</a> 37&#160;Â°C (99&#160;Â°F).<sup id="cite_ref-temperature-forum_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-temperature-forum-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> The race started at 15:30 <a href="/wiki/Time_in_Australia#Daylight_saving_time" title="Time in Australia">AEDT</a> (<a href="/wiki/Greenwich_Mean_Time" title="Greenwich Mean Time">GMT</a> +10). Hamilton, from pole position on the grid, held onto the lead into the first corner with Kubica behind in second position. Sebastian Vettel stalled his car on the grid and therefore pulled away very slowly, and lost several positions in the process. Behind the leading two, Massa spun coming out of turn one whilst racing Kovalainen, and damaged his front wing against the barriers, costing him a pit stop.
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Heidfeld_and_Rosberg_-_2008_Melb_GP.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/2a/Heidfeld_and_Rosberg_-_2008_Melb_GP.jpg/170px-Heidfeld_and_Rosberg_-_2008_Melb_GP.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="113" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/2a/Heidfeld_and_Rosberg_-_2008_Melb_GP.jpg/255px-Heidfeld_and_Rosberg_-_2008_Melb_GP.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/2a/Heidfeld_and_Rosberg_-_2008_Melb_GP.jpg/340px-Heidfeld_and_Rosberg_-_2008_Melb_GP.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1600" data-file-height="1067" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Heidfeld_and_Rosberg_-_2008_Melb_GP.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> finished second followed by <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> in third. The two are here seen during one of the <a href="/wiki/Safety_Car" class="mw-redirect" title="Safety Car">Safety Car</a> periods.</div></div></div>
<p>Nelson Piquet Jr. made contact with Italian <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a> at the first corner, putting Fisichella out of the race. In another incident at turn three Mark Webber, Anthony Davidson, Jenson Button and Sebastian Vettel came together, putting all four out of the race. <a href="/wiki/Kazuki_Nakajima" title="Kazuki Nakajima">Kazuki Nakajima</a> drove into Vettel as he spun and lost his front wing and had to pit in for a new one. The safety car was deployed in response to the accidents. At the front Lewis Hamilton took the early lead, followed by Kubica and Rosberg. Massa's teammate RÃ¤ikkÃ¶nen elevated himself from fifteenth to eighth on the first lap.
</p><p>The safety car came in on lap three and Hamilton quickly opened up a lead from Kubica in second. RÃ¤ikkÃ¶nen was stuck behind Rubens Barrichello, finally passing him lap 19 for sixth place. During the first set of <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit stops</a> Toyota driver <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a> was forced to retire due to electrical problems.
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Podium_2008_Australia.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b2/Podium_2008_Australia.jpg/220px-Podium_2008_Australia.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="330" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b2/Podium_2008_Australia.jpg/330px-Podium_2008_Australia.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b2/Podium_2008_Australia.jpg/440px-Podium_2008_Australia.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2102" data-file-height="3153" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Podium_2008_Australia.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The podium ceremony after the race.</div></div></div>
<p>On lap 26 Massa attempted to pass <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a>. The two collided; Coulthard retired from the race with serious damage to his car. Coulthard was irate at Massa after the race for not apologising for the incident. The safety car came out for the second time. Massa retired on lap 29 with Ferrari's first engine failure of the race. RÃ¤ikkÃ¶nen, who had been about to pit, had to stay out. Despite this he did not pit when the pit was re-opened two laps later, preferring to move up the field as other drivers pitted.
</p><p>The race restarted on lap 30, and immediately RÃ¤ikkÃ¶nen made an over-ambitious move on Heikki Kovalainen for second place and outbraked himself into turn 3, going right off the track and across the gravel, losing several places. Two laps later, he was chasing Timo Glock, but as he drifted to the outside of the track, he just put his left rear wheel onto the grass and spun 360 degrees, in exactly the same place as his earlier off . He went off the track again on lap 42.
</p><p>On lap 44 <a href="/wiki/Timo_Glock" title="Timo Glock">Timo Glock</a> ran wide onto the grass and the bottom of his car hit two substantial bumps, the second of which caused it to briefly fly up in the air and land with force sufficient to break the suspension, leaving the <a href="/wiki/Monocoque" title="Monocoque">monocoque</a> in contact with the ground and unable to be stopped, resulting in a sizable accident. Glock spun twice and came to rest against the wall further down the track. He was forced to retire and the safety car came out for a third time. On the same lap, due to a false signal from the chief mechanic, Barrichello left a pit stop before the fuel hose had been removed from his car. Several mechanics suffered minor injuries. He would also serve a ten-second penalty for this pit stop as it was taken while the pit lane was closed. Ultimately he was disqualified for exiting the pitlane when the red light was on.
</p><p>The race restarted on lap 47 and one lap later Kubica was forced out of the race after a collision  with Kazuki Nakajima. Fernando Alonso was able to move up the field as a result of the high number of retirements and overtaking of Kovalainen and RÃ¤ikkÃ¶nen in a single move,<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> and was fifth with eight laps left. However Kovalainen threatened to pass him in the last few laps. RÃ¤ikkÃ¶nen retired with Ferrari's second engine failure of the race on lap 54, after driving slowly into the pit lane.
</p><p>SÃ©bastien Bourdais's first Formula One race ended on lap 56 after suffering an engine failure (Ferrari's third of the race). He was running 4th place in the Scuderia Toro Rosso at the time and had completed his final pit stop. At the end of the penultimate lap, Kovalainen finally passed Alonso coming out of turn 13, to take fourth place. But moments later on the pit straight, he went to tear a strip off his visor, and accidentally hit the pit lane speed limiter, allowing Alonso back through.
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Rubens_barrichello_waves_to_crowd_-_08_Melb_GP.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1d/Rubens_barrichello_waves_to_crowd_-_08_Melb_GP.jpg/220px-Rubens_barrichello_waves_to_crowd_-_08_Melb_GP.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1d/Rubens_barrichello_waves_to_crowd_-_08_Melb_GP.jpg/330px-Rubens_barrichello_waves_to_crowd_-_08_Melb_GP.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1d/Rubens_barrichello_waves_to_crowd_-_08_Melb_GP.jpg/440px-Rubens_barrichello_waves_to_crowd_-_08_Melb_GP.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1600" data-file-height="1067" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Rubens_barrichello_waves_to_crowd_-_08_Melb_GP.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Rubens Barrichello waving to the crowd after finishing sixth. He would later be disqualified</div></div></div>
<p>Seven cars finished the race, but Barrichello was later disqualified for passing a red light at the pit lane exit. Lewis Hamilton won, having led all but 5 laps of the race. Nick Heidfeld came second and Nico Rosberg recorded his first podium in third. Kazuki Nakajima was the final car to finish, but was later given a ten-place grid penalty for the <a href="/wiki/2008_Malaysian_Grand_Prix" title="2008 Malaysian Grand Prix">next race</a> after hitting Kubica under safety car conditions.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_Australian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

It is great preparation for Malaysia so bring it on, I am really looking forward to it."<sup id="cite_ref-PC_23-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PC-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> He also commended the work of his McLaren team on both his scheduled stops,<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> on laps 18 and 43 respectively:
</p>
<style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r960796168">.mw-parser-output .templatequote{overflow:hidden;margin:1em 0;padding:0 40px}.mw-parser-output .templatequote .templatequotecite{line-height:1.5em;text-align:left;padding-left:1.6em;margin-top:0}</style><blockquote class="templatequote"><p>They did a fantastic job as always but perhaps a big and huge thank you to Vodafone McLaren Mercedes.
