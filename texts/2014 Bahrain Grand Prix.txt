<p>The <b>2014 Bahrain Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>2014 Formula 1 Gulf Air Bahrain Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 6 April at the <a href="/wiki/Bahrain_International_Circuit" title="Bahrain International Circuit">Bahrain International Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Sakhir" title="Sakhir">Sakhir</a>, Bahrain. It was the third round of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2014 Formula One season">2014 Formula One season</a>, the 900th Formula One World Championship event, and the eleventh running of the <a href="/wiki/Bahrain_Grand_Prix" title="Bahrain Grand Prix">race</a>. The 57-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> who started from second position. His teammate <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> finished second and <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> came in third. It was Hamilton's second victory of the season, his first in Bahrain, and the 24th of his Formula One career.
</p><p>Hamilton accelerated faster than Rosberg off the line and overtook him at the first corner. Both Mercedes drivers pulled away from the rest of the field and Rosberg conserved fuel which allowed him to attack Hamilton before the first round of <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit stops</a> on lap 19 with his teammate successfully defending the lead. He extended his advantage over Rosberg over the next 22 laps before an incident between <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> and <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban Gutiérrez">Esteban Gutiérrez</a> caused the <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a> to be deployed and his lead was reduced to nothing. Following instructions to bring the cars safely to the finish, Rosberg immediately battled Hamilton for first place, but was unable to get ahead of him and Hamilton maintained a steady advantage when his teammate's soft compound tyres were worn out to win the race.
</p><p>The result reduced Rosberg's lead over Hamilton in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> to 11 <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_points_scoring_systems" class="mw-redirect" title="List of Formula One points scoring systems">points</a>. <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> moved from sixth to third after finishing in fifth place, while <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> fell one place each to round out the top five. Mercedes increased their advantage in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a> to be 68 points ahead of second-placed Force India who moved from fifth to second because of their strong result. <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> were third with <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull" title="Red Bull">Red Bull</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> in positions fourth and fifth with 16 races left in the season.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Bahrain_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Bahrain_International_Circuit_back_straight.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f0/Bahrain_International_Circuit_back_straight.jpg/220px-Bahrain_International_Circuit_back_straight.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="171" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f0/Bahrain_International_Circuit_back_straight.jpg/330px-Bahrain_International_Circuit_back_straight.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f0/Bahrain_International_Circuit_back_straight.jpg/440px-Bahrain_International_Circuit_back_straight.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3596" data-file-height="2793" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Bahrain_International_Circuit_back_straight.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Bahrain_International_Circuit" title="Bahrain International Circuit">Bahrain International Circuit</a>, where the race was held.</div></div></div>
<p>The 2014 Bahrain Grand Prix was the third of 19 scheduled rounds of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2014 Formula One season">2014 Formula One season</a>, the eleventh running of the event, as well as the 900th Formula One World Championship race.<sup id="cite_ref-papers_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-papers-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-preview_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-preview-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It was held on 6 April 2014 at the <a href="/wiki/Bahrain_International_Circuit" title="Bahrain International Circuit">Bahrain International Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Sakhir" title="Sakhir">Sakhir</a>, Bahrain.<sup id="cite_ref-results_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-results-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyre" class="mw-redirect" title="Formula One tyre">Tyre</a> supplier <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> brought two types of tyre to the race: two dry compounds (soft "options" and medium "primes").<sup id="cite_ref-stats_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-stats-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Drag_reduction_system" title="Drag reduction system">drag reduction system</a> (DRS) had two activation zones for the race: one was on the <a href="/wiki/Straight_(racing)" title="Straight (racing)">straight</a> between turns 10 and 11, and the second was on the start/finish straight from the final to the first corners.<sup id="cite_ref-preview_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-preview-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Going into the race, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> with 43 <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_points_scoring_systems" class="mw-redirect" title="List of Formula One points scoring systems">points</a>, ahead of teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> in second, and <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> in third place. <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> was fourth on 23 points, three ahead of his McLaren teammate <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a> in fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-points_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Mercedes were leading the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a> with 68 points,  <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> were in second on 43 points.  <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> were third on 30 points, while <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> (20 points) and <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> (19) contended for fourth place.<sup id="cite_ref-points_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>With the potential of a title challenge between the two Mercedes drivers, Hamilton felt their relationship would be unaffected by the race, noting that the two had a healthy working association. Rosberg said that he recognised that his relationship with Hamilton could change as the season progressed, noting that the two drivers had battled for a championship when they were competing in go-karts. He was confident they would work through the circumstances.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton aimed to secure his first victory at the circuit, while Rosberg stated that he enjoyed racing at the track and hoped to continue his recent momentum in the race.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> team principal <a href="/wiki/Christian_Horner" title="Christian Horner">Christian Horner</a> felt the power advantage of both Mercedes cars would possibly be greater because of the Bahrain International Circuit being a "power-dominated" track. He believed the country's hot climate would affect his team's chances, but said they would attempt to make progress during the race weekend.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In January 2014, an alliance of Bahraini human rights organisations including the <a href="/wiki/Bahrain_Centre_for_Human_Rights" title="Bahrain Centre for Human Rights">Bahrain Centre for Human Rights</a> (BCHR) and the <a href="/wiki/Bahrain_Youth_Society_for_Human_Rights" title="Bahrain Youth Society for Human Rights">Bahrain Youth Society for Human Rights</a> wrote to <a href="/wiki/Jean_Todt" title="Jean Todt">Jean Todt</a>, the president of the sport's governing body, the <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_l%27Automobile" title="Fédération Internationale de l&#39;Automobile">Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile</a> (FIA), requesting that the Bahrain Grand Prix be suspended until the FIA's Ethics Committee could investigate the impact the race has on <a href="/wiki/Human_rights_in_Bahrain" title="Human rights in Bahrain">human rights in Bahrain</a>. According to the BCHR no response was received from the FIA.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Reports from the race meeting indicated that thousands of people attended a rally held on the Friday before the race itself outside the Bahraini capital of <a href="/wiki/Manama" title="Manama">Manama</a> protesting against the Grand Prix and the government. Clashes between protesters and police took place following the demonstration.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In October 2013, it was revealed by former McLaren team principal <a href="/wiki/Martin_Whitmarsh" title="Martin Whitmarsh">Martin Whitmarsh</a> that the race would occur between the early part of the evening and nightfall saying he had observed plans for the idea.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> This was confirmed by the race organisers one month later as part of an effort to celebrate the tenth anniversary of the event's first running. Its floodlighting system was tested at the <a href="/wiki/2013_6_Hours_of_Bahrain" title="2013 6 Hours of Bahrain">2013 6 Hours of Bahrain</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Following the success of the trial, it was later confirmed on 5 April by the chairperson of the Bahrain International Circuit, Zayed Al Zayani, that the Bahrain Grand Prix would remain a night race for future seasons. Sunday is a work day in Bahrain so scheduling the race in the evening resulted in sharply higher ticket sales. That, along with the investment in lighting, made it certain that all Grand Prix races in Bahrain would be evening events.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> Three drivers made their first appearances of the season in a Formula One car during the first practice session. <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Nasr" title="Felipe Nasr">Felipe Nasr</a> drove in place of Williams driver <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a> of <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban Gutiérrez">Esteban Gutiérrez</a> was taken over by <a href="/wiki/Giedo_van_der_Garde" title="Giedo van der Garde">Giedo van der Garde</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Robin_Frijns" title="Robin Frijns">Robin Frijns</a> utilised <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Bahrain_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Three practice sessions—two on Friday and a third on Saturday—were held before the Sunday race. The Friday morning and afternoon sessions lasted ninety minutes each; the third, one-hour session was held on Saturday morning.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton was fastest in the first practice session with a lap of 1 minute and 37.502 seconds; Rosberg, Alonso, <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a>, Button, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>, Magnussen, <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> rounded out the session's top ten drivers.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> In the second practice session, held in cooler weather, Hamilton recorded the day's fastest lap of 1 minute and 34.325 seconds, with teammate Rosberg in second. Alonso, <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>, Vettel, Magnussen, Pérez and Bottas followed in the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> damaged his car after his car went airborne from contact with the turn four exit <a href="/wiki/Curb" title="Curb">kerbs</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Max_Chilton" title="Max Chilton">Max Chilton</a> lost control of his <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia</a> braking for the same corner because of a front-left <a href="/wiki/Disc_brake" title="Disc brake">brake disc</a> failure.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Pérez swerved to avoid hitting Rosberg who slowed in the 13th and 14th turns. The stewards reprimanded Rosberg, while Gutiérrez was not penalised for impeding Massa in the same area.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> The turn four kerb was removed by course officials following complaints from several drivers. The FIA's race director <a href="/wiki/Charlie_Whiting" title="Charlie Whiting">Charlie Whiting</a> wrote to all teams warning that the track limits would be strictly enforced in the event any driver was shown to gain an advantage by going over this area.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> Several cars ran wide at the entry of turn 11 during the second session because of a breezy <a href="/wiki/Headwind_and_tailwind" title="Headwind and tailwind">tail wind</a> on the backstraight. Hamilton set the fastest lap with a time of 1 minute and 35.324 seconds, ahead of Rosberg, Pérez. Bottas, Massa, Button, Alonso, Hülkenberg, Kvyat and Räikkönen. Vettel missed the session's final 20 minutes because he spun at the second turn's exit and beached his car in the corner's <a href="/wiki/Run-off_area" title="Run-off area">gravel trap</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Bahrain_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_(cropped).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/51/Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg/170px-Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="206" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/51/Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg/255px-Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/51/Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg/340px-Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1890" data-file-height="2285" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_(cropped).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> had the fifth <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> of his career.</div></div></div>
<p>Saturday evening's <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#Q" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">qualifying session</a> was divided into three parts. The first part ran for 18 minutes, eliminating cars that finished 17th or below. The <a href="/wiki/107%25_rule" title="107% rule">107% rule</a> was in effect, requiring drivers to reach a time within 107 per cent of the quickest lap to qualify. The second session lasted 15 minutes, eliminating cars that finished 11th to 16th. The final session lasted 12 minutes and determined <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> to tenth. Cars who progressed to the final session were not allowed to change tyres for the race's start, using the tyres with which they set their quickest lap times in the second session.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> It was the first dry qualifying session of the season,<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> and the track temperature cooled gradually as it progressed.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg set the fastest times in the second and third sessions to clinch his first pole position of the season, the fifth of his career, and his second in Bahrain, with a time of 1 minute and 33.185 seconds.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:1_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> He was joined on the <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#G" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">grid</a>'s front row by Hamilton, who locked his brakes going into the first turn on his final timed lap, <a href="/wiki/Flat_spot" title="Flat spot">flat-spotting</a> his tyres and abandoning his attempt.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo qualified in third, but took a ten-place grid penalty for an unsafe <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit stop</a> release at the season's previous round in Malaysia.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_29-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> Hence, Bottas inherited the position and Pérez took fourth.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_27-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Räikkönen felt uncomfortable with his Ferrari and medium compound tyre issues restricted him to fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_25-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Button—in his 250th Grand Prix start—<sup id="cite_ref-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> took sixth while an <a href="/wiki/Understeer_and_oversteer" title="Understeer and oversteer">oversteer</a> left Massa in seventh.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_25-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Magnussen and Alonso were eighth and ninth respectively.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_27-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Vettel rounded out the top ten qualifiers due to a lack of track running following his final practice session spin, and a downshifting issue.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_29-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hülkenberg was the fastest driver not advancing into the final session;<sup id="cite_ref-:1_27-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> he went wide at the turn eleven exit kerb which cost him time.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_25-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Kvyat's fastest time was achieved in the track's first sector which qualified him 12th. After Ricciardo's penalty was applied, Kvyat was separated by teammate <a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a> who made an engine adjustment after feeling he was low on power. Gutiérrez took 15th ahead of the two <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a> cars of <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> and Maldonado who both struggled for pace.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_25-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> failed to advance beyond the first session due to slower cars causing him to abort his final timed lap.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_25-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> However, he incurred a five-place grid penalty after he was deemed by the stewards to have forced Grosjean wide between turns 13 and 14 and two penalty points on his <a href="/wiki/FIA_Super_Licence" title="FIA Super Licence">super licence</a> and started from the back of the field.<sup id="cite_ref-sutilpenalty_31-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sutilpenalty-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Kobayashi and <a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a> started from 18th and 19th;<sup id="cite_ref-:1_27-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Kobayashi did one timed lap on the soft compound tyres before entering the pit lane and rejoined the track with three minutes remaining for one further lap. Bianchi lost three-tenths of a second on the straights which he felt prevented him from qualifying ahead of Kobayashi.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_25-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Ericsson" title="Marcus Ericsson">Marcus Ericsson</a> used a new set of soft compound tyres but brake locking and an <a href="/wiki/Kinetic_energy_recovery_system" title="Kinetic energy recovery system">energy recovery system</a> issue slowed him. He made a pit stop for a new set of soft compound tyres and qualified in 20th, ahead of teammate Max Chilton in 21st.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_25-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Bahrain_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:First_lap_2014_Bahrain_Grand_Prix_(3).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/82/First_lap_2014_Bahrain_Grand_Prix_%283%29.jpg/220px-First_lap_2014_Bahrain_Grand_Prix_%283%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/82/First_lap_2014_Bahrain_Grand_Prix_%283%29.jpg/330px-First_lap_2014_Bahrain_Grand_Prix_%283%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/82/First_lap_2014_Bahrain_Grand_Prix_%283%29.jpg/440px-First_lap_2014_Bahrain_Grand_Prix_%283%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="4608" data-file-height="3072" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:First_lap_2014_Bahrain_Grand_Prix_(3).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The start of the race where <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> passed teammate <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> for the lead.</div></div></div>
<p>The race began at 18:00 <a href="/wiki/UTC%2B03:00" title="UTC+03:00">Arabia Standard Time</a> (UTC+03:00).<sup id="cite_ref-:5_33-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> The weather at the start was dry and hot with the air temperature between 23 to 26&#160;°C (73 to 79&#160;°F) and a track temperature ranging from 26 to 30&#160;°C (79 to 86&#160;°F).<sup id="cite_ref-34" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> The cooler temperatures reduced the advantage of the medium compound tyres over the soft compound to less than the two seconds observed during daylight conditions.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Approximately 38,140 people attended the event.<sup id="cite_ref-35" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>When the race started, Hamilton accelerated faster than teammate Rosberg off the line,<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_36-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> moving ahead of him by using the space between the pit wall heading into the first corner. Hamilton oversteered after leaving turn three and moved to the <a href="/wiki/Racing_line" title="Racing line">outside</a> of the track.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg attempted to retake the lead by going around the outside at the fourth turn, but Hamilton defended the position.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_36-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Alonso overtook teammate Raikkönen around the inside at the first turn and then made contact with Magnussen. Alonso continued with minor bodywork damage.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Vergne was squeezed by Maldonado at the turn eight entry and attempted to pass him. Maldonado drove defensively and both made contact.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:6_37-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Vergne had a <a href="/wiki/Flat_tire" title="Flat tire">punctured</a> right-rear tyre,<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_36-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> along with rear wing and floor damage,<sup id="cite_ref-:6_37-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> and drove to the pit lane for repairs.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_38-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Massa made the best start of the field, moving from seventh to third by the end of the first lap, while Vergne lost eight positions over the same distance because of the Maldonado collision.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_33-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> At the end of the first lap, Hamilton led Rosberg by one second followed by: Massa, Pérez, Bottas, Button, Alonso, Hülkenberg, Räikkönen and Vettel.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Having been unsuccessful in his attempts to retake the lead, Rosberg started to conserve fuel to prepare for an attack later in the race. Both he and Hamilton pulled away from the rest of the field.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Hülkenberg passed Alonso by braking later than him at the first turn on lap four to move into seventh.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> The Williams cars were harder on their tyres than the surrounding cars,<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> and third-placed Massa came under pressure from Pérez while Button closed the gap to Bottas.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_36-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo went to the outside of Magnussen on the ninth lap and overtook him for eleventh driving into turn one. Ricciardo went off onto the corner's run-off area onto the exit kerbs, and defended his position from Kvyat who went to the inside of him going into turn four.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Button battled Bottas around the inside heading towards the first turn on the following lap. The two drivers drove alongside each other through the following corner until Button moved in front of Bottas.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_36-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Jules_Bianchi_Bahre%C3%AFn_2014.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6e/Jules_Bianchi_Bahre%C3%AFn_2014.jpg/220px-Jules_Bianchi_Bahre%C3%AFn_2014.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6e/Jules_Bianchi_Bahre%C3%AFn_2014.jpg/330px-Jules_Bianchi_Bahre%C3%AFn_2014.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6e/Jules_Bianchi_Bahre%C3%AFn_2014.jpg/440px-Jules_Bianchi_Bahre%C3%AFn_2014.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3696" data-file-height="2464" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Jules_Bianchi_Bahre%C3%AFn_2014.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a> punctured his tyre in a collision with <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>Ricciardo caught up to Magnussen while Hülkenberg overtook Bottas for seventh position shortly afterwards.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_38-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo moved to the outside of Magnuseen on the backstraight and passed him after the McLaren slid while defending eleventh place on lap eleven. Pérez attacked Massa going into the fourth turn and overtook him for third place on the next lap through turns five and six. Massa attempted to reclaim the position driving into the turn seven <a href="/wiki/Hairpin_turn" title="Hairpin turn">hairpin</a>, but locked his tyres and temporarily bowed out of the battle.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Both Williams cars – as well as both Ferrari drivers – had to change from a two-stop to a three-stop strategy as a result of high tyre wear. Magnussen also converted to a three-stop strategy due to high tyre wear, but teammate Button was able to conserve his tyres well enough to continue with doing just two stops.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Sutil and Bianchi twice impacted each other at the first turn with the second collision causing Sutil to stop at the corner with a puncture,<sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> and debris was littered on the track.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_38-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Bianchi's right-rear tyre was punctured and he went to the pit lane for a replacement wheel, but his car's floor was damaged, causing a loss in <a href="/wiki/Downforce" title="Downforce">downforce</a> which meant he had difficulty driving his car for the remainder of the race.<sup id="cite_ref-:6_37-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Vettel's DRS stopped working and he was quickly caught by teammate Ricciardo by the 15th lap.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_36-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo told his team by radio that the two cars were losing time and Red Bull <a href="/wiki/Team_orders" title="Team orders">ordered</a> Vettel to cede sixth place to Ricciardo which he did immediately.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_38-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Hülkenberg made a pit stop on the same lap and overtook Räikkonen and Kvyat on his <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#O" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">out-lap</a> shortly afterwards.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Having saved more fuel, Rosberg had more horsepower available for an attack. After using DRS down the pit straight he managed to briefly get past Hamilton by out-braking on the inside into turn one. However, Rosberg's late braking forced him slightly wide, and Hamilton switched to the inside to immediately retake the position from Rosberg. On the following lap, Rosberg attempted the same move into the first turn again, and this time managed to stay ahead through the turn. However, Hamilton managed to come back at his teammate to claim the inside for turn two, with the two coming perilously close to contact as Hamilton cut across to retake first from Rosberg. This overtake was critical because as the lead Mercedes car, it meant that Hamilton was called into the pit lane first – diving into the pit lane at the end of the lap to be fitted with the soft compound tyre – while Rosberg would make a pit stop on lap 21 and take on the harder medium tyre.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ricciardo and Button made pit stops on lap 20 while Bianchi was issued with a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_regulations#Penalties" title="Formula One regulations">drive-through penalty</a> because he was judged to have caused the incident between himself and Sutil. Button caught Räikkönen and used DRS to overtake him heading into the first corner.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Sutil retired his Sauber after several slow laps on the track allowed him to gain more track experience.<sup id="cite_ref-:6_37-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Vettel moved in front of Räikkönen by going around the inside without using the DRS system between the fourth and fifth turns, while Massa defended third position from teammate Bottas at the first turn.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Three laps later, Massa came under pressure from Hülkenberg who attacked Massa by going around the inside heading towards the fourth turn and moved out of his <a href="/wiki/Drafting_(aerodynamics)" title="Drafting (aerodynamics)">slipstream</a>, but Massa defended the place. Pérez took advantage to attack before entering turn five and moved in front of Hülkenberg.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_36-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo locked his tyres going into turn one and narrowly avoided impacting Räikkönen, but caught him on the backstraight. Bottas quickly caught the battle between Räikkönen and Ricciardo. He passed Ricciardo around the outside at turn one on the 30th lap and swerved left off onto a run-off area to avoid striking Räikkönen. Ricciardo retook the position.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Sergio_P%C3%A9rez_Bahrain_2014.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c3/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez_Bahrain_2014.jpg/220px-Sergio_P%C3%A9rez_Bahrain_2014.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c3/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez_Bahrain_2014.jpg/330px-Sergio_P%C3%A9rez_Bahrain_2014.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c3/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez_Bahrain_2014.jpg/440px-Sergio_P%C3%A9rez_Bahrain_2014.jpg 2x" data-file-width="4608" data-file-height="3072" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Sergio_P%C3%A9rez_Bahrain_2014.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> secured his first podium finish since the <a href="/wiki/2012_Italian_Grand_Prix" title="2012 Italian Grand Prix">2012 Italian Grand Prix</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>Hamilton created a gap on the faster tyre over the next phase of the race and by lap 32 he was 8.5 seconds ahead of Rosberg.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_36-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo was able to pass Räikkönen and Bottas attempted to move in front of Räikkönen going into the downhill turn eight and passed him on the backstraight. Bottas closed up to Ricciardo while Massa passed Räikkönen on the backstraight for ninth.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Pérez made a pit stop on lap 35 and rejoined behind Alonso before immediately passing Alonso. Ricciardo lost seventh position when Massa passed him.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_36-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Ericsson pulled over to the side of the track to retire with an oil leak which followed a loss of engine power on the 37th lap.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_36-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:6_37-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo overtook Räikkönen around the outside on the following lap and moved in front of Magnuseen shortly afterwards.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton was around 10 seconds ahead of Rosberg with 17 laps remaining. However, with the soft compound tyre estimated to be as much as a second a lap faster than the medium compound, Rosberg still had a very good chance of catching up to his teammate in the remaining laps, as they were scheduled to be put on the opposite tyres for the final stint – Hamilton on medium tyres and Rosberg on the soft compound option.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After rejoining the track following a pit stop on lap 41, Maldonado was unable to react to Gutiérrez steering right into the first corner and hit his right-rear corner. Gutiérrez rolled over twice in the air before landing upright on the track.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> The ensuing debris resulted in the deployment of the <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_38-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Gutiérrez remained in his car for some time before climbing out to be transported to the local hospital for examination.<sup id="cite_ref-:7_40-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:7-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Magnussen retired with clutch problems on the 42nd lap.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_38-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Both Mercedes drivers made pit stops during the safety car period, but its deployment meant Hamilton's lead of over 10 seconds over Rosberg was reduced to nothing. With no gap between them, Hamilton would be at a big disadvantage on medium tyres that were estimated to be around a second a lap slower as the two Mercedes cars scrapped for the lead.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> As the safety car circulated, the team's technical director <a href="/wiki/Paddy_Lowe" title="Paddy Lowe">Paddy Lowe</a> spoke to both Mercedes drivers on the radio to remind them that both cars needed to finish the race.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_36-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> With ten laps to go, the safety car drove into the pit lane and racing resumed. Hamilton defended from Rosberg heading into turn one, but Rosberg got the better line through the corner and through turns two and three, and he attempted to overtake again on the outside of turn four. Hamilton defended again, holding his teammate out wide and managed to maintain the lead.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c3/Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg/170px-Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="237" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c3/Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg/255px-Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c3/Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg/340px-Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg 2x" data-file-width="784" data-file-height="1094" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> held off a long race challenge from teammate <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> to win the race.</div></div></div>
<p>Pérez fended off a challenge from teammate Hülkenberg for third. Button fell to seventh after being passed by Vettel on the backstraight and Ricciardo around the outside,<sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> while Maldonado was issued with a ten-second stop-and-go penalty for causing the incident between himself and Gutiérrez on lap 49 which he took immediately.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_36-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> With their drivers fighting hard for the lead, and with fuel no longer being a concern due to the safety car period, the Mercedes cars were being driven flat-out and the extent of their superiority was revealed for the first time; within three laps the two Mercedes had created a six-second gap over Pérez. Rosberg recorded the race's <a href="/wiki/Fastest_lap" title="Fastest lap">fastest lap</a> on the 49th lap, completing a circuit of one minute and 37.020 seconds.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo passed teammate Vettel for fifth going on lap 50 into the first turn. Vettel attacked going up the hill heading towards turn four but Ricciardo defended the position.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_38-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> On lap 52, Rosberg again attempted a late braking move into turn one, but Hamilton managed to stay ahead by waiting for Rosberg to run wide from his late braking manoeuvre and turned in to switch to the inside and maintain his lead. Rosberg continued his assault by attempting the move again on the following lap, and again Hamilton drove defensively heading towards turn four and managed to hold on to the lead.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hülkenberg made a mistake, allowing Ricciardo to take advantage and passed him at the turn eleven exit to move into fourth place on lap 53. Massa battled Vettel for sixth driving into the first corner and overtook him at the fourth turn, but Vettel retook the position.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_38-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Button slowed with a <a href="/wiki/Clutch" title="Clutch">clutch</a> problem and drove into his garage to become the race's final retirement on the 56th lap.<sup id="cite_ref-:6_37-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_38-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> With the two Mercedes drivers having repeatedly duelled for position over the closing laps, Rosberg had taken the best from his tyres by the start of the final lap.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_28-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton maintained his advantage over the rest of the lap to win the race.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_36-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg finished second, and Pérez narrowly held off a closing Ricciardo over the event's final few laps to clinch third.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportlive_39-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportlive-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> It was Pérez's first podium since the <a href="/wiki/2012_Italian_Grand_Prix" title="2012 Italian Grand Prix">2012 Italian Grand Prix</a> and Force India's first since the <a href="/wiki/2009_Belgian_Grand_Prix" title="2009 Belgian Grand Prix">2009 Belgian Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-41" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Hülkenberg finished fifth, ahead of Vettel and Massa in sixth and seventh. Bottas, Alonso and Räikkönen rounded out the top ten points-scoring finishers. Kvyat, Grosjean, Chilton, Maldonado and Kobayashi filled the next five positions, while Bianchi (one lap down) was the last of the classified finishers.<sup id="cite_ref-stats_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-stats-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> There were two lead changes in the race; two drivers reached the front of the field. Hamilton's total of 54 laps led was the most of any competitor.<sup id="cite_ref-results_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-results-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> It was Hamilton's second consecutive victory of the season, his first in Bahrain, and the 24th of his career, equalling the number of victories achieved by five-time World Champion <a href="/wiki/Juan_Manuel_Fangio" title="Juan Manuel Fangio">Juan Manuel Fangio</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-42" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Bahrain_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At the podium interviews, conducted by singer <a href="/wiki/Brian_Johnson" title="Brian Johnson">Brian Johnson</a>, Hamilton said he was "really, really proud" to have secured his first victory at the track and was grateful for his team for working hard to achieve the result. He praised Rosberg for his race performance and described the battle between the two drivers as "very fair".<sup id="cite_ref-:8_43-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg stated that he strongly disliked finishing behind Hamilton, and it was something he did not particularly enjoy, but felt the event was "the most exciting race I’ve ever done in my whole career".<sup id="cite_ref-:8_43-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> Pérez said his third-place result was "very special" to him as it was his third race with the Force India team. He said it had been easier for him until the deployment of the safety car, and revealed he would not have been able to fend off Ricciardo for another lap.<sup id="cite_ref-:8_43-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> In the later press conference, Hamilton said it was difficult to defend against Rosberg with the medium compound tyres, and that he had not driven as hard against him since they competed against each other early in their careers.<sup id="cite_ref-:8_43-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg stated he was pushing hard and revealed his strategy was planned by Mercedes and would undertake a similar move in the future.<sup id="cite_ref-:8_43-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> Pérez felt his podium position was important to him having never achieved the feat while driving for McLaren in <a href="/wiki/2013_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2013 Formula One season">2013</a>; finishing the race in third gave him "a great feeling".<sup id="cite_ref-:8_43-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/00/Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg/170px-Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="256" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/00/Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg/255px-Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/00/Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg/340px-Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg 2x" data-file-width="800" data-file-height="1205" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> <i>(pictured in 2015)</i> was penalised five grid places for the <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Grand_Prix" title="Chinese Grand Prix">Chinese Grand Prix</a> following contact with <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban Gutiérrez">Esteban Gutiérrez</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>Gutiérrez said he was surprised at the force of Maldonado's impact which forced his retirement, arguing that he was in front of the driver and could do to nothing to prevent the crash from occurring.<sup id="cite_ref-:7_40-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:7-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Maldonado claimed he was unable to avoid contact with Gutiérrez, but stated he would watch the incident again as he believed Gutiérrez missed his braking point and was off the racing line going into the first turn.<sup id="cite_ref-:7_40-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:7-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Maldonado incurred with a five-place grid penalty for the next round of the season in <a href="/wiki/2014_Chinese_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Chinese Grand Prix">China</a>, and three penalty points on his super licence.<sup id="cite_ref-44" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> The two drivers spoke to each other about the crash and Maldonado told him that he did not expect to steer into Gutiérrez, but maintained that he did not cause the incident.<sup id="cite_ref-45" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup> Sutil felt Bianchi was driving aggressively having forced him wide at the race's start and was unable to prevent the crash from occurring.<sup id="cite_ref-:6_37-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Bianchi said he was "really disappointed" describing it as "another tough race for me; the third one in a row".<sup id="cite_ref-:6_37-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> He argued that he was in front of Sutil driving into the first corner, but had enjoyed battling with the driver earlier in the race.<sup id="cite_ref-:6_37-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Media reactions to the race were positive, with <a href="/wiki/David_Tremayne" title="David Tremayne">David Tremayne</a> of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Independent" title="The Independent">The Independent</a></i> writing, "Thank the Lord that so many of Formula 1's detractors were present in Bahrain, where the 900th grand prix since the world championship was inaugurated turned out to be a humdinger that was easily the best held so far under the new regulations."<sup id="cite_ref-46" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup> With the amount of battling across the field, it was not "taxi driving" as Ferrari President <a href="/wiki/Luca_Cordero_di_Montezemolo" title="Luca Cordero di Montezemolo">Luca di Montezemolo</a> earlier claimed would be the outcome of Formula One's adoption of greener technology.<sup id="cite_ref-papers_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-papers-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Writing for <i><a href="/wiki/The_Sunday_Telegraph" title="The Sunday Telegraph">The Sunday Telegraph</a></i>, Daniel Johnson stated the day's racing was "some of the finest seen in years," and the discussion over how Formula One's "new rules had stifled competition, leaving little more than a series of fuel economy run" was premature.<sup id="cite_ref-47" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-47">&#91;47&#93;</a></sup> Kevin Eason of <i><a href="/wiki/The_Times" title="The Times">The Times</a></i> wrote, "It took team-mates of raw courage and epic skill to drag Formula One from the round of squalid infighting that polluted the cool night air of Bahrain yesterday."<sup id="cite_ref-48" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-48">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup> <i><a href="/wiki/The_Guardian" title="The Guardian">The Guardian</a>'</i>s motor racing correspondent Paul Weaver spoke of "an absolute thriller from lights to flag with more overtaking moves than you could shake your DRS at", and, "the fireworks that exploded into the night sky were a worthy celebration of the best race for years".<sup id="cite_ref-49" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-49">&#91;49&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The race ensured Rosberg remained the leader of the Drivers' Championship but his advantage over Hamilton had been reduced from 18 to 11 points. Hülkenberg's fifth-place finish promoted him from sixth to third on 28 points, while Alonso and Button's results fell one position from third and fourth places respectively.<sup id="cite_ref-points_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Mercedes increased their advantage at the top of the Constructors' Championship to 67 points. Force India's strong result elevated them from fifth to second, one point ahead of McLaren in third place. Red Bull were in fourth on 35 points after moving from sixth, with Ferrari dropping two positions to be a further two behind in fifth with 16 races left in the season.<sup id="cite_ref-points_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
