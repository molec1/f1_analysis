<p>The <b>2019 British Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 Rolex British Grand Prix 2019</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_racing" title="Formula One racing">Formula One motor race</a> held on 14 July 2019 at the <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Silverstone" title="Silverstone">Silverstone</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>. The race was the 10th round of the <a href="/wiki/2019_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2019 Formula One World Championship">2019 Formula One World Championship</a>. It marked the 74th running of the <a href="/wiki/British_Grand_Prix" title="British Grand Prix">British Grand Prix</a>, the 70th time that the race has been run as a World Championship event and the 53rd time that the World Championship event has been held at the Silverstone Circuit. <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> won the race for a record sixth time.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Formally called the "Formula 1 Rolex British Grand Prix 2019", this was a <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_Grands_Prix" title="List of Formula One Grands Prix">Formula One Grand Prix event</a> and the tenth round of the <a href="/wiki/2019_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2019 Formula One World Championship">2019 Formula One World Championship</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-F1_schedule_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-F1_schedule-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The event took place over the weekend of 12–14 July 2019 at the <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone motor racing circuit</a> in Northamptonshire.<sup id="cite_ref-F1_schedule_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-F1_schedule-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The circuit had a lap length of 5.891&#160;km (3.660 miles) and, prior to the race, a lap record time of 1'30.621, set by <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> during the <a href="/wiki/2017_British_Grand_Prix" title="2017 British Grand Prix">2017 British Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-F1_circuit_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-F1_circuit-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The race length was set as 306.198&#160;km (190.263 miles), which is 52 laps of the circuit.<sup id="cite_ref-F1_circuit_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-F1_circuit-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> With the first <a href="/wiki/British_Grand_Prix" title="British Grand Prix">British Grand Prix</a> having been held in 1926 and the second in 1927 and then there having been one held each year since 1948,<sup id="cite_ref-BBC_20140701_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBC_20140701-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> with each race since 1950 being a World Championship event,<sup id="cite_ref-BBC_20140701_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBC_20140701-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> the 2019 event was the 74th time that the British Grand Prix had been held and the 70th time it had been a World Championship event.
</p><p>The race was officially started by <a href="/wiki/Charlie_Whiting" title="Charlie Whiting">Charlie Whiting</a>'s son, Justin Whiting, as a tribute to his father, who had died prior to the season opening <a href="/wiki/2019_Australian_Grand_Prix" title="2019 Australian Grand Prix">2019 Australian Grand Prix</a>. Prior to the start of the race, <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> gave a brief speech in commemoration of Whiting. <sup id="cite_ref-Vettel_Whiting_speech_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Vettel_Whiting_speech-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Championship_standings_before_the_race">Championship standings before the race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Championship standings before the race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Heading into the race, <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> had a 31-point advantage over teammate <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> in the Drivers' Championship. In the Constructors' Championship, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes_in_Formula_One" class="mw-redirect" title="Mercedes in Formula One">Mercedes</a> held a 135-point advantage over <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Entrants">Entrants</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Entrants">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">Further information: <a href="/wiki/2019_Formula_One_World_Championship#Entries" title="2019 Formula One World Championship">2019 Formula One World Championship §&#160;Entries</a></div>
<p>The drivers and teams entered were the same as those on the season entry list with no additional stand-in drivers for either the race or practice.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The first practice session ended with <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a> fastest for <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> almost half a second ahead of second placed <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> who was a further 0.4 seconds ahead of Gasly's teammate <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a>. The only major incidents were <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> crashing his car on the pit exit and <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Alfa_Romeo_in_Formula_One" title="Alfa Romeo in Formula One">Alfa Romeo</a> stopping on the Wellington straight, the latter of which brought out a <a href="/wiki/Racing_flags#Red_flag" title="Racing flags">red flag</a>. The drivers all struggled with track grip levels throughout the session due to the track having been recently resurfaced and although there was some brief rain it had little impact on the running in the session.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race_summary">Race summary</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Race summary">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On Lap 1, the two <a href="/wiki/Haas_F1_Team" title="Haas F1 Team">Haas</a> drivers collided at the exit of turn five. Both cars pitted, but both would go on to retire as a result of the damage, <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a> on Lap 6 and <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> on Lap 9. <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> fought for the lead, with Bottas maintaining 1st place until Lap 4, when Hamilton overtook at turn seven. Bottas then regained the lead shortly after at turn nine.
</p><p>After battling for 3rd place in the preceding laps, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a> and <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> entered the pits at the same time on Lap 14, with Leclerc ahead. The <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> pit stop was the faster of the two and Verstappen emerged in front after the two cars were side by side whilst driving through the pit-lane. However, this lead would not last, as Verstappen ran wide shortly after at turn 4, allowing Leclerc through.
</p><p>Bottas pitted from the lead at the end of Lap 16, emerging behind Hamilton and <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>. Three laps later, <a href="/wiki/Antonio_Giovinazzi" title="Antonio Giovinazzi">Antonio Giovinazzi</a>, who was running in 9th place, suffered a mechanical failure and spun into the gravel at turn 16. This incident brought out the <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a>, allowing Hamilton an advantageous pit stop, maintaining the lead from Bottas. Other drivers including Vettel, Leclerc, Verstappen and <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a> also came into the pits.
</p><p>Racing resumed on Lap 24. <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> made contact shortly after the restart, damaging Pérez's front wing. This forced Pérez to pit and caused him to eventually finish the race last of the running cars in 17th place. Verstappen and Leclerc continued to battle for position, until Verstappen began to pull away and was allowed through by his teammate Gasly, taking 4th place.
</p><p>Leclerc overtook Gasly around the outside of turn 3 on Lap 36, taking 5th position. Shortly after, Verstappen took 3rd place from Vettel on the Hangar Straight. However, in an attempt to retake the position, Vettel locked up his brakes and collided with the back of the Red Bull at the entry to turn 16. Both cars spun into the gravel, but were able to escape and continue the race, with them both emerging behind Leclerc and Gasly. Vettel fell down the order over the remainder of the lap due to damage to his front wing, before pitting to have it replaced. He would go on to cross the line in 15th place, but was handed a 10-second penalty for causing a collision, demoting him to 16th.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> won the race for a record sixth time, claiming the extra point for recording the fastest lap of the race on his final lap, and setting a new lap record of 1:27.369.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-F1_circuit_post_race_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-F1_circuit_post_race-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On lap 12, for <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a>'s pit stop, the <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a> team broke the record for the fastest fastest pit stop time, with 1.91 seconds, beating the previous time of 1.92 seconds, which was jointly held by Red Bull and <a href="/wiki/Williams_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Williams F1">Williams</a> and had stood since 2013.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
