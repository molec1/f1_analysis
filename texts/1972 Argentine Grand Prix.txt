<p>The <b>1972 Argentine Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held at the <a href="/wiki/Aut%C3%B3dromo_Oscar_Alfredo_G%C3%A1lvez" class="mw-redirect" title="Autódromo Oscar Alfredo Gálvez">Buenos Aires</a> circuit on January 23, 1972. It was race 1 of 12 in both the <a href="/wiki/1972_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1972 World Championship of Drivers">1972 World Championship of Drivers</a> and the <a href="/wiki/1972_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1972 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1972 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>. The 95-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Tyrrell_Racing" title="Tyrrell Racing">Tyrrell</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Jackie_Stewart" title="Jackie Stewart">Jackie Stewart</a> after he started from second position. <a href="/wiki/Denny_Hulme" title="Denny Hulme">Denny Hulme</a> finished second for the <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> team and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Jacky_Ickx" title="Jacky Ickx">Jacky Ickx</a> came in third.
</p>
