<p>The <b>2017 Canadian Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 Grand Prix du Canada 2017</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> that took place on 11 June 2017 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_Gilles_Villeneuve" title="Circuit Gilles Villeneuve">Circuit Gilles Villeneuve</a> in <a href="/wiki/Montreal" title="Montreal">Montreal, Quebec</a>, Canada.<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The race was the seventh round of the <a href="/wiki/2017_FIA_Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="2017 FIA Formula One World Championship">2017 FIA Formula One World Championship</a>. It was the fifty-fourth running of the <a href="/wiki/Canadian_Grand_Prix" title="Canadian Grand Prix">Canadian Grand Prix</a>, and the forty-eighth time the event had been included as a round of the <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="Formula One World Championship">Formula One World Championship</a> since the inception of the series in <a href="/wiki/1950_Formula_One_season" title="1950 Formula One season">1950</a>, and the thirty-eighth time that a World Championship round had been held at Circuit Gilles Villeneuve.
</p><p>The race was won by <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> who took pole, led every lap of the race and set the fastest lap. Joining him on the podium were <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> who finished second and <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> who finished third. <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> dominated the weekend after a bad weekend at <a href="/wiki/2017_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="2017 Monaco Grand Prix">Monaco</a>. <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a> finished 9th and became the first driver from <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_drivers_from_Canada" title="Formula One drivers from Canada">Canada</a> to score a point since <a href="/wiki/Jacques_Villeneuve" title="Jacques Villeneuve">Jacques Villeneuve</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2006_British_Grand_Prix" title="2006 British Grand Prix">2006 British Grand Prix</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> returned to racing for <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>, after missing the previous round in <a href="/wiki/2017_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="2017 Monaco Grand Prix">Monaco</a> to participate in the <a href="/wiki/2017_Indianapolis_500" title="2017 Indianapolis 500">Indianapolis 500</a>.
</p><p>Tyre supplier <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> made the soft, supersoft and ultrasoft tyres available to teams for the race.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Free_practice">Free practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Free practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a>' <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> went fastest in first practice, setting a time of 1.13:809. He was followed by the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> and his teammate <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> in second and third.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>'s <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> continued to suffer from reliability issues as he broke down and retired from the session.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> In second practice the Ferrari of <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> was fastest followed by Hamilton and Vettel.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Saturday's third and final practice was topped by Vettel, with Raikkonen second and Hamilton third.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Vettel set the quickest time of all three practices with a 1.12:572.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_7-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Q1 ended with Pascal Wehrlein crashing his Sauber at turn one after touching the grass border of the track. Damage sustained in the accident meant he would start the race from the pit lane.<sup id="cite_ref-auto_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-auto-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In Q3 Lewis Hamilton secured pole position with a 1:11.459 matching Ayrton Senna's second all-time highest pole record,<sup id="cite_ref-auto_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-auto-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> 0.330 seconds quicker than Ferrari's Vettel in second. Third spot on the grid went to Valtteri Bottas with Raikkonen and Verstappen starting fourth and fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At the start, <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> advanced three positions to head into turn two right behind Hamilton who was in the lead. Third and fourth were Bottas and Vettel, the latter had sustained slight front wing damage from contact with Verstappen heading into turn 2. <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> moved into 5th when he passed <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> on the first lap. Räikkönen would eventually finish 7th after falling behind the Force Indias and struggling with brake problems in the late stages of the race. Also on the first lap, an incident occurred causing the retirement of <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz</a> and <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>, and a pit stop front wing change for <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a> was brought out to allow marshals to clear the track. At the restart Vettel's damaged wing partially collapsed as he accelerated back to racing speed. He pitted on lap 5 to repair the damage and dropped to 18th (last) position. On lap 11, Verstappen's <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> lost electrical power and forced his retirement from 2nd position. As the race continued, <a href="/wiki/Canadians" title="Canadians">Canadian</a> <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a> worked his way up into 9th position for what would be his first points in F1. On lap 66 <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>'s <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> suffered an engine failure preventing him from scoring his team's first points of the season. Hamilton finished the race 20 seconds ahead of teammate Bottas with Ricciardo in third. Sebastian Vettel overtook <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> on the penultimate lap to finish in 4th position close behind Ricciardo, following a race-long charge from the back and an alternate two-stop strategy. Earlier Pérez had ignored requests from his team to allow his faster teammate <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Ocon" title="Esteban Ocon">Esteban Ocon</a> past in order to challenge Ricciardo for the last podium place.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
