<p>The <b>2013 United States Grand Prix</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> that was held at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_of_the_Americas" title="Circuit of the Americas">Circuit of the Americas</a> on November 17, 2013. The race was the eighteenth round of the 2013 season, and marks the second running of the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Grand_Prix" title="United States Grand Prix">United States Grand Prix</a> in <a href="/wiki/Austin" class="mw-redirect" title="Austin">Austin</a>. The race was won by <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>-<a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>, for his <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_Drivers%27_Records#Most_consecutive_wins" class="mw-redirect" title="List of Formula One Drivers&#39; Records">eighth consecutive win</a>, a new F1 record for consecutive wins within a single season.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Driver_changes">Driver changes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Driver changes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> announced a week before the Grand Prix that he would be missing the final two races of 2013, in order to have back surgery.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> He had wanted to delay the operation until the off-season, but suffered increasing discomfort over the previous races. Räikkönen's decision meant that <a href="/wiki/2013_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix" title="2013 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix">the previous race</a> was his last race competing for <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a>, having agreed to join <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2014_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2014 Formula One World Championship">2014</a> season. His place was taken by <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a>, who signed a two-race deal with the team.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Tyres">Tyres</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Tyres">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Like the <a href="/wiki/2012_United_States_Grand_Prix" title="2012 United States Grand Prix">previous United States Grand Prix</a>, tyre supplier <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> will bring its orange-banded hard compound tyre as the harder "prime" tyre and the white-banded medium compound tyre as the softer "option" tyre.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Free_practice">Free practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Free practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> was given a three-place grid penalty for the race, after overtaking under red flags during the opening practice session.<sup id="cite_ref-Button_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Button-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Charles_Pic" title="Charles Pic">Charles Pic</a> was given a five-place penalty for the race after Caterham changed the gearbox in his car.<sup id="cite_ref-Pic_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pic-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban Gutiérrez">Esteban Gutiérrez</a> and <a href="/wiki/Max_Chilton" title="Max Chilton">Max Chilton</a> were given penalties for impeding other drivers in the first qualifying session. Gutiérrez, who blocked <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> was handed a ten-place grid penalty; Chilton will have to serve a drive-through penalty within the first five laps of the race after he impeded both Maldonado and <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Gutierrez_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Gutierrez-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a> was given a 20-second penalty for a collision with <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban Gutiérrez">Esteban Gutiérrez</a> on the final lap of the race. He moved down from 12th to 16th as a result.<sup id="cite_ref-Vergne_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Vergne-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notes">Notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Notes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>As of June&#160;2020<sup class="plainlinks noexcerpt noprint asof-tag update" style="display:none;"><a class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=2013_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit">&#91;update&#93;</a></sup>, this remains the last race where all three drivers on the podium drove <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a>-powered cars.</li>
