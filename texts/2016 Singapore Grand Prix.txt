<p>The <b>2016 Singapore Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>2016 Formula 1 Singapore Airlines Singapore Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> held on 18 September 2016 at the <a href="/wiki/Marina_Bay_Street_Circuit" title="Marina Bay Street Circuit">Marina Bay Street Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Marina_Bay,_Singapore" title="Marina Bay, Singapore">Marina Bay</a>, <a href="/wiki/Singapore" title="Singapore">Singapore</a>. It was the fifteenth round of the <a href="/wiki/2016_FIA_Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="2016 FIA Formula One World Championship">2016 FIA Formula One World Championship</a>, and marked the seventeenth running of the <a href="/wiki/Singapore_Grand_Prix" title="Singapore Grand Prix">Singapore Grand Prix</a> and the ninth time the race had been held at Marina Bay.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> entered the race leading the World Drivers' Championship by two points, ahead of teammate <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a>. In the World Constructors' Championship, Mercedes held a lead of 208 points. <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a> was placed second and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> third. Ferrari driver <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> was the <a href="/wiki/2015_Singapore_Grand_Prix" title="2015 Singapore Grand Prix">defending race winner</a>.
</p><p>Rosberg started the race from pole position<sup id="cite_ref-qual_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qual-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and led throughout, holding off a late challenge by <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> to win by less than half a second in his 200th race in Formula One. Sebastian Vettel was awarded the Driver of the Day award after moving 17 spots to claim 5th position.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
