<p>The <b>2013 Belgian Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>2013 Formula 1 Shell Belgian Grand Prix</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> on 25 August 2013 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps" title="Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps">Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps</a> in <a href="/wiki/Spa,_Belgium" title="Spa, Belgium">Spa</a>, <a href="/wiki/Belgium" title="Belgium">Belgium</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-FIA_calender_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FIA_calender-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It was the eleventh round of the <a href="/wiki/2013_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2013 Formula One season">2013 season</a>, and the 69th running of the <a href="/wiki/Belgian_Grand_Prix" title="Belgian Grand Prix">Belgian Grand Prix</a>.
</p><p>The race was won by <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> in a <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>-<a href="/wiki/Renault_Sport_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault Sport F1">Renault</a> in a time of 1:23:42, registering an average speed of 220.80&#160;km/h, thus extending his championship lead to 46 points. Second was <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> in a <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, and third was <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> (who had started from pole) in a <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Like the <a href="/wiki/2012_Belgian_Grand_Prix" title="2012 Belgian Grand Prix">2012 Belgian Grand Prix</a>, tyre supplier Pirelli brought its orange-banded hard compound tyre as the harder "prime" tyre and the white-banded medium compound tyre as the softer "option" tyre.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Alonso_at_Q1.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e6/F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Alonso_at_Q1.jpg/220px-F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Alonso_at_Q1.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e6/F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Alonso_at_Q1.jpg/330px-F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Alonso_at_Q1.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e6/F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Alonso_at_Q1.jpg/440px-F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Alonso_at_Q1.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1869" data-file-height="1246" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Alonso_at_Q1.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Fernando Alonso at Q1 using intermediate tyres.</div></div></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> had taken 7 of the last 8 poles, yet they have not shown their hands in practice sessions. On the other hand, <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> looks unbeatable to pole, but Spa's famous changing weather can make a lot of trouble for them. A shower came to the track right before qualifying got underway, with an air temperature of only 22°C.
</p><p>All the drivers started the session with intermediates, <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> set the first benchmark of 2:07.008. As the track continued drying, <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> all hit the front briefly. After the midpoint, most drivers pitted for a new set of intermediate tyres while <a href="/wiki/Giedo_van_der_Garde" title="Giedo van der Garde">Giedo van der Garde</a> took a gamble to switch onto the slicks. As times passing by, lap times improved rapidly, nearly pushed both <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferraris</a> out of Q2. But <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> managed to set the fastest time at the last moment and his team-mate <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> also made it into the next session. Van der Garde's decision proved a big success, for he impressively finished 3rd. In fact, three of the four drivers from <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a> and <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia</a> squeezed into Q2, the only one that failed to do so was <a href="/wiki/Charles_Pic" title="Charles Pic">Charles Pic</a>, he will start the race from the last place on the grid. Also eliminated were the <a href="/wiki/Williams_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Williams F1">Williams</a> duo, the two <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rossos</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a> of <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban Gutiérrez">Esteban Gutiérrez</a>.
</p><p>At the beginning of the 15 minutes period, drivers were divided into 2 groups, most of them used mediums, while several others including Webber and Alonso chose the hards. In the opening phase, Webber and Alonso were fighting for the top spot, their gap being merely two thousandths of a second. At the same time, Vettel and Hamilton stayed in the pits until there were about 7 minutes left on the board. After that it was time for mediums, first the two <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotuses</a>, then Sebastian Vettel, all challenging for the first place. But finally Kimi Räikkönen put the fight to an end with a 1:48.296. Eliminated in Q2 were Sauber's <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a>, <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a>, Caterham's Giedo van der Garde who achieved the team's best qualifying result ever, and both the Marussias.
</p><p>In fear of the incoming shower, all bar <a href="/wiki/Paul_di_Resta" title="Paul di Resta">Paul di Resta</a> queued at the pit exit with medium tyres. However just moments later, the Force India driver opted to use the inters. It soon turned out that the latter was right, as the other 9 drivers didn't even start a flying lap as the track was too wet for slicks and they had to pit immediately. Di Resta then set the fastest lap time, with Felipe Massa in second. Just when it looked like he was going to take his maiden pole position, the track started to dry out. As there was 3 minutes left, Räikkönen gave the first shot to come to 3rd, ahead of Alonso. And Rosberg behind them posted a 2:02.251 to take the provisional pole. But none of them could cross the line before the chequered flag. With the ever-improving track, polesitter would be decided among three that still had one timed lap to go—Webber, Vettel, and Hamilton. Webber finished first to reduce the quickest time by almost a second. Then Vettel beat his teammate by a further one tenth of a second, Lewis Hamilton subsequently secured his fourth successive pole. The Lotuses and the Ferraris had to settle in rows 4 and 5.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Start.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Start.jpg/220px-F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Start.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="131" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Start.jpg/330px-F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Start.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Start.jpg/440px-F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Start.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2705" data-file-height="1613" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:F1_2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix_-_Start.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Sebastian Vettel overtakes Lewis Hamilton on the <i>Kemmel</i> straight during the first lap. He will stay in the lead until the end of the race.</div></div></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> suffered clutch problems right from the start and fell from third to sixth after one lap. On the other hand, <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> both enjoyed a great getaway, running in fourth and fifth places respectively. <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> had a great run up Eau Rouge and overtook pole-sitter <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> down Kemmel Straight on the first lap and quickly took a commanding lead over the <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> duo. Alonso and Webber both passed Button and the Spaniard continued to fight his way up the field, overtaking <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> for third place. <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> was also working his way up the field, but excessive brake dust was coming out of the Finn's left front wheel at an early stage in the race.
</p><p>On the eighth lap, <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> was handed a drive through penalty for forcing <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> off the track. As a result, Grosjean had to straightline Les Combes and fell from eight to tenth as <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> took advantage. Räikkönen's brakes ultimately failed as the Finn went off massively at the Chicane, nearly collecting Massa in the process. The <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a> driver retired the car, which meant that his record run of consecutive points finishes ended at 27. It was later discovered that a loose visor tear-off got stuck inside the left front brake duct, causing the disc to overheat.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> After the first round of pit stops, Hamilton successfully retained second place from Alonso, but the latter quickly found his way through.
</p><p>Further down the order, a battle involving <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban Gutiérrez">Esteban Gutiérrez</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> duo was raging. As the four cars were approaching the final chicane, the young Mexican successfully made his move on both <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> and Maldonado. The German tried to follow, but clipped the <a href="/wiki/Williams_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Williams F1">Williams</a> driver's front wing. Maldonado dove for the pits only to collect the second Force India of <a href="/wiki/Paul_di_Resta" title="Paul di Resta">Paul di Resta</a>. As a result, the Venezuelan had to pit for a new front wing and received a ten seconds stop-and-go penalty while di Resta retired his car on the spot.
</p>

<p>Vettel won the race, ahead of Alonso and Hamilton. <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> successfully fought off Webber for fourth, finishing less than three seconds behind his teammate. Button took sixth for <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>, while the race-long scrap between Massa and Grosjean ended with the Brazilian in front for seventh. Sutil and <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> took ninth and tenth respectively.

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Greenpeace_protest">Greenpeace protest</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Greenpeace protest">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Greenpeace" title="Greenpeace">Greenpeace</a> used the Belgian Grand Prix as a main point of protests against <a href="/wiki/Royal_Dutch_Shell" title="Royal Dutch Shell">Shell</a>, a principal sponsor of the race. <a href="/wiki/Greenpeace" title="Greenpeace">Greenpeace</a> activists went to the track and put up a number of banners protesting against <a href="/wiki/Shell_Oil_Company" title="Shell Oil Company">Shell</a> drilling in the <a href="/wiki/Arctic" title="Arctic">Arctic</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> These included two remote controlled banners which scrolled up in front of the <a href="/wiki/Podium" title="Podium">podium</a> celebrations,<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> which were quickly disposed of by security, and a large banner on the main <a href="/wiki/Grandstand" title="Grandstand">grandstand</a>. The official world feed <a href="/wiki/Broadcast" class="mw-redirect" title="Broadcast">broadcast</a> did not show any banner in detail.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
