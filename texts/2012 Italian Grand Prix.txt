<p>The <b>2012 Italian Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 Gran Premio Santander d'Italia 2012</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race that took place at the <a href="/wiki/Autodromo_Nazionale_di_Monza" title="Autodromo Nazionale di Monza">Autodromo Nazionale di Monza</a> in <a href="/wiki/Monza" title="Monza">Monza</a>, <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italy</a> on 9 September 2012.<sup id="cite_ref-7Dec_calendar_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7Dec_calendar-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It was the thirteenth race of the <a href="/wiki/2012_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2012 Formula One season">2012 season</a>, and the final race in Europe before the teams returned to Asia for the <a href="/wiki/2012_Singapore_Grand_Prix" title="2012 Singapore Grand Prix">Singapore Grand Prix</a>.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> qualified on pole, ahead of teammate <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a>. The result gave <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> their sixty-second front-row lock-out, breaking the previous record set by <a href="/wiki/Williams_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Williams F1">Williams</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Monza_grid_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Monza_grid-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton won the race by four seconds from <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> completing the podium.<sup id="cite_ref-race_result_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-race_result-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Italian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Italian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Tyre supplier Pirelli brought its silver-banded hard compound tyre as the harder "prime" tyre and the white-banded medium compound tyre as the softer "option" tyre.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Circuit_changes">Circuit changes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Italian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Circuit changes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Drag_reduction_system" title="Drag reduction system">drag reduction system</a> zones used in <a href="/wiki/2011_Italian_Grand_Prix" title="2011 Italian Grand Prix">2011</a> were shortened for the 2012 race.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> The first DRS zone, located on the straight between the second Lesmo corner and the Variante Ascari, was shortened by fifty meters, while the DRS zone on the front straight was shortened by five meters.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Driver_penalties">Driver penalties</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Italian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Driver penalties">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> did not participate in the race due to receiving a one-race ban for his involvement in the first-lap incident at the <a href="/wiki/2012_Belgian_Grand_Prix" title="2012 Belgian Grand Prix">Belgian Grand Prix</a> that saw four cars crash heavily at the La Source hairpin.<sup id="cite_ref-Grosjean_EX_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Grosjean_EX-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Grosjean was replaced by the team's testing and reserve driver, <a href="/wiki/J%C3%A9r%C3%B4me_d%27Ambrosio" title="Jérôme d&#39;Ambrosio">Jérôme d'Ambrosio</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-DAM_Monza_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DAM_Monza-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> received two five-place grid penalties. the first for jumping the start of the Belgian Grand Prix and the second for causing an avoidable accident with <a href="/wiki/Timo_Glock" title="Timo Glock">Timo Glock</a> after Grosjean's accident.<sup id="cite_ref-MAL_GLO_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MAL_GLO-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Paul_di_Resta" title="Paul di Resta">Paul di Resta</a> was also demoted five places for an unscheduled gearbox change on Saturday morning, his second gearbox penalty of the season.<sup id="cite_ref-DIR_penalty_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DIR_penalty-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Driver_changes">Driver changes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Italian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Driver changes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><a href="/wiki/Ma_Qinghua" title="Ma Qinghua">Ma Qinghua</a> became the first Chinese driver to take part in a Grand Prix weekend when he drove <a href="/wiki/Narain_Karthikeyan" title="Narain Karthikeyan">Narain Karthikeyan</a>'s <a href="/wiki/HRT_F112" title="HRT F112">HRT F112</a> in the first free practice session on Friday morning.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> drove <a href="/wiki/Bruno_Senna" title="Bruno Senna">Bruno Senna</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Williams_FW34" title="Williams FW34">Williams FW34</a> in the same session, while <a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a> took <a href="/wiki/Paul_di_Resta" title="Paul di Resta">Paul di Resta</a>'s place at <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Free_practice">Free practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Italian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Free practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes AMG</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> was fastest in the first practice session, three-tenths of a second quicker than <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> For <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a>, <a href="/wiki/J%C3%A9r%C3%B4me_d%27Ambrosio" title="Jérôme d&#39;Ambrosio">Jérôme d'Ambrosio</a> finished the session fifteenth, over a second and a half slower than Schumacher and sixth-tenths down on teammate <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>. <a href="/wiki/Ma_Qinghua" title="Ma Qinghua">Ma Qinghua</a> was the slowest runner after ninety minutes, nearly six seconds slower than Schumacher, and within two seconds of teammate <a href="/wiki/Pedro_de_la_Rosa" title="Pedro de la Rosa">Pedro de la Rosa</a>. At the end of the session, both <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> were observed to stop on the circuit with technical problems.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> was the fastest driver in the second session,<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> narrowly edging out Button and Alonso, the latter of whom had another disrupted session with a series of technical problems surrounding the gearbox and brakes that limited his time on the circuit. Having set the fastest time in the morning session, Schumacher finished tenth following a series of off-track excursions. He was highly-critical of the speed bumps placed around the Variante della Roggia chicane, claiming they were a safety hazard that could result in serious injury if a driver hit them at speed.
</p><p>Hamilton was once again fastest in the third session, just one thousandth of a second ahead of Alonso.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Paul_di_Resta" title="Paul di Resta">Paul di Resta</a> finished the session third, while <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> suffered a loss of power on his final lap and pulled over at the Roggia chicane. The <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_RB8" title="Red Bull RB8">Red Bull RB8</a> was consistently one of the slowest cars through the speed trap, evidenced by the way neither <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> driver featured in the top ten runners in any of the free practice sessions, with the exception of <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a>, who set the ninth-fastest time of the first session.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Italian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Vettel_Monza_2012.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/22/Vettel_Monza_2012.jpg/220px-Vettel_Monza_2012.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="331" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/22/Vettel_Monza_2012.jpg/330px-Vettel_Monza_2012.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/22/Vettel_Monza_2012.jpg/440px-Vettel_Monza_2012.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2025" data-file-height="3049" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Vettel_Monza_2012.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Vettel walking back to the pits after his retirement</div></div></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> made a great start to pass Jenson Button for second place. He pulled alongside pole sitter Lewis Hamilton going into the first chicane, but Hamilton had the inside line and was able to stay ahead. Fernando Alonso was able to make his way up from 10th to 7th place by the end of the first lap, and passed Kimi Räikkönen for 6th going into the first chicane on the second lap.
</p><p>On lap 4, Sebastian Vettel passed Michael Schumacher under braking into the first chicane for 4th place. One lap later, Bruno Senna attempted to pass Nico Rosberg around the outside into the first chicane. Senna turned into Rosberg, they banged wheels, and Senna went straight through the chicane to take 11th. Fernando Alonso was able to pass Schumacher coming out of the Parabolica on lap 7.
</p><p>On lap 8, Bruno Senna tried to pass Paul di Resta on the outside going into the second chicane. Di Resta moved over, forcing Senna onto the gravel. Senna went straight through the chicane and back onto the racing line right in front of Mark Webber, who dodged around him as they entered the first Lesmo corner. On the next lap, <a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a> had a right rear suspension failure under braking into the first chicane. He spun and slid sideways over one of the curbs, launching his car into the air over the chicane.
</p><p>Jenson Button retook 2nd place from Felipe Massa in the 2nd chicane on lap 19. Fernando Alonso and Sebastian Vettel pitted at the same time on lap 21, and came out right behind Massa with only a car's length separating each of the three. Lewis Hamilton pitted on lap 24, allowing Sergio Pérez, who hadn't pitted yet, to take the lead from 2nd.
</p><p>On lap 26, Fernando Alonso tried to go around the outside of Sebastian Vettel through Curva Grande. Vettel moved to the outside, pushing Alonso off the track and onto the gravel. The move was similar to a move Vettel pulled on Alonso the year before, but this time Vettel was given a drive-through penalty. Alonso was able to pass Vettel on lap 29, and the drive-through on lap 35 put Vettel in 9th place behind Webber.
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:2012_Italian_GP_-_Alonso_cameraman.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c9/2012_Italian_GP_-_Alonso_cameraman.jpg/220px-2012_Italian_GP_-_Alonso_cameraman.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="331" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c9/2012_Italian_GP_-_Alonso_cameraman.jpg/330px-2012_Italian_GP_-_Alonso_cameraman.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c9/2012_Italian_GP_-_Alonso_cameraman.jpg/440px-2012_Italian_GP_-_Alonso_cameraman.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2848" data-file-height="4288" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:2012_Italian_GP_-_Alonso_cameraman.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>After the podium ceremony, third-placed Alonso took over a TV camera</div></div></div>
<p>Lewis Hamilton retook the lead from Sergio Pérez on lap 29. Pérez pitted on the next lap and came back out in 8th place. Jenson Button retired on lap 34 following a sudden loss of fuel pressure. This moved Felipe Massa back up to 2nd with Fernando Alonso and Sergio Pérez gaining on him. Massa was passed by Alonso on lap 40, and then by Pérez three laps later. Pérez quickly chased down Alonso and passed him on lap 46.
</p><p>Sebastian Vettel retired on lap 47 with a failed alternator. On lap 51, Mark Webber clipped the curb on the exit of the Ascari chicane and spun. He fell out of the point-scoring positions and retired due to severely flat-spotted tires.
</p><p>Lewis Hamilton won the race, with Sergio Pérez, who started 12th, in second and quickly catching him. Fernando Alonso completed the podium, having started 10th.
</p>
