<p>The <b>2012 Korean Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>2012 Formula 1 Korean Grand Prix</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race that was the sixteenth round of the <a href="/wiki/2012_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2012 Formula One season">2012 Formula One season</a>. It was held on 14 October 2012 at the <a href="/wiki/Korea_International_Circuit" title="Korea International Circuit">Korea International Circuit</a> near <a href="/wiki/Yeongam" class="mw-redirect" title="Yeongam">Yeongam</a> in <a href="/wiki/South_Korea" title="South Korea">South Korea</a>'s <a href="/wiki/South_Jeolla_Province" title="South Jeolla Province">South Jeolla Province</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7Dec_calendar_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7Dec_calendar-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> The race marked the third running of the <a href="/wiki/Korean_Grand_Prix" title="Korean Grand Prix">Korean Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> started the race from pole alongside <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-WEB_pole_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-WEB_pole-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> who went on to win the race.<sup id="cite_ref-VET_win_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-VET_win-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> With championship rival <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> finishing third, Vettel took a six-point championship lead.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The organisers of the race invited Korean rapper <a href="/wiki/Psy" title="Psy">PSY</a> to wave the chequered flag.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Korean_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Korean_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In the week before the race, the <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_l%27Automobile" title="Fédération Internationale de l&#39;Automobile">FIA</a> announced that the <a href="/wiki/Drag_reduction_system" title="Drag reduction system">drag reduction system</a> zone on the circuit would be altered from its <a href="/wiki/2011_Korean_Grand_Prix" title="2011 Korean Grand Prix">2011</a> position, bringing the activation point forward by 200 metres (660&#160;ft).<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> The detection point will also be brought forward; in 2011, it was positioned between the first two corners, but for 2012, it was moved to be 90 metres (300&#160;ft) before the first corner.
</p><p>Like the <a href="/wiki/2011_Korean_Grand_Prix" title="2011 Korean Grand Prix">2011 Korean Grand Prix</a>, tyre supplier Pirelli brought its yellow-banded soft compound tyre as the harder "prime" tyre and the red-banded supersoft compound tyre as the softer "option" tyre.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Charles_Pic" title="Charles Pic">Charles Pic</a> was given a ten-place grid penalty for exceeding the maximum number of engines that he was permitted to use over the course of the season.<sup id="cite_ref-PIC_10_place_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PIC_10_place-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Several reserve drivers took the place of regular drivers for the first free practice session. <a href="/wiki/Giedo_van_der_Garde" title="Giedo van der Garde">Giedo van der Garde</a> drove in place of <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Vitaly_Petrov" title="Vitaly Petrov">Vitaly Petrov</a>, while <a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a> replaced <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> at <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a>, <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> drove <a href="/wiki/Bruno_Senna" title="Bruno Senna">Bruno Senna</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Williams_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Williams F1">Williams</a> and <a href="/wiki/Dani_Clos" title="Dani Clos">Dani Clos</a> took <a href="/wiki/Narain_Karthikeyan" title="Narain Karthikeyan">Narain Karthikeyan</a>'s place at <a href="/wiki/HRT_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="HRT F1">HRT</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-HAM_FP1_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HAM_FP1-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Free_practice">Free practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Korean_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Free practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:YEONGAM_KOREAN_GRAND_PRIX_OCT_2012_(8169536114).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/af/YEONGAM_KOREAN_GRAND_PRIX_OCT_2012_%288169536114%29.jpg/220px-YEONGAM_KOREAN_GRAND_PRIX_OCT_2012_%288169536114%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="165" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/af/YEONGAM_KOREAN_GRAND_PRIX_OCT_2012_%288169536114%29.jpg/330px-YEONGAM_KOREAN_GRAND_PRIX_OCT_2012_%288169536114%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/af/YEONGAM_KOREAN_GRAND_PRIX_OCT_2012_%288169536114%29.jpg/440px-YEONGAM_KOREAN_GRAND_PRIX_OCT_2012_%288169536114%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1656" data-file-height="1240" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:YEONGAM_KOREAN_GRAND_PRIX_OCT_2012_(8169536114).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Räikkönen leads Massa during the race. The pair would swap places before the finish</div></div></div>
<p>In contrast to the practice sessions of the <a href="/wiki/2010_Korean_Grand_Prix" title="2010 Korean Grand Prix">2010</a> and <a href="/wiki/2011_Korean_Grand_Prix" title="2011 Korean Grand Prix">2011</a> races, the first free practice session of the 2012 race took place in dry conditions. <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> ended the session as fastest driver, three tenths of a second ahead of <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> — who had led most of the ninety-minute session — with <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> in third place.<sup id="cite_ref-HAM_FP1_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HAM_FP1-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> was fastest in the second session,<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> leading teammate Webber and Alonso in third. Like the first session, the second passed without incident, although <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> was forced out after forty-five minutes of running when his <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a> lost all power and ground to a halt at Turn 12. Pérez was able to safely park the car, allowing the session to continue uninterrupted.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Korean_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a>, who qualified on pole, lost his lead at start to teammate <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> at the first turn. Lewis Hamilton lost his third place to Alonso on first lap, while his team mate <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> had to retire after a collision with Kobayashi, who also collided with <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> of Mercedes. <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> moved to 4th place after overtaking Hamilton who was suffering from an anti-roll bar failure on his car, which led him to lose couple of places in the race. Massa was quickly gaining on his teammate when he was told to hold his position on his team radio as Alonso was the championship contender. At the end of the race Vettel was continuously told on his team radio to slow down and save tyres as he had a problem with tyre degradation on his right-front tyre. Vettel finished first and took a six-point lead in the Championship over third-placed <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>, who had led since the <a href="/wiki/2012_European_Grand_Prix" title="2012 European Grand Prix">European Grand Prix</a>.  Webber finished second, setting the fastest lap.  Lewis Hamilton finished 10th after battling for position with the two <a href="/wiki/Toro_Rosso" class="mw-redirect" title="Toro Rosso">Toro Rossos</a> of <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a>, but fell back in the closing laps as a piece of <a href="/wiki/AstroTurf" title="AstroTurf">AstroTurf</a> got stuck on one of his car's sidepods.
</p>
