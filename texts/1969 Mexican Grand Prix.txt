<p>The <b>1969 Gran Premio de Mexico</b> (<a href="/wiki/Mexican_Grand_Prix" title="Mexican Grand Prix">Mexican Grand Prix</a>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Motor_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Motor race">motor race</a> held at the <a href="/wiki/Aut%C3%B3dromo_Hermanos_Rodr%C3%ADguez" title="Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez">Ciudad Deportiva Magdalena Mixhuca</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mexico_City" title="Mexico City">Mexico City</a> on October 19, 1969, two weeks after the <a href="/wiki/1969_United_States_Grand_Prix" title="1969 United States Grand Prix">United States Grand Prix</a> at <a href="/wiki/Watkins_Glen_International" title="Watkins Glen International">Watkins Glen</a>. It was race 11 of 11 in both the <a href="/wiki/1969_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1969 World Championship of Drivers">1969 World Championship of Drivers</a> and the <a href="/wiki/1969_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1969 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1969 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>. The 65-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Denny_Hulme" title="Denny Hulme">Denny Hulme</a> after he started from fourth position. <a href="/wiki/Jacky_Ickx" title="Jacky Ickx">Jacky Ickx</a> finished second for the <a href="/wiki/Brabham" title="Brabham">Brabham</a> team and his teammate <a href="/wiki/Jack_Brabham" title="Jack Brabham">Jack Brabham</a> came in third.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1969_Mexican_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Entry">Entry</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1969_Mexican_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Entry">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>A total of 17 <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">F1</a> cars were entered for this event, the last of the season. <a href="/wiki/Team_Lotus" title="Team Lotus">Team Lotus</a> had had mixed fortunes at <a href="/wiki/Watkins_Glen_International" title="Watkins Glen International">Watkins Glen</a>, although <a href="/wiki/Jochen_Rindt" title="Jochen Rindt">Jochen Rindt</a> had won the race, his teammate <a href="/wiki/Graham_Hill" title="Graham Hill">Graham Hill</a> had broken both legs in an accident. At this event, <a href="/wiki/Team_Lotus" title="Team Lotus">Lotus</a> decided not to replace him. However they did run a second car, a <a href="/wiki/Lotus_63" title="Lotus 63">Lotus 63</a> developmental car with <a href="/wiki/Four_wheel_drive" class="mw-redirect" title="Four wheel drive">four wheel drive</a> for <a href="/wiki/John_Miles_(racing_driver)" title="John Miles (racing driver)">John Miles</a>, as <a href="/wiki/Mario_Andretti" title="Mario Andretti">Mario Andretti</a> was busy winning the <a href="/wiki/1969_USAC_Championship_Car_season" title="1969 USAC Championship Car season">Dan Gurney 200</a> at <a href="/wiki/Pacific_Raceways" title="Pacific Raceways">Pacific Raceways</a>, USA.<sup id="cite_ref-grandprix1_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-grandprix1-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The rest of the field was unchanged, with only one <a href="/wiki/Ferrari_312" title="Ferrari 312">Ferrari 312</a> entered by <a href="/wiki/North_American_Racing_Team" title="North American Racing Team">NART</a> for local hero, <a href="/wiki/Pedro_Rodr%C3%ADguez_(racing_driver)" title="Pedro Rodríguez (racing driver)">Pedro Rodríguez</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-grandprix1_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-grandprix1-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1969_Mexican_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Brabham" title="Brabham">Motor Racing Developments Ltd</a> team proved to be very fast in Mexico, as they dominated qualifying in their <a href="/wiki/Brabham" title="Brabham">Brabham</a>-<a href="/wiki/Cosworth" title="Cosworth">Cosworth</a> BT26A. <a href="/wiki/Jack_Brabham" title="Jack Brabham">Jack Brabham</a> secured <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>, for the team, averaging a speed of 96.087&#160;mph. Next fastest was his teammate, <a href="/wiki/Jacky_Ickx" title="Jacky Ickx">Jacky Ickx</a>. The 1969 World Champion, <a href="/wiki/Jackie_Stewart" title="Jackie Stewart">Jackie Stewart</a> was third fastest in his <a href="/wiki/Matra_MS80" title="Matra MS80">Matra-Cosworth MS80</a>, who shared the second row with the <a href="/wiki/McLaren_M7A" title="McLaren M7A">McLaren-Cosworth M7A</a> of <a href="/wiki/Denny_Hulme" title="Denny Hulme">Denny Hulme</a>. A pair of <a href="/wiki/Lotus_49" title="Lotus 49">Lotus 49s</a> were on row three, with <a href="/wiki/Jo_Siffert" title="Jo Siffert">Jo Siffert</a> ahead of <a href="/wiki/Jochen_Rindt" title="Jochen Rindt">Jochen Rindt</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-grandprix1_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-grandprix1-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1969_Mexican_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The race was held over 65 laps of the <i><a href="/wiki/Aut%C3%B3dromo_Hermanos_Rodr%C3%ADguez" title="Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez">Autódromo Hermanos Rodríguez</a></i>, however, for the second consecutive race, <a href="/wiki/Bruce_McLaren" title="Bruce McLaren">Bruce McLaren</a> failed to make the start. Of the 16 remaining cars, <a href="/wiki/Jackie_Stewart" title="Jackie Stewart">Jackie Stewart</a> made the best start, with the <a href="/wiki/Brabham" title="Brabham">Brabhams</a> of <a href="/wiki/Jacky_Ickx" title="Jacky Ickx">Jacky Ickx</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jack_Brabham" title="Jack Brabham">Jack Brabham</a> in pursuit, <a href="/wiki/Jochen_Rindt" title="Jochen Rindt">Jochen Rindt</a> fourth, and <a href="/wiki/Denny_Hulme" title="Denny Hulme">Denny Hulme</a> close behind. By the end of the second lap, led, while Ickx was putting pressure on Stewart. By lap six, Ickx was through, into the lead, and Hulme past Brabham to take third. Hulme overtook Stewart on the next lap, and set about chasing down Ickx.  On lap 10, Hulme's <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> was ahead. Meanwhile, Stewart’s <a href="/wiki/Equipe_Matra_Sports" title="Equipe Matra Sports">Matra</a> had dropped behind Brabham, and this is how the top four remained throughout the remainder of the race. Rindt ran fifth early on in the race, but bent his suspension on a curb, leading to his retirement, so fifth went to <a href="/wiki/Jean-Pierre_Beltoise" title="Jean-Pierre Beltoise">Jean-Pierre Beltoise</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Jackie_Oliver" title="Jackie Oliver">Jackie Oliver</a> finishing sixth in the <a href="/wiki/BRM_P139" title="BRM P139">BRM P139</a>, two laps adrift.<sup id="cite_ref-grandprix1_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-grandprix1-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-formula1969_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formula1969-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-gpupdate1_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gpupdate1-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-f1-fansite1969_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-f1-fansite1969-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hulme won in a time of 1hr 54min 5.3sec, an average speed of 99.618&#160;mph (160.320&#160;km/h). Hulme finished just 2.56 seconds ahead of Ickx.<sup id="cite_ref-formula1969_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formula1969-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-gpupdate1_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gpupdate1-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-f1-fansite1969_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-f1-fansite1969-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
