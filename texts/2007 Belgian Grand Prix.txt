<p>The <b>2007 Belgian Grand Prix</b> was the fourteenth race of the <a href="/wiki/2007_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2007 Formula One season">2007 Formula One season</a>, returning to the <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">F1</a> calendar after a year's absence. It was held on 16 September at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps" title="Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps">Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps</a>, <a href="/wiki/Spa,_Belgium" title="Spa, Belgium">Spa, Belgium</a>.
</p><p>Going into the race, <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> led the Drivers' Championship by three points from teammate <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> led the Constructors' Championship by 57 points from <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a> after the exclusion of <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a> from the Constructors' Championship following the <a href="/wiki/2007_Formula_One_espionage_controversy" title="2007 Formula One espionage controversy">Ferrari/McLaren spy saga</a>. Otherwise McLaren would have been leading the Championship.
</p><p>The race also marked the 100th race start for <a href="/wiki/Australian" class="mw-redirect" title="Australian">Australian</a> <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The usual drivers were eliminated in the first qualifying session—<a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sakon_Yamamoto" title="Sakon Yamamoto">Sakon Yamamoto</a> went out in the <a href="/wiki/Spyker_F1" title="Spyker F1">Spykers</a> along with <a href="/wiki/Takuma_Sato" title="Takuma Sato">Takuma Sato</a> and <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Davidson" title="Anthony Davidson">Anthony Davidson</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Super_Aguri_F1" title="Super Aguri F1">Super Aguris</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Honda_Racing_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Honda Racing F1">Honda</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>.
</p><p>The second qualifying session was unusual as six drivers in six different cars were eliminated—<a href="/wiki/Alexander_Wurz" title="Alexander Wurz">Alexander Wurz</a> of <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a>, <a href="/wiki/Vitantonio_Liuzzi" title="Vitantonio Liuzzi">Vitantonio Liuzzi</a> of Toro Rosso, <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> of Honda, <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> of <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a>. <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a> of <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ralf_Schumacher" title="Ralf Schumacher">Ralf Schumacher</a> of <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyota</a>. Fisichella was later relegated right to the back of the grid after changing his engine after qualifying.
</p><p>This left seven different teams in the top 10. <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> qualified tenth for Renault, just behind <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a> in the Toyota. Ahead of them were <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a>'s Red Bull and <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> in the BMW Sauber. <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> did very well in the Williams to qualify on row 3 alongside <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> in the second BMW Sauber. However, the Pole was given a ten place grid drop for an engine change. Once again, <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> occupied the top four places. <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> pipped McLaren teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> to 3rd place, but Ferrari dominated, with <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> beating <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> to get his 14th career pole and lock out the front row for the Italian team.
</p><p>Due to FIA rules about the order in which penalties are applied, Kubica's penalty was applied first. This left him 15th and behind Fisichella. Then the Italian's penalty was applied, so Kubica ended up 14th. This meant Coulthard, Button and Liuzzi all gained two places as both drivers had been ahead of them.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The order from the start remained fairly unchanged with the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferraris</a> of <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> led away from <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>, although there was a lot of pushing between the <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLarens</a> as Hamilton attempted to pass around the outside of turn one, but Alonso pushed him wide onto the tarmac on the outside of the corner.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a> suffered a suspension failure on lap 2, making him the first retirement of the race. He had been running last. Also on lap 2, <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Wurz" title="Alexander Wurz">Alexander Wurz</a> spun in the <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a>, slipping to the back of the field.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> soon suffered a handling problem, probably caused by a collision, which eliminated his <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a> from the race on lap 9.
</p><p>Massa briefly took the lead on lap 16, but Räikkönen reclaimed it a lap later after his pitstop.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> suffered a hydraulic failure in his <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> on lap 30. He was quickly followed with a fuel pressure problem for Wurz, who was still last, on lap 35, and the <a href="/wiki/Honda_Racing_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Honda Racing F1">Honda</a> of <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a>, which dropped out on lap 37 with another hydraulic failure.
</p><p>Massa once again led for just one lap during Räikkönen's second stop, but he retook it the following lap.
</p><p>Räikkönen finally came home to win from Massa, clinching the World Constructors Championship for Ferrari. Alonso, Hamilton, <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> in the <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a>'s Williams, <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> in the Red Bull and <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a>'s Renault rounded up the top eight. The only changes from the starting top eight were Heidfeld and Rosberg swapping places and Kovalainen passing <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyota</a> to take eighth.
</p><p>The race was overshadowed by the death of former <a href="/wiki/World_Rally_Champion" class="mw-redirect" title="World Rally Champion">World Rally Champion</a> <a href="/wiki/Colin_McRae" title="Colin McRae">Colin McRae</a> in a helicopter crash during Saturday's qualifying.
</p>
