<p>The <b>1964 Italian Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held at the <a href="/wiki/Autodromo_Nazionale_di_Monza" title="Autodromo Nazionale di Monza">Autodromo Nazionale di Monza</a> on September 6, 1964. It was race 8 of 10 in both the <a href="/wiki/1964_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1964 World Championship of Drivers">1964 World Championship of Drivers</a> and the <a href="/wiki/1964_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1964 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1964 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>. The 78-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/John_Surtees" title="John Surtees">John Surtees</a> after he started from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>. <a href="/wiki/Bruce_McLaren" title="Bruce McLaren">Bruce McLaren</a> finished second for the <a href="/wiki/Cooper_Car_Company" title="Cooper Car Company">Cooper</a> team and Ferrari driver <a href="/wiki/Lorenzo_Bandini" title="Lorenzo Bandini">Lorenzo Bandini</a> came in third.
</p>
