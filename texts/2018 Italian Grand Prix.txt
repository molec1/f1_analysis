<p>The <b>2018 Italian Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>Formula 1 Gran Premio Heineken d'Italia 2018</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_racing" title="Formula One racing">Formula One motor race</a> held on 2 September 2018 at the <a href="/wiki/Autodromo_Nazionale_di_Monza" title="Autodromo Nazionale di Monza">Autodromo Nazionale di Monza</a> in <a href="/wiki/Monza,_Italy" class="mw-redirect" title="Monza, Italy">Monza, Italy</a>. The race was the fourteenth round of the <a href="/wiki/2018_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2018 Formula One World Championship">2018 Formula One World Championship</a> and marked the 88th running of the <a href="/wiki/Italian_Grand_Prix" title="Italian Grand Prix">Italian Grand Prix</a> and the 83rd time the race was held at Monza.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> entered the round with a 17-point lead over <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> in the Drivers' Championship. In the World Constructors' Championship, Mercedes led <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> by 15 points.
</p>
