<p>The <b>1966 British Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held at <a href="/wiki/Brands_Hatch" title="Brands Hatch">Brands Hatch</a> on 16 July 1966. It was race 4 of 9 in both the <a href="/wiki/1966_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1966 World Championship of Drivers">1966 World Championship of Drivers</a> and the <a href="/wiki/1966_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1966 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1966 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>. It was the 21st <a href="/wiki/British_Grand_Prix" title="British Grand Prix">British Grand Prix</a> and the second to be held at Brands Hatch. It was held over 80 laps of the four kilometre circuit for a race distance of 341 kilometres.
</p><p>The race, the first of the new three-litre engine regulation era where starters reached 20 cars, was won for the third time by Australian driver <a href="/wiki/Jack_Brabham" title="Jack Brabham">Jack Brabham</a> in his <a href="/wiki/Brabham_BT19" title="Brabham BT19">Brabham BT19</a>, his second win in succession after winning the <a href="/wiki/1966_French_Grand_Prix" title="1966 French Grand Prix">French Grand Prix</a> two weeks earlier. New Zealand driver <a href="/wiki/Denny_Hulme" title="Denny Hulme">Denny Hulme</a> finished second in his <a href="/wiki/Brabham_BT20" title="Brabham BT20">Brabham BT20</a>, a first 1â€“2 win for the <a href="/wiki/Brabham" title="Brabham">Brabham</a> team. The pair finished a lap ahead of third placed British driver <a href="/wiki/Graham_Hill" title="Graham Hill">Graham Hill</a> in his <a href="/wiki/BRM_P261" title="BRM P261">BRM P261</a>.
</p><p>Brabham's win ended a streak of 4 consecutive wins by <a href="/wiki/Jim_Clark" title="Jim Clark">Jim Clark</a> at the British Grand Prix.
</p><p>Brabham's win put him ten points clear in the championship chase over Austrian Cooper racer <a href="/wiki/Jochen_Rindt" title="Jochen Rindt">Jochen Rindt</a> with Hulme and Ferrari's <a href="/wiki/Lorenzo_Bandini" title="Lorenzo Bandini">Lorenzo Bandini</a> a point further back.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race_summary">Race summary</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1966_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Race summary">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Brabham and Hill duelled at the start, until rain storms arrived on the circuit. Whilst Brabham maintained his lead, Rindt who had changed to rain tyres was catching him rapidly, whilst <a href="/wiki/John_Surtees" title="John Surtees">John Surtees</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Cooper_Car_Company" title="Cooper Car Company">Cooper</a> was in third, well clear of the rest of the field. Hill and <a href="/wiki/Jim_Clark" title="Jim Clark">Jim Clark</a> then battled for second and third until Clark pitted and then Hulme took over second place. As the rain dried, Rindt fell back. Surtees retired with mechanical problems, leaving Clark in fourth. Brabham duly led home teammate Hulme, Hill, Clark, Rindt and <a href="/wiki/Bruce_McLaren" title="Bruce McLaren">Bruce McLaren</a>.
</p>
