<p>The <b>1967 Italian Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held at the <a href="/wiki/Autodromo_Nazionale_di_Monza" title="Autodromo Nazionale di Monza">Autodromo Nazionale di Monza</a> on 10 September 1967. It was race 9 of 11 in both the <a href="/wiki/1967_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1967 World Championship of Drivers">1967 World Championship of Drivers</a> and the <a href="/wiki/1967_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1967 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1967 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>. The race was won by British driver <a href="/wiki/John_Surtees" title="John Surtees">John Surtees</a> driving a <a href="/wiki/Honda" title="Honda">Honda</a>. It was the sixth and final career victory for Surtees, as well as the first ever race for the <a href="/wiki/Honda_RA300" title="Honda RA300">Honda RA300</a> which he drove to victory. This was the first Formula One race where start lights were used.
</p><p>This race is considered one of <a href="/wiki/Jim_Clark" title="Jim Clark">Jim Clark</a>'s greatest performances in Formula One.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (December 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> He led the race until lap 12 when he picked up a puncture and lost an entire lap. He then spent the next 48 laps recovering through the field, taking the lead on lap 60, and pulled away. But on the final lap, a faulty fuel pump<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (June 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> allowed <a href="/wiki/Jack_Brabham" title="Jack Brabham">Jack Brabham</a> and Surtees to pass the Scotsman and finish first and second, with Surtees ahead by less than a car length at the line. This was the second victory for the <a href="/wiki/Honda_Racing_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Honda Racing F1">Honda F1 team</a>, and the last for the factory team until <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> won the <a href="/wiki/2006_Hungarian_Grand_Prix" title="2006 Hungarian Grand Prix">2006 Hungarian Grand Prix</a>.  Some fans and journalists still consider this race to be, in terms of race action and excitement, the greatest Grand Prix of all time.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (March 2018)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>
