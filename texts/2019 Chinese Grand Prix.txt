<p>The <b>2019 Chinese Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 Heineken Chinese Grand Prix 2019</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_racing" title="Formula One racing">Formula One motor race</a> that took place on 14 April 2019 at the <a href="/wiki/Shanghai_International_Circuit" title="Shanghai International Circuit">Shanghai International Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Shanghai" title="Shanghai">Shanghai, China</a>. The race was the 3rd round of the <a href="/wiki/2019_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2019 Formula One World Championship">2019 Formula One World Championship</a>, and marked the 16th time that the <a href="/wiki/Chinese_Grand_Prix" title="Chinese Grand Prix">Chinese Grand Prix</a> had been run as a round of the Formula One World Championship. The race also marked the 1000th World Championship race since the <a href="/wiki/1950_British_Grand_Prix" title="1950 British Grand Prix">first World Championship race</a> was held at the <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/1950_Formula_One_season" title="1950 Formula One season">1950</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Championship_standings_before_the_race">Championship standings before the race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Championship standings before the race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Heading into the race, <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> was leading the Drivers' Championship by one point from <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> Bottas's team <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> was leading the Constructors' Championship by 39 points from <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Entrants">Entrants</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Entrants">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">Further information: <a href="/wiki/2019_Formula_One_World_Championship#Entries" title="2019 Formula One World Championship">2019 Formula One World Championship §&#160;Entries</a></div>
<p>The drivers and teams were the same as the season entry list with no additional stand in drivers for either the race or practice.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Free_practice">Free practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Free practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> was fastest in the first practice session with <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> fastest in the second practice.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Bottas was again fastest in FP3 on Saturday, however the session was brought to a premature end when <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Albon" title="Alexander Albon">Alexander Albon</a> lost control and hit the wall on the exit of the final corner, smashing into the wall rear-first with an impact of 49g.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Alexander_Albon" title="Alexander Albon">Alexander Albon</a> was unable to compete in Qualifying after a crash in Free Practice 3 earlier in the day. 
</p><p>The first qualifying session finished with no incidents with <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> setting the fastest lap of the session ahead of <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a> and teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>. Qualifying ended with the elimination of <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a>, <a href="/wiki/George_Russell_(racing_driver)" title="George Russell (racing driver)">George Russell</a>, <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a>, <a href="/wiki/Antonio_Giovinazzi" title="Antonio Giovinazzi">Antonio Giovinazzi</a> and Albon, the last two of whom failed to set a time in the session.
</p><p>The second qualifying session ended without incident with Hamilton setting the fastest lap on the medium tyre compound. Bottas, <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>, Leclerc and <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> also set their laps on the medium compound tyre. <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz Jr.</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">Lando Norris</a> were eliminated from qualifying.
</p><p>The third qualifying session ended without incident with most drivers having two chances to set a fastest lap. After the first runs, Bottas was quickest with Hamilton and Vettel rounding off the top 3. When  setting up their second runs, Verstappen was overtaken at turn 14 by Vettel and both <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a> cars, this prevented him crossing the line before the end of the session. <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a> and both <a href="/wiki/Haas_F1_Team" title="Haas F1 Team">Haas</a> cars were also unable to start a lap before the session ended as a result. At the end of the session, Bottas was on pole position 0.023 seconds ahead of Hamilton in second. Vettel qualified third. The rest of the top ten consisted of, in qualifying order, Leclerc, Verstappen, Gasly, <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> won the race ahead of his teammate <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>At the start of the race Bottas got a bad start allowing Hamilton to lead into the first corner. At the fourth corner <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a>, <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz Jr.</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">Lando Norris</a> collided which would cause Norris and Kvyat to retire later in the race. On lap 11 <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a> was ordered by Ferrari to let his teammate Vettel past. <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> retired on lap 17 with an <a href="/wiki/MGU-K" class="mw-redirect" title="MGU-K">MGU-K</a> problem. <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> managed to score his first points of the season and his first points for <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a>. <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a> got the first fastest lap of his career.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> It was also <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a>' third 1–2 finish of the <a href="/wiki/2019_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2019 Formula One World Championship">season</a> and the first time that a team has managed a 1-2 in the first three races of a season since <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Mansell" title="Nigel Mansell">Nigel Mansell</a> and <a href="/wiki/Riccardo_Patrese" title="Riccardo Patrese">Riccardo Patrese</a> did so for <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> in <a href="/wiki/1992_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="1992 Formula One World Championship">1992</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
