<p>The <b>2014 Belgian Grand Prix</b> (officially the <b>2014 Formula 1 Shell Belgian Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps" title="Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps">Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps</a> in <a href="/wiki/Stavelot" title="Stavelot">Stavelot</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wallonia" title="Wallonia">Wallonia</a>, on 24 August. It was the twelfth round of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2014 Formula One World Championship">2014 Formula One World Championship</a> and the 58th running of the <a href="/wiki/Belgian_Grand_Prix" title="Belgian Grand Prix">event</a> as part of the series. The 44-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> from a fifth position start. <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> finished second for <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> and <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> of <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> took third.
</p><p>Although Rosberg won the <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> by posting the fastest lap in qualifying, he was immediately passed by his teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> and the second Red Bull of <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> at the start. He returned to second after Vettel made a driver error at Les Combes corner and took the lead following contact with the right-hand front wing end plate and Hamilton's left-rear tyre on lap two. Rosberg made a <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit stop</a> to replace the front wing at the conclusion of lap eight, relinquishing the lead to Ricciardo. Over the course of the remainder of the race, Ricciardo maintained the lead and responded to the faster Rosberg in the final ten laps to achieve his second victory in a row and the third of his career.
</p><p>Rosberg was booed by the crowd and he received an undisclosed punishment from Mercedes for his contact with Hamilton; the two were allowed to race each other without <a href="/wiki/Team_orders" title="Team orders">team orders</a> being imposed. The result kept Ricciardo third in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> and Rosberg increased his lead over Hamilton to 29 <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Championship_points_scoring_systems" title="List of Formula One World Championship points scoring systems">points</a> after Hamilton retired with bodywork damage. <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> maintained fourth from Bottas in fifth. Mercedes retained its lead over Red Bull in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a> with seven races left in the season.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps,_April_22,_2018_SkySat_(cropped).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/77/Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps%2C_April_22%2C_2018_SkySat_%28cropped%29.jpg/220px-Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps%2C_April_22%2C_2018_SkySat_%28cropped%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/77/Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps%2C_April_22%2C_2018_SkySat_%28cropped%29.jpg/330px-Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps%2C_April_22%2C_2018_SkySat_%28cropped%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/77/Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps%2C_April_22%2C_2018_SkySat_%28cropped%29.jpg/440px-Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps%2C_April_22%2C_2018_SkySat_%28cropped%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2880" data-file-height="1920" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps,_April_22,_2018_SkySat_(cropped).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps" title="Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps">Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps</a> <i>(pictured in 2018)</i>, where the race was held</div></div></div>
<p>The race was the 12th of the 19 rounds in the <a href="/wiki/2014_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2014 Formula One World Championship">2014 Formula One World Championship</a> and the 58th <a href="/wiki/Belgian_Grand_Prix" title="Belgian Grand Prix">Belgian Grand Prix</a> Formula One race. It was held at the 20-turn 7.004&#160;km (4.352&#160;mi) <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps" title="Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps">Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps</a> in <a href="/wiki/Stavelot" title="Stavelot">Stavelot</a>, <a href="/wiki/Wallonia" title="Wallonia">Wallonia</a> on 24 August.<sup id="cite_ref-FIAPreview_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FIAPreview-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyres" title="Formula One tyres">Tyre</a> supplier <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> brought four types of tyre to the race: two dry compounds (yellow-banded soft and the white-banded medium) and two <a href="/wiki/Rain_tyre" title="Rain tyre">wet-weather</a> compounds (intermediate and full wet).<sup id="cite_ref-F1RacingStats_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-F1RacingStats-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Drag_reduction_system" title="Drag reduction system">drag reduction system</a> (DRS) had two activation zones for the race, one was on the <a href="/wiki/Straight_(racing)" title="Straight (racing)">straight</a> linking the Bus Stop <a href="/wiki/Chicane" title="Chicane">chicane</a> and La Source corner, and the second was on the Kemmel Straight linking Radillion and Les Combes turns.<sup id="cite_ref-NBCPreview_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-NBCPreview-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Following the <a href="/wiki/2013_Belgian_Grand_Prix" title="2013 Belgian Grand Prix">2013 race</a>, new debris fences were erected at La Source turn, the barrier on the inside of turn 11 was renewed and track drainage was improved.<sup id="cite_ref-GPUpdateDebris_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GPUpdateDebris-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Entering the race, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">World Drivers' Championship</a> with 202 <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Championship_points_scoring_systems" title="List of Formula One World Championship points scoring systems">points</a>, eleven ahead of his teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> in second. <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> of <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> was third with 131 points after winning the preceding <a href="/wiki/2014_Hungarian_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Hungarian Grand Prix">Hungarian Grand Prix</a> four weeks earlier and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> was fourth with 115 points. <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> for the <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> team was fifth with 95 points.<sup id="cite_ref-points_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> Mercedes led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">World Constructors' Championship</a> with 393 points and they were ahead of Red Bull in second with 213 points. Ferrari were with 142 points and Williams were seven points behind in fourth. With 98 points, <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> held fifth place.<sup id="cite_ref-points_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Andre_Lotterer_2012_WEC_Fuji.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/17/Andre_Lotterer_2012_WEC_Fuji.jpg/170px-Andre_Lotterer_2012_WEC_Fuji.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="254" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/17/Andre_Lotterer_2012_WEC_Fuji.jpg/255px-Andre_Lotterer_2012_WEC_Fuji.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/17/Andre_Lotterer_2012_WEC_Fuji.jpg/340px-Andre_Lotterer_2012_WEC_Fuji.jpg 2x" data-file-width="800" data-file-height="1193" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Andre_Lotterer_2012_WEC_Fuji.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Andr%C3%A9_Lotterer" title="André Lotterer">André Lotterer</a> <i>(pictured in 2012)</i> made his début at a Formula One race weekend, replacing <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a> at <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>At the preceding Hungarian Grand Prix, Hamilton ignored <a href="/wiki/Team_orders" title="Team orders">team orders</a> from Mercedes to allow Rosberg to pass him in the final laps. He reduced the Drivers' Championship deficit to 11 points with a third-place finish as Rosberg took fourth. The two discussed how the situation should be diffused in a meeting on the night of 21 August, three days before the Belgian Grand Prix.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> For his part, Hamilton said he desired a recreation of his best form from earlier in the season and sought to put his recent below-par qualifying performances behind him,
</p>
<blockquote><p>"I had some difficult weekends in the first part of the season, but then a bit of good fortune at the last race in Budapest kept me from damaging my car on the wall during that first lap, so hopefully that was a sign that my fortunes are changing at just the right time.There were so many positives to take from the opening eleven races, but both myself and the team always want more and know we are capable of more. The aim is to put the more difficult times of some of the previous weekends behind us and to recreate the best moments at every race from now on. I love driving at Spa, so there are few better places to start."<sup id="cite_ref-FoxMercPreview_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FoxMercPreview-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup></p></blockquote>
<p>Rosberg said he would not underestimate the duel for the championship due to pace of the other teams, "In Formula One you just can't afford to give away any advantage and I know that both the team and myself will be working harder than ever to keep improving and maintain our position right to the very end. On top of that, the battle with Lewis has been so close all season – and it could well stay that way right up to the final race – so every last point will be crucial in that contest, too."<sup id="cite_ref-FoxMercPreview_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FoxMercPreview-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>There was one change of driver for the race. Three-time co-winner of the <a href="/wiki/24_Hours_of_Le_Mans" title="24 Hours of Le Mans">24 Hours of Le Mans</a> and <a href="/wiki/2011_Formula_Nippon_Championship" title="2011 Formula Nippon Championship">2011 Formula Nippon</a> champion <a href="/wiki/Andr%C3%A9_Lotterer" title="André Lotterer">André Lotterer</a> drove in lieu of <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a> at <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a>. At 32 years, 288 days old, Lotterer was the oldest driver to debut in Formula One since <a href="/wiki/Giovanni_Lavaggi" title="Giovanni Lavaggi">Giovanni Lavaggi</a> at the <a href="/wiki/1995_German_Grand_Prix" title="1995 German Grand Prix">1995 German Grand Prix</a> and it was his first time in a Formula One car since he was a <a href="/wiki/Test_driver" title="Test driver">test driver</a> for <a href="/wiki/Jaguar_Racing" title="Jaguar Racing">Jaguar</a> in <a href="/wiki/2002_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2002 Formula One World Championship">2002</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia</a> announced that <a href="/wiki/2014_GP2_Series" title="2014 GP2 Series">GP2 Series</a> competitor and test and reserve driver <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Rossi" title="Alexander Rossi">Alexander Rossi</a> would be entered in place of regular driver <a href="/wiki/Max_Chilton" title="Max Chilton">Max Chilton</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> because of reported "contractual issues" but the organisation who represented Chilton said that he relinquished his role "voluntarily".<sup id="cite_ref-RossiChilton_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-RossiChilton-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Chilton's seat was subsequently reinstated following a successful request to the stewards by Marussia, meaning Rossi drove only in the first practice session.<sup id="cite_ref-RossiChilton_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-RossiChilton-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> In that session, <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a> reserve driver <a href="/wiki/Giedo_van_der_Garde" title="Giedo van der Garde">Giedo van der Garde</a> drove <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban Gutiérrez">Esteban Gutiérrez</a>'s car.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/00/Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg/170px-Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="256" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/00/Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg/255px-Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/00/Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg/340px-Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg 2x" data-file-width="800" data-file-height="1205" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Pastor_Maldonado_2015_Malaysia.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> <i>(pictured in 2015)</i> crashed in the second practice session and stopped the session temporarily.</div></div></div>
<p>Per the regulations for the 2014 season, three practice sessions were held, two 90-minute sessions on Friday and one 60-minute session on Saturday.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> In the first session, held in dry weather,<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg was fastest with a lap of 1 minute, 51.577 seconds, almost a tenth of a second quicker than his teammate Hamilton in second. The Ferrari cars of Alonso and <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> were third and fifth; they were separated by <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a>'s <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> in fourth. <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> of Force India led McLaren's <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a>, the other Force India of <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a>, Ricciardo and Bottas in positions six to ten.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportFP1_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportFP1-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> During the session, Alonso briefly stopped at the edge of Eau Rouge corner with engine problems,<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> and his teammate Räikkönen spun leaving the La Source <a href="/wiki/Hairpin_turn" title="Hairpin turn">hairpin</a> before his car's <a href="/wiki/Kinetic_energy_recovery_system" title="Kinetic energy recovery system">energy recovery system</a> overheated.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Button's DRS was lodged open, Vettel's session ended early with a temperature-related <a href="/wiki/Exhaust_system" title="Exhaust system">exhaust</a> failure and <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Ericsson" title="Marcus Ericsson">Marcus Ericsson</a> spun his Caterham at the La Source hairpin.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportFP1_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportFP1-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hamilton set the day's fastest lap on the soft compound tyre during a qualifying simulation in the second session at 1 minute, 49.189 seconds. Rosberg was 0.604 seconds slower in second and Alonso was third. <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> of Williams was fourth, Button fifth and Bottas sixth. <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a>, Ricciardo, Magnussen and Hülkenberg followed in the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportFP2_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportFP2-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Just after eight minutes into practice, <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> ran onto the grass on the outside exiting Rivage corner and heading into Pouhon turn. He lost control of his <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a>, speared across into a barrier on the inside, causing a <a href="/wiki/Racing_flags#Red_flag" title="Racing flags">red flag</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportFP2_18-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportFP2-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> The right-rear wheel was deranged from Maldonado's car and <a href="/wiki/Motorsport_marshal" title="Motorsport marshal">track marshals</a> cleared debris.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> He was transported to the circuit's medical centre for a pre-cautionary check-up and was released soon after with no injuries found.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> A gearbox fault caused Gutiérrez to spin soon after at Blanchimont corner, prompting a second stoppage since he was unable to continue driving.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportFP2_18-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportFP2-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the second session, Vettel, who was sidelined with an <a href="/wiki/Ignition_system" title="Ignition system">ignition</a> failure in one <a href="/wiki/Cylinder_(engine)" title="Cylinder (engine)">cylinder</a> breaking the exhaust and overheating the kinetic <a href="/wiki/Motor%E2%80%93generator" title="Motor–generator">motor–generator unit</a>, changed his engine, his fifth of the season. He avoided incurring a ten-place grid penalty because he reverted to an old engine used in the preceding Hungarian Grand Prix.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Heavy overnight rain made the circuit damp and little on-track activity occurred in the first 30 minutes of the final session.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> Wet and intermediate tyres were used before the dry soft and medium tyres during the rest of the session as teams prepared for qualifying. Bottas was fastest on the drying track with a lap of 1 minute, 49.465 seconds late in the session. Ricciardo, Rosberg, Räikkönen, Hamilton, Alonso, Kvyat, Button, Massa and Toro Rosso's <a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a> completed the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_(cropped).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/51/Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg/170px-Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="206" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/51/Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg/255px-Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/51/Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg/340px-Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1890" data-file-height="2285" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_(cropped).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> achieved the 11th <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> of his career in a rain-affected qualifying session.</div></div></div>
<p>Saturday afternoon's <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#Q" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">qualifying session</a> was divided into three parts. The first part ran for 18 minutes, eliminating cars that finished the session 17th or below. The <a href="/wiki/107%25_rule" title="107% rule">107% rule</a> was in effect, requiring drivers to reach a time within 107 per cent of the quickest lap to qualify. The second part lasted 15 minutes, eliminating cars that finished 11th to 16th. The final 12-minute session determined <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> to tenth. Cars who progressed to the final session were not allowed to change tyres for the race's start, using the tyres with which they set their quickest lap times in the second session.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Heavy rain returned 40 minutes before qualifying commenced and accompanied a hailstorm, making the circuit slippery. Although it later stopped, uncertainty over the weather caused teams to use wet-weather tyres. Rain returned at the conclusion of the first session and it remained for most of the following session before it turned sunny for the final session.<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Rosberg was fastest in the first and third sessions and took his first pole position at Spa-Francorchamps, his fourth in a row, his seventh of 2014, and the 11th of his career with a time of 2 minutes, 5.591 seconds.<sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> He was joined on the <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#G" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">grid</a>'s front row by his teammate Hamilton who had a grazed front left brake disc that caused his car to jerk and lost time by changing his braking points.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> Vettel and Alonso took third and fourth after their first timed laps of the final session and both drivers could not better the two Mercedes.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportQuali_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportQuali-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo qualified fifth after running wide at Blanchimont corner on his final lap. A loss of time in the first third of the lap caused Bottas to start from sixth. An <a href="/wiki/Understeer_and_oversteer" title="Understeer and oversteer">understeer</a> from <a href="/wiki/Flat_spot" title="Flat spot">flat-spotting</a> his front tyres at the La Source hairpin in the second session gave Magnussen seventh.<sup id="cite_ref-SatQuotes_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SatQuotes-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Multiple driving errors from Räikkönen placed him in eighth. Ninth-placed Massa had a grazed front right disc in the track's heavy braking zones. Traffic on his warm-up lap comprised his tyre warming and slowed him.<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> Button took tenth after locking his brakes cresting a hill into Les Combes corner.<sup id="cite_ref-SatQuotes_28-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SatQuotes-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Kvyat was the fastest driver not to qualify in the top ten because he was demoted to 11th in the final seconds of the second session.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportQuali_27-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportQuali-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> An exhaust system fault limited his teammate Vergne to three timed laps and 12th place. The wet track prevented Pérez from going faster and he took 13th. Sauber's <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> pushed hard to start from 14th and a loss of rear car control left Lotus' <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> in 15th.<sup id="cite_ref-SatQuotes_28-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SatQuotes-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Marussia driver <a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a> entered the second session for the second successive year in Belgium and the third time in the last four races;<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportQuali_27-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportQuali-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-SkyPole_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SkyPole-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> he took 16th after balance issues at Les Combes corner and brake grazing problems caused him to run deep at Bruxelles turn. Maldonado, 17th. failed to progress beyond the first session after spinning on his fastest lap.<sup id="cite_ref-SatQuotes_28-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SatQuotes-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Hülkenberg in 18th could not progress past the first session for the first time since the 2012 season due to a loss of brake feel and a minor driver error.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportQuali_27-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportQuali-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-SkyPole_30-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SkyPole-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> A lack of <a href="/wiki/Automotive_battery" title="Automotive battery">battery</a> power put Chilton 19th and <a href="/wiki/Powertrain" title="Powertrain">powertrain</a> problems left Gutiérrez in 20th.<sup id="cite_ref-SatQuotes_28-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SatQuotes-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Lotterer in 21st out-qualified his Caterham teammate Ericsson in 22nd by almost a second.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportQuali_27-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportQuali-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The race began from a <a href="/wiki/Standing_start" title="Standing start">standing start</a> at 14:00 <a href="/wiki/Central_European_Summer_Time" title="Central European Summer Time">Central European Summer Time</a> (<a href="/wiki/UTC%2B02:00" title="UTC+02:00">UTC+02:00</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> The weather at the start was dry with dark clouds,<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> and some rain had fallen.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCRaceLive_34-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCRaceLive-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> The air temperature was between 13 to 15&#160;°C (55 to 59&#160;°F) and the track temperature from 21 and 31&#160;°C (70 and 88&#160;°F);<sup id="cite_ref-35" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> forecasts on Sunday predicted rain to fall but not with the same intensity as on Saturday.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCRaceLive_34-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCRaceLive-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Every driver, except for Sutil, began on the soft compound tyres.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCRaceLive_34-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCRaceLive-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> The soft compound tyre was predicted to be 13 seconds faster over 15 laps and degradation rate research prompted most teams to make two pit stops for their drivers.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> As the Ferrari mechanics were about to start Alonso's car on the grid, it failed to respond because its battery had been emptied for unspecified reasons. They retrieved a jump-start battery from their garage and used it on his car, giving Alonso a five-second time penalty taken at his first pit stop since Ferrari broke a regulation mandating all team personnel to vacate the track 15 seconds before the start of the <a href="/wiki/Parade_lap" title="Parade lap">formation lap</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> Alonso's mechanics completed the installation of the spare battery before the last car passed him, allowing him to start from fourth.<sup id="cite_ref-36" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Although he stopped slightly outside of his grid slot and reversed to find a good angle, Hamilton used his teammate Rosberg's slower start to lead into the La Source hairpin.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLive_37-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLive-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Vettel out-braked Rosberg on the left and passed him going uphill towards Eau Rouge corner putting himself between the two Mercedes. He deployed 160&#160;hp (120&#160;kW) of <a href="/wiki/Electrical_energy" title="Electrical energy">electrical energy</a> on the Kemmel Straight as Hamilton attempted to prevent Vettel from clinging onto his <a href="/wiki/Drafting_(aerodynamics)" title="Drafting (aerodynamics)">slipstream</a> by defending on the right, encouraing Vettel to steer left and he <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#G" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">out-braked</a> Hamilton on cold tyres. He locked his tyres and used the run-off area to rejoin the track in third, behind Rosberg but ahead of Ricciardo.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> Elsewhere, Bianchi and Grosjean made contact at the exit to the La Source hairpin, <a href="/wiki/Flat_tire" title="Flat tire">puncturing</a> Bianchi's right-rear tyre and forcing him into the pit lane for a replacement wheel.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLive_37-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLive-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> At the end of the first lap, Hamilton led his teammate Rosberg by half a second,<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> with Vettel third. Alonso and Ricciardo were fourth and fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLive_37-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLive-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Maldonado became the first driver to retire from the Grand Prix with a suspected exhaust failure causing smoke to emit from the rear of his car on lap two.<sup id="cite_ref-LotusReport_38-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LotusReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c3/Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg/170px-Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="237" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c3/Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg/255px-Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c3/Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg/340px-Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg 2x" data-file-width="784" data-file-height="1094" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_October_2014.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> was put out of contention from contact with his teammate Rosberg on lap two. He subsequently implored his team <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> to retire him and was allowed to on the 39th lap.</div></div></div>
<p>That lap, Hamilton again went defensive but this time from Rosberg, his teammate, who slipstreamed him. Rosberg put Hamilton on the outside line as his front wing was level with Hamilton's side pod when the two entered Les Combes corner.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> As Hamilton was halfway through the left-hand turn,<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLive_37-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLive-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> his left-rear tyre got punctured by Rosberg's front right wing endplate. A section of endplate was launched airborne; much of Rosberg's front wing remained intact. Hamilton's tyre delaminated at the exit to Pouhon corner and it caused downforce-related bodywork damage.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> He drove slowly to the pit lane and rejoined the race in 19th.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCRaceLive_34-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCRaceLive-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLive_37-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLive-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> DRS was enabled on the third lap.<sup id="cite_ref-STRace_39-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-STRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> On that lap, Ricciardo's low-downforce setup gave him an efficient DRS to pass Alonso for third into Les Combes corner. Ricciardo quickly drew close to his teammate Vettel. After Vettel ran wide onto the damp artificial grass through Pouhon corner on lap five, he slid luridly and Ricciardo got past for second.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLive_37-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLive-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> On the same lap,<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLive_37-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLive-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Lotterer was retired from a loss of power, possibly caused by him driving over a kerb too hard at Blancimont turn.<sup id="cite_ref-40" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Pérez passed Massa for ninth on lap six and Bottas used DRS to overtake Alonso for fourth on the Kemmel Straight.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCRaceLive_34-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCRaceLive-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Rosberg was called into the pit lane at the end of lap eight for a replacement front wing and the medium compound tyres. He rejoined the race in 15th. Räikkönen made a pit stop on the same lap in Ferrari's attempt to put him ahead of Bottas and Vettel after the pit stops ended.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> On lap 10,<sup id="cite_ref-BBCRaceLive_34-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCRaceLive-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> a section of tyre carcass from Bianchi's punctured tyre wrapped itself around Rosberg's <a href="/wiki/Antenna_(radio)" title="Antenna (radio)">radio antenna</a>, diverting his attention before releasing itself.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> Between laps 10 and 12, Vettel, Ricciardo, Bottas and Alonso made their first pit stops. After the pit stops, Ricciardo led Räikkönen by 2.4 seconds, with Vettel a further three seconds behind in third, Rosberg was fourth and Bottas fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLive_37-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLive-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Vergne was overtaken for seventh at the Bus Stop chicane by Magnussen on the 15th lap.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCRaceLive_34-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCRaceLive-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Back in fourth, Rosberg's pit stop on the eighth lap compromised him strategy-wise and he could not pass Vettel on the straights. He attempted to brake later than Vettel on the outside at the Bus Stop chicane on lap 17; he locked his tyres on the run-off area.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> Bottas then slipstreamed Rosberg on the Kemmel Straight and deployed DRS to overtake him for fourth.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> Two laps later, Rosberg made a second pit stop to replace his flat-spotted tyres with the soft compounds and emerged in 11th.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-BBCRaceLive_34-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCRaceLive-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Räikkönen was almost eight seconds behind Ricciardo when Ferrari asked him to enter the pit lane on the 20th lap because his rear tyres had degraded enough to prompt a tyre change. On lap 21, Hamilton in 17th implored Mercedes to retire him because of his car's bodywork damage; the team insisted he continue in anticipation of a <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a> deployment.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-STRace_39-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-STRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg passed Pérez for seventh on lap 22.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCRaceLive_34-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCRaceLive-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> That lap, Vettel who lapped in the 1 minute, 55 second range to Rosberg's 1 minute, 51 seconds made his pit stop and fell in eighth, behind Rosberg and Räikkönen.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> During lap 23, Magnussen in fourth blocked Alonso from overtaking him three times.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLive_37-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLive-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> That lap, Rosberg passed Button for fourth, only for Button to reclaim the position because Rosberg had a fuel limit issue. On the following lap, Rosberg tried again to pass Button and he was successful that time round. He overtook Alonso on the Kemmel Straight for third on lap 25.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCRaceLive_34-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCRaceLive-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-STRace_39-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-STRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> That lap, Hamilton overtook Grosjean for 16th and Button lost fifth to Vettel at the Bus Stop chicane.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLive_37-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLive-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_(04).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/0b/Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2804%29.jpg/170px-Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2804%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="255" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/0b/Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2804%29.jpg/255px-Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2804%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/0b/Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2804%29.jpg/340px-Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2804%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2425" data-file-height="3637" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Daniel_at_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_(04).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> <i>(pictured at the <a href="/wiki/2014_Italian_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Italian Grand Prix">Italian Grand Prix</a>)</i> took his second consecutive victory and claimed the third win of his career.</div></div></div>
<p>Ricciardo made a second pit stop from the lead for the medium compound tyres at the conclusion of the 27th lap.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCRaceLive_34-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCRaceLive-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLive_37-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLive-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> He exited the pit lane 3.7 seconds ahead of Rosberg in third. Bottas took the lead for one lap, before making his own stop at the end of the lap 28 and emerged in fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-STRace_39-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-STRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Bottas overtook Vettel on the outside at Les Combes corner for fourth on lap 30.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLive_37-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLive-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Four laps later, Grosjean retired from downforce-related damage sustained to his Lotus at his second pit stop.<sup id="cite_ref-LotusReport_38-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-LotusReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> After Ricciardo re-assumed the lead earlier, Rosberg's soft compound tyres were worn and was unable to match Ricciardo's pace. Mercedes decided to put Rosberg on a third pit stop to get the win from Ricciardo on the final lap. Red Bull gave Ricciardo a target lap time of 1 minute, 53.400 seconds to prevent Rosberg from overtaking him, which he achieved consistently.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg's pit stop came at the end of lap 34. He passed Bottas and Räikkönen to return to second by lap 36.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLive_37-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLive-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hamilton's request to retire was granted on the 39th lap and he entered the pit lane to be pushed into the garage.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCRaceLive_34-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCRaceLive-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Around that period, Bottas challenged his compatriot Räikkönen for third on the outside of Les Combes corner but Räikkönen prevented Bottas from completing the pass. Bottas attempted again on lap 40 and passed Räikkönen on the Kemmel Straight.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLive_37-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLive-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> One lap later, Bianchi retired with a failed gearbox.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCRaceLive_34-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCRaceLive-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> As Alonso slipstreamed Magnussen at 320&#160;km/h (200&#160;mph) on the Kemmel Straight and lapped Ericsson on lap 42, Magnussen turned right and Alonso put half of his Ferrari onto the grass to avoid a major crash. Alonso's loss of momentum allowed Button past him into Les Combes corner. Button failed to pass his teammate Magnussen at Rivage turn, allowing Alonso and Vettel to demote him to seventh. Alonso tried again to pass Magnussen on lap 43 at Les Combes corner though it encouraged Vettel to draw alongside him.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> He tried for a third time at Rivage corner but was put onto the grass and Vettel overtook Alonso.<sup id="cite_ref-MotorSportMagReport_33-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-MotorSportMagReport-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Starting the final lap, Vettel overtook Magnussen into the La Source hairpin and Alonso attempted to pass the latter pass on the inside. Alonso made contact with the rear of Magnussen's car, breaking his front wing, and Button passed Alonso for seventh.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCRaceLive_34-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCRaceLive-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Meanwhile, Ricciardo maintained the lead under pressure from Rosberg for his second successive win, and the third of his career.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_41-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg in second followed 3.383 seconds later and Bottas took third.<sup id="cite_ref-results_42-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-results-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> Off the podium, Räikkönen took his best result of 2014 in fourth and his highest finish since the <a href="/wiki/2013_Korean_Grand_Prix" title="2013 Korean Grand Prix">2013 Korean Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCRaceLive_34-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCRaceLive-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLive_37-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLive-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Magnussen finished fifth on the road and Vettel sixth. Button, Alonso, Pérez and Kvyat were in seventh to tenth. Outside the top ten, Hülkenberg, Vergne, Massa, Sutil, Gutiérrez, Chilton, Ericsson and Bianchi were the final finishers.<sup id="cite_ref-results_42-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-results-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At the podium interviews, conducted by former team owner <a href="/wiki/Eddie_Jordan" title="Eddie Jordan">Eddie Jordan</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-PressConf_43-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PressConf-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg was booed by the crowd four times for his contact with Hamilton on lap two,<sup id="cite_ref-44" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> which they perceived as intentional.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_41-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> He blamed the reaction on a small group of Hamilton's British support and urged them to acquaint themselves with the regulations and a drivers' code of conduct before booing.<sup id="cite_ref-45" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo said he had to maintain his pace and not hit anything after Rosberg entered the pit lane. Rosberg spoke of his confidence Mercedes could have dominated because of their quick pace and Bottas said it would be difficult for Williams to win because of Mercedes' outright speed.<sup id="cite_ref-PressConf_43-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PressConf-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> In the later press conference, Ricciardo said he had foreseen himself accruing enough points to put himself in contention for the Drivers' Championship. Rosberg said his strategy was altered because of the damage to his car and declined to reveal details of the meeting with Mercedes after the Hungarian Grand Prix concerning envisaging contact with Hamilton. Bottas stated a slow start caused other cars to delay him and called the duel with compatriot Räikkönen "a clean and fair fight".<sup id="cite_ref-PressConf_43-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PressConf-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The collision between Rosberg and Hamilton on lap two overshadowed the race. The stewards investigated the contact and declared it "a racing incident", with neither driver to blame.<sup id="cite_ref-46" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton claimed after a meeting that Rosberg admitted to have made wilful contact to "prove a point" and that Hamilton could have avoided the situation. Rosberg initially denied any wrongdoing, saying he discerned no risk in attempting the manoeuvre.<sup id="cite_ref-47" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-47">&#91;47&#93;</a></sup> Mercedes team principal <a href="/wiki/Toto_Wolff" title="Toto Wolff">Toto Wolff</a> called the contact "absolutely unacceptable" and the team's non-executive chairman <a href="/wiki/Niki_Lauda" title="Niki Lauda">Niki Lauda</a> echoed his colleague's view, "If these things happen at the end of the race when they are fighting, we can talk about it. But on the second lap, it is ridiculous."<sup id="cite_ref-48" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-48">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg denied Hamlton's version of the internal meeting two days after the race and that there was "a very good and important discussion after the race, everybody gave their opinion and now we move forward".<sup id="cite_ref-49" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-49">&#91;49&#93;</a></sup> The championship's governing body, the <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_l%27Automobile" title="Fédération Internationale de l&#39;Automobile">Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile</a> (FIA), said Hamilton's claims did not warrant a review under the <a href="/wiki/International_Sporting_Code" title="International Sporting Code">FIA International Sporting Code</a>, which states if "a new element was discovered" regardless of the stewards' decision, a second meeting had to be convened to address it.<sup id="cite_ref-50" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-50">&#91;50&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Five days after the race Mercedes held a meeting between senior members of the team (including Wolff and technical director <a href="/wiki/Paddy_Lowe" title="Paddy Lowe">Paddy Lowe</a>) and both drivers to determine what measures were to be taken to maintain their championship aspirations.<sup id="cite_ref-51" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-51">&#91;51&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg incurred an undisclosed punishment during the half hour conference and he and Hamilton were allowed to continue battling each other for the rest of the season without the imposition of <a href="/wiki/Team_orders" title="Team orders">team orders</a> favouring one driver over the other.<sup id="cite_ref-DTPunish_52-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DTPunish-52">&#91;52&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg accepted responsibility for causing the contact from an "error of judgement" and apologised to Hamilton, Mercedes and the fans of Formula One.<sup id="cite_ref-53" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-53">&#91;53&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton admitted through a statement he made errors during the rivalry with Rosberg over the course of the season but believed it would be an error to apportion blame to one driver, "There is a deep foundation that still exists for me and Nico to work from, in spite of our difficult times and differences."<sup id="cite_ref-DTPunish_52-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-DTPunish-52">&#91;52&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The stewards deemed Magnussen to have provided Alonso with inadequate space and forced him to run off the track on the Kemmel Straight while lapping Ericsson. They imposed a 20-second time penalty on Magnussen, demoting him from fifth to twelfth and elevating Alonso to seventh and Hülkenberg to tenth.<sup id="cite_ref-54" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-54">&#91;54&#93;</a></sup> Alonso said he was unconcerned since he was battling for position outside of the top five, "He was lapping a Caterham and we were all in the slipstream. I tried to overtake and he closed maybe a little bit too much because half the car was on the grass."<sup id="cite_ref-BBCPenalty_55-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCPenalty-55">&#91;55&#93;</a></sup> Magnussen commented on the penalty, "Everyone around me had slightly fresher tyres and they're not the easiest guys to keep behind. So I just tried my best to defend my position as well as I could."<sup id="cite_ref-BBCPenalty_55-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCPenalty-55">&#91;55&#93;</a></sup> The McLaren racing director <a href="/wiki/%C3%89ric_Boullier" title="Éric Boullier">Éric Boullier</a> argued that Magnussen's move needed to be embraced by Formula One and believed the former had made adequate progress over the year,
</p>
<blockquote><p>"Obviously it is part of the experience he is gaining over the season. He is very motivated. He is new to the category and it is nice to see the big boys a pushing a little bit to show him 'stay behind, kid'. It is part of the learning process, so it is good. I think it is not easy to step into F1, especially with a top team. There is a lot of pressure in delivering when there is no more testing. It is not easy."<sup id="cite_ref-56" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-56">&#91;56&#93;</a></sup></p></blockquote>
<p>The race result increased Rosberg's lead in the Drivers' Championship to 29 points over his teammate Hamilton in second place. Ricciardo's victory kept him in third with 126 points, while Alonso's seventh-place finish moved him to eleven points ahead of Bottas in fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-points_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> In the Constructors' Championship, Mercedes (with 411 points) maintained their lead over second-placed Red Bull (254 points). Ferrari in third place increased the gap over Williams in fourth to ten points as McLaren overtook Force India for fifth with seven races left in the season.<sup id="cite_ref-points_6-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
