<p>The <b>2019 Canadian Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 Pirelli Grand Prix du Canada 2019</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> held on 9 June 2019 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_Gilles_Villeneuve" title="Circuit Gilles Villeneuve">Circuit Gilles Villeneuve</a> in <a href="/wiki/Montreal" title="Montreal">Montreal, Quebec</a>, Canada.<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It was the 7th round of the <a href="/wiki/2019_FIA_Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="2019 FIA Formula One World Championship">2019 FIA Formula One World Championship</a>. It was the 56th running of the <a href="/wiki/Canadian_Grand_Prix" title="Canadian Grand Prix">Canadian Grand Prix</a>, the 50th time the event had been included as a round of the <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="Formula One World Championship">Formula One World Championship</a> since the inception of the series in <a href="/wiki/1950_Formula_One_season" title="1950 Formula One season">1950</a>, and the 40th time that a World Championship had been held at Circuit Gilles Villeneuve. The race was won by <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> after a controversial penalty was given to race leader <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Championship_standings_before_the_race">Championship standings before the race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Championship standings before the race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Heading into the race <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> had a 17-point advantage over team mate <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> in the Drivers' Championship. In the Constructors' Championship it was <a href="/wiki/Mercedes_in_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Mercedes in F1">Mercedes</a> who held a 118-point advantage over <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Entrants">Entrants</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Entrants">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">Further information: <a href="/wiki/2019_Formula_One_World_Championship#Entries" title="2019 Formula One World Championship">2019 Formula One World Championship §&#160;Entries</a></div>
<p>The drivers and teams were the same as the season entry list with no additional stand-in drivers for the race. However, <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_Latifi" title="Nicholas Latifi">Nicholas Latifi</a> drove in the first practice session for <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Philip_Morris_International" title="Philip Morris International">Mission Winnow</a>, the title sponsor of <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, was banned from this race as it did not comply with local laws governing tobacco sponsorship.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> won pole position ahead of <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> and Vettel's teammate <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a>. <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a>  could only finish 11th in Q2 following a crash at the final corner from <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a>, who therefore failed to set a lap time in Q3, and started the race from the pit lane. <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> qualified in 4th place for <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a>, the team's highest qualifying position since <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> qualified 4th at the <a href="/wiki/2010_Japanese_Grand_Prix" title="2010 Japanese Grand Prix">2010 Japanese Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:1_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> started on <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole</a> and maintained the lead from <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>, who started in second place. The opening lap saw <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> lose sixth place to <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a>, and saw <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Albon" title="Alexander Albon">Alexander Albon</a> squeezed between <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> and <a href="/wiki/Antonio_Giovinazzi" title="Antonio Giovinazzi">Antonio Giovinazzi</a> at turn one, making contact with Giovinazzi and causing Albon to lose his front wing. Debris from Albon was blown back into the cockpit of <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Haas_F1_Team" title="Haas F1 Team">Haas</a>, catching on the car's <a href="/wiki/Halo_(safety_device)" title="Halo (safety device)">halo</a> and causing Grosjean to go straight on at turn two whilst he removed the debris with his hand, losing positions. Albon pitted at the end of the lap for a replacement, having fallen to last place. His pace suffered for the rest of the race, and the team eventually opted to retire his damaged car on lap 60. <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz Jr.</a> was forced to pit at the end of the third lap after his <a href="/wiki/Disc_brake#Calipers" title="Disc brake">brake calipers</a> began to overheat due to a duct blockage. The problem was cleared and Sainz emerged from the pits in 19th place.
</p><p>On lap nine, <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">Lando Norris</a>, who was running in eighth place, pulled over to the side of the pit exit after suffering a broken right-rear suspension. It later emerged that the <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>'s brakes had overheated and set on fire, burning through the suspension. This was unconnected to the problem encountered earlier by his teammate, which was caused by a helmet visor tear-off.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> No <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a> was deployed, since Norris' car had stopped away from the track.
</p><p>Hülkenberg made his first pit stop at the end of lap 16, allowing Bottas and <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> to take fourth and fifth place respectively. Vettel pitted at the end of lap 26, with Hamilton inheriting the race lead. Vettel emerged in third place ahead of Bottas and Verstappen. On the following lap, Giovinazzi spun at turn two, coming to a stop on the grass and avoiding the barriers. He was able to recover and continue racing. Hamilton pitted at the end of lap 28, coming out behind Vettel and handing the lead to <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a>. Bottas pitted on lap 30, emerging in sixth place behind the <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a> of <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a>. Leclerc pitted on lap 33, resulting in Vettel re-taking the lead. Bottas made it past Ricciardo on lap 38 after struggling to overtake the Renault since his pit stop.
</p><p>Hamilton had been closely following Vettel until turn three of lap 48, when Vettel went straight on over the grass, missing turn four. As the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> re-joined the track, Hamilton was squeezed towards the outside wall and was forced to slow down to avoid a collision. A lap later, Verstappen was the last of the front-runners to make a pit stop, emerging in seventh place behind both Renaults. By lap 52 the <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> had made it up to fifth place.
</p><p>On lap 57, Vettel was handed a five-second time penalty for the lap 48 incident, for re-entering the track unsafely and forcing another driver off the track. Over the next thirteen laps, Vettel attempted to build a five-second gap to Hamilton in second. Vettel ultimately crossed the finish line in first place, but was only 1.3 seconds ahead, meaning the victory was awarded to Hamilton. At the conclusion of the race, rather than park his car in parc ferme, Vettel pulled over much earlier in the pit lane and had to be collected by an official to attend the podium.  On the way, Vettel removed the #1 sign from in front of Hamilton's car and moved it to the empty space where his car should have been parked.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>  Ferrari announced it would appeal the penalty.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:0_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:4_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> On 21 June the FIA denied the appeal, stating that Ferrari had supplied "...no significant and relevant new elements which were unavailable to the parties at the time [the penalty was given],..."<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
