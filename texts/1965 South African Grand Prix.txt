<p>The <b>1965 South African Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Motor_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Motor race">motor race</a> held at <a href="/wiki/Prince_George_Circuit" title="Prince George Circuit">East London</a> on 1 January 1965. It was race 1 of 10 in both the <a href="/wiki/1965_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1965 World Championship of Drivers">1965 World Championship of Drivers</a> and the <a href="/wiki/1965_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1965 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1965 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>. The 85-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Team_Lotus" title="Team Lotus">Lotus</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Jim_Clark" title="Jim Clark">Jim Clark</a> after he started from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>. <a href="/wiki/John_Surtees" title="John Surtees">John Surtees</a> finished second for the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> team and <a href="/wiki/British_Racing_Motors" title="British Racing Motors">BRM</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Graham_Hill" title="Graham Hill">Graham Hill</a> came in third. This was the debut race of the future world champion <a href="/wiki/Jackie_Stewart" title="Jackie Stewart">Jackie Stewart</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race_report">Race report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1965_South_African_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Race report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Jim_Clark" title="Jim Clark">Jim Clark</a> celebrated <a href="/wiki/Hogmanay" title="Hogmanay">Hogmanay</a> by dominating the race, leading from pole and breaking the 100&#160;mph barrier, winning by half a minute from <a href="/wiki/Graham_Hill" title="Graham Hill">Graham Hill</a> and <a href="/wiki/John_Surtees" title="John Surtees">John Surtees</a> and even had time to complete an extra lap after the chequered flag was waved a lap too early. <a href="/wiki/Mike_Spence" title="Mike Spence">Mike Spence</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bruce_McLaren" title="Bruce McLaren">Bruce McLaren</a> and débutant <a href="/wiki/Jackie_Stewart" title="Jackie Stewart">Jackie Stewart</a> completed the points positions. <a href="/wiki/Goodyear_Tire_and_Rubber_Company" title="Goodyear Tire and Rubber Company">Goodyear</a> made their Grand Prix début with the <a href="/wiki/Brabham" title="Brabham">Brabham</a> team, challenging the <a href="/wiki/Dunlop_Rubber" title="Dunlop Rubber">Dunlop</a> monopoly.
</p>
