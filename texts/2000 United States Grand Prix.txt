<p>The <b>2000 United States Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>XXIX SAP United States Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 24 September 2000 at <a href="/wiki/Indianapolis_Motor_Speedway" title="Indianapolis Motor Speedway">Indianapolis Motor Speedway</a> in <a href="/wiki/Indianapolis,_Indiana" class="mw-redirect" title="Indianapolis, Indiana">Indianapolis, Indiana</a>. It was the fifteenth race of the <a href="/wiki/2000_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2000 Formula One season">2000 Formula One season</a> and the 34th <a href="/wiki/United_States_Grand_Prix" title="United States Grand Prix">United States Grand Prix</a>. The 73-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> after starting from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>. His teammate <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a> finished second with <a href="/wiki/Jordan_Grand_Prix" title="Jordan Grand Prix">Jordan</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Heinz-Harald_Frentzen" title="Heinz-Harald Frentzen">Heinz-Harald Frentzen</a> third.
</p><p>McLaren driver <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> started second, alongside Michael Schumacher. However, Coulthard was forced to serve a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_regulations#Penalties" title="Formula One regulations">ten-second stop-go penalty</a> on lap eight because he moved forward into the lead before the start. Michael Schumacher's main rival in the championship, <a href="/wiki/Mika_H%C3%A4kkinen" title="Mika Häkkinen">Mika Häkkinen</a>, started from fourth but retired with an blown engine on lap 26. Michael Schumacher thus had an unchallenged lead and was able to clinch his seventh win of the season, despite a spin in the final stages of the race.
</p><p>As a consequence of the race, Michael Schumacher retook the lead in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> by eight points over Häkkinen. Coulthard's fifth place in the Grand Prix eliminated his hopes of winning the Championship, and reduced his lead over fourth-placed Barrichello to eight points. In the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a>, Ferrari took over the lead held by McLaren and established a ten-point advantage, with two races of the season remaining.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2000_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The Grand Prix was contested by eleven teams, each of two drivers.<sup id="cite_ref-entrants_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-entrants-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The teams, also known as <a href="/wiki/Formula_One#Constructors" title="Formula One">constructors</a>, were <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jordan_Grand_Prix" title="Jordan Grand Prix">Jordan</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jaguar_Racing" title="Jaguar Racing">Jaguar</a>, <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a>, <a href="/wiki/Benetton_Formula" title="Benetton Formula">Benetton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Prost_Grand_Prix" title="Prost Grand Prix">Prost</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a>, <a href="/wiki/Arrows_Grand_Prix_International" title="Arrows Grand Prix International">Arrows</a>, <a href="/wiki/Minardi" title="Minardi">Minardi</a> and <a href="/wiki/British_American_Racing" title="British American Racing">BAR</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-entrants_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-entrants-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyre" class="mw-redirect" title="Formula One tyre">Tyre</a> supplier <a href="/wiki/Bridgestone" title="Bridgestone">Bridgestone</a> brought two different tyre compounds to the race; the Hard and the Extra Hard dry compound tyres.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The race marked the return to the Formula One calendar for the United States after being off the calendar since <a href="/wiki/1991_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="1991 Formula One season">1991</a>. The race was removed from the calendar because of low attendance and its slot was given to the <a href="/wiki/South_African_Grand_Prix" title="South African Grand Prix">South African Grand Prix</a> for the <a href="/wiki/1992_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="1992 Formula One season">1992 season</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It was reinstated in December 1998 following a seven-year campaign pursued by the <a href="/wiki/Indianapolis_Motor_Speedway" title="Indianapolis Motor Speedway">Indianapolis Motor Speedway</a> owners <a href="/wiki/Hulman_%26_Company" title="Hulman &amp; Company">Hulman and Co.</a><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> To celebrate its return to the calendar, Jaguar drivers <a href="/wiki/Eddie_Irvine" title="Eddie Irvine">Eddie Irvine</a> and <a href="/wiki/Johnny_Herbert" title="Johnny Herbert">Johnny Herbert</a> drove a <a href="/wiki/Demonstration_sport" title="Demonstration sport">demonstration lap</a> aboard a <a href="/wiki/Jaguar_R1" title="Jaguar R1">Jaguar R1</a> and a <a href="/wiki/Taxicab" title="Taxicab">Taxicab</a> around <a href="/wiki/Broadway_(Manhattan)" title="Broadway (Manhattan)">Broadway</a> in <a href="/wiki/New_York_City" title="New York City">New York City</a> on the Tuesday afternoon before the race.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Going into the race, McLaren driver <a href="/wiki/Mika_H%C3%A4kkinen" title="Mika Häkkinen">Mika Häkkinen</a> led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> with 80 <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Championship_points_scoring_systems" title="List of Formula One World Championship points scoring systems">points</a>, ahead of <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> on 78 points and <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> on 61 points. <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a> was fourth on 49 points with <a href="/wiki/Ralf_Schumacher" title="Ralf Schumacher">Ralf Schumacher</a> fifth on 24 points.<sup id="cite_ref-pointsprerace_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pointsprerace-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> In the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a>, McLaren were leading with 131 points ahead of their rivals Ferrari on 127 points. Williams were third with 34 points, while Benetton on 20 and Jordan with 17 points contended for fourth place.<sup id="cite_ref-pointsprerace_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pointsprerace-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> McLaren and Ferrari had so far dominated the championship, winning the previous fourteen races. Championship competitors Barrichello and <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a> had gained second-place finishes while Ralf Schumacher and <a href="/wiki/Heinz-Harald_Frentzen" title="Heinz-Harald Frentzen">Heinz-Harald Frentzen</a> had each achieved third place podium finishes.<sup id="cite_ref-pointsprerace_6-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pointsprerace-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Following the <a href="/wiki/2000_Italian_Grand_Prix" title="2000 Italian Grand Prix">Italian Grand Prix</a> on 10 September, the teams conducted testing sessions at various circuits across Europe between 11 and 15 September. Ferrari, McLaren and Sauber opted to test at the <a href="/wiki/Mugello_Circuit" title="Mugello Circuit">Mugello Circuit</a>. McLaren test driver <a href="/wiki/Olivier_Panis" title="Olivier Panis">Olivier Panis</a> was fastest on the first day, ahead of Sauber's <a href="/wiki/Pedro_Diniz" title="Pedro Diniz">Pedro Diniz</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-firstdaytests_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-firstdaytests-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Panis remained fastest on the second and final days. The Williams and Benetton teams chose to test at the <a href="/wiki/Aut%C3%B3dromo_do_Estoril" class="mw-redirect" title="Autódromo do Estoril">Autódromo do Estoril</a> circuit where Ralf Schumacher set the first day's fastest times.<sup id="cite_ref-firstdaytests_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-firstdaytests-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Jaguar and BAR performed their testing at the <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone Circuit</a> where Jaguar's test driver <a href="/wiki/Luciano_Burti" title="Luciano Burti">Luciano Burti</a> was fastest on the first day.<sup id="cite_ref-firstdaytests_7-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-firstdaytests-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The circuit received a mixed response. Willams driver <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> described the track as a "great circuit".<sup id="cite_ref-trackreview_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-trackreview-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> However, BAR's <a href="/wiki/Jacques_Villeneuve" title="Jacques Villeneuve">Jacques Villeneuve</a> was vocally critical of the track claiming that it would be "very difficult to get a good lap" and he also found the infield section "very slow".<sup id="cite_ref-trackreview_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-trackreview-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Mika_Salo" title="Mika Salo">Mika Salo</a> of Sauber stated that he disliked the first three hairpins although he was satisfied with the rest of the circuit.<sup id="cite_ref-trackreview_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-trackreview-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2000_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Four practice sessions were held before the Sunday race—two on Friday, and two on Saturday. The Friday morning and afternoon sessions each lasted an hour. The third and final practice sessions were held on Saturday morning and lasted 45 minutes.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The Friday morning and afternoon practice sessions took place in warm and dry conditions.<sup id="cite_ref-fridaypractice_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fridaypractice-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Michael Schumacher was fastest in the first practice session, with a time of 1 minute and 14.927 seconds; Barrichello had the second fastest time. Häkkinen set the third fastest time although his car's gearbox was changed. Button was fourth fastest, ahead of Villeneuve and <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Wurz" title="Alexander Wurz">Alexander Wurz</a>. The two Jordan drivers were seventh and eighth (with Trulli ahead of Frentzen). Irvine and <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> rounded out the top ten fastest drivers.<sup id="cite_ref-fridaypractice_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fridaypractice-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> A electrical failure prevented Coulthard from participating as he was pushed onto the circuit by <a href="/wiki/Motorsport_marshal" title="Motorsport marshal">marshals</a> and Fisichella removed his Benetton's left front wheel in a crash at turn 11.<sup id="cite_ref-fridaypractice_10-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fridaypractice-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> In the second practice session, Coulthard set the fastest lap time of the day, a 1:14.561, one tenth of a second quicker than teammate Häkkinen.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Michael Schumacher and Barrichello slipped to third and fourth fastest respectively although both drivers stated that they were pleased with their pace. Frentzen and Trulli set the fifth and seventh fastest times respectively; they were separated by Ralf Schumacher. Button, <a href="/wiki/Marc_Gen%C3%A9" title="Marc Gené">Marc Gené</a> and Villeneuve completed the top ten fastest drivers in the session. A crash by Herbert at turn 1 damaged his car's rear wheel and ended his session early.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Saturday morning sessions were held on a wet track from overnight rain, resulting in low levels of grip and some drivers went off the track during the session.<sup id="cite_ref-practice3_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-practice3-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Häkkinen set the third session's fastest lap, a 1:15.802; Coulthard ended with the third fastest lap. Button was running quicker and set the second fastest time. Both Ferrari drivers were fourth and fifth–Barrichello quicker than Michael Schumacher. Fisichella, Frentzen, Ralf Schumacher, Verstappen and Diniz followed in the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-practice3_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-practice3-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> In the final practice session, Both Ferraris continued their good pace; Michael Schumacher was the quickest driver with a lap of 1:14.804. Barrichello had the second fastest time. The McLaren drivers remained quick—Coulthard in third and Häkkinen fifth—they were separated by Button. Frentzen was sixth quickest and found it difficult to adjust to the changeable conditions. Fisichella, Villeneuve, Ralf Schumacher and Trulli completed the top ten fastest drivers ahead of qualifying.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2000_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="quotebox pullquote floatright" style="width:33%;&#32;;"><style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r887775652">.mw-parser-output .quotebox{background-color:#F9F9F9;border:1px solid #aaa;box-sizing:border-box;padding:10px;font-size:88%;max-width:100%}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatleft{margin:0.5em 1.4em 0.8em 0}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatright{margin:0.5em 0 0.8em 1.4em}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.centered{margin:0.5em auto 0.8em auto}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatleft p,.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatright p{font-style:inherit}.mw-parser-output .quotebox-title{background-color:#F9F9F9;text-align:center;font-size:larger;font-weight:bold}.mw-parser-output .quotebox-quote.quoted:before{font-family:"Times New Roman",serif;font-weight:bold;font-size:large;color:gray;content:" “ ";vertical-align:-45%;line-height:0}.mw-parser-output .quotebox-quote.quoted:after{font-family:"Times New Roman",serif;font-weight:bold;font-size:large;color:gray;content:" ” ";line-height:0}.mw-parser-output .quotebox .left-aligned{text-align:left}.mw-parser-output .quotebox .right-aligned{text-align:right}.mw-parser-output .quotebox .center-aligned{text-align:center}.mw-parser-output .quotebox cite{display:block;font-style:normal}@media screen and (max-width:360px){.mw-parser-output .quotebox{min-width:100%;margin:0 0 0.8em!important;float:none!important}}</style>
<div class="quotebox-quote left-aligned" style="">"I was hoping it was not going to rain at the start of the session when I stayed in the garage. I was fairly confident it would not, but I saw a few drops on my visor and pushed harder to finish the lap. We played team tactics, just like the others did at the end, except that we did it differently so that we both got a run. We changed the car a little bit during the session, because as the morning had not been completely dry we still had a few things we wanted to try. We are well prepared for the race."</div>
<p><cite class="left-aligned" style=""><a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a>, speaking after the qualifying session.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup></cite>
</p>
</div>
<p>Saturday's afternoon one hour qualifying session saw each driver limited to twelve laps, with the starting order decided by their fastest laps. During this session, the <a href="/wiki/107%25_rule" title="107% rule">107% rule</a> was in effect, which necessitated each driver set a time within 107 per cent of the quickest lap to qualify for the race.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The session was held in cloudy weather with occasional rain showers.<sup id="cite_ref-racetemp_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-racetemp-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> Michael Schumacher clinched his seventh <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> for the season, his first at the circuit, with a time of 1:14.266. He was joined on the front row of the <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#G" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">grid</a> by Coulthard, who was one-tenth of a second slower and was helped by running in the <a href="/wiki/Drafting_(aerodynamics)" title="Drafting (aerodynamics)">slipstream</a> of teammate Häkkinen on the main straight. Häkkinen qualified third and stated that he was not unhappy with his starting position. Barrichello qualified fourth, three-tenths of a second behind Michael Schumacher, and said he had more grip in his car to improve his time. Trulli qualified fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-fiaquali_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiaquali-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Button took sixth and was happy with his effort despite encountering a <a href="/wiki/Racing_flags#Yellow_flag" title="Racing flags">yellow flag</a> on his third run and admitted to pushing too hard on his fourth attempt.<sup id="cite_ref-af1quali_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-af1quali-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Frentzen slid onto the grass during the session and managed to record the seventh quickest time.<sup id="cite_ref-af1quali_18-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-af1quali-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Villeneuve who secured eighth believed he could have set a faster time as he spun while on a quick lap.<sup id="cite_ref-fiaquali_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiaquali-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Diniz took ninth having removed some downforce which improved the feel of his car.<sup id="cite_ref-fiaquali_17-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiaquali-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Ralf Schumacher rounded out the top ten having suffered his spin on his fourth run.<sup id="cite_ref-af1quali_18-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-af1quali-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Wurz qualified eleventh and said that he was happy with his car. <a href="/wiki/Ricardo_Zonta" title="Ricardo Zonta">Ricardo Zonta</a> qualified twelfth, blaming his car for his qualifying performance. He was ahead of Verstappen and Salo.<sup id="cite_ref-af1quali_18-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-af1quali-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Fisichella, who started 15th, stated that understeer and poor grip prevented him from setting a faster lap time. He managed to qualify ahead of Heidfeld and Irvine,<sup id="cite_ref-fiaquali_17-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiaquali-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> who spun during the session.<sup id="cite_ref-af1quali_18-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-af1quali-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Arrows driver <a href="/wiki/Pedro_de_la_Rosa" title="Pedro de la Rosa">Pedro de la Rosa</a>, Herbert and Alesi filled the next three positions; the latter had a lack of running due to a pneumatic valve system failure in his engine.<sup id="cite_ref-af1quali_18-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-af1quali-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> The two Minardi drivers qualified at the rear of the grid; <a href="/wiki/Gast%C3%B3n_Mazzacane" title="Gastón Mazzacane">Gastón Mazzacane</a> in front of Gené.<sup id="cite_ref-af1quali_18-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-af1quali-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Warm-up">Warm-up</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2000_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Warm-up">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The drivers took to the track at 09:30 <a href="/wiki/Eastern_Standard_Time_(North_America)" class="mw-redirect" title="Eastern Standard Time (North America)">Eastern Standard Time</a> (<a href="/wiki/Coordinated_Universal_Time" title="Coordinated Universal Time">UTC</a>-5) for a 30-minute warm-up session.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> It took place in wet conditions, where grip was poor and many drivers were forced onto the grass after sliding off the track. Both McLarens maintained their good performance from qualifying; Coulthard had the fastest time, a 1:23.14. Häkkinen was second in the other McLaren car. Michael Schumacher was third fastest and Villeneuve rounded out the top four, eight-tenths of a second behind Coulthard.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> After the warm-up session, but before the race, the <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_l%27Automobile" title="Fédération Internationale de l&#39;Automobile">Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile</a> (FIA) safety delegate, <a href="/wiki/Charlie_Whiting" title="Charlie Whiting">Charlie Whiting</a>, announced that he had moved the front row of the grid back eight meters after concerns were raised by Coulthard and Michael Schumacher who had experienced wheel spin when crossing the "Yard of Bricks". Whiting also confirmed that the pit lane speed limit zone would be advanced deeper into turn 13.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2000_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Ims.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/75/Ims.jpg/220px-Ims.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="146" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/75/Ims.jpg/330px-Ims.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/75/Ims.jpg/440px-Ims.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3455" data-file-height="2291" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Ims.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The start of the Grand Prix.</div></div></div>
<p>The race started at 14:00 local time.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_9-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The conditions on the grid were dry and damp before the race; the air temperature was 12&#160;°C (54&#160;°F) and the track temperature 14&#160;°C (57&#160;°F).<sup id="cite_ref-racetemp_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-racetemp-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> Approximately 250,000 people attended the race, breaking the record attendance figure set at the <a href="/wiki/1995_Australian_Grand_Prix" title="1995 Australian Grand Prix">1995 Australian Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> Every driver except for Herbert started on the intermediate compound tyre.<sup id="cite_ref-fiarace_23-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiarace-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Coulthard, who started alongside Michael Schumacher, jumped the start and moved into the lead. Coulthard was later issued with a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_regulations#Penalties" title="Formula One regulations">ten-second stop-go penalty</a>. Going into the first corner, Häkkinen withstood Barrichello attempts to pass him for third place. Herbert made a poor start, dropping from 19th to 22nd at the end of the first lap. Frentzen lost seventh place mid-lap after running wide, losing two positions to Villeneuve and Ralf Schumacher.<sup id="cite_ref-fiarace_23-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiarace-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> At the end of the first lap, Coulthard led from Michael Schumacher, Häkkinen, Barrichello, Trulli, Button, Villeneuve, Ralf Schumacher, Frentzen, Diniz, Zonta, Wurz, Irvine, Verstappen, Heidfeld, Salo, de la Rosa, Alesi, Fisichella, Gene, Mazzacane and Herbert.<sup id="cite_ref-lapbylap_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lapbylap-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> Coulthard had built a 1.7-second lead over Michael Schumacher by the start of lap two. On the back straight, Button attempted to overtake Trulli for fifth, but the two drivers collided. Both drivers made pit stops for repairs and emerged at the back of the field. Frentzen passed Ralf Schumacher for sixth position, while Zonta overtook Diniz for eighth and Verstappen moved up two positions to tenth after overtaking Irvine and Wurz.<sup id="cite_ref-fiarace_23-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiarace-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Heidfeld was overtaken by de la Rosa for 13th on lap three, while Gené passed Fisichella for 17th position. On the next lap, Coulthard's lead to Michael Schumacher was reduced by one second. de la Rosa continued moving up the field by passing Wurz for twelfth. On the same lap, Alesi became the first driver to pit for dry tyres. Michael Schumacher began attacking Coulthard for the lead on lap five with the McLaren driver moving to defend his position. On lap six, Barrichello became the first of the frontrunners to pit, while Irvine lost eleventh position to de la Rosa. Häkkinen led several cars in the pit lane to change to dry tyres on lap seven, while Ralf Schumacher, Zonta, Verstappen, Irvine and Wurz. Coulthard took his penalty on lap eight and dropped from second to sixth.<sup id="cite_ref-fiarace_23-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiarace-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-lapbylap_24-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lapbylap-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> Fisichella made a pit stop to serve his identical penalty on the same lap, from twenty-second position and remained in that position.<sup id="cite_ref-lapbylap_24-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lapbylap-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> Michael Schumacher, Frentzen, Diniz and Mazzacane were the only drivers not to change tyres by lap 9,<sup id="cite_ref-lapbylap_24-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lapbylap-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> while Coulthard made a pit stop and rejoined in 16th.<sup id="cite_ref-fiarace_23-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiarace-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Over the next three laps, de la Rosa and Zonta traded twelfth position, while Ralf Schumacher passed Barrichello for seventh. Diniz, who was running third, made a pit stop on lap twelve and emerged in sixth. Both Williams drivers began trading fastest laps on the same lap. During lap 13, Mazzacane began resisting Häkkinen's attempts to overtake him and Ralf Schumacher passed Diniz for fifth. Trulli became the first retirement of the race on the same lap after making a pit stop. Frentzen and Mazzacane made their pit stops on lap 14, allowing Häkkinen into second. Button retired two laps later due to mechanical issues.<sup id="cite_ref-fiarace_23-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiarace-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Michael Schumacher made a pit stop on lap 16 and emerged ten seconds ahead of Häkkinen in the lead. Häkkinen began gradually reducing Michael Schumacher's lead.<sup id="cite_ref-lapbylap_24-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lapbylap-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> Salo spun off on lap 19 and became the race's third retirement. Seven laps later, Häkkinen had reduced Michael Schumacher's lead to four seconds before the McLaren driver suffered an engine failure. Herbert made a pit stop for a new nose cone on lap 27 and came out in 17th. Coulthard passed Irvine for ninth one lap later. Verstappen suffered from an brake failure on lap 35 and retired.<sup id="cite_ref-fiarace_23-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiarace-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Usgp_2000_ims_finish_line.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9a/Usgp_2000_ims_finish_line.jpg/220px-Usgp_2000_ims_finish_line.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9a/Usgp_2000_ims_finish_line.jpg/330px-Usgp_2000_ims_finish_line.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9a/Usgp_2000_ims_finish_line.jpg/440px-Usgp_2000_ims_finish_line.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3288" data-file-height="2192" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Usgp_2000_ims_finish_line.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> celebrates his second consecutive race victory.</div></div></div>
<p>Coulthard continued to gain positions by passing de la Rosa for seventh on lap 37. Ralf Schumacher became the first driver to make a scheduled pit stop two laps later.<sup id="cite_ref-fiarace_23-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiarace-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> On lap 40, Coulthard set the fastest lap of the race, a 1:14.710,<sup id="cite_ref-lapbylap_24-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lapbylap-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> while moved into fifth after overtaking Diniz and made his pit stop one lap later, rejoining in eleventh. On the next lap, Ralf Schumacher made another pit stop and remerged in 15th. de la Rosa retired with an gearbox failure on lap 46, while Fisichella went out one lap later with engine problems. Race leader Michael Schumacher made a pit stop on lap 49 and retained a ten-second lead over Frentzen.<sup id="cite_ref-fiarace_23-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiarace-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Mazzacane made a pit stop on the same lap and missed his stopping position striking one of his team's mechanics. Barrichello became the final driver to make a scheduled pit stop on lap 53.<sup id="cite_ref-lapbylap_24-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lapbylap-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> By the end of lap 55, with the scheduled pit stops completed, the order was Michael Schumacher, Barrichello, Frentzen, Villeneuve, Coulthard, Diniz, Zonta, Irvine, Alesi, Heidfeld, Wurz, Herbert, Gene, Ralf Schumacher and Mazzacane.<sup id="cite_ref-lapbylap_24-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lapbylap-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> Diniz made two more pit stops on laps 58 and 59 and dropped to tenth.<sup id="cite_ref-lapbylap_24-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lapbylap-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>At the start of lap 65, Villeneuve attempted to pass Frentzen for the third position heading into turn one but overshot the manoeuvre. Alesi became the race's final retirement when he retired with an engine failure on the same lap. Four laps later, Michael Schumacher spun off after putting a wheel onto the grass but retained the lead.<sup id="cite_ref-fiarace_23-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiarace-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> He crossed the finish line on lap 73 to win his seventh win of the season in a time of 1'36:30.883 at an average speed of 190.240 kilometres per hour (118.210&#160;mph). Barrichello finished second 12.1 seconds behind, ahead of Frentzen in third, Villeneuve in fourth, Coulthard in fifth and Zonta rounding out the points scoring positions in sixth. Irvine in seventh was the last driver on the lead lap, with Diniz, Heidfeld, Wurz, Herbert, and Gené the last of the classified runners, albeit one lap behind.<sup id="cite_ref-results_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-results-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2000_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quotebox pullquote floatright" style="width:33%;&#32;;"><link rel="mw-deduplicated-inline-style" href="mw-data:TemplateStyles:r887775652"/>
<div class="quotebox-quote left-aligned" style="">"This is really, really nice! It is the first time we have come here and I did not expect such a reception from the American fans. Another one-two for Ferrari is just the best. After the start, we knew that Coulthard might have a penalty, but I had to pass him as he was slowing me. Of course that was done so that Mika could catch me, which is fair enough as they are team mates. I think he tried a bit too hard to stop me and we touched. In the early stages I was concentrating on saving my tyres. I was in front when Mika went out and I believe I could have stayed there if he had continued. We beat them fair and square."</div>
<p><cite class="left-aligned" style="">Michael Schumacher, speaking after the race.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup></cite>
</p>
</div>
<p>The top three drivers appeared on the podium to collect their trophies and in the subsequent <a href="/wiki/News_conference" class="mw-redirect" title="News conference">press conference</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_9-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Michael Schumacher explained that his late race spin was due to a lack of concentration after his team told him to reduce his pace. Nevertheless, he was happy with his victory and stated that his team would be concentrated on winning the Drivers' and Constructors' championships.<sup id="cite_ref-pressconf_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pressconf-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Barrichello said that the wet-weather tyres allowed him to lap two seconds faster than on dry tyres. He also added that although his advantage lay on the wet track, he had struggled with excessive oversteer and maintaining his car on a heavy fuel load after taking his pitstop.<sup id="cite_ref-pressconf_27-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pressconf-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> During the post-race interview Frentzen described his race as "hard" due to the amount of time spent overtaking other drivers.<sup id="cite_ref-pressconf_27-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pressconf-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> He also praised his team for his result after a period of poor results.<sup id="cite_ref-pressconf_27-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pressconf-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Villeneuve expressed his disappointment at missing out on a third-place finish which would have been his team's first podium position: "Today was a lot of fun and we were really quick in the race, but it's frustrating to get so close to a podium. I made a couple of mistakes, but it probably wouldn't have made a difference in the end."<sup id="cite_ref-barace_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-barace-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Coulthard said that he felt disappointed over his jump-start for which he apologised.<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> Zonta scored for the second consecutive race, having finished in sixth in the previous race in <a href="/wiki/2000_Italian_Grand_Prix" title="2000 Italian Grand Prix">Italy</a>. He said that he was pleased with the result, and that his rivals were quicker in the early stages of the race due to fuel loads but said that he managed to keep up with them.<sup id="cite_ref-barace_28-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-barace-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the race, Michael Schumacher accused Coulthard of "unsporting behaviour" in the battle for the race lead. The accusation arose from Coulthard's minor collision with the Ferrari driver. Michael Schumacher believed that Coulthard was driving dangerously: "He [Coulthard] ran in a way that he thought it [touching] could happen, and he didn't try to avoid that, that's pretty simple. As he is not fighting for the championship.....I just want to make sure that we don't see teammates helping the drivers fighting for the championship in the way which is not appropriate."<sup id="cite_ref-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Coulthard denied any wrongdoing: "I could have easily pushed him off the circuit. But I didn't and I won't in the future because it is not sporting and it is not the way I do things."<sup id="cite_ref-31" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> There was similar ill-feeling between Trulli and Button after their collision on lap two. Trulli called the Williams driver an "idiot" and accused him of "driving like a crazy".<sup id="cite_ref-trullibutton_32-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-trullibutton-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Button defended his actions and suggested that Trulli had moved slightly towards him although he believed he took the right line. He also believed Trulli moved towards him under braking.<sup id="cite_ref-trullibutton_32-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-trullibutton-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Fifteen out of twenty <a href="/wiki/Indianapolis_500" title="Indianapolis 500">Indianapolis 500</a> winners praised the inaugural running of the United States Grand Prix at the Indianapolis Motor Speedway. 1985 Indianapolis 500 winner <a href="/wiki/Danny_Sullivan" title="Danny Sullivan">Danny Sullivan</a> heralded the race organisers for their efforts, "Tony George has done a phenomenal job of integrating it into (the schedule) and not losing the integrity of the current Indianapolis 500 track. I think this shows this is the place it (Formula One) should be."<sup id="cite_ref-indywinners_33-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-indywinners-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> 1993 winner and double Formula One World Champion <a href="/wiki/Emerson_Fittipaldi" title="Emerson Fittipaldi">Emerson Fittipaldi</a> believed that it would create a new era of Formula One motor racing in the United States.<sup id="cite_ref-indywinners_33-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-indywinners-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> Fittipaldi's view was backed up by 1998 winner <a href="/wiki/Eddie_Cheever" title="Eddie Cheever">Eddie Cheever</a> who thought the event would inspire more Americans to move into Formula One.<sup id="cite_ref-34" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> The Grand Prix was awarded the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_Promotional_Trophy_winners" title="List of Formula One Promotional Trophy winners">Race Promoters' Trophy</a> at the FIA Prize Giving Awards in <a href="/wiki/Monte_Carlo" title="Monte Carlo">Monte Carlo</a> in December 2000.<sup id="cite_ref-35" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As a consequence of the race, Michael Schumacher regained the lead in the Drivers' Championship, on 88 points, taking the Championship lead for the second time in the 2000 season. Häkkinen, who failed to score points, lost the lead in the Drivers' Championship, falling eight points behind Michael Schumacher. Coulthard, who finished fifth, remained third, twenty-five points behind Michael Schumacher which ruled out any chance of Coulthard claiming the Drivers' Championship.<sup id="cite_ref-review_36-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-review-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> The poor results of the McLaren team, combined with the Ferraris clinching a one-two finish, meant that Ferrari retook the lead of the Constructors' Championship, eight points in front of McLaren.<sup id="cite_ref-review_36-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-review-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
