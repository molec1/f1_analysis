<p>The <b>1994 British Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Motor_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Motor race">motor race</a> held at <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone</a> on 10 July 1994. It was the eighth race of the <a href="/wiki/1994_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="1994 Formula One World Championship">1994 Formula One World Championship</a>.
</p><p>The 60-lap race was won from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> by local driver <a href="/wiki/Damon_Hill" title="Damon Hill">Damon Hill</a>, driving a <a href="/wiki/Williams_FW16" title="Williams FW16">Williams</a>-<a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a>. Drivers' Championship leader <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> crossed the line second in his <a href="/wiki/Benetton_B194" title="Benetton B194">Benetton</a>-<a href="/wiki/Ford_Motor_Company" title="Ford Motor Company">Ford</a>, but was subsequently disqualified for overtaking Hill on the formation lap and failing to serve a stop-go penalty in time; he was also given a two-race ban. <a href="/wiki/Jean_Alesi" title="Jean Alesi">Jean Alesi</a> was thus classified second in his <a href="/wiki/Ferrari_412_T1" title="Ferrari 412 T1">Ferrari</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Mika_H%C3%A4kkinen" title="Mika Häkkinen">Mika Häkkinen</a> third in a <a href="/wiki/McLaren_MP4/9" title="McLaren MP4/9">McLaren</a>-<a href="/wiki/Peugeot" title="Peugeot">Peugeot</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1994_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Pre-Race">Pre-Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1994_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Pre-Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Following his appearance at the previous round in <a href="/wiki/1994_French_Grand_Prix" title="1994 French Grand Prix">France</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Mansell" title="Nigel Mansell">Nigel Mansell</a> had returned to his CART commitments in America so <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> returned in the second <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a> to partner <a href="/wiki/Damon_Hill" title="Damon Hill">Damon Hill</a>. <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> led the World Drivers Championship by 37 points with 66 out of a possible 70, with <a href="/wiki/Damon_Hill" title="Damon Hill">Damon Hill</a> in second position on 29 points.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1994_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Qualifying was extremely close with Hill, Schumacher and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Gerhard_Berger" title="Gerhard Berger">Gerhard Berger</a> in competition for pole position. Berger collided with the barrier at the end of the pitlane as he attempted to have another lap. Hill took pole position with a time of 1:24.960. Schumacher took second position on the grid, three thousandths of a second slower than Hill and Berger third, two hundredths of a second behind Hill.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1994_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>On the formation lap, Schumacher overtook Hill twice (once when leaving the dummy grid, and then once more further round the lap), before dropping back to take his second place on the grid for the start.
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Hakkinen_Silverstone1994.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/14/Hakkinen_Silverstone1994.jpg/220px-Hakkinen_Silverstone1994.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="179" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/14/Hakkinen_Silverstone1994.jpg/330px-Hakkinen_Silverstone1994.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/1/14/Hakkinen_Silverstone1994.jpg 2x" data-file-width="423" data-file-height="345" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Hakkinen_Silverstone1994.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Mika_H%C3%A4kkinen" title="Mika Häkkinen">Mika Häkkinen</a> finished third in a <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>-<a href="/wiki/Peugeot" title="Peugeot">Peugeot</a>.</div></div></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> stalled on the grid at the start, forcing him to start from the back of the grid – he fought back to finish 5th. This prompted another formation lap, on which <a href="/wiki/Eddie_Irvine" title="Eddie Irvine">Eddie Irvine</a>'s car broke down. Again on this second formation lap, Schumacher overtook Hill twice. The <a href="/wiki/Peugeot" title="Peugeot">Peugeot</a> <a href="/wiki/V10_engine" title="V10 engine">V10 engine</a> in <a href="/wiki/Martin_Brundle" title="Martin Brundle">Martin Brundle</a>'s <a href="/wiki/McLaren_MP4/9" title="McLaren MP4/9">McLaren MP4/9</a> failed on the second start in a cloud of smoke.
</p><p>On lap 14, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> was handed a five-second stop-go penalty for overtaking Hill on the first formation lap. He failed to serve the penalty by lap 21, and as a result was shown the black flag twice, requiring him to stop immediately at the pits. Schumacher did not acknowledge the black flag, and later claimed that he had not seen it. Benetton told the race officials that there had been a misunderstanding over the 5-second stop-go penalty, and after discussing the issue with the team the officials withdrew the black flag and Schumacher finally served the stop-go penalty at the end of lap 27.<sup id="cite_ref-matchett_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-matchett-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Mika_H%C3%A4kkinen" title="Mika Häkkinen">Mika Häkkinen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a> collided while battling for 3rd place at the final corner. Barrichello pulled his damaged car into the pits, without realising that he was on the final lap. This allowed Häkkinen to limp over the finish line before Barrichello reached it, despite his car being more severely damaged.
</p><p>Behind them, <a href="/wiki/Ukyo_Katayama" title="Ukyo Katayama">Ukyo Katayama</a> scored what proved to be his final point.
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:252px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Coulthard_Williams.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/de/Coulthard_Williams.jpg/250px-Coulthard_Williams.jpg" decoding="async" width="250" height="94" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/de/Coulthard_Williams.jpg/375px-Coulthard_Williams.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/de/Coulthard_Williams.jpg/500px-Coulthard_Williams.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1280" data-file-height="479" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Coulthard_Williams.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a>, in only his third Grand Prix, finished fifth in a <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a>-<a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a>.</div></div></div>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-Race">Post-Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1994_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Post-Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The stewards fined Benetton $25,000 and gave the team and their driver Michael Schumacher a severe reprimand for ignoring Schumacher's five-second stop-go penalty and the subsequent black flag. On 26 July, the FIA World Motorsport Council increased the penalty to a $500,000 fine for the team and a two race ban for Schumacher. The WMSC also disqualified Schumacher from his second place at the British Grand Prix.  The penalty was upheld on appeal on 30 August.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
