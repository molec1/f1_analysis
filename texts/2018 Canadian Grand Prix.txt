<p>The <b>2018 Canadian Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 Grand Prix Heineken du Canada 2018</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> is a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> that took place on 10 June 2018 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_Gilles_Villeneuve" title="Circuit Gilles Villeneuve">Circuit Gilles Villeneuve</a> in <a href="/wiki/Montreal" title="Montreal">Montreal, Quebec</a>, Canada.<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The race was the 7th round of the <a href="/wiki/2018_FIA_Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="2018 FIA Formula One World Championship">2018 FIA Formula One World Championship</a>. The race was won by <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> of <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>. It was Vettel's 50th career win, becoming the fourth driver in Formula One history to achieve such a feat (joining Michael Schumacher, Lewis Hamilton and <a href="/wiki/Alain_Prost" title="Alain Prost">Alain Prost</a>). It was the 55th running of the <a href="/wiki/Canadian_Grand_Prix" title="Canadian Grand Prix">Canadian Grand Prix</a>, the 49th time the event had been included as a round of the <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="Formula One World Championship">Formula One World Championship</a> since the inception of the series in <a href="/wiki/1950_Formula_One_season" title="1950 Formula One season">1950</a>, and the 39th time that a World Championship was held at Circuit Gilles Villeneuve. <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> also celebrated his 300th Grand Prix entry at this race. This race was Ferrari's first win in Canada since <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> won here in 2004.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> went in to the race as the defending race winner.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> He entered the round with a 14-point lead over <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> in the World Drivers' Championship. In the World Constructors' Championship, Mercedes held a 17-point lead over <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Tyres">Tyres</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Tyres">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>The tyre compounds provided for this race were the hypersoft, ultrasoft  and supersoft.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> was fastest across all three practice sessions, improving his time in each successive session.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> In FP2, Grosjean hit a groundhog that was on the track, damaging his front wing, and reducing the Haas team's spare parts.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a>'s car had a major engine failure as it left the pitlane in Q1 meaning he failed to set a lap time, and raced at the stewards' discretion. In Q2, when <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> was completing his final flying lap, he aborted the lap because <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> and a number of other cars were driving slowly along the back straight, leading him to pull into the pits. In Q3, Vettel took pole with a new track record however his teammate made a mistake in Turn 2, meaning he would qualify in P5.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The grid got away largely without incident from the line, but in turn 5, <a href="/wiki/Brendon_Hartley" title="Brendon Hartley">Brendon Hartley</a> and local driver <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a> collided, forcing their immediate retirement and the deployment of the safety car. At the restart, <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> touched Sainz, forcing Pérez off the track, losing positions. On Lap 18, <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> overcut Lewis Hamilton for the fourth position. On lap 46, Fernando Alonso retired from the race with an exhaust issue.
</p><p>Race officials erroneously directed model <a href="/wiki/Winnie_Harlow" title="Winnie Harlow">Winnie Harlow</a> to wave the chequered flag before race leader Vettel completed lap 69 (the scheduled penultimate lap) and therefore the results were taken from lap 68, according to the Formula 1 sporting regulations article 43.2.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> This meant that Ricciardo's successively faster fastest laps on laps 69 and 70 were voided, and <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a>'s overtake on <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a> for 13th did not stand.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
