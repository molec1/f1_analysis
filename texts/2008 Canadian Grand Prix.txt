<p>The <b>2008 Canadian Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>XLV Grand Prix du Canada</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 8 June 2008 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_Gilles_Villeneuve" title="Circuit Gilles Villeneuve">Circuit Gilles Villeneuve</a>, <a href="/wiki/Montreal" title="Montreal">Montreal</a>, <a href="/wiki/Quebec" title="Quebec">Quebec</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>. It was the 7th&#160;race of the <a href="/wiki/2008_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2008 Formula One season">2008 Formula One season</a>. The 70-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> for the <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a> team after starting from second position. <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> finished second in a BMW Sauber car, with <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> third in a <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>, who started from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>, failed to finish the race, retiring on lap&#160;19 after crashing into the back of <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> car in the <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit lane</a>.
</p><p>Kubica's win promoted him into the lead of the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> for the first time in his career, overtaking Hamilton, Räikkönen and Massa. Massa and Hamilton tied for second place, four points behind Kubica, while Räikkönen was fourth. In the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors Championship</a>, BMW passed <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> for second position, three points behind <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Grand Prix was contested by 20&#160;drivers, in ten teams of two.<sup id="cite_ref-classification_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-classification-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The teams, also known as "<a href="/wiki/Formula_One#Constructors" title="Formula One">constructors</a>", were <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a>-<a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_HighPerformanceEngines" class="mw-redirect" title="Mercedes-Benz HighPerformanceEngines">Mercedes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a>, <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a>, <a href="/wiki/Honda_Racing_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Honda Racing F1">Honda</a>, <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a>, <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyota</a>, <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a>, <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-classification_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-classification-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Before the race, McLaren driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a>, with 38 points, ahead of Ferrari's <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>, on 35 points and his teammate <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> on 34 points. BMW driver <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> was fourth, ahead of Kubica's teammate <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> in fifth. In the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors Championship</a>, Ferrari were leading on 69 points; 16 points ahead of McLaren with 53 points; BMW were a further point behind them in third.
</p><p>Ahead of the race, the organizers unveiled a new paddock and media center facilities at Circuit Gilles Villeneuve.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Also the site where <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> crashed in <a href="/wiki/2007_Canadian_Grand_Prix" title="2007 Canadian Grand Prix">the previous year's race</a> was modified. The wall on which Kubica crashed was moved closer to the track to decrease angle of impact. Additionally, debris fencing was put on the wall to prevent any hazard to cars on the other side of the wall.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice_and_qualifying">Practice and qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Practice and qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Felipe_Massa_2008_Canada.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9c/Felipe_Massa_2008_Canada.jpg/220px-Felipe_Massa_2008_Canada.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9c/Felipe_Massa_2008_Canada.jpg/330px-Felipe_Massa_2008_Canada.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9c/Felipe_Massa_2008_Canada.jpg/440px-Felipe_Massa_2008_Canada.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3576" data-file-height="2385" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Felipe_Massa_2008_Canada.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>, seen here correcting <a href="/wiki/Understeer_and_oversteer" title="Understeer and oversteer">oversteer</a>, set the fastest lap time in the first free practice session on Friday.</div></div></div>
<p>Three practice sessions were held before the Sunday race—two on Friday, and a third on Saturday. The Friday morning and afternoon sessions each lasted 90&#160;minutes. The third session was held on Saturday morning and lasted an hour.<sup id="cite_ref-sporting_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sporting-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
The Friday practice session started in wet conditions which resulted in most of the drivers venturing out in the latter half of the hour, some of them, such as <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>, emerging only during final minutes of the session. <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> recorded the fastest lap in this session, followed by <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> and <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> In the second session of the day, Lewis Hamilton aced while Kubica continued to record good timings at number two. <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> recorded third fastest lap time compared to his fifth fastest in the earlier session. <a href="/wiki/Timo_Glock" title="Timo Glock">Timo Glock</a> suffered a minor accident after he hit the wall on turn four.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Nico Rosberg, however, sprung a surprise by topping the Saturday session ahead of Räikkönen and Hamilton. The session was <a href="/wiki/Racing_flag#Red_flag" class="mw-redirect" title="Racing flag">red flagged</a> 45 minutes in when <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> hit the wall after losing control at turn nine, resulting in a number of drivers being unable to complete their flying laps. Further, Vettel's teammate <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A9bastien_Bourdais" title="Sébastien Bourdais">Sébastien Bourdais</a> suffered a crash at turn five.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div style="clear:both;"></div>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_rounds_turn_2_Canada_2008.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fe/Lewis_rounds_turn_2_Canada_2008.jpg/220px-Lewis_rounds_turn_2_Canada_2008.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fe/Lewis_rounds_turn_2_Canada_2008.jpg/330px-Lewis_rounds_turn_2_Canada_2008.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fe/Lewis_rounds_turn_2_Canada_2008.jpg/440px-Lewis_rounds_turn_2_Canada_2008.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3252" data-file-height="2167" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_rounds_turn_2_Canada_2008.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> took <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> for <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>Saturday afternoon's qualifying session was divided into three parts. In the first 20-minute period, cars finishing 16th or lower were eliminated. The second qualifying period lasted for 15 minutes, at the end of which the fastest ten cars went into the final period, to determine their grid positions for the race. Cars failing to make the final period were allowed to be refuelled before the race but those competing in it were not, and so carried more fuel than they had done in the earlier qualifying sessions.<sup id="cite_ref-sporting_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sporting-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Lewis Hamilton recorded the fastest time for the session, ahead of Felipe Massa and Heikki Kovalainen. Sebastian Vettel's poor season continued after he could not compete the qualifying following his crash in third practice session. Others eliminated in this session were <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A9bastien_Bourdais" title="Sébastien Bourdais">Sébastien Bourdais</a>, <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a>, <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a>. Bourdais received a five place grid penalty due to a gearbox change following the earlier practice session. Button too suffered a gearbox problem during his first lap run and clocked the slowest time for the session.
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Track_cleaning_2008_Canada.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1a/Track_cleaning_2008_Canada.jpg/220px-Track_cleaning_2008_Canada.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1a/Track_cleaning_2008_Canada.jpg/330px-Track_cleaning_2008_Canada.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1a/Track_cleaning_2008_Canada.jpg/440px-Track_cleaning_2008_Canada.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3064" data-file-height="2045" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Track_cleaning_2008_Canada.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Resurfaced sections of the track began to break up in practice and qualifying and had to be repaired on numerous occasions.</div></div></div>
<p>Qualifying conditions were hampered by a disintegrating track, which caused most drivers to record slower times than in Q1. Track officials were seen clearing the track of debris in between sessions.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyota</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a> suffered significantly from these conditions, his car spinning twice during the second session. Trulli, along with <a href="/wiki/Timo_Glock" title="Timo Glock">Timo Glock</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kazuki_Nakajima" title="Kazuki Nakajima">Kazuki Nakajima</a>, <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nelson_Piquet_Jr." title="Nelson Piquet Jr.">Nelson Piquet Jr.</a>, was unable to progress to the next session.
</p><p>Hamilton once again topped this session, with Massa and Räikkönen coming second and third respectively.
</p><p>Hamilton recorded quick times during early laps of the session, which were only surpassed by Robert Kubica towards the end of the session. But Hamilton on his final flying lap overcame Kubica's time claiming his second pole position at Montreal. Räikkönen came in third with teammate Massa pushed down to sixth position. <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> exhibited remarkable performances, taking positions four and five respectively.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a>, who also became a victim of the breaking track surface, could not compete in Q3 after damaging his car at the end of second session.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:202px;"><a href="/wiki/File:2008_Canadian_GP_lap_1_turn_2.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/59/2008_Canadian_GP_lap_1_turn_2.jpg/200px-2008_Canadian_GP_lap_1_turn_2.jpg" decoding="async" width="200" height="130" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/59/2008_Canadian_GP_lap_1_turn_2.jpg/300px-2008_Canadian_GP_lap_1_turn_2.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/59/2008_Canadian_GP_lap_1_turn_2.jpg/400px-2008_Canadian_GP_lap_1_turn_2.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2917" data-file-height="1893" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:2008_Canadian_GP_lap_1_turn_2.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> leads <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> and the rest of the field at the start of the race.</div></div></div>
<p>There was severe criticism from drivers about track conditions.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> The track was said to be breaking up on turn two, the exit of turn seven and also the apex and exit of turn 10.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> The authorities applied chemicals on turn 2 and carried out further resurfacing of track on turn 11&#160;– the hairpin&#160;– following Saturday's qualifying session.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After overnight track work to fix problems with the track at turn 10,<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> the race started with Hamilton holding his lead and all the cars making it through the first corner. Hamilton built up a lead of over 5 seconds over <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> before <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> had a gearbox failure<sup id="cite_ref-FIndia_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FIndia-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> on the 16th lap and parked his car after turn 3.<sup id="cite_ref-report1_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-report1-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> Sutil's car was off the track but after it caught fire the safety car was brought out, and there was a rush of drivers into the pits as all of the six lead drivers went in for their <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit stops</a>. Hamilton led Räikkönen and Kubica into the pitlane but Räikkönen took the lead as they left their garages, with Kubica alongside him and Hamilton behind.
</p>
<div style="clear:both;"></div>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Pitlane_collision_2008_Canada.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f3/Pitlane_collision_2008_Canada.jpg/220px-Pitlane_collision_2008_Canada.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f3/Pitlane_collision_2008_Canada.jpg/330px-Pitlane_collision_2008_Canada.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f3/Pitlane_collision_2008_Canada.jpg/440px-Pitlane_collision_2008_Canada.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1848" data-file-height="1231" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Pitlane_collision_2008_Canada.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>In the aftermath of the collision, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> and Hamilton retired from the race, whilst <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> continued with a broken front wing.</div></div></div>

<div class="quotebox-quote left-aligned" style="">"...as I exited the box, I saw two cars jostling for position ahead of me in the pit lane. Obviously, I didn't want to get involved in their tussle, and was trying not to do so, and then all of a sudden they stopped. </p>
</div>
<p>Räikkönen came to a stop at the end of the pit lane as the exit was closed, with the red indicator light on. Räikkönen was alongside Kubica at the end of the pit lane when Hamilton sped towards them not noticing the red light at first.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton slid into the back of Räikkönen's car, with Nico Rosberg further hitting the back of Hamilton. Both Hamilton and Räikkönen retired from the race, each leaving their vehicles at the exit of the pitlane. Rosberg continued the race, but stopped again for a new wing. Meanwhile, Massa had to make a second stop in the following lap, as his car had not been refuelled during his first stop due to technical problems.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton later said that he saw the light too late and could not avoid hitting the Ferrari.<sup id="cite_ref-Penalty_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Penalty-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> It was Hamilton's only retirement of the 2008 season.<sup id="cite_ref-williams_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-williams-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>There were seven different race leaders over the next section of the race until most of the drivers each took pit stops leaving Heidfeld in the lead. Kubica was in a much lighter and faster car and overtook Heidfeld after only his first lap out of the pits.
</p>
<div style="clear:both;"></div>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:252px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Kubica_%2B_Heidfeld_%2B_Alonso_2008_Canada.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/84/Kubica_%2B_Heidfeld_%2B_Alonso_2008_Canada.jpg/250px-Kubica_%2B_Heidfeld_%2B_Alonso_2008_Canada.jpg" decoding="async" width="250" height="82" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/84/Kubica_%2B_Heidfeld_%2B_Alonso_2008_Canada.jpg/375px-Kubica_%2B_Heidfeld_%2B_Alonso_2008_Canada.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/84/Kubica_%2B_Heidfeld_%2B_Alonso_2008_Canada.jpg/500px-Kubica_%2B_Heidfeld_%2B_Alonso_2008_Canada.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2238" data-file-height="732" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Kubica_%2B_Heidfeld_%2B_Alonso_2008_Canada.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Kubica passing teammate <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> for the net lead of the race after both had made their first pitstops.</div></div></div>
<p>The remainder of the race had fewer incidents, despite the surface of the circuit severely degrading over the course of the race. <a href="/wiki/Nelson_Piquet_Jr." title="Nelson Piquet Jr.">Nelson Piquet Jr.</a> suffered another retirement, his fifth of the year from seven races, on the 39th lap because of abnormal brake wear.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> Piquet had also spun off the circuit earlier in the race while running in a points-scoring position due to brake failure, but fell to the back of the field before he reversed his car to get back onto the track. Piquet's teammate at <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault F1</a> and former world champion <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> was running in a strong third place and was pushing Nick Heidfeld for second place before spinning off and damaging his front wing and suspension. <a href="/wiki/Kazuki_Nakajima" title="Kazuki Nakajima">Kazuki Nakajima</a> broke his front wing after hitting the back of <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> in turn ten on the 46th lap, and was forced to retire when the front wing detached itself completely and was stuck under the chassis as he was about to enter pitlane. Giancarlo Fisichella was running last after his car engine stalled during his first pit stop,<sup id="cite_ref-FIndia_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FIndia-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> and later he spun and hit the wall after the chicane at turn nine.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> fought back towards the end of the race, after finding himself last since making three pit stops in total due to earlier incidents. Massa overtook both Barrichello and Kovalainen at the same time through the hairpin at turn ten as his rivals struggled for grip. With a few laps to spare the Brazilian overtook <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a> when the Italian got caught up behind <a href="/wiki/Timo_Glock" title="Timo Glock">Timo Glock</a>, who was recovering from running wide at turn two.
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Robert_Kubica_2008_Canada_2.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/dc/Robert_Kubica_2008_Canada_2.jpg/220px-Robert_Kubica_2008_Canada_2.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/dc/Robert_Kubica_2008_Canada_2.jpg/330px-Robert_Kubica_2008_Canada_2.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/dc/Robert_Kubica_2008_Canada_2.jpg/440px-Robert_Kubica_2008_Canada_2.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3221" data-file-height="2147" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Robert_Kubica_2008_Canada_2.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The race was won by <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a>, who took his and <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a>'s only F1 victory.</div></div></div>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Barrichello_%2B_Vettel_%2B_Kovalainen_Canada_2008.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/83/Barrichello_%2B_Vettel_%2B_Kovalainen_Canada_2008.jpg/220px-Barrichello_%2B_Vettel_%2B_Kovalainen_Canada_2008.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="138" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/83/Barrichello_%2B_Vettel_%2B_Kovalainen_Canada_2008.jpg/330px-Barrichello_%2B_Vettel_%2B_Kovalainen_Canada_2008.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/83/Barrichello_%2B_Vettel_%2B_Kovalainen_Canada_2008.jpg/440px-Barrichello_%2B_Vettel_%2B_Kovalainen_Canada_2008.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1748" data-file-height="1098" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Barrichello_%2B_Vettel_%2B_Kovalainen_Canada_2008.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>A late-race scrap between <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> and <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> provided on-track entertainment.</div></div></div>
<p>Kubica had built up a substantial lead over the course of the race and was never threatened while he raced towards the finish. Heidfeld held onto second place leading to the first one-two finish for the BMW Sauber team. <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> finished third to score his first points of the season and his first podium finish since the <a href="/wiki/2006_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="2006 Monaco Grand Prix">2006 Monaco Grand Prix</a>, but had to run to the podium after his car ran out of fuel 50 meters from where the podium finishers park. <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyota</a> drivers finished fourth and sixth, with Massa between them in fifth. Barrichello kept the seventh position and Vettel defended eighth place from Kovalainen. Thus, no <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a> driver finished in the points for the first time since the <a href="/wiki/2006_United_States_Grand_Prix" title="2006 United States Grand Prix">2006 United States Grand Prix</a>, and neither <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> nor <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a> took a podium finish for the first time since the <a href="/wiki/2006_Malaysian_Grand_Prix" title="2006 Malaysian Grand Prix">2006 Malaysian Grand Prix</a>. Kovalainen, Rosberg, Button, Webber and the lapped Bourdais were the last of the finishing drivers.
</p><p>The win for BMW Sauber marked the first time a German constructor had won a Formula One Grand Prix since the <a href="/wiki/1962_French_Grand_Prix" title="1962 French Grand Prix">1962 French Grand Prix</a>, as well as the first and only win for <a href="/wiki/BMW_in_Formula_One" title="BMW in Formula One">BMW</a> engines since the <a href="/wiki/2004_Brazilian_Grand_Prix" title="2004 Brazilian Grand Prix">2004 Brazilian Grand Prix</a> which <a href="/wiki/Juan_Pablo_Montoya" title="Juan Pablo Montoya">Juan Pablo Montoya</a> won for <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2008_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The top three finishers appeared on the <a href="/wiki/Podium#Usage_in_motorsport" title="Podium">podium</a> and in the subsequent <a href="/wiki/Press_conference" title="Press conference">press conference</a>. Kubica was delighted with his first race victory, saying, "I'm very happy at having won the first race for the BMW Sauber F1 Team. I'm also happy for Poland and all my fans. It was a very difficult race for me. I started on the dirty side of the track and Kimi Raikkonen was nearly able to pass me, but I just managed to keep him behind."<sup id="cite_ref-GrandPrix.com_quotes_23-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GrandPrix.com_quotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> After the race, Heidfeld said,
</p>
<style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r960796168">.mw-parser-output .templatequote{overflow:hidden;margin:1em 0;padding:0 40px}.mw-parser-output .templatequote .templatequotecite{line-height:1.5em;text-align:left;padding-left:1.6em;margin-top:0}</style><blockquote class="templatequote"><p>Congratulations to Robert for winning this race, which he really deserved! I made a poor start and lost a position to Rubens Barrichello. Fortunately I was able to pass him, and from this moment on I was one of the fastest cars on the track. I stayed out when most of the other cars pitted because I was on a long stint. I was pushing very hard at that stage. We then decided to switch to a one stop strategy, with a very high fuel load and with the soft option tyres which was a challenge.<sup id="cite_ref-GrandPrix.com_quotes_23-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GrandPrix.com_quotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup></p></blockquote>
<p>Coulthard said, "I'm delighted to get a podium for the team, they've had a lot of work on at the previous races and back at base. You can expect some unusual results here, so we fuelled it long; but we expected the incidents would occur on track, not in the pit lane."<sup id="cite_ref-GrandPrix.com_quotes_23-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GrandPrix.com_quotes-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hamilton and Rosberg were both given ten place grid penalties for the French Grand Prix, meaning that whatever their qualifying position, they could start no better than 11th.<sup id="cite_ref-Penalty_20-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Penalty-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> After the penalty was given, McLaren's CEO, <a href="/wiki/Martin_Whitmarsh" title="Martin Whitmarsh">Martin Whitmarsh</a>, said that he thought the penalty was "severe", citing a similar incident at <a href="/wiki/2008_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="2008 Monaco Grand Prix">Monaco</a> where Räikkönen had crashed into the back of Force India's Adrian Sutil.<sup id="cite_ref-Pen_McL_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pen_McL-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> There, no penalty had been given.<sup id="cite_ref-Pen_McL_24-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Pen_McL-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> However, Rosberg said that the penalties were "deserved".<sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the race, Kubica moved into the lead of the Drivers' Championship, on 42 points, taking the Championship lead for the first time in his Formula One career. Hamilton lost the lead of the Drivers' Championship, falling four points behind Kubica. Massa tied on points with Hamilton, while Räikkönen was three points further behind. Heidfeld remained fifth. Before the race, Ferrari had been 16 points ahead of McLaren in the Constructors' Championship; after the race, BMW moved up into second position and reduced Ferrari's lead to 3 points. McLaren fell down to third place, 17 points behind BMW.
</p>
