<p>The <b>2019 Azerbaijan Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 SOCAR Azerbaijan Grand Prix 2019</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_racing" title="Formula One racing">Formula One motor race</a> that took place on 28 April 2019 at the <a href="/wiki/Baku_City_Circuit" title="Baku City Circuit">Baku City Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Baku" title="Baku">Baku, Azerbaijan</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The race was the 4th round of the <a href="/wiki/2019_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2019 Formula One World Championship">2019 Formula One World Championship</a>, marked the 3rd time that the <a href="/wiki/Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix" title="Azerbaijan Grand Prix">Azerbaijan Grand Prix</a> had been run as a World Championship round, and the 4th time that the Baku circuit has been part of the Formula One World Championship (the 2016 race was run as the <a href="/wiki/2016_European_Grand_Prix" title="2016 European Grand Prix">European Grand Prix</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Championship_standings_before_the_race">Championship standings before the race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Championship standings before the race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Heading into the race <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> held a 6-point advantage in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> over <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> with <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> holding a 57-point advantage over <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Entrants">Entrants</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Entrants">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">Further information: <a href="/wiki/2019_Formula_One_World_Championship#Entries" title="2019 Formula One World Championship">2019 Formula One World Championship §&#160;Entries</a></div>
<p>The drivers and teams were the same as the season entry list with no additional stand in drivers for either the race or practice.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Penalties">Penalties</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Penalties">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>In the lead up to the weekend it was announced that <a href="/wiki/Alfa_Romeo_Racing" class="mw-redirect" title="Alfa Romeo Racing">Alfa Romeo</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Antonio_Giovinazzi" title="Antonio Giovinazzi">Antonio Giovinazzi</a> would be receiving new control electronics for the Grand Prix. As this was his third set of control electronics he was given a 10 place grid penalty.<sup id="cite_ref-GIO_grid_pen_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GIO_grid_pen-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Free_practice">Free practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Free practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The first practice session ended after only 12 minutes (the session was scheduled for 90) after the Williams car driven by <a href="/wiki/George_Russell_(racing_driver)" title="George Russell (racing driver)">George Russell</a> was badly damaged when it hit a loose <a href="/wiki/Drain_cover" title="Drain cover">drain cover</a>. The cover, between turns two and three, appeared to be loosened when <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a>'s Ferrari passed over it earlier.<sup id="cite_ref-BBC_practice_1_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBC_practice_1-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> The truck towing Russell's car back to the pit lane then hit a pedestrian bridge, leaking hydraulic fluid onto the track and Russell's car. As a result of these two incidents, race director Michael Masi cancelled the session, in order to check the other manholes on the circuit and to check the damage to the pedestrian bridge. <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> determined Russell's chassis needed replacing meaning he would not be allowed to run in the second practice session.<sup id="cite_ref-BBC_practice_1_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBC_practice_1-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> The session therefore ended with only the two <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferraris</a> setting lap times with Leclerc ahead of <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The first major incident of second practice was <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a> crashing 15 minutes into the session at turn 2.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a> also crashed at turn 7 with about 25 minutes to go.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> At the end of the session <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a> was instructed visually to bring the car to be weighed, however he missed the visual cue and continued to his garage where the mechanics performed work on the car, changing the tyres in a practice pit-stop. This was against the rules and as a result Gasly was forced to start the race from the pit lane.<sup id="cite_ref-GAS_pit_start_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GAS_pit_start-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> The session ended with the Ferrari of Leclerc fastest followed by his teammate Vettel.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Third practice passed without incident with Leclerc finishing the session fastest ahead of Vettel.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The first qualifying session ended when <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a>'s Williams crashed into the wall. During the session, Pierre Gasly had set the fastest lap ahead of Charles Leclerc and Lewis Hamilton.
<a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a>, <a href="/wiki/George_Russell_(racing_driver)" title="George Russell (racing driver)">George Russell</a>, and Robert Kubica were eliminated.
In the second qualifying section, Charles Leclerc crashed exactly into the same wall Kubica had crashed earlier, ending his qualifying session. During the session, <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> had set the fastest lap, followed by <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>. <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz</a>, <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Albon" title="Alexander Albon">Alexander Albon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a> were eliminated in Q2. 
Valtteri Bottas took pole position after setting a lap time of 1:40.495, beating his teammate Lewis Hamilton.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:302px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Ilham_Aliyev_watched_the_opening_ceremony_of_the_2019_Formula-1_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix_and_final_race_19.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1b/Ilham_Aliyev_watched_the_opening_ceremony_of_the_2019_Formula-1_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix_and_final_race_19.jpg/300px-Ilham_Aliyev_watched_the_opening_ceremony_of_the_2019_Formula-1_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix_and_final_race_19.jpg" decoding="async" width="300" height="201" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1b/Ilham_Aliyev_watched_the_opening_ceremony_of_the_2019_Formula-1_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix_and_final_race_19.jpg/450px-Ilham_Aliyev_watched_the_opening_ceremony_of_the_2019_Formula-1_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix_and_final_race_19.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1b/Ilham_Aliyev_watched_the_opening_ceremony_of_the_2019_Formula-1_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix_and_final_race_19.jpg/600px-Ilham_Aliyev_watched_the_opening_ceremony_of_the_2019_Formula-1_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix_and_final_race_19.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1930" data-file-height="1292" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Ilham_Aliyev_watched_the_opening_ceremony_of_the_2019_Formula-1_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix_and_final_race_19.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The <a href="/wiki/Podium#In_motorsport" title="Podium">podium</a> ceremony</div></div></div>
<p>The opening lap occurred without any accidents or incidents. <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> initially challenged <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> for the lead taking the inside line from his teammate into turns 1 and 2. Bottas retained his lead on the outside of both corners and had extended it by the end of the lap. <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> lost 4th place to <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> whilst <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a> fell from 8th to 10th after being overtaken by <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz Jr.</a> and <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a>.
</p><p>Verstappen recovered 4th place on lap 6 and Leclerc passed Pérez to take 5th on the following lap. Leclerc then overtook Verstappen on the pit straight at the end of lap 9. <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> was the first of the front-runners to make a pit stop at the end of lap 11 entering in 3rd place and emerging in 5th. Race leader Bottas made his stop on the following lap exiting the pits in 4th place ahead of Vettel. Hamilton, who had inherited the lead, made his pit stop a lap later emerging in 4th place behind his teammate and handing the race lead to Leclerc. 
</p><p>On lap 14 <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> was issued with a drive-through penalty. The stewards found that <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> had released Kubica into the pit lane earlier than permitted at the start of the race,<sup class="noprint Inline-Template" style="margin-left:0.1em; white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Please_clarify" title="Wikipedia:Please clarify"><span title="The text near this tag may need clarification or removal of jargon. (October 2019)">clarification needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> a rule the team were not aware of. Verstappen made his pit stop from 2nd place at the end of the same lap emerging from the pits in 6th behind teammate <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a> who was yet to stop. Verstappen later passed Gasly on lap 17. On lap 25 <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> locked up his brakes at turn 15 and went up the escape road before rejoining the track dropping him from 11th down to 15th place. On lap 31 Bottas retook the lead of the race from Leclerc who was still yet to make his pit stop. 
</p><p>Shortly afterwards an incident occurred between <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a> and <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a>, who were running in 10th and 11th respectively, at turn 3. Ricciardo attempted an overtake on Kvyat on the inside of the corner but locked up his brakes sending him down the escape road. Kvyat was forced to miss the corner to avoid turning into the <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a> and came to a stop before his car hit the wall. Ricciardo then reversed into Kvyat whilst attempting to rejoin the track causing damage to both cars which was sufficient to retire them from the race. Both cars were able to return to the pits avoiding the need for a <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a>.
</p><p>By lap 34 Leclerc had been passed by Hamilton and Vettel as his tyres degraded. He made his pit stop at the end of the lap emerging in 6th place behind <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a> and almost 20 seconds behind Verstappen after having been ahead of both <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bulls</a> before Verstappen's first stop. A lap later Leclerc made it past Gasly to take 5th. The order of the top five was now Bottas, Hamilton, Vettel, Verstappen and Leclerc. Lap 39 saw both Gasly and Grosjean retire from the race with driveshaft and brake issues respectively, this marked Grosjean's third retirement in four races. Grosjean made it back to the pits but Gasly pulled into an escape road causing a brief <a href="/wiki/Virtual_safety_car" class="mw-redirect" title="Virtual safety car">virtual safety car</a> whilst his Red Bull was recovered.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> crossed the line to take his fifth career victory. <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> finished in second place securing <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a>' fourth consecutive one-two finish of the <a href="/wiki/2019_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2019 Formula One World Championship">season</a> with <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> taking third place. <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a> set the fastest lap and was awarded Driver of the Day.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> After the race Ricciardo was issued with a three-place grid penalty for the following race in <a href="/wiki/2019_Spanish_Grand_Prix" title="2019 Spanish Grand Prix">Spain</a> for causing the accident with Kvyat. Ricciardo stated that his reversing manoeuvre was "just a sense of urgency and a bit of panic" and that he was not aware of the Toro Rosso behind him. Kvyat responded by saying "These things happen, it's racing" and that he would "buy [Ricciardo] a rear view mirror for the next race!"<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
