<p>The <b>2012 Belgian Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>2012 Formula 1 Shell Belgian Grand Prix</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-Spa_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Spa-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race that took place at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps" title="Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps">Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps</a> in <a href="/wiki/Spa,_Belgium" title="Spa, Belgium">Spa</a>, <a href="/wiki/Belgium" title="Belgium">Belgium</a> on 2 September 2012,<sup id="cite_ref-7Dec_calendar_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7Dec_calendar-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> the first race after a five-week summer break. It was the twelfth race of the <a href="/wiki/2012_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2012 Formula One season">2012 season</a>, and the seventy-seventh running of the <a href="/wiki/Belgian_Grand_Prix" title="Belgian Grand Prix">Belgian Grand Prix</a>.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> started from pole position<sup id="cite_ref-BUT_KOB_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BUT_KOB-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and won the race. <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> finished second and <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> third.<sup id="cite_ref-BUT_win_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BUT_win-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>This was the 300th race for <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> led the championship by forty points from <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>, with fourth-placed <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> a further seven points behind.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Tyre supplier Pirelli brought its silver-banded hard compound tyre as the harder "prime" tyre and the white-banded medium compound tyre as the softer "option" tyre.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Dani_Clos" title="Dani Clos">Dani Clos</a> replaced <a href="/wiki/Narain_Karthikeyan" title="Narain Karthikeyan">Narain Karthikeyan</a> at <a href="/wiki/HRT_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="HRT F1">HRT</a> in the first practice session, while <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> drove <a href="/wiki/Bruno_Senna" title="Bruno Senna">Bruno Senna</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Williams_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Williams F1">Williams</a> once again.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Free_practice">Free practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Free practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The first free practice session began in wet conditions. <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a> ended the session fastest, having chosen to stay out early while other drivers pitted to begin work on their car setup. <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari's</a> <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> failed to set a lap time when his car ground to a halt as he was about to enter the pits at the end of the installation lap. The second practice session saw even more rain fall, and large puddles of standing water on the circuit made running impractical. Only a handful of drivers set lap times, with <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia's</a> <a href="/wiki/Charles_Pic" title="Charles Pic">Charles Pic</a> finishing the session fastest. The third and final session on Saturday morning was dry, allowing teams just one hour to complete as much of their testing programmes as possible ahead of qualifying and the race. <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> went on to finish the session fastest.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Accident">Accident</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Accident">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> let his clutch slip a fraction too soon and started his race before the lights had gone out, passing the Saubers. Grosjean made a good start and moved up to the inside of La Source, but in doing so, squeezed Hamilton between himself and the pitwall. The two touched wheels, both drivers losing control. Grosjean then speared into the back of Pérez and became airborne, crashing heavily into Alonso, missing Alonso's head by a few inches. Grosjean came to rest at the outside wall. Hamilton crashed into Kobayashi as well as Alonso after Grosjean's heavy impact. Pérez lost his rear wing from Grosjean's hit and touched Maldonado when the accident happened, making Maldonado spin. Only the latter two emerged from the crash, Kobayashi with a substantial hole in the side of his car. Kobayashi who had started on the front row for Sauber, pitted after the accident and resumed in last place.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="After_the_accident">After the accident</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: After the accident">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Once Maldonado recovered from his spin he hit <a href="/wiki/Timo_Glock" title="Timo Glock">Timo Glock</a> after the safety car restart. <a href="/wiki/Narain_Karthikeyan" title="Narain Karthikeyan">Narain Karthikeyan</a>'s HRT spun off backwards and hit into the tyre wall late in the race after a wheel came loose. After starting the race in 14th, Felipe Massa fought hard to get the fifth place, ahead of Red Bull’s Mark Webber. Bruno Senna had eighth position secured but due to a slow puncture he had to make a pitstop with only four laps left in the race, dropping him to 12th.
</p><p>Button, who started on pole, was never under threat as Red Bull's Sebastian Vettel fought from 10th to 2nd, ahead of Lotus's Kimi Räikkönen.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Romain Grosjean was fined €50,000 and was given a one-race ban at the <a href="/wiki/2012_Italian_Grand_Prix" title="2012 Italian Grand Prix">Italian Grand Prix</a> for his role in the crash at the start.
</p><p>Pastor Maldonado was given two five-place grid penalties at the Italian Grand Prix, the first of which was for his jump start and the second for causing an avoidable collision with Glock.
</p>
