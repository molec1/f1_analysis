<p>The <b>2014 Brazilian Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>Formula 1 Grande Prêmio Petrobras do Brasil 2014</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 9 November at the <a href="/wiki/Aut%C3%B3dromo_Jos%C3%A9_Carlos_Pace" title="Autódromo José Carlos Pace">Autódromo José Carlos Pace</a> in <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A3o_Paulo" title="São Paulo">São Paulo</a>. It was the 18th and penultimate round of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2014 Formula One World Championship">2014 Formula One World Championship</a> and the 42nd running of the <a href="/wiki/Brazilian_Grand_Prix" title="Brazilian Grand Prix">event</a> as part of the series. The 71-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>. His teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> finished second and <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> was third. It was Rosberg's fifth victory of the season, his first in Brazil, and the eighth of his career.
</p><p>Rosberg won the pole position by setting the fastest lap in qualifying and maintained the lead until his first <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit stop</a> at the end of lap seven. <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> led after Hamilton's lap eight pit stop and held it until Rosberg overtook him six laps later. Hamilton returned to the lead when Rosberg made a second pit stop 12 laps later but a spin at turn four during the extra lap he was on the track lost him the position. On blistered front tyres, Hamilton remained close by Rosberg by the time of the third cycle of pit stops but Rosberg held off his teammate to win the race.
</p><p>The result allowed Rosberg to lower Hamilton's lead in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">World Drivers' Championship</a> to 17 points while <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> secured third place despite retiring with a <a href="/wiki/Suspension_(vehicle)" class="mw-redirect" title="Suspension (vehicle)">suspension</a> issue. <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> each gained one position to move into fourth and fifth places. Mercedes increased their unassailable lead in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">World Constructors' Championship</a> to 278 points over <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a> with one race left in the season.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Brazilian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Curva_do_s.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Curva_do_s.jpg/220px-Curva_do_s.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Curva_do_s.jpg/330px-Curva_do_s.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/8a/Curva_do_s.jpg/440px-Curva_do_s.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1500" data-file-height="1000" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Curva_do_s.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The <a href="/wiki/Aut%C3%B3dromo_Jos%C3%A9_Carlos_Pace" title="Autódromo José Carlos Pace">Autódromo José Carlos Pace</a> (pictured in 2006), where the race was held</div></div></div>
<p>The 2014 <a href="/wiki/Brazilian_Grand_Prix" title="Brazilian Grand Prix">Brazilian Grand Prix</a> was the 18th of the 19 rounds in the <a href="/wiki/2014_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2014 Formula One World Championship">2014 Formula One World Championship</a> and the 42nd running of the event as part of the series.<sup id="cite_ref-fiapreview_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiapreview-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It was held on 9 November at the 4.309&#160;km (2.677&#160;mi) 15-turn <a href="/wiki/Aut%C3%B3dromo_Jos%C3%A9_Carlos_Pace" title="Autódromo José Carlos Pace">Autódromo José Carlos Pace</a> in <a href="/wiki/S%C3%A3o_Paulo" title="São Paulo">São Paulo</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fiapreview_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiapreview-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> As they were at the <a href="/wiki/2014_United_States_Grand_Prix" title="2014 United States Grand Prix">United States Grand Prix</a> the week before, Formula One's commercial rights owner <a href="/wiki/Bernie_Ecclestone" title="Bernie Ecclestone">Bernie Ecclestone</a> granted <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a> and <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia</a> dispensation to miss the race due to their ongoing financial struggles coupled with the high cost of travel to the western hemisphere and the one-week gap between the United States and Brazilian Grand Prix.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Drag_reduction_system" title="Drag reduction system">drag reduction system</a> (DRS) had two activation zones for the race: one was on the <a href="/wiki/Straight_(racing)" title="Straight (racing)">straight</a> linking turns three and four and the second was on the straight linking the final and first corners.<sup id="cite_ref-fiapreview_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiapreview-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The event's official name was the <i>Formula 1 Grande Prêmio Petrobras do Brasil 2014.</i><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Before the race, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> with 316 <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Championship_points_scoring_systems" title="List of Formula One World Championship points scoring systems">points</a>, 24 ahead of teammate <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> in second and <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> third. <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> was fourth on 155 points, six ahead of <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> in fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-points_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> While the Drivers' Championship had not been won, Mercedes taken the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a> at the <a href="/wiki/2014_Russian_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Russian Grand Prix">Russian Grand Prix</a>. <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> ensured they would finish second, while <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> with 238 points were third, <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> with 196 were fourth and <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-points_4-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> With the introduction of double points for the season-ending <a href="/wiki/2014_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix">Abu Dhabi Grand Prix</a>, the Drivers' Championship could not be won in Brazil as Hamilton winning and Rosberg scoring no points would keep Rosberg in mathematical contention by being 49 points behind Hamilton.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In mid-2014, the track was resurfaced in an effort to reduce its bumpiness and the <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit lane</a> was re-profiled with the entry brought forward off the racing line at the Arquibancadas corner and a <a href="/wiki/Chicane" title="Chicane">chicane</a> added to the pit lane to further slow cars following a series of accidents in national races. The pit lane exit was moved further away from the track to allow for a <a href="/wiki/Run-off_area" title="Run-off area">run-off area</a> to the left of turn two.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> In addition, in response to an crash by <a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a> in the <a href="/wiki/2014_Japanese_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Japanese Grand Prix">Japanese Grand Prix</a>, procedures relating to the location of a tractor crane at the Senna "S" chicane were altered.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> originally nominated the orange-banded Hard and white-banded Medium <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyres" title="Formula One tyres">tyres</a>, as it had done since <a href="/wiki/2012_Brazilian_Grand_Prix" title="2012 Brazilian Grand Prix">2012</a>. However, following the Russian Grand Prix one month prior, many drivers criticised the low level of <a href="/wiki/Grip_(auto_racing)" title="Grip (auto racing)">grip</a> and wear shown on the new tarmac of the <a href="/wiki/Sochi_Autodrom" title="Sochi Autodrom">Sochi Autodrom</a>. Since the Autódromo José Carlos Pace was completely repaved, there was concern that the hard compound would be "very dangerous".<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Along with unanimous agreement from all eleven teams,<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;n 1&#93;</a></sup> Pirelli ultimately decided to bring the Medium and yellow-banded Soft tyres to Brazil.<sup id="cite_ref-tyrechange_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-tyrechange-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During practice, the sport's governing body, the <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_l%27Automobile" title="Fédération Internationale de l&#39;Automobile">Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile</a> re-tested its <a href="/wiki/Safety_car#Virtual_safety_car" title="Safety car">Virtual Safety Car</a> system, which was proposed for the <a href="/wiki/2015_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2015 Formula One World Championship">2015</a> season to better deal with race track emergencies, following the incident suffered by Bianchi in the Japanese Grand Prix. Changes made to this system, relative to the version first tested in the United States, satisfied the drivers.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> There were driver changes for the first practice session. <a href="/wiki/2014_Deutsche_Tourenwagen_Masters" title="2014 Deutsche Tourenwagen Masters">Deutsche Tourenwagen Masters</a> racer <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Juncadella" title="Daniel Juncadella">Daniel Juncadella</a> replaced <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> at <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> for the third time in 2014,<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> while <a href="/wiki/2014_GP2_Series" title="2014 GP2 Series">GP2 Series</a> competitor <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Nasr" title="Felipe Nasr">Felipe Nasr</a> used Bottas's car, and the <a href="/wiki/2014_FIA_Formula_3_European_Championship" title="2014 FIA Formula 3 European Championship">Formula Three European Championship</a> third-place finisher <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> drove <a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a> car.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-13">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Brazilian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Three practice sessions—two on Friday and a third on Saturday—were held before the Sunday race. The Friday morning and afternoon sessions lasted 90 minutes each; the third, one-hour session was held on Saturday morning.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-14">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg was fastest in the first session, which took place in dry and warm weather, with a lap of 1 minute, 12.764 seconds, two-tenths of a second faster than teammate Hamilton in second. <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a>, <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>, <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>, Verstappen, <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a> and Ricciardo occupied positions three to ten.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-13">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> During the session, where drivers struggled with grip on the resurfaced track, Juncadella spun at turn six, and lost control of his car's rear, hitting the turn eight barrier,<sup id="cite_ref-:0_13-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-13">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> damaging his car's nose cone and <a href="/wiki/Suspension_(vehicle)" class="mw-redirect" title="Suspension (vehicle)">suspension</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-:1_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-15">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Racing_flags#Red_flag" title="Racing flags">stopping</a> the session. Force India replaced several engine hydraulic components following a heat soak and Pérez missed the second session.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-16">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> was unable to participate when he stopped at the entry to the pit lane with an <a href="/wiki/Kinetic_energy_recovery_system" title="Kinetic energy recovery system">energy recovery system</a> failure.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_15-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-15">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban Gutiérrez">Esteban Gutiérrez</a> was also not able to record a lap because of engine electrical issues.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_13-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-13">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the second session, Rosberg recorded the day's fastest time of 1 minute, 12.123 seconds; teammate Hamilton, Räikkönen, Ricciardo, Bottas, Massa, Alonso, Kvyat, Vettel and Magnussen followed in the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-17">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> The session was disrupted three times: Vergne stopped on track at turn four when his engine suddenly lost power after 11 minutes. The second stoppage was caused by Alonso's engine catching fire and him stopping on the straight between turns three and four. He vacated his Ferrari to get a fire extinguisher to stop the blaze before <a href="/wiki/Motorsport_marshal" title="Motorsport marshal">marshals</a> could reach his car. Gutiérrez caused the final stoppage when his car stopped with an energy recovery system failure with 13 minutes left.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-16">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:3_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-17">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> The final session was held in dry but overcast weather and saw Rosberg complete a free practice sweep with a lap of 1 minute, 10.446 seconds on soft compound tyres. Hamilton was second-fastest and spun under braking for the Senna S chicane. Hamilton was later delayed by a <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a> car while on a fast lap. The Williams duo of Massa and Bottas were third and fourth; Ricciardo, Button, Räikkönen, Alonso, Magnussen and Kvyat completed the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Brazilian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_(cropped).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/51/Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg/170px-Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="206" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/51/Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg/255px-Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/51/Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg/340px-Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_%28cropped%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1890" data-file-height="2285" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Nico_Rosberg_Stars_and_Cars_2014_2_amk_(cropped).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> won the first FIA Pole Trophy after achieving <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> and overtook <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a>'s ten-year qualifying track record.</div></div></div>
<p>Saturday afternoon's <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#Q" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">qualifying session</a> was divided into three parts. The first part ran for 18 minutes, eliminating cars that finished the session 15th or below. The <a href="/wiki/107%25_rule" title="107% rule">107% rule</a> was in effect, requiring drivers to reach a time within 107 per cent of the quickest lap to qualify. The second part lasted 15 minutes, eliminating cars in 11th to 14th. The final 12-minute session determined <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> to tenth. Cars who progressed to the final session were not allowed to change tyres for the race's start, using the tyres with which they set their quickest lap times in the second session.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-14">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg was fastest in all three sessions, taking his tenth pole position of the season, and the 14th of his career, with a time of 1 minute, 10.023 seconds, breaking <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a>'s 2004 pole lap record by six-tenths of a second.<sup id="cite_ref-rosbergpole_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rosbergpole-19">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> He won the first FIA Pole Trophy as Hamilton could not overtake his poles total with one race left.<sup id="cite_ref-rosbergpole_19-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rosbergpole-19">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> He was joined on the <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#G" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">grid</a>'s front row by Hamilton who lost time entering turn ten too fast, causing him to lose control of the rear of his car. Massa qualified third; traffic slowed his final timed lap and he locked his tyres at the Senna S chicane.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-20">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Fuel pressure problems shut down Massa's engine.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Bottas in fourth aborted his final lap after locking his tyres three times.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Button and Vettel took fifth and sixth.<sup id="cite_ref-rosbergpole_19-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rosbergpole-19">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Button's fastest timed lap was his first but he failed to improve on his second. It allowed Vettel to challenge him but was 0.080 seconds slower than Button on his second timed lap.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportquali_23-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportquali-23">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> Magnussen in seventh did not feel the soft tyres helped him and was unable to go faster.<sup id="cite_ref-qualiquotes_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualiquotes-24">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> The Ferrari duo of Alonso and Räikkönen were eighth and tenth; Ricciardo separated them,<sup id="cite_ref-quali_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-25">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> believing minor pre-qualifying fine-tuning adjustments to his car affected its balance.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Gutiérrez, considered as "one of the standout performers" of qualifying,<sup id="cite_ref-:4_20-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-20">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> was the fastest driver not to qualify for the final session.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_25-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-25">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> had difficulty finding a rhythm and took 12th, ahead of <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a>'s slower <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a> car.<sup id="cite_ref-qualiquotes_24-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualiquotes-24">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Kvyat qualified 14th after electing not to record a lap in the second session and was demoted ten places on the grid for carrying over an engine penalty from the United States Grand Prix.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_20-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-20">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Hence, <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> inherited the position,<sup id="cite_ref-quali_25-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-25">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> after Lotus made overnight adjustments to his car.<sup id="cite_ref-qualiquotes_24-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualiquotes-24">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Vergne had difficulty in driving and an incorrect set-up left him in 15th.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_20-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-20">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Pérez originally took 16th but a seven-place grid penalty was applied after he was deemed to have caused an avoidable accident with Sutil in the United States and Maldonado inherited the place.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportquali_23-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportquali-23">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Brazilian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The race began at 14:00 <a href="/wiki/Time_in_Brazil" title="Time in Brazil">Brasilia Time</a> (<a href="/wiki/UTC%E2%88%9202:00" title="UTC−02:00">UTC−02:00</a>).<sup id="cite_ref-fiapreview_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiapreview-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The weather at the start was dry and sunny with an air temperature between 23 to 24&#160;°C (73 to 75&#160;°F) and a track temperature ranging from 46 to 50&#160;°C (115 to 122&#160;°F);<sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> forecasts of rain for Sunday did not materialise. Sutil started from the pit lane after his mechanics reconfigured his car's cooling system. The first ten starters began on the soft compound tyres with four on the medium tyres.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-28">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> When the race began, Rosberg maintained the lead at the Senna S chicane as Bottas was slow but returned to fourth place.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Heading into the Curva do Sol turn, Vettel over-committed in the braking zone and ran wide while defending from Magnussen. By doing so, Magnussen and Alonso demoted Vettel to eighth behind teammate Ricciardo.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_28-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-28">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Kvyat moved from 17th to 13th by the end of the first lap, while Sutil lost five positions over the same distance.<sup id="cite_ref-:6_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-30">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> At the end of the first lap, Rosberg led Hamilton by 0.8 seconds,<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> who was followed in turn by, Massa, Bottas, Button, Magnussen, Alonso, Ricciardo and Gutiérrez.<sup id="cite_ref-:6_30-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-30">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Felipe_Massa_podium_-_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_(02).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fe/Felipe_Massa_podium_-_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2802%29.jpg/170px-Felipe_Massa_podium_-_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2802%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="213" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fe/Felipe_Massa_podium_-_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2802%29.jpg/255px-Felipe_Massa_podium_-_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2802%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fe/Felipe_Massa_podium_-_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2802%29.jpg/340px-Felipe_Massa_podium_-_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_%2802%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="786" data-file-height="983" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Felipe_Massa_podium_-_2014_Italian_Grand_Prix_(02).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> finished third after a five-second time penalty for speeding in the <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit lane</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>Rosberg began to pull away from Hamilton, opening up a 1.2 second lead over his teammate while Massa was a further one second behind Hamilton.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo attempted to pass Vettel into the Senna S chicane on the start of the third lap but was not close enough to overtake.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Massa was the first driver to have severe blistering on the soft compound tyres and was the first of the leading drivers to make a pit stop at the end of lap five for the change to the medium compound tyres, with Vettel, Bottas and Button making similar stops on the following lap. Rosberg held a one-second lead over Hamilton and made his first pit stop from the lead at the end of lap seven. Hamilton was more conservative on his tyres and went half a second faster in <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#C" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">clear air</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_28-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-28">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Massa was imposed a five-second penalty for speeding in the pit lane,<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> and Hamilton made his pit stop from the lead at the conclusion of lap eight and came out narrowly behind teammate Rosberg.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_32-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-32">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>With Hamilton's and Rosberg's pit stops completed, Hülkenberg and Kvyat were first and second, followed by Grosjean.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Traffic slowed Rosberg, allowing Hamilton to potentially attack his teammate.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_32-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-32">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg was protected from Hamilton overtaking on the main straight because he used DRS to defend himself since he was close behind Grosjean. He drew nearer to Kvyat and passed him for second on lap 12.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> On the same lap, Button overtook Sutil for eighth place.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Mercedes instructed Hamilton to manage his right-rear tyre temperatures and lost more time to Rosberg by following Kvyat, who allowed Hamilton past before the Senna S chicane just as Hülkenberg had Rosberg close behind. Hülkenberg reported a blistered front-right tyre to Force India and Rosberg overtook him for the lead into the Senna S chicane at the start of lap 14.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Massa passed Grosjean and Bottas overtook Grosjean at the Senna S chicane for sixth place on lap 15.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> With his front tyres blistered,<sup id="cite_ref-:5_28-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-28">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton used DRS to pass Hülkenberg into the Senna S chicane on the following lap.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Massa drew closer to Kvyat and passed him into the Senna chicane for fourth place on lap 17 as Alonso overtook Sutil at the same turn for tenth on that lap. At the same time, Hülkenberg made his pit stop and rejoined the track in 15th.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton's right-front tyre was blistered but was not significantly hindered and set a new fastest lap to be 2.1 seconds behind Rosberg at the start of lap 20,<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> after Rosberg became aware of not over stressing his tyres and Hamilton being faster. The two drivers pulled away from Massa. Button drove close behind Bottas and used his DRS to pull away from Magnussen, Vettel, Alonso and Ricciardo.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_28-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-28">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Magnussen steered left to pass Grosjean for sixth on lap 23. Alonso attempted to pass Grosjean on the outside into the Senna chicane on the next lap but Grosjean blocked him. Alonso tried again on the main straight and overtook Grosjean before the Descida do Lago turn for seventh. Vettel also passed Grojean at the same turn.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_32-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-32">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Massa served his penalty on the 26th lap, and after taking it, had new tyres installed on his car.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Rosberg made his second pit stop on the same lap, returning the lead to Hamilton.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_32-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-32">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Bottas also made a pit stop but a delay in fitting his unbuckled seat belt and adjusting it, allowed Button into fourth as Bottas fell to 12th.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_28-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-28">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton drove faster than any driver to try and take the lead after his pit stop.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Mercedes asked Hamilton on lap 28 to make an energy recovery switch to raise the harvesting rate but not a forward brake bias adjustment. When Hamilton braked for the Descida do Lado turn, his rear tyre locked, <a href="/wiki/Understeer_and_oversteer" title="Understeer and oversteer">oversteered</a> and spun onto the run-off area. Hamilton rejoined the track seven seconds later.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_28-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-28">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton made his pit stop on that lap and emerged in second, 7.4 seconds behind teammate Rosberg.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Button closed up to Kvyat and waited until the main straight to pass with DRS into the Senna chicane on lap 30.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_32-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-32">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton moved to within five seconds of his teammate Rosberg on blistered rear tyres by lap 35.<sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_32-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-32">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Räikkönen made a pit stop from third on the same lap but lost time after the front jack was lowered before his right-front tyre was installed. He rejoined the track in 13th, behind Grosjean.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_2014_China.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f1/Lewis_Hamilton_2014_China.jpg/170px-Lewis_Hamilton_2014_China.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="204" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f1/Lewis_Hamilton_2014_China.jpg/255px-Lewis_Hamilton_2014_China.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f1/Lewis_Hamilton_2014_China.jpg/340px-Lewis_Hamilton_2014_China.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2827" data-file-height="3399" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_2014_China.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> finished second to allow the title to be decided at the season-ending <a href="/wiki/2014_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix">Abu Dhabi Grand Prix</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>Vettel overtook Kvyat at the Senna chicane for fifth on lap 37.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo's front-left suspension began to move unsteadily on the main straight on lap 39, and veered right into the Senna chicane. Ricciardo entered the pit lane to retire, ending a 15-race finishing streak extending back to the <a href="/wiki/2014_Malaysian_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Malaysian Grand Prix">Malaysian Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:8_33-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-33">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Hülkenberg passed Bottas for eighth by putting him onto the run-off area at the Senna chicane on lap 42. Bottas defended from Räikkönen on the outside at Descida do Lago turn before going wide on the exit.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Bottas had a problematic pit stop on lap 44: one of his mechanics struggled to adjust the front wing and Bottas fell to 16th.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Magnussen had worse tyre degradation than his teammate Button and Alonso could not pass Button on the outside into the Senna S chicane on lap 45. The battle continued through the following corners but Button's defence into Descida do Lago turn compromised his exit and Alonso overtook him on the inside of turn five.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_28-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-28">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hamilton closed up to Rosberg by 1.9 seconds by the start of lap 50. Rosberg made his final pit stop from the lead that same lap.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Slower traffic delayed Hamilton, who rejoined just behind his teammate after his own pit stop.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_28-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-28">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-telegraphrace_32-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telegraphrace-32">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Massa and Button made pit stops on that lap and they exited the pit lane in fifth and sixth, respectively. Button had closed up to Massa after the latter drove onto the incorrect pit box. Hülkenberg moved to third but Massa passed him into the Senna chicane on lap 54.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton attacked Rosberg, who responded by going faster than his teammate but Hamilton stayed close by with DRS on the straights. The duo pulled away from Massa and Räikkönen.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_28-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-28">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Slower traffic did not affect Rosberg and Hamilton enough to be slowed.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> On lap 61, Räikkönen—on 25-lap worn tyres—blocked a pass by Button for fourth into the Senna chicane. Button held the <a href="/wiki/Racing_line" title="Racing line">racing line</a> and drew alongside Räikkönen on the back straight before passing on the outside at the Descida do Lago turn.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Räikkönen lost momentum at the corner and Vettel passed him. Alonso began to battle teammate Räikkönen who defended over the next few laps.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_28-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-28">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Grosjean stopped on track near a fire marshal with smoke billowing from his engine on lap 65 and retired from the race.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-bbcwebrace_31-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcwebrace-31">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Hülkenberg passed Magnussen for eighth on lap 68,<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> while Räikkönen lost traction in his tyres at the bottom of the hill at the Senna S chicane, allowing teammate Alonso into sixth on the same lap.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_28-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-28">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton moved to within 0.8 seconds of his teammate Rosberg at the start of the final lap,<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> but Hamilton could not get close enough to pass Rosberg, who won the race.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_28-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-28">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Massa finished third, with Button fourth, Vettel fifth, Alonso sixth from teammate Räikkönen, Hülkenberg eighth, and Magnussen ninth. Bottas held off Kvyat in the final laps to claim tenth. Maldonado, Vergne, Gutiérrez, Pérez and Sutil were the final finishers.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_29-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-29">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> It was Rosberg's fifth victory of the season, his first in Brazil, and the eighth of his career.<sup id="cite_ref-:7_34-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:7-34">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> Mercedes's one-two finish was their 11th of the season, breaking McLaren's 1988 record with <a href="/wiki/Alain_Prost" title="Alain Prost">Alain Prost</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ayrton_Senna" title="Ayrton Senna">Ayrton Senna</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:7_34-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:7-34">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> It was also the team's 15th victory of 2014, equalling McLaren and Ferrari's joint record.<sup id="cite_ref-35" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-35">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> There were seven lead changes in the race: three drivers reached the front of the field. Rosberg led four times for a total of 59 laps, more than any other driver.<sup id="cite_ref-raceresults_36-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-raceresults-36">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Brazilian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>At the podium interviews, conducted by three-time World Champion <a href="/wiki/Nelson_Piquet" title="Nelson Piquet">Nelson Piquet</a>, Rosberg declared his happiness over the weekend sweep, having been able to feel comfortable to attack in his Mercedes.<sup id="cite_ref-:9_37-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:9-37">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton said despite his turn four spin, it had been "an amazing race" and praised his team for the work they put in and enjoyed his battle with Rosberg.<sup id="cite_ref-:9_37-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:9-37">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Massa said he was "grateful" for his fast car.<sup id="cite_ref-:9_37-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:9-37">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> In the later press conference, Rosberg said it was important for him to improve after the United States, and was confident that he could control the gap to Hamilton and ensure he did not battle for the win.<sup id="cite_ref-:9_37-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:9-37">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> When asked if his spin prevented him from winning the race, Hamilton said he felt it did and admitted his error. He said he felt the team's record-breaking race was "absolutely incredible" and called it an "unbelievable job" by Mercedes.<sup id="cite_ref-:9_37-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:9-37">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Massa said he thought his chances of a podium finish were diminished with his five-second time penalty but was unworried about parking in the wrong pit stall.<sup id="cite_ref-:9_37-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:9-37">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Vettel explained his poor start triggered memories of his first lap collision with <a href="/wiki/Bruno_Senna" title="Bruno Senna">Bruno Senna</a> in 2012, saying he felt Red Bull would be stronger against McLaren at the start but that it was highly competitive and was hoping to possibly finish in a higher position.<sup id="cite_ref-38" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-38">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Williams' Head of Vehicle Performance <a href="/wiki/Rob_Smedley" title="Rob Smedley">Rob Smedley</a> was angry with himself for not stopping Massa driving through the McLaren pit stall after the team switched garage positions for the race and exchanged words afterwards. He felt it should have been given more consideration and given advance warning. However he praised Bottas for scoring an extra point and described the Williams team's race as a "character building" event, something he was taught at Ferrari by technical director <a href="/wiki/Ross_Brawn" title="Ross Brawn">Ross Brawn</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-39" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-39">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Bottas said he had severe left-rear tyre graining on his final stint, causing him to slide massively and lose positions: "I was managing a lot at the beginning and then could just avoid [Daniil] Kvyat from overtaking. At one point there was some debris in the rear wing [as well], a tear off or something. [As I said] it was just not my day."<sup id="cite_ref-40" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-40">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Following Button's fourth-place finish, which was the fifth time in 2014 he achieved a top four finish, he said he did not compete to attempt to prove anything but was in the sport "to do my job and my job is to do the best job I can for myself and for the team and that's exactly what I did."<sup id="cite_ref-:10_41-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:10-41">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> However, he was delighted to duel the Williams team, saying, "It was a lot of fun. And then the battle with Kimi Raikkonen, going past him was mega – I really enjoyed that. We're not the best of friends but we battle hard but clean."<sup id="cite_ref-:10_41-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:10-41">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Alonso was not frustrated with his teammate Räikkönen over their late-race battle for sixth: "The most important thing was not the battle with him, it was the battle with the other teams and we got some good points for Ferrari today. Even if I could have overtaken Kimi a little quicker I had to save a lot of fuel in the last couple of laps so a battle with Sebastian was never a possibility."<sup id="cite_ref-:11_42-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:11-42">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Räikkönen dismissed the battle as "normal racing" and felt it had not hindered his team because it would score the same number of points regardless of his finishing position.<sup id="cite_ref-:11_42-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:11-42">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The press praised Rosberg. Andrew Benson of <i><a href="/wiki/BBC_Sport" title="BBC Sport">BBC Sport</a></i> said his weekend was "copybook" and it was needed to "re-establish his position as a credible championship contender" to many people.<sup id="cite_ref-43" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-43">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> <i><a href="/wiki/L%27%C3%89quipe" title="L&#39;Équipe">L'Équipe</a></i> said Rosberg "demonstrated above all that he knew how to let the past, and his recent disappointments, behind him. He is psychologically ready to face his rival and lead the hard life until the last round of the last Grand Prix."<sup id="cite_ref-44" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-44">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/The_Daily_Telegraph" title="The Daily Telegraph">The Daily Telegraph</a>'s Formula One correspondent Daniel Johnson described Rosberg's performance as "the race of his life under relentless pressure" and called his victory "the finest of his career."<sup id="cite_ref-45" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-45">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> Writing for <i><a href="/wiki/The_Independent" title="The Independent">The Independent</a></i>, <a href="/wiki/David_Tremayne" title="David Tremayne">David Tremayne</a> wrote Rosberg had "finally avenged himself" on teammate Hamilton by winning and gave him realistic title chances by proving he could resist pressure.<sup id="cite_ref-46" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-46">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The result reduced Hamilton's World Drivers' Championship lead over Rosberg by seven points to 17.<sup id="cite_ref-points_4-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Despite his retirement, Ricciardo had secured third place as no other driver could overtake his points total with one race remaining.<sup id="cite_ref-:8_33-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-33">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Vettel move to fourth with 159 points, two points ahead of fifth-placed Alonso.<sup id="cite_ref-points_4-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Mercedes further extended their unassailable lead in the World Constructors' Championship to 278 points over Red Bull. Williams and Ferrari remained in third and fourth with 254 and 210 points and McLaren were fifth on 161 points with one race left in the season.<sup id="cite_ref-points_4-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-points-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Despite his lead, Hamilton said he would take "zero comfort" for the season's final race: "There are 50 points to gain. The last race, you never know whats going to happen, so I'm going there to win."<sup id="cite_ref-47" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-47">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
