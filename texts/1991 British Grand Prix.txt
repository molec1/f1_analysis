<p>The <b>1991 British Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held at <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone</a> on 14 July 1991. It was the eighth race of the <a href="/wiki/1991_FIA_Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="1991 FIA Formula One World Championship">1991 FIA Formula One World Championship</a>.
</p><p>The 59-lap race was won from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> by local driver <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Mansell" title="Nigel Mansell">Nigel Mansell</a>, driving a <a href="/wiki/Williams_FW14" title="Williams FW14">Williams</a>-<a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a>. <a href="/wiki/Gerhard_Berger" title="Gerhard Berger">Gerhard Berger</a> finished second in a <a href="/wiki/McLaren_MP4/6" title="McLaren MP4/6">McLaren</a>-<a href="/wiki/Honda_in_Formula_One" title="Honda in Formula One">Honda</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Alain_Prost" title="Alain Prost">Alain Prost</a> third in a <a href="/wiki/Ferrari_643" title="Ferrari 643">Ferrari</a>. Drivers' Championship leader, <a href="/wiki/Ayrton_Senna" title="Ayrton Senna">Ayrton Senna</a>, ran out of fuel in the second McLaren-Honda on the final lap and was classified fourth. As well as winning from pole position, Mansell led every lap and posted the fastest lap of the race. It was the first of two consecutive <a href="/wiki/Grand_Slam_(Formula_One)" class="mw-redirect" title="Grand Slam (Formula One)">grand slams</a> at the Silverstone circuit for Mansell, who repeated the feat the following year in 1992.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Pre-race">Pre-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1991_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Pre-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Mansell-Mania greeted the drivers as they arrived at Silverstone for the British Grand Prix. The track had been vastly remodelled for 1991, which garnered largely positive reviews from the drivers. The track was no longer the fastest on the F1 calendar, but it now included many challenging corners for the drivers.  Elsewhere <a href="/wiki/Tom_Walkinshaw" title="Tom Walkinshaw">Tom Walkinshaw</a> had bought a 35% stake in the <a href="/wiki/Benetton_Formula" title="Benetton Formula">Benetton</a> team, but the driver line-ups were unchanged from the <a href="/wiki/1991_French_Grand_Prix" title="1991 French Grand Prix">previous race</a>. <a href="/wiki/Stefan_Johansson" title="Stefan Johansson">Stefan Johansson</a> continued to stand-in for the injured Alex Caffi at Footwork, although it was the last race he would do so.
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1991_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Pre-qualifying_report">Pre-qualifying report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1991_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Pre-qualifying report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Friday morning pre-qualifying session resulted in a return to the <a href="/wiki/Dallara" title="Dallara">Dallara</a> / <a href="/wiki/Jordan_Grand_Prix" title="Jordan Grand Prix">Jordan</a> lockout seen three times earlier in the season. <a href="/wiki/JJ_Lehto" title="JJ Lehto">JJ Lehto</a> was fastest in the Dallara, with <a href="/wiki/Andrea_de_Cesaris" title="Andrea de Cesaris">Andrea de Cesaris</a>' Jordan nearly seven tenths of a second slower in second position. Third was the Dallara of <a href="/wiki/Emanuele_Pirro" title="Emanuele Pirro">Emanuele Pirro</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Bertrand_Gachot" title="Bertrand Gachot">Bertrand Gachot</a>'s Jordan a couple of tenths further back in fourth.
</p><p>After pre-qualifying successfully at the last two Grands Prix, <a href="/wiki/Olivier_Grouillard" title="Olivier Grouillard">Olivier Grouillard</a> missed out this time in fifth place for <a href="/wiki/Fondmetal" title="Fondmetal">Fondmetal</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Modena_(racing_team)" title="Modena (racing team)">Modena Lambos</a> were again sixth and seventh, with <a href="/wiki/Nicola_Larini" title="Nicola Larini">Nicola Larini</a> again faster than <a href="/wiki/Eric_van_de_Poele" title="Eric van de Poele">Eric van de Poele</a>. Nine tenths of a second further back in eighth was <a href="/wiki/Pedro_Chaves" title="Pedro Chaves">Pedro Chaves</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Coloni" title="Scuderia Coloni">Coloni</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Walker_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Walker-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1991_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race_report">Race report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1991_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Race report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:352px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Mansell_and_Senna_at_Silverstone.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a7/Mansell_and_Senna_at_Silverstone.jpg/350px-Mansell_and_Senna_at_Silverstone.jpg" decoding="async" width="350" height="329" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a7/Mansell_and_Senna_at_Silverstone.jpg/525px-Mansell_and_Senna_at_Silverstone.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a7/Mansell_and_Senna_at_Silverstone.jpg/700px-Mansell_and_Senna_at_Silverstone.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2472" data-file-height="2324" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Mansell_and_Senna_at_Silverstone.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Nigel_Mansell" title="Nigel Mansell">Nigel Mansell</a> gives <a href="/wiki/Ayrton_Senna" title="Ayrton Senna">Ayrton Senna</a> a lift back to the paddock at Silverstone during his victory lap, after Senna had run out of fuel.</div></div></div>
<p>Senna made a rocket start to lead Mansell, while Patrese was the first casualty of the day, having been bumped off by Berger (Patrese retired after the first lap as a result). Senna's lead did not last for long as Mansell re-passed him going into Stowe corner. Roberto Moreno in the Benetton retired from sixth place with a gearbox failure on lap 22.
</p><p>Mansell and Senna proceeded to rocket off into the distance while Berger, Prost, and Alesi squabbled over third place, with Alesi emerging ahead; he would later retire after colliding with <a href="/wiki/Aguri_Suzuki" title="Aguri Suzuki">Aguri Suzuki</a> while trying to lap the Japanese driver's <a href="/wiki/Lola_Cars" title="Lola Cars">Lola</a>. 
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Andrea_de_Cesaris" title="Andrea de Cesaris">Andrea de Cesaris</a> had a huge accident on Lap 41 when his suspension failed at Abbey corner, his car bounced back across the track and narrowly missed <a href="/wiki/Satoru_Nakajima" title="Satoru Nakajima">Satoru Nakajima</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Tyrrell_Racing" title="Tyrrell Racing">Tyrrell</a>; de Cesaris was unhurt.
</p><p>At the front it was all Mansell as the Englishman recorded his second win in succession, while rival Senna ran out of fuel on the last lap and he was classified fourth. Berger ended up second, followed by Prost, Senna, Piquet, and <a href="/wiki/Bertrand_Gachot" title="Bertrand Gachot">Bertrand Gachot</a>'s Jordan. Mansell now trailed Senna by only 18 points and momentum was on his side.
</p><p>In terms of having to pre-qualify, Brabham would have needed their top car at least in 7th position to demote the <a href="/wiki/Leyton_House" class="mw-redirect" title="Leyton House">Leyton House</a> team, but neither Brundle nor Blundell finished the race (Brundle retired with a broken throttle and <a href="/wiki/Mark_Blundell" title="Mark Blundell">Mark Blundell</a> retired with engine failure later in the race on lap 53).
</p>
