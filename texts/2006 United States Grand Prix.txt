<p>The <b>2006 United States Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>XXXV Vodafone United States Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> held at the <a href="/wiki/Indianapolis_Motor_Speedway" title="Indianapolis Motor Speedway">Indianapolis Motor Speedway</a> in <a href="/wiki/Indianapolis" title="Indianapolis">Indianapolis</a>, <a href="/wiki/Indiana" title="Indiana">Indiana</a> on 2 July 2006. It was the tenth race of the <a href="/wiki/2006_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2006 Formula One season">2006 Formula One season</a> and the 40th United States Grand Prix. The 73-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> after starting from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>. Teammate <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> finished second with <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a> third.
</p><p>As a consequence of the race, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> scored his third win of the season, ending championship rival <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>'s run of four consecutive wins and reducing the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">2005 World Drivers' Champion</a>'s lead in the points standings to 19 over the German. Schumacher's Ferrari teammate <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> made it a Ferrari one-two by finishing in second place as the Maranello-based team also reduced <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a>'s constructors points advantage to 26.
</p><p>The race also marked <a href="/wiki/2005_United_States_Grand_Prix" title="2005 United States Grand Prix">the anniversary of the tire controversy a year earlier</a>, when 14 cars withdrew before the race began because of safety concerns about the supply of Michelin tyres provided to them&#160;– which had resulted in crashes during practice. This was also the last race for <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Juan_Pablo_Montoya" title="Juan Pablo Montoya">Juan Pablo Montoya</a>, after he announced that he would be leaving to race in <a href="/wiki/NASCAR" title="NASCAR">NASCAR</a> in 2007.
</p><p>This was the last race until the <a href="/wiki/2015_Malaysian_Grand_Prix" title="2015 Malaysian Grand Prix">2015 Malaysian Grand Prix</a> that neither McLaren was classified.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2006_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2006_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Grand Prix was contested by eleven teams with two drivers each. The teams (also known as <a href="/wiki/Formula_One#Constructors" title="Formula One">Constructors</a>) were <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a>, <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyota</a>, <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a>, <a href="/wiki/Honda_in_Formula_One" title="Honda in Formula One">Honda</a>, <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>, <a href="/wiki/BMW_in_Formula_One" title="BMW in Formula One">BMW Sauber</a>, <a href="/wiki/Midland_F1_Racing" title="Midland F1 Racing">MF1</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a> and <a href="/wiki/Super_Aguri_F1" title="Super Aguri F1">Super Aguri</a>.
</p><p>Going into the race, Renault driver <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> with 84 points, ahead of <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> on 59 points and <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> on 39 points. <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a> was fourth with 37 points while <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> was fifth on 28 points. In the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a>, Renault were leading with 121 points, Ferrari and McLaren were second and third with 87 and 65 points respectively, while Honda on 29 and BMW Sauber on 19 points contended for fourth place. Renault and Fernando Alonso had so far won the majority of the races, including victory in the preceding <a href="/wiki/2006_Canadian_Grand_Prix" title="2006 Canadian Grand Prix">Canadian Grand Prix</a>. Championship contenders Schumacher and Fisichella had each gained victories, and Räikkönen, <a href="/wiki/Juan_Pablo_Montoya" title="Juan Pablo Montoya">Juan Pablo Montoya</a> had each gained second-place finishes. Massa, <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ralf_Schumacher" title="Ralf Schumacher">Ralf Schumacher</a> and <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> had achieved third place podium finishes.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice_and_qualifying">Practice and qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2006_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Practice and qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Felipe_Massa_2006_USA.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5c/Felipe_Massa_2006_USA.jpg/220px-Felipe_Massa_2006_USA.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5c/Felipe_Massa_2006_USA.jpg/330px-Felipe_Massa_2006_USA.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5c/Felipe_Massa_2006_USA.jpg/440px-Felipe_Massa_2006_USA.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1024" data-file-height="682" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Felipe_Massa_2006_USA.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Scuderia Ferrari</a> locked out the front row on the grid.</div></div></div>
<p>Three practice sessions were held before the Sunday race – two on Friday, and one on Saturday. The Friday morning and afternoon sessions each lasted 90 minutes; the third session, on Saturday morning, lasted for an hour.<sup id="cite_ref-sportingregulations_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sportingregulations-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Saturday afternoon's qualifying session was divided into three parts. The first part ran for 15 minutes, and cars that finished the session 17th position or lower were eliminated from qualifying. The second part of the qualifying session lasted 15 minutes and eliminated cars that finished in positions 11 to 16. The final part of the qualifying session ran for 20 minutes which determined the positions from first to tenth, and decided pole position. Cars which failed to make the final session could refuel before the race, so ran lighter in those sessions.<sup id="cite_ref-sportingregulations_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-sportingregulations-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> and <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> earned <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> a front row lockout, ahead of <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a>. <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a>'s car was still being repaired when cars were sent to parc fermé; because of that, Trulli had to start from the pit lane. <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> was penalised for ignoring a signal during the qualification session, requiring him to get his car weighed at the <a href="/wiki/FIA" class="mw-redirect" title="FIA">FIA</a> garage. The FIA cancelled all his qualifying times, and he started from 21st position. <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Vitantonio_Liuzzi" title="Vitantonio Liuzzi">Vitantonio Liuzzi</a> had the engine of his car changed, and had to start from 20th position on the grid.
</p>
<div style="clear:both;"></div>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2006_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Juan_Pablo_Montoya_2006_USA.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/16/Juan_Pablo_Montoya_2006_USA.jpg/220px-Juan_Pablo_Montoya_2006_USA.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="150" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/16/Juan_Pablo_Montoya_2006_USA.jpg/330px-Juan_Pablo_Montoya_2006_USA.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/16/Juan_Pablo_Montoya_2006_USA.jpg/440px-Juan_Pablo_Montoya_2006_USA.jpg 2x" data-file-width="800" data-file-height="545" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Juan_Pablo_Montoya_2006_USA.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Juan_Pablo_Montoya" title="Juan Pablo Montoya">Juan Pablo Montoya</a> was involved in an incident at the first corner of the race.</div></div></div>
<p>The race started with two big accidents at the first corner, which eliminated 7 cars. At Turn 1, <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> collided with <a href="/wiki/Christian_Klien" title="Christian Klien">Christian Klien</a>, who then spun around and was taken out by <a href="/wiki/Franck_Montagny" title="Franck Montagny">Franck Montagny</a>. In Turn 2, <a href="/wiki/Juan_Pablo_Montoya" title="Juan Pablo Montoya">Juan Pablo Montoya</a> nudged his teammate <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> into a spin. Montoya then clouted <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> whose front right tyre got caught by <a href="/wiki/BMW_in_Formula_One" title="BMW in Formula One">BMW Sauber</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> who was launched into a triple barrel roll; Montoya also clipped <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Scuderia Toro Rosso</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Scott_Speed" title="Scott Speed">Scott Speed</a> in the incident. None of the drivers were injured. After that, the order stayed the same at the front, the only action being a battle at the back between <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Vitantonio_Liuzzi" title="Vitantonio Liuzzi">Vitantonio Liuzzi</a>. Rosberg lost out and was the only finishing driver not to score in the race, with Liuzzi gaining Toro Rosso's first ever point. Montoya departed <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> days after the race, announcing his move to <a href="/wiki/NASCAR" title="NASCAR">NASCAR</a> for the 2007 season for <a href="/wiki/Chip_Ganassi_Racing" title="Chip Ganassi Racing">Chip Ganassi Racing</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
