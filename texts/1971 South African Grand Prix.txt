<p>The <b>1971 South African Grand Prix</b>, formally the <b>Fifth AA Grand Prix of South Africa</b> (<a href="/wiki/Afrikaans" title="Afrikaans">Afrikaans</a>: <i>Vyfde AA Suid-Afrikaanse Grand Prix</i><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>), was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Motor_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Motor race">motor race</a> held at <a href="/wiki/Kyalami" title="Kyalami">Kyalami Circuit</a> on 6 March 1971. It was race 1 of 11 in both the <a href="/wiki/1971_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1971 World Championship of Drivers">1971 World Championship of Drivers</a> and the <a href="/wiki/1971_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1971 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1971 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>. The race was won by <a href="/wiki/Mario_Andretti" title="Mario Andretti">Mario Andretti</a> who was driving for the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> team in what was his first Formula One victory, but would not win another Formula One race until <a href="/wiki/1976_Formula_One_season" title="1976 Formula One season">1976</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1971_South_African_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>It was expected the 12-cylinder cars would be dominant over the V8 Ford Cosworths – <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> had won four out of the last five Grands Prix in <a href="/wiki/1970_Formula_One_season" title="1970 Formula One season">1970</a>, and they had a strong line-up consisting of <a href="/wiki/Mario_Andretti" title="Mario Andretti">Mario Andretti</a>, <a href="/wiki/Clay_Regazzoni" title="Clay Regazzoni">Clay Regazzoni</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jacky_Ickx" title="Jacky Ickx">Jacky Ickx</a>.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Jackie_Stewart" title="Jackie Stewart">Jackie Stewart</a> took pole in the <a href="/wiki/Tyrrell_Racing" title="Tyrrell Racing">Tyrrell</a> to join <a href="/wiki/Chris_Amon" title="Chris Amon">Chris Amon</a> and Regazzoni on the front row. <a href="/wiki/Denny_Hulme" title="Denny Hulme">Denny Hulme</a> was suffering with a new suspension that he said was behaving very strangely.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2016)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p><p>At the start, Regazzoni led the field away at the start – both Stewart and Amon suffering slow starts. <a href="/wiki/Emerson_Fittipaldi" title="Emerson Fittipaldi">Emerson Fittipaldi</a> managed to hold off Hulme until lap three when he dived through to chase the Ferraris. On lap 17, he squeezed through under braking and the orange <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> once again led a race. <a href="/wiki/Pedro_Rodr%C3%ADguez_(racing_driver)" title="Pedro Rodríguez (racing driver)">Pedro Rodríguez</a> was forced to retire from fourth place when his bodywork started to melt and the hot air began burning his feet. The heat also took its toll on <a href="/wiki/Jo_Siffert" title="Jo Siffert">Jo Siffert</a>, whose engine overheated and <a href="/wiki/Howden_Ganley" title="Howden Ganley">Howden Ganley</a> who was taken ill with heat exhaustion. On lap 37, <a href="/wiki/John_Surtees" title="John Surtees">John Surtees</a> who had steadily been moving up the field, managed to pass Regazzoni. Surtees however, suffered a gearbox failure on lap 57. Andretti took his first career victory, driving a <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>; his teammate Regazzoni finished in third place behind Stewart.
</p>
