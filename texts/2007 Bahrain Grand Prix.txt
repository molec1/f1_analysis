<p>The <b>2007 Gulf Air Bahrain Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race and was the third round of the <a href="/wiki/2007_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2007 Formula One season">2007 Formula One season</a>. It was held on 15 April at the <a href="/wiki/Bahrain_International_Circuit" title="Bahrain International Circuit">Bahrain International Circuit</a> in Sakhir, <a href="/wiki/Bahrain" title="Bahrain">Bahrain</a>.
</p><p>In practice 1 and 2, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> topped the charts. <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> also placed well. <a href="/wiki/Vitantonio_Liuzzi" title="Vitantonio Liuzzi">Vitantonio Liuzzi</a> got a surprise 8th-place result in 1st practice. <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> won the Grand Prix from Lewis Hamilton, who made history to become the first driver ever to finish on the podium in all his first three races.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Bahrain_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Bahrain_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Friday morning practice session saw both <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferraris</a> top the time sheets with <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> the fastest with a time of 1:33.162 with his teammate, <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>, just over half a second slower than the <a href="/wiki/Finland" title="Finland">Finn</a> in second. The <a href="/wiki/Italy" title="Italy">Italian</a> team's nearest competition was the two <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLarens</a> of <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> who were third and fourth respectively, Hamilton just under a second slower than Räikkönen's time.<sup id="cite_ref-Practice_1_-_Report_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Practice_1_-_Report-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Practice_1_-_Results_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Practice_1_-_Results-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Räikkönen was fastest again in the afternoon session, clocking a time of 1:33.527, just under four tenths slower than his fastest time during the morning session. Unlike the morning session, however, Ferrari did not have both their cars occupying the top two as Massa's fastest time was fourth fastest, with Hamilton and <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> separating the two Ferraris.<sup id="cite_ref-Practice_2_-_Report_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Practice_2_-_Report-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Practice_2_-_Results_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Practice_2_-_Results-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>It was six thousandths of a second that prevented Räikkönen from being fastest in all three practice sessions, but his run was ended by Lewis Hamilton with the lead BMW Sauber of <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> in third; Kubica fourth; <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Davidson" title="Anthony Davidson">Anthony Davidson</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Super_Aguri_F1" title="Super Aguri F1">Super Aguri</a> in fifth and Massa completing the top six, four tenths down on Hamilton's time.<sup id="cite_ref-Practice_3_-_Report_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Practice_3_-_Report-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Practice_3_-_Results_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Practice_3_-_Results-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Bahrain_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Qualifying was played out in temperatures of over 34&#160;°C and despite a small shower of rain just after the morning practice session was over, the track itself was dry. <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams'</a> <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Wurz" title="Alexander Wurz">Alexander Wurz</a> was the first out onto the circuit with former Williams drivers <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> following him. Like the first practice session on Friday, the two Ferraris set the early pace, both recording laps under 1:33.000, the only two cars to do so during Part One of Qualifying. The first six to be eliminated from qualifying were <a href="/wiki/Takuma_Sato" title="Takuma Sato">Takuma Sato</a>, who was seventeen thousandths of a second slower than sixteenth place Jenson Button; both <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rossos</a> of <a href="/wiki/Vitantonio_Liuzzi" title="Vitantonio Liuzzi">Liuzzi</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scott_Speed" title="Scott Speed">Speed</a>; both <a href="/wiki/Spyker_F1" title="Spyker F1">Spykers</a> of <a href="/wiki/Christijan_Albers" title="Christijan Albers">Albers</a> and <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Sutil</a> and David Coulthard joined Albers at the back of grid after his <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> suffered a gearbox problem.<sup id="cite_ref-Qualifying_-_Report_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Qualifying_-_Report-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Qualifying_-_Results_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Qualifying_-_Results-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The two <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyotas</a> of <a href="/wiki/Ralf_Schumacher" title="Ralf Schumacher">Ralf Schumacher</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a> left the pits first for Part Two of qualifying. Like in the first session, Ferrari were setting the pace, this time however it was Massa who was leading the way, with both their drivers setting times under 1:32:000, only to be split to Hamilton's McLaren. Button, who only just made the cut for the second round of qualifying, was slowest in the session, over two seconds slower than Massa's time. Joining the Brit was fellow <a href="/wiki/Honda_Racing_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Honda Racing F1">Honda</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a>; Ralf Schumacher; Davidson; <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> and Wurz.<sup id="cite_ref-Qualifying_-_Report_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Qualifying_-_Report-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Qualifying_-_Results_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Qualifying_-_Results-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hamilton and the two BMW Saubers were first out in the final Part of qualifying, with Massa setting the early pace. The final part of the session saw Massa improve his time, lapping with a time of 1:32.652. Hamilton came second, just under three tenths slower than Massa, putting him on the front row in his third Formula One race. Kimi Räikkönen and <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> made up row two, both BMW Saubers of Heidfeld and Kubica were fifth and sixth respectively; <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> made up row four and Jarno Trulli and <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> completed the top ten ninth and tenth respectively.<sup id="cite_ref-Qualifying_-_Report_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Qualifying_-_Report-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Qualifying_-_Results_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Qualifying_-_Results-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Bahrain_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:202px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Ralf_Schumacher_2007_(crop).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3f/Ralf_Schumacher_2007_%28crop%29.jpg/200px-Ralf_Schumacher_2007_%28crop%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="200" height="78" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3f/Ralf_Schumacher_2007_%28crop%29.jpg/300px-Ralf_Schumacher_2007_%28crop%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3f/Ralf_Schumacher_2007_%28crop%29.jpg/400px-Ralf_Schumacher_2007_%28crop%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="403" data-file-height="158" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Ralf_Schumacher_2007_(crop).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Ralf_Schumacher" title="Ralf Schumacher">Ralf Schumacher</a> in the pits en route to 12th.</div></div></div>
<p>At the start, the two leaders maintained their positions with Alonso passing Räikkönen into the second corner to take third. Further back, <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scott_Speed" title="Scott Speed">Scott Speed</a> were involved in a collision in turn four, with Button and Speed out on the spot, while Sutil managed to get back to the pits. The Safety Car was brought out to clear up the wreckage.
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:177px;"><a href="/wiki/File:2007_Bahrain_GP_podium.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fa/2007_Bahrain_GP_podium.jpg/175px-2007_Bahrain_GP_podium.jpg" decoding="async" width="175" height="131" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fa/2007_Bahrain_GP_podium.jpg/263px-2007_Bahrain_GP_podium.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fa/2007_Bahrain_GP_podium.jpg/350px-2007_Bahrain_GP_podium.jpg 2x" data-file-width="800" data-file-height="600" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:2007_Bahrain_GP_podium.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The podium ceremony (from left to right: <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Luca_Baldisserri" title="Luca Baldisserri">Luca Baldisserri</a>.)</div></div></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Vitantonio_Liuzzi" title="Vitantonio Liuzzi">Vitantonio Liuzzi</a> had a drive-through penalty on lap nine for overtaking under a safety car. On the same lap, Rosberg had an off-track excursion, losing a place to Coulthard in the process. The front two stayed in the same positions after the first round of stops, but Räikkönen got back past Alonso to take third.
</p><p>Alonso began to lose ground, and was passed by Heidfeld. He stayed behind the BMW for the remainder of the race. The Renaults also began to struggle, with Coulthard managing to overtake Fisichella. However, both Red Bulls retired in quick succession, promoting Trulli and Fisichella to the points.
</p><p>Massa won the Grand Prix, 2.3 seconds in front of rookie Hamilton.<sup id="cite_ref-Race_Report_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Race_Report-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Race_Results_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Race_Results-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the race, Alonso, Räikkönen and Hamilton were equal on points. The last time the top three were level on points was in <a href="/wiki/1950_Formula_One_season" title="1950 Formula One season">1950</a> after the <a href="/wiki/1950_Indianapolis_500" title="1950 Indianapolis 500">Indianapolis 500</a>. By finishing second, Hamilton became the first driver in Formula One history to finish on the podium in each of his first three World Championship Grands Prix; the previous record was two consecutive podium finishes, achieved by <a href="/wiki/Peter_Arundell" title="Peter Arundell">Peter Arundell</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1964_Formula_One_season" title="1964 Formula One season">1964 season</a>.
</p>
