<p>The <b>1969 Dutch Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_Zandvoort" title="Circuit Zandvoort">Zandvoort Circuit</a> on June 21, 1969. It was race 4 of 11 in both the <a href="/wiki/1969_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1969 World Championship of Drivers">1969 World Championship of Drivers</a> and the <a href="/wiki/1969_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1969 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1969 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>. The 90-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Equipe_Matra_Sports" title="Equipe Matra Sports">Matra</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Jackie_Stewart" title="Jackie Stewart">Jackie Stewart</a> after he started from second position. <a href="/wiki/Jo_Siffert" title="Jo Siffert">Jo Siffert</a> finished second for the <a href="/wiki/Team_Lotus" title="Team Lotus">Lotus</a> team and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Chris_Amon" title="Chris Amon">Chris Amon</a> came in third.
</p>
