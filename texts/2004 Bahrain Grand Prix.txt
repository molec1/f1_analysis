<p>The <b>2004 Bahrain Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 4 April 2004 at the <a href="/wiki/Bahrain_International_Circuit" title="Bahrain International Circuit">Bahrain International Circuit</a>. It was the first <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> race to be held in the Kingdom of <a href="/wiki/Bahrain" title="Bahrain">Bahrain</a>, and the <a href="/wiki/Middle_East" title="Middle East">Middle East</a>. The 57-lap race was the third round of the <a href="/wiki/2004_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2004 Formula One season">2004 Formula One season</a>. The race was won by <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a>. His teammate <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a> completed a 1-2 for the team, whilst <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> completed the podium for the <a href="/wiki/British_American_Racing" title="British American Racing">BAR</a> team by finishing in third position.
</p>
