<p>The <b>2014 British Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>2014 Formula 1 Santander British Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 6 July 2014 at the <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Silverstone" title="Silverstone">Silverstone</a>, United Kingdom before a crowd of 122,000 people. It was the ninth round of the <a href="/wiki/2014_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2014 Formula One World Championship">2014 Formula One World Championship</a>, and the 65th <a href="/wiki/British_Grand_Prix" title="British Grand Prix">British Grand Prix</a> to be held as part of the series. <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> team won the 52-lap race, from a sixth position start. <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> finished second, with <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> third for the <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> squad. It was Hamilton's fifth victory of 2014, his second at Silverstone, and the 27th of his career.
</p><p>Hamilton's teammate <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> recorded the fastest lap in qualifying to start from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>. The race was suspended for one hour, five minutes after a first-lap accident involving <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> left a section of guardrail barrier needing repair. Rosberg led for 18 laps after the restart as Hamilton moved to second, then made a <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit stop</a> for tyres, giving the lead to Hamilton for six laps. Hamilton took the lead for good when Rosberg retired from a <a href="/wiki/Transmission_(mechanics)" title="Transmission (mechanics)">gearbox</a> fault on the 29th lap; he won by more than &#x200b;<span class="frac nowrap"><sup>1</sup>&#8260;<sub>2</sub></span> a minute over Bottas. Ricciardo held off <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> in the final laps to take third.
</p><p>Rosberg's non-finish left him with a reduced lead of four <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Championship_points_scoring_systems" title="List of Formula One World Championship points scoring systems">points</a> over Hamilton in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a>. Ricciardo and <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> of Ferrari maintained third and fourth respectively, and Bottas passed Red Bull driver <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> for fifth. In the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a>, Mercedes held a 158-point lead over Red Bull and Ferrari was third. Williams overtook <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> for fourth with ten races left in the season.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The 2014 <a href="/wiki/British_Grand_Prix" title="British Grand Prix">British Grand Prix</a> was the 9th of the 19 races in the <a href="/wiki/2014_FIA_Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="2014 FIA Formula One World Championship">2014 FIA Formula One World Championship</a> and the 65th edition of the event as a round of the World Championship. It was held at the 18-turn 5.891&#160;km (3.660&#160;mi) <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Silverstone" title="Silverstone">Silverstone</a>, <a href="/wiki/Northamptonshire" title="Northamptonshire">Northamptonshire</a>, England on 6 July.<sup id="cite_ref-FIAPreview_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FIAPreview-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyres" title="Formula One tyres">Tyre</a> supplier <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> brought a white-banded Medium and orange-banded Hard dry-compounds to the race as well as two <a href="/wiki/Rain_tyre" title="Rain tyre">wet-weather compounds</a>, the intermediate and full wet tyres.<sup id="cite_ref-F1RacingStats_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-F1RacingStats-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The <a href="/wiki/Drag_reduction_system" title="Drag reduction system">drag reduction system</a> (DRS), an adjustable flap at the back of each car that helps with overtaking when activated, had two activation zones for the race: one on the Wellington Straight linking the Arena and Brookands turns, and one on the Hangar Straight between Chapel and Stowe corners.<sup id="cite_ref-SkySportsPreview_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SkySportsPreview-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> After the <a href="/wiki/2013_British_Grand_Prix" title="2013 British Grand Prix">2013 race</a>, <a href="/wiki/Artificial_turf" title="Artificial turf">artificial grass</a> was removed at the exits at three corner, the inside side barrier at the entrance to Brooklands corner was enlargened, and circuit drainage was improved.<sup id="cite_ref-FIAPreview_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FIAPreview-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After the <a href="/wiki/2014_Austrian_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Austrian Grand Prix">Austrian Grand Prix</a> two weeks earlier, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> with 165 <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Championship_points_scoring_systems" title="List of Formula One World Championship points scoring systems">points</a>, ahead of his second-placed teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> with 136 points. <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> of the <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> team was third with 83 points, and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> followed close behind in fourth with 79 points. <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>, in the second Red Bull car, was fifth with 60 points.<sup id="cite_ref-2014standings_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2014standings-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Mercedes led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a> with 301 points with Red Bull second with 143 points. Ferrari (98 points), <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> (87) and <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> (85) battled for third position.<sup id="cite_ref-2014standings_5-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2014standings-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Susie_Wolff_Williams_FW36_Silverstone_2014.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a8/Susie_Wolff_Williams_FW36_Silverstone_2014.jpg/220px-Susie_Wolff_Williams_FW36_Silverstone_2014.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="125" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a8/Susie_Wolff_Williams_FW36_Silverstone_2014.jpg/330px-Susie_Wolff_Williams_FW36_Silverstone_2014.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a8/Susie_Wolff_Williams_FW36_Silverstone_2014.jpg/440px-Susie_Wolff_Williams_FW36_Silverstone_2014.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2816" data-file-height="1596" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Susie_Wolff_Williams_FW36_Silverstone_2014.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Susie_Wolff" title="Susie Wolff">Susie Wolff</a> was the first woman to drive in a Formula One race weekend since <a href="/wiki/Giovanna_Amati" title="Giovanna Amati">Giovanna Amati</a> in 1992.</div></div></div>
<p>After a series of errors in the previous four rounds left Hamilton 29 points behind Rosberg, the Mercedes non-executive chairman <a href="/wiki/Niki_Lauda" title="Niki Lauda">Niki Lauda</a> suggested this would motivate Hamilton to perform better at the British Grand Prix and saw no reason to guide him in his driving, "Lewis will fight back, there is no worry on my case about this at all. He is not in any way disturbed. He will keep fighting. Don't worry."<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> said Williams intended to run well at Silverstone and sought to build on the momentum from Austria, "I am really looking forward to Silverstone. It's always a great event and means a lot to us as it's the team's home Grand Prix, so hopefully some of the guys from the factory can come up and support us as well."<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo said he was looking forward to the race as he liked the Silverstone track.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>There were four driver changes for the first practice session. Williams development driver <a href="/wiki/Susie_Wolff" title="Susie Wolff">Susie Wolff</a> drove for the team in lieu of Bottas, becoming the first woman to take part in a Formula One Grand Prix weekend since <a href="/wiki/Giovanna_Amati" title="Giovanna Amati">Giovanna Amati</a>'s attempted to qualify for the <a href="/wiki/1992_Brazilian_Grand_Prix" title="1992 Brazilian Grand Prix">1992 Brazilian Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Force India gave <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Juncadella" title="Daniel Juncadella">Daniel Juncadella</a>, the team's reserve and simulator driver and <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_motorsport" title="Mercedes-Benz in motorsport">Mercedes-Benz</a> <a href="/wiki/Deutsche_Tourenwagen_Masters" title="Deutsche Tourenwagen Masters">Deutsche Tourenwagen Masters</a> racer, his first Formula One race appearance in <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a>'s car at Silverstone.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Former <a href="/wiki/GP2_Series" title="GP2 Series">GP2 Series</a> competitor <a href="/wiki/Robin_Frijns" title="Robin Frijns">Robin Frijns</a> drove <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a> for the second time in the 2014 season; the <a href="/wiki/2014_Bahrain_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Bahrain Grand Prix">Bahrain Grand Prix</a> in April was the first.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Finally, and for the fourth time in 2014, <a href="/wiki/Giedo_van_der_Garde" title="Giedo van der Garde">Giedo van der Garde</a> took <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a> seat.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>

<p>Per the regulations for the 2014 season, three practice sessions were held: two 90-minute sessions on Friday and another 60-minute session on Saturday.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> In the first session, Rosberg set the fastest lap at 1 minute, 35.424 seconds. Hamilton, Alonso, Ricciardo, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>, Vettel, <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a>, <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a>, <a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a> were in positions two to ten.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportFP1_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportFP1-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Four minutes in, <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> ran onto artificial grass at the exit of Stowe corner and was sent backwards into a sideways spin, hitting the outside tyre barrier at 130&#160;mph (210&#160;km/h). His damaged <a href="/wiki/Williams_FW36" title="Williams FW36">Williams FW36</a> car stopped near the entry to the <a href="/wiki/Pit_stop" title="Pit stop">pit lane</a> and the session was halted for 17 minutes as <a href="/wiki/Motorsport_marshal" title="Motorsport marshal">track marshals</a> removed it. Later, an <a href="/wiki/Oil_pressure" title="Oil pressure">oil pressure</a> problem caused Wolff's engine to shut down after four laps, and <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Ericsson" title="Marcus Ericsson">Marcus Ericsson</a> spun at Becketts turn.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportFP1_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportFP1-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> After the session, Ricciardo and Rosberg were summoned to the stewards for overtaking Alonso and Kvyat under <a href="/wiki/Racing_flags#Red_flag" title="Racing flags">red flag</a> conditions. Both drivers were deemed to have driven safely under series regulations after data examination and their explanations convinced the stewards not to penalise them.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hamilton used soft compound tyres to record the fastest lap of the second session with an early time of 1 minute, 35.508 seconds; his running was curtailed because his engine cut out with &#x200b;<span class="frac nowrap"><sup>1</sup>&#8260;<sub>2</sub></span> an hour to go. Rosberg, Alonso, the Red Bull pair of Ricciardo and Vettel, Bottas, Button, Magunssen, Räikkönen and Vergne rounded out the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportFP2_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportFP2-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> A localised explosion tore through the left-hand side of Bottas' engine cover and loosened it with five minutes to go. Vergne's left-front wheel detached at Abbey corner and he had to stop at the side of the track.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportFP2_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportFP2-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> On Saturday morning, FIA race director <a href="/wiki/Charlie_Whiting" title="Charlie Whiting">Charlie Whiting</a> adopted a "zero tolerance policy" and cautioned drivers not to run wide leaving Copse and Club without being forced or their lap times would be deleted in a bid to enforce stricter regulations on driving off the track.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Overnight rain made the track wet and a north-westerly wind was a factor in the final session; four drivers chose not to set a timed lap and some went off the slippery track.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Vettel set the fastest lap of 1 minute, 52.522 seconds, with his teammate Ricciardo second. <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a>, <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a>, Sutil, Kvyat, Magnussen, Button, Bottas and Räikkönen completed the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-ESPNFP3_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ESPNFP3-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a> broke his front wing in an impact with the barrier at Stowe corner. Sutil also avoided hitting the wall at Stowe turn and exited the corner's <a href="/wiki/Run-off_area" title="Run-off area">gravel trap</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-ESPNFP3_22-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ESPNFP3-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Nico_Rosberg_%E2%80%93_2014_British_Grand_Prix_%E2%80%93_Qualifying_on_Saturday.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/02/Nico_Rosberg_%E2%80%93_2014_British_Grand_Prix_%E2%80%93_Qualifying_on_Saturday.jpg/220px-Nico_Rosberg_%E2%80%93_2014_British_Grand_Prix_%E2%80%93_Qualifying_on_Saturday.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="124" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/02/Nico_Rosberg_%E2%80%93_2014_British_Grand_Prix_%E2%80%93_Qualifying_on_Saturday.jpg/330px-Nico_Rosberg_%E2%80%93_2014_British_Grand_Prix_%E2%80%93_Qualifying_on_Saturday.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/0/02/Nico_Rosberg_%E2%80%93_2014_British_Grand_Prix_%E2%80%93_Qualifying_on_Saturday.jpg/440px-Nico_Rosberg_%E2%80%93_2014_British_Grand_Prix_%E2%80%93_Qualifying_on_Saturday.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3101" data-file-height="1747" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Nico_Rosberg_%E2%80%93_2014_British_Grand_Prix_%E2%80%93_Qualifying_on_Saturday.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> had the fourth <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> of his season and the eighth of his career.</div></div></div>
<p>Saturday afternoon's <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#Q" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">qualifying session</a> was divided into three parts. The first part ran for 18 minutes, eliminating cars that finished qualifying 17th or below. The <a href="/wiki/107%25_rule" title="107% rule">107% rule</a> was in effect, requiring drivers to reach a time within 107 per cent of the fastest lap to qualify. The second part lasted 15 minutes, eliminating cars that finished 11th to 16th. The final session lasted 12 minutes and determined <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> to tenth. Cars who progressed to the final session were not allowed to change tyres for the race's start, using the tyres with which they set their best lap times in the second session.<sup id="cite_ref-regulations_13-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-regulations-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> A damp track worsened as rain began to fall with increasing, if intermittent, severity; all drivers used <a href="/wiki/Rain_tyre" title="Rain tyre">intermediate compound tyres</a> for the first two sessions. The rain stopped at the start of the third session and drivers used dry tyres in its first minutes. Rain soon returned and they drove to the pit lane. Several drivers returned to the circuit with three minutes left for one last try at improving their laps.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg took his fourth pole position of the season and the eighth of his career with a 1-minute, 35.766 seconds lap.<sup id="cite_ref-APQuali_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-APQuali-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> He was joined on the <a href="/wiki/Glossary_of_motorsport_terms#G" title="Glossary of motorsport terms">grid</a>'s front row by Vettel who bowed out of his first try due to the rain.<sup id="cite_ref-APQuali_24-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-APQuali-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportQuali_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportQuali-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Third-place Button went wide at Aintree corner and <a href="/wiki/Understeer_and_oversteer" title="Understeer and oversteer">oversteered</a> on the run to the Hangar Straight and began from the top three at Silverstone for the first time since the <a href="/wiki/2005_British_Grand_Prix" title="2005 British Grand Prix">2005 British Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-ESPNQuotes_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ESPNQuotes-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Hülkenberg in fourth briefly held pole position as he was the first driver to improve his lap on the drying track. Magnussen was fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportQuali_25-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportQuali-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton provisionally held the pole position after all first timed laps; he fell to sixth after he abandoned his final attempt because it was slower and he felt the track was not dry enough to improve his lap times.<sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Lotus_E22_Grosjean_Silverstone_2014_(1).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/26/Lotus_E22_Grosjean_Silverstone_2014_%281%29.jpg/220px-Lotus_E22_Grosjean_Silverstone_2014_%281%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="133" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/26/Lotus_E22_Grosjean_Silverstone_2014_%281%29.jpg/330px-Lotus_E22_Grosjean_Silverstone_2014_%281%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/26/Lotus_E22_Grosjean_Silverstone_2014_%281%29.jpg/440px-Lotus_E22_Grosjean_Silverstone_2014_%281%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2101" data-file-height="1266" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Lotus_E22_Grosjean_Silverstone_2014_(1).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> was the first of the 22 drivers not to qualify within the top ten positions.</div></div></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> in seventh ran wide at Chapel turn and lost heat in his tyres. Eighth-placed Ricciardo did not anticipate the improvement in track conditions and he remained in the pit lane. The Toro Rosso duo of Kvyat and Vergne were ninth and tenth. Grosjean was the fastest driver not to advance to the final session, because a <a href="/wiki/Racing_flags#Yellow_flag" title="Racing flags">yellow flag</a> at Brooklands corner required him to slow. <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia</a> fuelled their cars for the second session and its reaction to the changing weather saw Bianchi qualify 12th and <a href="/wiki/Max_Chilton" title="Max Chilton">Max Chilton</a> 13th.<sup id="cite_ref-ESPNQuotes_27-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ESPNQuotes-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> However, Chilton incurred a five-place grid penalty for changing his <a href="/wiki/Transmission_(mechanics)" title="Transmission (mechanics)">gearbox</a> after the third practice session.<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban Gutiérrez">Esteban Gutiérrez</a> lost traction in his vehicle on a kerb leaving Brooklands corner and was sent backwards into a guardrail barrier at Luffield turn. He was 14th and also incurred a ten-place grid penalty for an unsafe release at the preceding round in Austria.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportQuali_25-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportQuali-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Q_result_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Q_result-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Maldonado in 15th had a fuel pressure problem in the second session requiring him to stop his car. He was demoted five places for not providing the FIA with at least 1&#160;l (0.22&#160;imp&#160;gal; 0.26&#160;US&#160;gal) of fuel for analysis.<sup id="cite_ref-31" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Hence, Sutil started 13th after he lost control of his car's rear and got beached in a gravel trap. A strategy error left the Williams pair of Bottas and Massa to drive on a wet track on dry compound tyres and took 14th and 15th.<sup id="cite_ref-ESPNQuotes_27-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ESPNQuotes-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Alonso in 16th (who spun at Brooklands turn) and his teammate Räikkönen in 17th were also caught out by the weather and used the incorrect tyre compound, after Ferrari spent longer than most teams deciding their strategy.<sup id="cite_ref-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> The Caterham cars of Ericsson and <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a> were slow enough to transgress the 107 per cent rule. The stewards granted them dispensation to start the race.<sup id="cite_ref-ESPNQuotes_27-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ESPNQuotes-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Q_result_30-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Q_result-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_crashes_2014_British_GP.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/36/R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_crashes_2014_British_GP.jpg/220px-R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_crashes_2014_British_GP.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="100" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/36/R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_crashes_2014_British_GP.jpg/330px-R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_crashes_2014_British_GP.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/36/R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_crashes_2014_British_GP.jpg/440px-R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_crashes_2014_British_GP.jpg 2x" data-file-width="4288" data-file-height="1947" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen_crashes_2014_British_GP.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> crashed on the opening lap of the race. Crash barrier damage forced its suspension for an hour.</div></div></div>
<p>The weather at the start was dry and sunny with the air temperature between 17 and 19&#160;°C (63 and 66&#160;°F) and the track temperature from 33 to 39&#160;°C (91 to 102&#160;°F);<sup id="cite_ref-34" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> no rain was forecast.<sup id="cite_ref-STLiveReport_35-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-STLiveReport-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> The 52-lap race began before 120,000 people at 13:00 local time,<sup id="cite_ref-FIAPreview_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-FIAPreview-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AFPReport_36-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AFPReport-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> with Rosberg making a brisk start to hold the lead into Abbey corner. Vettel in second had a slow start and Button and Magnussen passed him.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCSportTicker_37-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCSportTicker-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton moved from sixth to fourth as Massa stalled from 15th.<sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> On the Wellington Straight, the rear of Räikkönen's <a href="/wiki/Ferrari_F14_T" title="Ferrari F14 T">Ferrari F14 T</a> car got loose and he drove onto the run-off area. He identified a run-off area without grass to reemerge on the track, rejoining at an angle at 150&#160;mph (240&#160;km/h), causing him to hit a bump on a grass verge and unsettling his car. He was vaulted right at an angle and into the guardrail barrier close to the pillar of an overhead bridge in a 47&#160;<i>g<sub>0</sub></i> (460&#160;m/s<sup>2</sup>) impact.<sup id="cite_ref-KimiCrash_40-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-KimiCrash-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-IndyKimi_41-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IndyKimi-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> The Ferrari's right-front tyre flew by Chilton, who had to duck his head to avoid being struck; his front wing and front-left <a href="/wiki/Disc_brake" title="Disc brake">brake duct</a> were damaged.<sup id="cite_ref-KimiCrash_40-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-KimiCrash-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-42" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<p>Räikkönen ricocheted across the track and into the path of Kobayashi and Massa.<sup id="cite_ref-KimiCrash_40-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-KimiCrash-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup> Kobayashi grazed the front of the Ferrari and veered onto the grass; Massa applied the brakes upon seeing the impact, trying to spin to the right to avoid a <a href="/wiki/Side_collision" title="Side collision">T-bone collision</a>. The rear of Massa's car made contact with the front of Räikkönen's.<sup id="cite_ref-KimiCrash_40-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-KimiCrash-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-IndyKimi_41-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IndyKimi-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-GuardianKimi_43-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GuardianKimi-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> A track marshal assisted Räikkönen, who had hip and heel injuries, out of his car and into an ambulance for transport to the circuit's medical centre. The <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a> was deployed for a lap until Whiting discovered the barriers were dented and needed repair, and stopped the race.<sup id="cite_ref-IndyKimi_41-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IndyKimi-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-GuardianKimi_43-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GuardianKimi-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> Every driver was directed to stop on the grid. Damage to Massa's rear <a href="/wiki/Suspension_(vehicle)" class="mw-redirect" title="Suspension (vehicle)">suspension</a> necessitated his retirement.<sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> In the meantime, Chilton incurred a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_regulations#Penalties" title="Formula One regulations">drive-through penalty</a> for entering the pit lane under red flag conditions.<sup id="cite_ref-IndyKimi_41-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-IndyKimi-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Repairs to the barrier prevented the resumption of the race for an hour, five minutes until course workers were finished at 14:05 local time. The race was restarted behind the safety car with the drivers in the positions held before the suspension.<sup id="cite_ref-STLiveReport_35-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-STLiveReport-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Both Red Bulls switched to the hard-compound tyres on the grid during the stoppage and Alonso used the softer compounds to improve the possibility of making a solitary pit stop by driving on the more durable harder tyres later on. Rosberg was first, followed by the McLarens of Button and Magnussen.<sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Bottas overtook Kvyat at Brooklands corner to move into ninth and Hamilton began to challenge Magnussen for third.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> On lap three, Hamilton drew alongside Magnussen through Woodcote turn and passed him for third entering Copse corner as Magnussen ran off the track. That same lap, Bottas overtook Ricciardo on the Hangar Straight for seventh as Alonso passed Gutiérrez for twelfth.<sup id="cite_ref-STLiveReport_35-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-STLiveReport-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> During the next lap, Hamilton drew alongside Button and overtook him on the outside at Brooklands turn for second.<sup id="cite_ref-STLiveReport_35-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-STLiveReport-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-BBCSportTicker_37-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCSportTicker-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Alonso passed Sutil for eleventh before Brooklands corner and then Bianchi for tenth as Hülkenberg lost sixth to Bottas in Stowe turn on the fifth lap.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> DRS was enabled on lap six. That lap, Alonso used DRS to pass Kvyat on the outside leaving Woodcote corner and into Luffield turn for ninth.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCSportTicker_37-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCSportTicker-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo overtook Hülkenberg at Stowe corner and the subsequent loss of momentum allowed Alonso to get by him around the outside at Vale corner for eighth on lap seven.<sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Marcus_Ericsson_2014_British_GP_001.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6e/Marcus_Ericsson_2014_British_GP_001.jpg/220px-Marcus_Ericsson_2014_British_GP_001.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="129" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6e/Marcus_Ericsson_2014_British_GP_001.jpg/330px-Marcus_Ericsson_2014_British_GP_001.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6e/Marcus_Ericsson_2014_British_GP_001.jpg/440px-Marcus_Ericsson_2014_British_GP_001.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2381" data-file-height="1399" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Marcus_Ericsson_2014_British_GP_001.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Marcus_Ericsson" title="Marcus Ericsson">Marcus Ericsson</a> retired on the 12th lap with a <a href="/wiki/Suspension_(vehicle)" class="mw-redirect" title="Suspension (vehicle)">suspension</a> failure on his <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>On lap eight, Alonso used DRS to pass Hülkenberg on the outside at Brooklands turn after a short battle.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCSportTicker_37-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCSportTicker-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Further back Sutil passed Bianchi for eleventh as Ricciardo battled Hülkenberg for eighth at Luffield turn on lap ten but could not pass due to the latter's higher straight-line speed. Ricciardo tried again into Stowe corner and succeeded.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> On the inside at Club turn on the same lap, Gutiérrez attempted to pass Maldonado, who sought to overtake Bianchi. Gutiérrez hit Maldonado's left-hand side; contact continued through the corner and Maldonado was launched airborne off Gutiérrez's right-front tyre. Gutiérrez got beached in a gravel trap and retired with rear car damage.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCSportTicker_37-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCSportTicker-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> The first pit stops for tyres occurred on lap 11 as Vettel entered the pit lane to try and pass the McLarens; the faster Bottas, behind him, was thus freed to try to catch Magnussen.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCSportTicker_37-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCSportTicker-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> On the next lap, Ericsson's suspension failed on the Brooklands corner kerb and he drove to the pit lane to retire.<sup id="cite_ref-STLiveReport_35-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-STLiveReport-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-SkyDriverQuotes_44-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SkyDriverQuotes-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> Alonso incurred a five-second stop-and-go penalty on the 13th lap because he was deemed to have been out of position on his grid slot for the restart.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>By the 14th lap Bottas had caught Magnussen, and entering Stowe corner used his DRS and better speed to pass him on the outside for fourth.<sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Alonso overtook Magnussen around the outside of Stowe corner for fifth as Ricciardo made a pit stop for the soft compound tyres on lap 15.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Two laps later, Bottas passed Button at Stowe turn for third.<sup id="cite_ref-STLiveReport_35-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-STLiveReport-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-BBCSportTicker_37-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCSportTicker-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> On that lap, Ricciardo passed Sutil on the approach to Stowe corner for ninth.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg made his first pit stop from the lead on the 18th lap. Hamilton led for six laps; the plan was for him to run the middle stint on the hard-compound tyres before his own stop at the conclusion of lap 24.<sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> In the meantime, Rosberg began developing <a href="/wiki/Transmission_(mechanics)" title="Transmission (mechanics)">gearbox</a> problems on lap 20.<sup id="cite_ref-SkyDriverQuotes_44-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SkyDriverQuotes-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> As Alonso battled with Button, a stone lodged in his rear wing slot gap that remained there for the rest of the Grand Prix. He twice oversteered coming through Becketts corner as Ricciardo passed Hülkenberg for eighth. Rosberg had increased his advantage over Hamilton, whose pit stop lasted 1.7 seconds than expected as his hard-compound left-rear wheel was slow to install at his pit box, to six seconds.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCSportTicker_37-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCSportTicker-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On the 25th lap, Vettel passed Magnussen for sixth at the exit of Luffield turn. Three laps later, Alonso overtook Hülkenberg through Copse corner for eighth after the former took his five-second stop-and-go penalty.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> As Hamilton reduced Rosberg's lead from six to four seconds within three laps on his newer tyres, the latter had gear-selection problems.<sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg attempted to change his gearbox's settings; this did not work as he slowed at Village corner and Hamilton took the lead on the Wellington Straight on lap 29.<sup id="cite_ref-STLiveReport_35-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-STLiveReport-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-SkyDriverQuotes_44-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SkyDriverQuotes-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg steered onto the grass down the approach to Becketts corner, and retired due to his gearbox.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Attention then focused on the battle for third. Ricciardo's pit stop allowed him to change his strategy into a one-stopper, as tyre degradation rates were less than during the two Friday practice sessions.<sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Bottas made his second pit stop on lap 32 and emerged in third behind Vettel.<sup id="cite_ref-STLiveReport_35-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-STLiveReport-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> Two laps later, Bottas glimpsed space and passed Vettel for second on the Hangar Straight.<sup id="cite_ref-STLiveReport_35-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-STLiveReport-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Vettel_vs_Alonso_(14714903060).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/dc/Vettel_vs_Alonso_%2814714903060%29.jpg/220px-Vettel_vs_Alonso_%2814714903060%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="117" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/dc/Vettel_vs_Alonso_%2814714903060%29.jpg/330px-Vettel_vs_Alonso_%2814714903060%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/dc/Vettel_vs_Alonso_%2814714903060%29.jpg/440px-Vettel_vs_Alonso_%2814714903060%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="7202" data-file-height="3843" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Vettel_vs_Alonso_(14714903060).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The battle between <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> for fifth in the final laps of the race.</div></div></div>
<p>Vettel immediately made a second pit stop at the end of the lap. He rejoined ahead of Alonso in fifth place.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Alonso twice passed Vettel around the outside at Copse corner during a duel for fifth between laps 35 and 36.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCSportTicker_37-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCSportTicker-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> On lap 37, Vettel had optimum <a href="/wiki/Operating_temperature" title="Operating temperature">tyre temperature</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> and used DRS on the Wellington Straight to pass Alonso on the outside under braking. Alonso then went across the front of Vettel leaving Brooklands corner to retain fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Vettel saw space to pass Alonso at Brooklands corner on lap 42, but the latter blocked his pass on the <a href="/wiki/Racing_line" title="Racing line">racing line</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-STLiveReport_35-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-STLiveReport-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> Three laps later, Vettel turned to the outside of Luffield corner. Although he did not pass Alonso, Vettel entered the straight faster than the former who reacted by turning to the inside past the former Grand Prix pit lane to block him.<sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> On the 47th lap, Vettel turned to the outside at Brooklands corner to draw alongside Alonso through Woodcote turn but Alonso kept fifth by braking later than him.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCSportTicker_37-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCSportTicker-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>

<p>Vettel was aided by the rear of Alonso's car stepping out into Copse corner and overtook him on the outside for fifth. Once through, Vettel pulled away from Alonso.<sup id="cite_ref-BBCSportTicker_37-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCSportTicker-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> In the meantime, Button drew closer to Ricciardo; he had made a pit stop earlier than the latter, and his two-stop strategy left him the faster of the two.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Maldonado's <a href="/wiki/Exhaust_system" title="Exhaust system">exhaust</a> failed on the 51st lap,<sup id="cite_ref-BBCSportTicker_37-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBCSportTicker-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:0_45-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup> and he retired at the side of the track at Abbey turn with smoke billowing from the rear of his car.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Unchallenged in the final 23 laps, Hamilton achieved his second victory at Silverstone, his fifth of the season, and the 27th of his career to equal three-time World Champion <a href="/wiki/Jackie_Stewart" title="Jackie Stewart">Jackie Stewart</a>'s <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_Grand_Prix_winners#By_driver" title="List of Formula One Grand Prix winners">race wins total</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-AFPReport_36-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AFPReport-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> Bottas followed 30.135 seconds later in second and Ricciardo was another 16 seconds behind in third. Button could not get close enough to pass Ricciardo and finished fourth. Vettel, Alonso and Magnussen were in positions five to seven.<sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> Hülkenberg held off Kvyat in ninth to take eighth and Vergne was tenth.<sup id="cite_ref-AutosportLiveRace_39-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AutosportLiveRace-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> The final finishers were Pérez, Grosjean, Sutil, Bianchi, Kobayashi, Chilton and Maldonado.<sup id="cite_ref-HughesReport_38-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HughesReport-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:3rd_Place_-_Daniel_Ricciardo_(14918098746).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9b/3rd_Place_-_Daniel_Ricciardo_%2814918098746%29.jpg/220px-3rd_Place_-_Daniel_Ricciardo_%2814918098746%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="146" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9b/3rd_Place_-_Daniel_Ricciardo_%2814918098746%29.jpg/330px-3rd_Place_-_Daniel_Ricciardo_%2814918098746%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9b/3rd_Place_-_Daniel_Ricciardo_%2814918098746%29.jpg/440px-3rd_Place_-_Daniel_Ricciardo_%2814918098746%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="4288" data-file-height="2848" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:3rd_Place_-_Daniel_Ricciardo_(14918098746).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> celebrating finishing in third place on the podium.</div></div></div>
<p>At the podium interviews, conducted by former driver <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a>, Hamilton said that his weekend had demonstrated perseverance, "Yesterday wasn’t a case of giving up, I didn’t think I could do the lap, I was really shocked that the last sector was so fast. But coming here today I had my family with, just focused, the support from the fans, as I said, just spurred me on, and I really couldn't have done it without them."<sup id="cite_ref-PressConf_46-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PressConf-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup> Bottas said he was enjoyed to pass other drivers and was complimentary of his team and car, "The plan was to come as high as possible, as high as the pace of the car is giving the possibility. I think as a team we made the most out of it."<sup id="cite_ref-PressConf_46-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PressConf-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo spoke of his feeling that had the race lasted one more lap, Button would have passed him.<sup id="cite_ref-PressConf_46-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PressConf-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup> In the subsequent press conference, Hamilton said the fans' positive attitude helped him and that the win had no comparison to his one in <a href="/wiki/2008_British_Grand_Prix" title="2008 British Grand Prix">2008</a> as that race was held on a wet track.<sup id="cite_ref-PressConf_46-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PressConf-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup> Bottas said Williams predicted Mercedes and Red Bull would be strong opponents and the team targeted points not the podium.<sup id="cite_ref-PressConf_46-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PressConf-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo stated Red Bull restarted on the hard tyres for track position and called his third-place result "one of my best podiums" of the season.<sup id="cite_ref-PressConf_46-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-PressConf-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Räikkönen was declared fit for the following <a href="/wiki/2014_German_Grand_Prix" title="2014 German Grand Prix">German Grand Prix</a>, and said he felt the crash was not his largest and hoped to avoid such incidents in the future.<sup id="cite_ref-47" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-47">&#91;47&#93;</a></sup> Mercedes non-executive chairman <a href="/wiki/Niki_Lauda" title="Niki Lauda">Niki Lauda</a> claimed that the delay to repair the damaged wall was too long, unnecessary, and an over-regulation by Formula One. "This over-nursing of F1, being over cautious, over-controlling and over-regulating, drives me mad. This little guardrail issue is another example. There are too many people involved in making F1 as safe as the roads, which is wrong. They should have fixed it quickly, done something instantly, and then 10–15 minutes later the race would have gone on."<sup id="cite_ref-48" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-48">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup> Massa stated his belief that Räikkönen might not have crashed had he been more cautious in slowing and remerging onto the track.<sup id="cite_ref-49" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-49">&#91;49&#93;</a></sup> The FIA rejected suggestions Räikkönen should have been penalised because it decided he did not rejoin unsafely. Whiting responded to Lauda by noting safety was enhanced following Massa's <a href="/wiki/2009_Hungarian_Grand_Prix" title="2009 Hungarian Grand Prix">2009 Hungarian Grand Prix</a> injury from being struck in the helmet by a <a href="/wiki/Coil_spring" title="Coil spring">spring</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-50" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-50">&#91;50&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Alonso told the press he expected to lose the battle with Vettel because his car's rear-wing aerodynamic deficiencies led it to not operate correctly, and Ferrari contemplated retiring him. "Vettel overtook me by going out of the track. He finished in front, but I was very proud of my 13 laps. If he had not passed me on the lap he did it would have been the next one because we were in slow motion and they were at real speed."<sup id="cite_ref-ALOVETDuel_51-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ALOVETDuel-51">&#91;51&#93;</a></sup> Vettel described a series of radio transmissions where he complained of a series of suspected illegal manoeuvres such as driving off the track during his duel as "silly"; "I got the message that I should respect the limits and that he was complaining, so I was doing the same thing. I'm not sure who won the list-keeping. I think twice it was maybe a bit too harsh into Turn 6 [Brooklands], but it was good to get the move at the end."<sup id="cite_ref-ALOVETDuel_51-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ALOVETDuel-51">&#91;51&#93;</a></sup> Nevertheless, former driver <a href="/wiki/Allan_McNish" title="Allan McNish">Allan McNish</a> said on a <a href="/wiki/BBC_Sport" title="BBC Sport">BBC Sport</a> online column the battle was "some of the best racing you will ever see" and it demonstrated "two world champions having to really work for their living."<sup id="cite_ref-52" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-52">&#91;52&#93;</a></sup> Gutierrez was found to be "predominantly at fault" for his collision at Club corner on lap line, and incurred a five-place grid penalty for the following German Grand Prix and three penalty points on his <a href="/wiki/FIA_Super_Licence" title="FIA Super Licence">super licence</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-53" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-53">&#91;53&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The result reduced Rosberg's Drivers' Championship lead to four points over Hamilton. Ricciardo's third-place finish increased his points tally over fourth-place Alonso to eleven and Bottas' second-place finish advanced him past Vettel and from seventh to fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-2014standings_5-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2014standings-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> In the Constructors' Championship, Mercedes maintained its lead with 326 points; their advantage over Red Bull remained at 158 points. Ferrari stayed third while Williams overtook Force India for fourth with ten races left in the season.<sup id="cite_ref-2014standings_5-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2014standings-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
