<p>The <b>2014 Malaysian Grand Prix</b>, formally known as the <b>2014 Formula 1 <a href="/wiki/Petronas" title="Petronas">Petronas</a> Malaysia Grand Prix</b>,<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> that was held on 30 March 2014 at the <a href="/wiki/Sepang_International_Circuit" title="Sepang International Circuit">Sepang International Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Selangor" title="Selangor">Selangor</a>, <a href="/wiki/Malaysia" title="Malaysia">Malaysia</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Malaysian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Malaysian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Like the <a href="/wiki/2013_Malaysian_Grand_Prix" title="2013 Malaysian Grand Prix">2013 Malaysian Grand Prix</a>, tyre supplier <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> brought its orange-banded hard compound tyre as the harder "prime" tyre and the white-banded medium compound tyre as the softer "option" tyre.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Prior to the race, there was a minute's silence as a mark of respect to the passengers of <a href="/wiki/Malaysia_Airlines_Flight_370" title="Malaysia Airlines Flight 370">Malaysia Airlines Flight 370</a>, which disappeared over the <a href="/wiki/Indian_Ocean" title="Indian Ocean">Indian Ocean</a> three weeks prior. Drivers also carried messages on their cars and helmets.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Malaysian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> put his <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> on pole position, the 33rd of his career. He thus equalled <a href="/wiki/Jim_Clark" title="Jim Clark">Jim Clark</a> for most poles of a <a href="/wiki/Great_Britain" title="Great Britain">British</a> <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> driver. Behind him was <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> and Hamilton's teammate <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Malaysian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> dominated the race, flying away at the start. <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico HÃ¼lkenberg">Nico HÃ¼lkenberg</a> also had a good start from seventh place, briefly battling with <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi RÃ¤ikkÃ¶nen">Kimi RÃ¤ikkÃ¶nen</a> before getting stuck up behind <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>. <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio PÃ©rez">Sergio PÃ©rez</a> did not start the race due to a gearbox issue.
</p><p>There was a first lap incident when <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a> collided with one another, effectively ending the race for both of the drivers when they received mechanical complications. Bianchi would later receive a penalty for his actions despite Marussia officials' claims that the incident was due to a collision with Toro Rosso's <a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Ãric Vergne">Jean-Ãric Vergne</a> hitting the back of the Marussia. At the end of the first lap, the top four&#160;â Hamilton, <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> and <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a>&#160;â had effectively escaped the rest of the pack and the grid was unchanged until the last ten laps of the race.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>HÃ¼lkenberg fought a lengthy battle with Alonso for fourth place, ultimately losing it due to Alonso's fresh tyres, as <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> were the only team in the top five to have successfully executed a two-stop pit strategy, while the others stuck to three stops. <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>, <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> battled for sixth place, during which Massa received the radio message, "Felipe, Valtteri is faster than you," reminding him of his years as a number-two driver for <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Both the <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Saubers</a> of <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> and <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban GutiÃ©rrez">Esteban GutiÃ©rrez</a> had retired after Sutil experienced an electrical failure and GutiÃ©rrez experienced a gearbox failure, preventing him from getting out of neutral gear. Ricciardo was having a good race until his third pit-stop, when he was released before his left-front tyre was correctly fastened. He then had to be pushed back into the pits. He then experienced the failure of his front wing, leading him to make the decision to retire.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>RÃ¤ikkÃ¶nen had a terrible race from the start after <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a>'s McLaren clipped his right rear tyre going into the first corner on lap 2, causing a puncture. He ultimately finished twelfth, 0.1 seconds behind the Lotus of <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a>. Hamilton led every lap of the race and set the fastest lap and thus took the first <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_driver_records#Grand_Slam" title="List of Formula One driver records">Grand Chelem</a> of his career, giving <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> their first 1-2 finish since the <a href="/wiki/1955_Italian_Grand_Prix" title="1955 Italian Grand Prix">1955 Italian Grand Prix</a>, after Rosberg finished second and Vettel third. Alonso came in fourth after a last-lap battle with HÃ¼lkenberg who finished fifth, almost forty seconds ahead of McLaren's <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a>, who was able to hold off both of Williams' drivers, Massa and Bottas, who finished seventh and eighth respectively giving Williams a one-point lead over Force India. Magnussen finished ninth for McLaren and the top ten was completed by <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a> of <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>. <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a>  and <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Ericsson" title="Marcus Ericsson">Marcus Ericsson</a> finished thirteenth and fourteenth for <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a>, effectively putting them ahead of <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia</a> in the Constructors' Championship standings.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2014_Malaysian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> was given a 10-place grid penalty for the <a href="/wiki/2014_Bahrain_Grand_Prix" title="2014 Bahrain Grand Prix">Bahrain Grand Prix</a> after the <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a> team released his car in an unsafe manner from his pit box.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a> both received two penalty points each, along with <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> after Bottas was accused of impeding Ricciardo during qualifying.<sup id="cite_ref-Bottas_PP_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Bottas_PP-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
