<p>The <b>1995 Portuguese Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>XXIV Grande Prémio de Portugal</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 24 September 1995 at the <a href="/wiki/Aut%C3%B3dromo_do_Estoril" class="mw-redirect" title="Autódromo do Estoril">Autódromo do Estoril</a>, <a href="/wiki/Estoril" title="Estoril">Estoril</a>, <a href="/wiki/Portugal" title="Portugal">Portugal</a>. It was the thirteenth&#160;race of the <a href="/wiki/1995_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="1995 Formula One season">1995 Formula One season</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pg198-199_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pg198-199-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The 71-lap race was the first Formula One win for <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> of the <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a> team after starting from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>. <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> was second in a <a href="/wiki/Benetton_Formula" title="Benetton Formula">Benetton</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Damon_Hill" title="Damon Hill">Damon Hill</a> third in the latter Williams car.<sup id="cite_ref-formula1.com_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formula1.com-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> After several controversial incidents previously involving Schumacher and Hill in the 1995 season, including collisions at Silverstone, Spa and Monza (the latter two of which saw confrontations between the two drivers respectively on the podium and at track-side), Schumacher and Hill shook hands with each other at the podium presentation following this race.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1995_Portuguese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1995_Portuguese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>To optimise their chances of winning at the <a href="/wiki/Aut%C3%B3dromo_do_Estoril" class="mw-redirect" title="Autódromo do Estoril">Autódromo do Estoril</a>, Williams brought an upgraded chassis to the race, a "B" specification of their <a href="/wiki/Williams_FW17" title="Williams FW17">FW17</a> car. The upgraded chassis would be used throughout the remainder of the season.<sup id="cite_ref-pg201_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pg201-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice_and_qualifying">Practice and qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1995_Portuguese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Practice and qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Two practice sessions were held before the race; the first was held on Friday morning and the second on Saturday morning. Both sessions lasted 1&#160;hour and 45&#160;minutes with weather conditions dry throughout.<sup id="cite_ref-atlas-pacificmain_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-atlas-pacificmain-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Häkkinen set the fastest time in the first session, posting a lap of 1:23.073, one-tenth of a second quicker than Hill and Schumacher, in second and third places respectively. Coulthard was fourth, with the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> cars fifth and sixth fastest; <a href="/wiki/Gerhard_Berger" title="Gerhard Berger">Gerhard Berger</a> ahead of <a href="/wiki/Jean_Alesi" title="Jean Alesi">Jean Alesi</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-pg198-199_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pg198-199-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Häkkinen was eleventh in the second practice session, two seconds slower than Hill, who was fastest with a time of 1:21.443. Coulthard was second in the Williams, eight-tenths of a second behind Hill. Schumacher was third, with Alesi fourth, both over a second behind Hill. <a href="/wiki/Heinz-Harald_Frentzen" title="Heinz-Harald Frentzen">Heinz-Harald Frentzen</a> in a <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a> was fifth with <a href="/wiki/Martin_Brundle" title="Martin Brundle">Martin Brundle</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Equipe_Ligier" title="Equipe Ligier">Ligier</a>, <a href="/wiki/Eddie_Irvine" title="Eddie Irvine">Eddie Irvine</a> in a <a href="/wiki/Jordan_Grand_Prix" title="Jordan Grand Prix">Jordan</a> and Berger rounding out the top eight positions.<sup id="cite_ref-pg198-199_1-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pg198-199-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1995_Portuguese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>As the race began from its <a href="/wiki/Standing_start" title="Standing start">standing start</a>, <a href="/wiki/Ukyo_Katayama" title="Ukyo Katayama">Ukyo Katayama</a> who qualified in 16th moved into the racing path of <a href="/wiki/Luca_Badoer" title="Luca Badoer">Luca Badoer</a>, whose <a href="/wiki/Minardi" title="Minardi">Minardi</a> began in 18th. The contact caused Katayama's <a href="/wiki/Tyrrell_Racing" title="Tyrrell Racing">Tyrrell</a> to launch airborne and spin several times upside down along the start/finish straight. Katayama was extracted from the car and hospitalized for two days<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> with what was later diagnosed with a strained neck and bruising in the crash.<sup id="cite_ref-pg194_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pg194-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-grandprix-katayama_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-grandprix-katayama-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
