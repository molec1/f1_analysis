<p>The <b>2017 Azerbaijan Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> that was held on 25 June 2017 at the <a href="/wiki/Baku_City_Circuit" title="Baku City Circuit">Baku City Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Baku" title="Baku">Baku</a>, <a href="/wiki/Azerbaijan" title="Azerbaijan">Azerbaijan</a>. The race was the eighth round of the <a href="/wiki/2017_FIA_Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="2017 FIA Formula One World Championship">2017 FIA Formula One World Championship</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-prov._calendar_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-prov._calendar-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> and was the maiden running of the <a href="/wiki/Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix" title="Azerbaijan Grand Prix">Azerbaijan Grand Prix</a> as a round of the Formula One World Championship. It was the second time that a race was held at the circuit and the second time that a Grand Prix was held in Azerbaijan.<sup id="cite_ref-Azerbaijan_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Azerbaijan-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2016_race_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2016_race-7">&#91;N 1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> entered the round with a twelve-point lead over <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes's</a> <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> in the World Drivers' Championship, while Mercedes led Ferrari by eight points in the World Constructors' Championship. Lewis Hamilton started the race from pole.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_result_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali_result-8">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> recovered from a crash in qualifying to claim his fifth career victory ahead of the <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> of <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-AZE_race_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AZE_race-9">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> while <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a> became the first <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_drivers_from_Canada" title="Formula One drivers from Canada">Canadian</a> to stand on the podium since <a href="/wiki/Jacques_Villeneuve" title="Jacques Villeneuve">Jacques Villeneuve</a> at the <a href="/wiki/2001_German_Grand_Prix" title="2001 German Grand Prix">2001 German Grand Prix</a>, and <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_driver_records#Youngest_drivers_to_score_a_podium_finish" title="List of Formula One driver records">the second youngest driver ever</a> to finish on the podium.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Free_practice">Free practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Free practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull's</a> <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> went fastest in first practice, setting a time of 1.44:410. He was followed by his teammate <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> in second with the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> of <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> in third.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-10">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> In second practice the Red Bull of Max Verstappen was fastest (despite crashing heavily into the barriers at Turn 1 in the closing stages of FP2) followed by Bottas and Ricciardo.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> Saturday's third and final practice was topped by Bottas, with <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> second and Hamilton third.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-12">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Bottas set the quickest time of all three practices with a 1.42.742.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-12">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Jolyon_Palmer" title="Jolyon Palmer">Jolyon Palmer</a> failed to set a lap time in Q1 due to a fire during Practice 3.
</p><p>In Q3, Lewis Hamilton secured pole position with a 1:40.593, 0.434 seconds quicker than teammate Bottas in second, and 1.100 seconds quicker than Räikkönen in third. Lance Stroll out-qualified teammate <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> for the first time in the season. Daniel Ricciardo hit the wall and started in tenth.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Hamilton led from the start, followed by Bottas, Räikkönen and Vettel. <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a> ran wide at turn 1. As he was returning to the track, his teammate <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz Jr.</a> spun off, to avoid a collision. In corner 3 of the first lap, both Finnish drivers (Bottas and Räikkönen) collided, causing a puncture in Bottas's front right tyre. Vettel took advantage to seize second place, followed by <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> and <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a>. Bottas was forced to stop in the pits, falling one lap behind the leaders.
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Daniel_Ricciardo_won_Azerbaijan_GP_2017.png" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/ab/Daniel_Ricciardo_won_Azerbaijan_GP_2017.png/220px-Daniel_Ricciardo_won_Azerbaijan_GP_2017.png" decoding="async" width="220" height="308" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/ab/Daniel_Ricciardo_won_Azerbaijan_GP_2017.png/330px-Daniel_Ricciardo_won_Azerbaijan_GP_2017.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/ab/Daniel_Ricciardo_won_Azerbaijan_GP_2017.png/440px-Daniel_Ricciardo_won_Azerbaijan_GP_2017.png 2x" data-file-width="500" data-file-height="700" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Daniel_Ricciardo_won_Azerbaijan_GP_2017.png" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> on the podium with the winner's trophy</div></div></div>
<p>Ricciardo pitted on lap 6 to remove debris from his brakes. He rejoined in 17th place. His teammate Verstappen had to retire due to an issue with his engine on lap 12. Hamilton led Vettel until the first <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a> was deployed on the same lap to remove the stranded Toro Rosso of Daniil Kvyat. This safety car period allowed Bottas to unlap himself. On lap 17, racing resumed but another safety car was deployed almost immediately to clear more debris off the track, which came from Räikkönen's Ferrari.
</p><p>During both safety car periods, Hamilton repeatedly complained that the safety car was moving too slowly. On lap 19 whilst still under safety car conditions Vettel ran into the back of Hamilton, damaging his front wing and Hamilton's rear diffuser. He then drove alongside Hamilton and swerved into his car, to protest what he believed was Hamilton brake-testing him. However, TV replays and car data showed that Hamilton had not applied his brakes at any point during the first collision. The race stewards put the incident under investigation. Racing resumed on lap 20 with <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> moving up to third and pressurizing Vettel, but within a few corners, the Force Indias of <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Ocon" title="Esteban Ocon">Esteban Ocon</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> collided and left debris on the track. The collision gave Ocon a puncture and dropped him to the back of the field. Pérez lost his front wing, and suffered damage to the front left wishbone which forced him to retire after making his way to the pit lane. Räikkönen, who was following the two Force Indias closely, got a puncture as a result of debris from the crash, and after the broken tire caused damage to the floor of his car as well as his rear wing, he too was pushed to his garage to retire. A third safety car was then deployed almost immediately, and after <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> complained over the radio about the amount of debris, the race was <a href="/wiki/Racing_flags#Red_flag" title="Racing flags">red-flagged</a> on lap 22 so that <a href="/wiki/Motorsport_marshal" title="Motorsport marshal">marshals</a> could clear the track.
</p><p>During the red-flag period, which lasted over 20 minutes, teams worked on their cars. Force India replaced Ocon's tyres and Ferrari replaced Vettel's front wing. Mercedes worked on Hamilton's rear diffuser, which only had minor cosmetic damage. Other drivers got out of their cars and talked to their engineers and team principals. The previously retired cars of Räikkönen and Pérez were also being worked on by their teams in their garages. Works were completed in time and both drivers were allowed to rejoin the race, albeit a lap down.
</p><p>After the track was cleared of debris, the race was restarted behind the safety car. Hamilton led at the restart from Vettel, Massa, Stroll, and Ricciardo. Ricciardo passed both Williams cars at turn 1 and settled into third place. Massa however was struggling with an issue on his Williams, the front suspension shaking violently at high speed. <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a> in the uncompetitive Haas managed to take advantage of Massa's issue and a lax <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> to overtake both for 5th place whilst Massa was easily passed by the entire field. Hülkenberg then collided with the wall on lap 25 and was forced to retire from 6th place with suspension damage. On the next lap, Massa retired due to the problem with his car, which turned out to be a broken suspension damper.
</p><p>By lap 29 Hamilton, Vettel, Ricciardo and <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a> had managed to pull away from the rest of the field and built a gap of more than 10 seconds back to Magnussen in 5th. But on this lap, the headrest on Hamilton's car suddenly became loose. He initially tried to fix it himself by pushing it back into its position, but on lap 31 he was forced by race control to pit for safety reasons. On the same lap, Vettel was given a 10-second stop/go penalty for dangerous driving, for his incident with Hamilton. Vettel served his penalty on lap 33, rejoining the track in 7th place just ahead of Hamilton, who became stuck in traffic following his unscheduled stop. This meant the lead was gifted to Ricciardo ahead of Stroll, with Kevin Magnussen third. The group of faster drivers consisting of Ocon, Bottas, Vettel and Hamilton started chasing the leaders, overtaking Alonso and Magnussen relatively comfortably. Bottas would then clear Ocon to move into third and a podium position, having come back from a lap down. Räikkönen and Pérez were both given drive-through penalties since during the red-flag period their cars were being worked on in their garages instead of the pit lane. Both drivers eventually retired from the race.
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Lance_Stroll_2017_Azerbaijan_GP.png" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/26/Lance_Stroll_2017_Azerbaijan_GP.png/220px-Lance_Stroll_2017_Azerbaijan_GP.png" decoding="async" width="220" height="293" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/26/Lance_Stroll_2017_Azerbaijan_GP.png/330px-Lance_Stroll_2017_Azerbaijan_GP.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/26/Lance_Stroll_2017_Azerbaijan_GP.png/440px-Lance_Stroll_2017_Azerbaijan_GP.png 2x" data-file-width="450" data-file-height="600" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Lance_Stroll_2017_Azerbaijan_GP.png" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a>, second youngest podium finisher</div></div></div>
<p>With Ricciardo comfortably in the lead, Stroll had a 12-second advantage over Bottas, Vettel and Hamilton, but was being caught rapidly. Hamilton asked for team orders to be applied and for Bottas to help him overtake Vettel by slowing his pace, but the team refused since Bottas still had a good chance of catching Stroll in the closing laps. The Saubers of <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Ericsson" title="Marcus Ericsson">Marcus Ericsson</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pascal_Wehrlein" title="Pascal Wehrlein">Pascal Wehrlein</a> collided as they were fighting for 10th place, but both drivers managed to continue without major damage. At the chequered flag, Ricciardo cruised to victory, taking Red Bull's first win in the 2017 season. Bottas then managed to pass Stroll on the start–finish straight just metres from the line, taking second place by 0.105 seconds. However, Stroll still picked up his maiden podium finish in third, becoming the second-youngest driver to finish in the top three. Vettel finished in fourth, Hamilton in fifth, Ocon in sixth, Magnussen in seventh, Sainz in eighth, Alonso in ninth (scoring McLaren's first points of the season), and Wehrlein in tenth. In all, nine different constructors scored points.
</p><p>As a result of the race, Vettel increased his lead over Hamilton in the World Drivers' Championship to 14 points, while Mercedes increased their lead over Ferrari in the World Constructors' Championship to 24 points.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
