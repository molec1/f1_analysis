<p>The <b>1960 British Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Motor_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Motor race">motor race</a> held at the <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone Circuit</a>, <a href="/wiki/Northamptonshire" title="Northamptonshire">Northamptonshire</a>, <a href="/wiki/England" title="England">England</a>, on 16 July 1960. It was race 7 of 10 in the <a href="/wiki/1960_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1960 World Championship of Drivers">1960 World Championship of Drivers</a> and race 6 of 9 in the <a href="/wiki/1960_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1960 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1960 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>. The race was won by reigning World Champion <a href="/wiki/Jack_Brabham" title="Jack Brabham">Jack Brabham</a> and <a href="/wiki/Innes_Ireland" title="Innes Ireland">Innes Ireland</a> finished in third place. Between the two, multiple motorcycle Grand Prix World Champion <a href="/wiki/John_Surtees" title="John Surtees">John Surtees</a> (in only his second ever Formula One Grand Prix) took second place.
</p>
