<p>The <b>2016 British Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>2016 Formula 1 British Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> that was held on 10 July 2016 at the <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Silverstone" title="Silverstone">Silverstone</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>. This race was the seventy-first running of the <a href="/wiki/British_Grand_Prix" title="British Grand Prix">British Grand Prix</a>, the first having been held in 1926, and was the tenth round of the <a href="/wiki/2016_FIA_Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="2016 FIA Formula One World Championship">2016 FIA Formula One World Championship</a> and the fiftieth time that the race was held at the Silverstone Circuit since the Formula One series inception in <a href="/wiki/1950_Formula_One_season" title="1950 Formula One season">1950</a>.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> entered the round with an eleven-point lead over teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> in the Drivers' Championship. Mercedes held a 103-points advantage over <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> in the Constructors' Championship. During Saturday's qualifying session, Hamilton achieved the 55th <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> of his career to start from the front of the grid, ahead of Rosberg and <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a>.
</p><p>The race began behind the <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a> due to rain shortly before the start. Hamilton was able to convert his pole position into a third straight victory at the British Grand Prix, leading home Rosberg and Verstappen in changing conditions. Rosberg came under investigation by the race stewards after he received detailed instructions by his team how to work around a <a href="/wiki/Gearbox" class="mw-redirect" title="Gearbox">gearbox</a> problem in the closing laps of the race, a practice forbidden under Formula One regulations. He was later handed a ten-second time penalty, demoting him to third place. The result saw Rosberg's championship lead reduced to just a single point over Hamilton.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2016_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2016_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Going into the weekend, <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> was leading the Drivers' Championship with 153 points, eleven ahead of his teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>. <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> drivers <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> were third and fourth respectively, with 96 points each, ahead of <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> with 88.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> In the Constructors' Championship standings, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> was in front with 295 points, 103 ahead of Ferrari, with Red Bull Racing following in third with 168 points.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Following the collision between Lewis Hamilton and Nico Rosberg on the last lap of the <a href="/wiki/2016_Austrian_Grand_Prix" title="2016 Austrian Grand Prix">Austrian Grand Prix</a>, the second of such incidents in five races, Mercedes introduced a new driver management policy, dictating the terms by which Hamilton and Rosberg could race one another and promising penalties in the event of further on-track altercations.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Tyre supplier <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> provided teams with the hard, medium and soft compounds of tyre—the three hardest compounds available—for the race.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Just as <a href="/wiki/2015_British_Grand_Prix" title="2015 British Grand Prix">the year before</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> the race stewards warned drivers about exceeding the track limits around the circuit, stressing that lap times during qualifying would be disallowed should drivers not stay within the lines indicating the track borders.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The second mid-season test of the year was scheduled to be held at the circuit following the race. <a href="/wiki/Santino_Ferrucci" title="Santino Ferrucci">Santino Ferrucci</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nikita_Mazepin" title="Nikita Mazepin">Nikita Mazepin</a> made their first appearances for Haas and <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> respectively, while <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a> drove for Red Bull Racing and <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc_(racing_driver)" class="mw-redirect" title="Charles Leclerc (racing driver)">Charles Leclerc</a> for Ferrari.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> After missing the first test in <a href="/wiki/2016_Spanish_Grand_Prix" title="2016 Spanish Grand Prix">Spain</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sauber_F1_Team" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber F1 Team">Sauber</a> once again skipped the post-race test. The reason behind this decision was stated as to "consider cost effectiveness",<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> as the team did not have any new components to introduce.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> Sauber did however introduce their first updates of the season to their car during free practice at the event, with <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Ericsson" title="Marcus Ericsson">Marcus Ericsson</a> doing a trial run with a new rear wing.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Further aerodynamic updates that the team had announced the week before did not arrive in time for Silverstone. Sauber did however receive the benefit of Ferrari's updated version of their power unit, used by the works team since the <a href="/wiki/2016_Canadian_Grand_Prix" title="2016 Canadian Grand Prix">Canadian Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Haas_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Haas F1">Haas</a> was able to use the upgraded units as well.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> used an updated version of their <a href="/wiki/Honda_in_Formula_One" title="Honda in Formula One">Honda</a> power unit as well, with two tokens – changes allowed to the units – applied to the combustion engine.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Free_practice">Free practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2016_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Free practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Ferrari_SF16-H_Halo_British_GP_(2).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c3/Ferrari_SF16-H_Halo_British_GP_%282%29.jpg/220px-Ferrari_SF16-H_Halo_British_GP_%282%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="122" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c3/Ferrari_SF16-H_Halo_British_GP_%282%29.jpg/330px-Ferrari_SF16-H_Halo_British_GP_%282%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c3/Ferrari_SF16-H_Halo_British_GP_%282%29.jpg/440px-Ferrari_SF16-H_Halo_British_GP_%282%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3504" data-file-height="1943" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Ferrari_SF16-H_Halo_British_GP_(2).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> tested the "<a href="/wiki/Halo_(safety_device)" title="Halo (safety device)">halo</a>" safety device in first practice.</div></div></div>
<p>Per the regulations for the <a href="/wiki/2016_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2016 Formula One World Championship">2016</a> season, two 90-minute practice sessions were held on Friday and another 60-minute session was held before qualifying on Saturday.<sup id="cite_ref-pracreg_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pracreg-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Lewis Hamilton was fastest in the first practice session on Friday morning, setting a time of 1:31.654, less than a tenth of a second ahead of teammate Nico Rosberg. After setting their fast times, the Mercedes drivers went on to complete long stints to test race performance on the harder tyre compounds. <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> and Sebastian Vettel in third and fourth were the only drivers to set a time within one second of the Mercedes. Charles Leclerc had his first practice outing with Haas, taking over the car usually run by <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban Gutiérrez">Esteban Gutiérrez</a>. He was a tenth of a second faster than the other test driver, <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Ocon" title="Esteban Ocon">Esteban Ocon</a>, who took over <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Renault_RS16" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault RS16">Renault RS16</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fp1_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fp1-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> Vettel did a test with an updated version of Ferrari's "<a href="/wiki/Halo_(safety_device)" title="Halo (safety device)">halo</a>"-system designed to improve the protection of the driver's head inside the cockpit.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> He described the device as "not great", lamenting that it had "quite a bit of impact on visibility".<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hamilton was again fastest in the second practice on Friday afternoon, while Rosberg was unable to partake due to a water leak on his car. Hamilton's time of 1:31.660 was over three-tenths of a second faster than that of second places Daniel Ricciardo. Fellow Red Bull driver <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> was third, ahead of Vettel, all within one second of the fastest time. Ferrari split their practice strategy, with Kimi Räikkönen running the hard-compound tyre early in the session, while Vettel used the softer medium compound, before both switched to the soft tyres. At their home race, McLaren had a mixed session. While <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> placed a good sixth, teammate <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> had to take to the garage for repairs twice, before coming out on track again late in the session, eventually setting the ninth fastest time. <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a> was 13th fastest, but damaged his front wing when he went over a kerb at Club corner.<sup id="cite_ref-fp2_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fp2-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Rosberg_FP_British_GP_2016_2.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6b/Rosberg_FP_British_GP_2016_2.jpg/220px-Rosberg_FP_British_GP_2016_2.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="138" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6b/Rosberg_FP_British_GP_2016_2.jpg/330px-Rosberg_FP_British_GP_2016_2.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6b/Rosberg_FP_British_GP_2016_2.jpg/440px-Rosberg_FP_British_GP_2016_2.jpg 2x" data-file-width="5139" data-file-height="3217" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Rosberg_FP_British_GP_2016_2.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> during free practice</div></div></div>
<p>Lewis Hamilton made it a clean sweep of fastest times in practice on Saturday morning, setting a time of 1:30.904, again less than a tenth of a second ahead of Rosberg. The session started in wet conditions following a rain shower thirty minutes before it commenced. It was interrupted with 22 minutes to go, after Marcus Ericsson crashed heavily at the exit of Stowe corner. With the car cleared from the track, lengthy repair work at the barriers followed, leaving just five minutes of running when practice was restarted. The drivers then set their fastest times of the session. Again, it were the Red Bull drivers closest to the Mercedes, with Ricciardo ahead of Verstappen in third, both about half a second off Hamilton's time.<sup id="cite_ref-fp3_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fp3-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> Ericsson was sent to hospital for tests following his accident<sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> and received a five-place grid penalty since his <a href="/wiki/Gearbox" class="mw-redirect" title="Gearbox">gearbox</a> had to be changed. Vettel received the same penalty after his gearbox failed towards the end of the third practice session.<sup id="cite_ref-gearboxpen_28-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gearboxpen-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2016_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_2016_British_Grand_Prix_(2).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e4/Lewis_Hamilton_2016_British_Grand_Prix_%282%29.jpg/220px-Lewis_Hamilton_2016_British_Grand_Prix_%282%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e4/Lewis_Hamilton_2016_British_Grand_Prix_%282%29.jpg/330px-Lewis_Hamilton_2016_British_Grand_Prix_%282%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/e/e4/Lewis_Hamilton_2016_British_Grand_Prix_%282%29.jpg/440px-Lewis_Hamilton_2016_British_Grand_Prix_%282%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="5472" data-file-height="3648" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_2016_British_Grand_Prix_(2).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> started from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> after posting the fastest time in qualifying.</div></div></div>
<p>Qualifying consisted of three parts, 18, 15 and 12 minutes in length respectively, with six drivers eliminated from competing after each of the first two sessions.<sup id="cite_ref-pracreg_21-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pracreg-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Following his crash, Ericsson did not take part in qualifying and was therefore automatically eliminated, leaving five drivers in danger of not going through into the second session.<sup id="cite_ref-telqual_29-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-telqual-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> In the first part of qualifying (Q1), several drivers were investigated for exceeding track limits emerged. Jenson Button ended the session 17th due to a damaged rear wing, a disappointment after having started third a week before in Austria.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-31" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> Also eliminated were the other Sauber driver <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Nasr" title="Felipe Nasr">Felipe Nasr</a>, both <a href="/wiki/Manor_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Manor F1">Manor</a> cars and <a href="/wiki/Jolyon_Palmer" title="Jolyon Palmer">Jolyon Palmer</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_30-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Lewis Hamilton set the pace in Q2, seven-tenths of a second ahead of Rosberg. Kimi Räikkönen was in danger of being eliminated for the majority of the session, as he spun while preparing for his first timed lap and then ran wide over the track limits on his second attempt, but managed to proceed into Q3 with his third fast lap. He initially demoted Fernando Alonso outside the top ten with his time, but the McLaren driver hit back with a time sufficient to move on. This left Magnussen, Daniil Kvyat, Gutiérrez, <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a>, <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> on the sidelines for Q3.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_30-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On his first timed run in Q3, Hamilton failed to improve on his lap time from Q2, but was still well ahead of his teammate. However, his time was disallowed for not respecting track limits, meaning that he had to set another fast lap. Even though he slowed down in the middle sector of the lap to avoid making the same mistake again, he recorded a time just short of his earlier effort to achieve the 55th <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> of his career.<sup id="cite_ref-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Verstappen slotted into third on the grid, almost a second down on Hamilton, with his teammate Ricciardo next to him in fourth. The Red Bull pair was followed by the Ferrari cars of Räikkönen and Vettel, though Vettel's grid penalty meant that he would start 11th. <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz Jr.</a> was eighth on the grid ahead of Hülkenberg and Alonso. Both had originally set times faster than Sainz's, but those were disallowed due to them exceeding the track limits.<sup id="cite_ref-quali_30-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quali-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> Following qualifying, the paddock was split in its opinion over the strict interpretation of the track limit rules. Hamilton and Hülkenberg, who both fell victim to erased lap times, supported the stewards' decision,<sup id="cite_ref-33" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> as did Jenson Button.<sup id="cite_ref-34" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> However, Jolyon Palmer was angered by what he called "a mess" and "rubbish for the fans", saying that having times changed after the end of the session, as it happened in Hülkenberg's case, was "confusing for fans and less enjoyable".<sup id="cite_ref-35" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2016_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:First_lap_2016_British_Grand_Prix_(1).jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d9/First_lap_2016_British_Grand_Prix_%281%29.jpg/220px-First_lap_2016_British_Grand_Prix_%281%29.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="165" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d9/First_lap_2016_British_Grand_Prix_%281%29.jpg/330px-First_lap_2016_British_Grand_Prix_%281%29.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d9/First_lap_2016_British_Grand_Prix_%281%29.jpg/440px-First_lap_2016_British_Grand_Prix_%281%29.jpg 2x" data-file-width="4608" data-file-height="3456" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:First_lap_2016_British_Grand_Prix_(1).jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Due to rain shortly before the start, the race commenced behind the <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>Due to rain shortly before the start of the race, it commenced behind the <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a>, with every car equipped with full <a href="/wiki/Rain_tyre" title="Rain tyre">wet tyres</a>. The safety car came back into the pitlane at the end of lap five and racing began properly. While the top four drivers stayed out, several behind chose to make a pitstop for intermediate tyres. Just one lap later, <a href="/wiki/Pascal_Wehrlein" title="Pascal Wehrlein">Pascal Wehrlein</a> became the first casualty of the still damp track, sliding off the track and getting stuck in the gravel, leading to a brief <a href="/wiki/Safety_car#Virtual_Safety_Car_.28VSC.29" title="Safety car">virtual safety car</a> period. During this time, both Hamilton and Rosberg changed tyres. The biggest winner of the first few laps was Sergio Pérez, who had worked up his way from tenth on the grid to fourth, ahead of Ricciardo. Meanwhile, at the front, Hamilton had established a five-second advantage over Rosberg, who soon came under attack by Verstappen, who was running well on the intermediate tyres. Bottas came into a spin on lap 11, losing positions and dropping back to 13th place. On lap 16, Sebastian Vettel was the first driver to make the switch to <a href="/wiki/Slick_tyre" class="mw-redirect" title="Slick tyre">slick tyres</a>, while Verstappen moved ahead of Rosberg into second position.<sup id="cite_ref-ticker_36-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ticker-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Lap 18 saw more drivers making the change to slick tyres, including Räikkönen and Palmer, but the latter was let go from his pitbox before one of the tyres was properly fitted, costing him time and handing him a ten-second stop-and-go penalty. Both Mercedes drivers and Verstappen made their second pitstops by lap 20, with the order remaining the same. On lap 21, Ricciardo moved ahead of Pérez into fourth position. A mistake by Räikkönen two laps later saw him lose sixth place to Sainz. Fernando Alonso spun on lap 25, but was able to continue, unlike <a href="/wiki/Rio_Haryanto" title="Rio Haryanto">Rio Haryanto</a>, who retired following a spin one lap later. Hamilton retained the lead, but not without problems, as he also had a short excursion off the track, which was mirrored by Verstappen behind him. As a result, the top three moved closer together. Vettel was still running at the back end of the point-scoring positions, overtaking Kvyat for ninth on lap 29.<sup id="cite_ref-ticker_36-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ticker-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Nico Rosberg started to close on Verstappen for second place, starting overtaking attempts on lap 33 and finally succeeding on lap 38. Meanwhile, Vettel was judged to have forced Felipe Massa off the track when he overtook him for eighth place, handing him a five-second time penalty. On lap 40, Jolyon Palmer retired in the pitlane. While Rosberg initially closed in on Hamilton, the latter started to improve his lap times towards the end of the race. As Räikkönen got back into fifth at the expense of Pérez, Rosberg reported problems with his gearbox on the team radio.<sup id="cite_ref-ticker_36-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-ticker-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> His team told him to avoid using seventh gear,<sup id="cite_ref-37" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> following which Rosberg was able to consolidate his slim lead over Verstappen, which had initially come down due to his problem. Hamilton was unaffected by this and crossed the line to take victory, 6.9 seconds ahead of Rosberg.<sup id="cite_ref-38" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> He was the first driver to win the <a href="/wiki/British_Grand_Prix" title="British Grand Prix">British Grand Prix</a> three times in a row and also became the record winner at the Silverstone circuit, with four victories.<sup id="cite_ref-39" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> It was the 47th victory of his career.<sup id="cite_ref-40" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2016_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Nico_Rosberg_2016_British_GP.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/46/Nico_Rosberg_2016_British_GP.jpg/220px-Nico_Rosberg_2016_British_GP.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="118" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/46/Nico_Rosberg_2016_British_GP.jpg/330px-Nico_Rosberg_2016_British_GP.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/46/Nico_Rosberg_2016_British_GP.jpg/440px-Nico_Rosberg_2016_British_GP.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2252" data-file-height="1204" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Nico_Rosberg_2016_British_GP.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> finished third for <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> after receiving a ten-second time penalty for illegal radio instructions from his team.</div></div></div>
<p>On the podium, Nico Rosberg was booed by the home crowd, mirroring a similar incident towards Hamilton at the previous race in Austria.<sup id="cite_ref-41" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton later told the press that he encouraged his fans not to act in such a way towards his teammate, saying: "I honestly think us as Brits, the British fans, I think we're better than that."<sup id="cite_ref-42" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup> During the interviews at the podium ceremony, conducted by former Formula One driver <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a>, Hamilton had expressed his delight at the fact that the weather had made the race more difficult. Rosberg spoke of "an exciting race" against Verstappen and highlighted his "very critical problem" with his gearbox, but stressed that he felt confident that the instructions he received from his team to fix it were within the rules.<sup id="cite_ref-pressconf_43-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pressconf-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> However, the stewards – led by former driver <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Mansell" title="Nigel Mansell">Nigel Mansell</a> – did not share his view and applied a ten-second time penalty for what they felt were instructions not compliant with the sporting regulations, demoting Rosberg to third place.<sup id="cite_ref-rosbergpen_44-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-rosbergpen-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> The rules on instructions from the team to the driver had been tightened before the <a href="/wiki/2016_Australian_Grand_Prix" title="2016 Australian Grand Prix">first race of the season</a>, specifying that "the driver shall drive the car alone and unaided".<sup id="cite_ref-45" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ferrari team was unhappy with its performance over the weekend, with Vettel summing up: "Overall it was not our day, not quick enough simple as that."<sup id="cite_ref-vettelpen_46-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-vettelpen-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup> He also expressed his irritation at receiving a five-second time penalty and two penalty points on his <a href="/wiki/FIA_Super_Licence" title="FIA Super Licence">FIA Super Licence</a> for an overtaking manoeuvre on Massa.<sup id="cite_ref-47" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-47">&#91;47&#93;</a></sup> Kimi Räikkönen on the other had declared that Ferrari's poor showing would prove to be a one-time incident, saying: "I'm sure in the next races will be much more normal for us."<sup id="cite_ref-48" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-48">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As a result of the race, Rosberg's lead in the Drivers' Championship over Hamilton was reduced to a single point (168 to 167 points), with Räikkönen following in third with 106 points. Sebastian Vettel fell back to fifth with 98 points, two points behind Daniel Ricciardo.<sup id="cite_ref-49" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-49">&#91;49&#93;</a></sup> In the Constructors' standings, Mercedes extended its lead over Ferrari to 131 points, while Red Bull closed in on Ferrari and was only six points behind.<sup id="cite_ref-50" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-50">&#91;50&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
