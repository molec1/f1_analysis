</div></td></tr></tbody></table><p>The <b>1992 Portuguese Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held at <a href="/wiki/Aut%C3%B3dromo_do_Estoril" class="mw-redirect" title="Autódromo do Estoril">Autódromo do Estoril</a> on 27 September 1992. It was the fourteenth round of the <a href="/wiki/1992_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="1992 Formula One World Championship">1992 Formula One World Championship</a>.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Nigel_Mansell" title="Nigel Mansell">Nigel Mansell</a> won the race from pole position, in the process setting new records for most wins (nine) and most points (108) in one season, with <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> drivers <a href="/wiki/Gerhard_Berger" title="Gerhard Berger">Gerhard Berger</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ayrton_Senna" title="Ayrton Senna">Ayrton Senna</a> second and third. 
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Pre-race">Pre-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1992_Portuguese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Pre-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Fondmetal" title="Fondmetal">Fondmetal</a> team did not arrive in Estoril for the race, suffering financial problems. Team boss <a href="/wiki/Gabriele_Rumi" title="Gabriele Rumi">Gabriele Rumi</a> attempted to bring in paydriver <a href="/wiki/Giuseppe_Bugatti" title="Giuseppe Bugatti">Giuseppe Bugatti</a> to help ease the team's cashflow problems but this was not enough to allow the team to race.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> Although they hoped at the time to return for the final two races of the season in Japan and Australia, they ultimately proved unable to do so. This was the third race in succession where a team exited Formula One, after <a href="/wiki/Brabham" title="Brabham">Brabham</a> and <a href="/wiki/Andrea_Moda_Formula" title="Andrea Moda Formula">Andrea Moda</a> at the previous two races. Brabham had also hoped to return as the team was put up for sale, but before this weekend their full withdrawal was announced.<sup id="cite_ref-Walker_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Walker-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Fondmetal's absence meant that there were only 26 cars on the entry list, so this Grand Prix was the first since 1987 in which all cars automatically qualified for the race, regardless of qualifying lap times.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying_report">Qualifying report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1992_Portuguese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The top six on the grid lined up in pairs, with the <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a>, <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> and <a href="/wiki/Benetton_Formula" title="Benetton Formula">Benetton</a> drivers occupying the first three rows. <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Mansell" title="Nigel Mansell">Nigel Mansell</a> took pole from <a href="/wiki/Riccardo_Patrese" title="Riccardo Patrese">Riccardo Patrese</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Ayrton_Senna" title="Ayrton Senna">Ayrton Senna</a>, <a href="/wiki/Gerhard_Berger" title="Gerhard Berger">Gerhard Berger</a>, <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> and <a href="/wiki/Martin_Brundle" title="Martin Brundle">Martin Brundle</a> lining up behind.<sup id="cite_ref-Walker_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Walker-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1992_Portuguese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race_report">Race report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1992_Portuguese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Race report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The race is often remembered for the accident between Berger and Mansell's <a href="/wiki/Williams_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Williams F1">Williams</a> team-mate <a href="/wiki/Riccardo_Patrese" title="Riccardo Patrese">Riccardo Patrese</a>. Intending to make a pit stop, Berger moved towards the right side of the track at the beginning of the start/finish straight, with Patrese following in his slipstream. Failing to realise Berger's intentions, Patrese swerved to avoid him, but his right front wheel hit Berger's left rear and the Williams was launched into the air, almost hitting a pedestrian bridge over the track. Patrese escaped the accident shaken but unhurt, and neither driver was punished by the stewards. The debris from the crash, however, caused numerous other incidents, with <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pierluigi_Martini" title="Pierluigi Martini">Pierluigi Martini</a> suffering punctures, and <a href="/wiki/JJ_Lehto" title="JJ Lehto">JJ Lehto</a> suffering slight injury as the driveshaft of the Williams went through the undertray of his <a href="/wiki/Dallara" title="Dallara">Dallara</a>, hitting him on the leg.
</p><p>This race was the last time a <a href="/wiki/Honda_in_Formula_One" title="Honda in Formula One">Honda</a>-powered car set the fastest lap until <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> repeated the achievement in the <a href="/wiki/2016_Italian_Grand_Prix" title="2016 Italian Grand Prix">2016 Italian Grand Prix</a>, and the last time McLaren would score a double points result with Honda engines until the <a href="/wiki/2015_Hungarian_Grand_Prix" title="2015 Hungarian Grand Prix">2015 Hungarian Grand Prix</a>.
</p>
