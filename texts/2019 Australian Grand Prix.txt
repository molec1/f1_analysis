<p>The <b>2019 Australian Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 Rolex Australian Grand Prix 2019</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_racing" title="Formula One racing">Formula One motor race</a> that was held on 17 March 2019 in <a href="/wiki/Melbourne" title="Melbourne">Melbourne</a>, <a href="/wiki/Victoria,_Australia" class="mw-redirect" title="Victoria, Australia">Victoria</a>. The race was contested at the <a href="/wiki/Melbourne_Grand_Prix_Circuit" class="mw-redirect" title="Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit">Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit</a> and was the first round of the <a href="/wiki/2019_FIA_Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="2019 FIA Formula One World Championship">2019 FIA Formula One World Championship</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-calendar_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-calendar-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The race marked the 84th race in the combined history of the <a href="/wiki/Australian_Grand_Prix" title="Australian Grand Prix">Australian Grand Prix</a> – which dates back to the <a href="/wiki/1928_Australian_Grand_Prix" title="1928 Australian Grand Prix">100 Miles Road Race</a> of 1928 – the 24th time the event was held at the Albert Park circuit and the 35th time the Australian Grand Prix had been a part of the Formula One World Championship.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> entered the round as the defending <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">World Drivers' Champion</a> and his team, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a>, are the defending <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">World Constructors' Champions</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton got pole position for the race equalling the record for most poles at one Grand Prix (8), but it was his Mercedes team mate <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> who won the Grand Prix from second on the grid.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Australian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Fed_Square_August_2007.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/81/Fed_Square_August_2007.jpg/220px-Fed_Square_August_2007.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="165" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/81/Fed_Square_August_2007.jpg/330px-Fed_Square_August_2007.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/81/Fed_Square_August_2007.jpg/440px-Fed_Square_August_2007.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1200" data-file-height="900" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Fed_Square_August_2007.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Federation_Square" title="Federation Square">Federation Square</a> hosted the first Formula One season launch.</div></div></div>
<p>The Australian Grand Prix was officially confirmed as the first of twenty-one races of the <a href="/wiki/2019_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2019 Formula One World Championship">2019 Formula One World Championship</a> at an <a href="/wiki/FIA_World_Motor_Sport_Council" title="FIA World Motor Sport Council">FIA World Motor Sport Council</a> meeting in Paris in December 2018. The race took place at the fifteen-turn, 5.303&#160;km (3.295&#160;mi) <a href="/wiki/Melbourne_Grand_Prix_Circuit" class="mw-redirect" title="Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit">Melbourne Grand Prix Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Melbourne" title="Melbourne">Melbourne</a>, <a href="/wiki/Victoria_(Australia)" title="Victoria (Australia)">Victoria</a> on 17 March 2019.<sup id="cite_ref-calendar_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-calendar-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> Melbourne's <a href="/wiki/Federation_Square" title="Federation Square">Federation Square</a> hosted a season launch event, the first of its kind for the sport, in the week before the race.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Race Director <a href="/wiki/Charlie_Whiting" title="Charlie Whiting">Charlie Whiting</a> died suddenly in the week before the race, so Deputy Race Director Michael Masi was named as Whiting's temporary successor.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-SpeedCafe_20190320_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SpeedCafe_20190320-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Local officials were appointed to fill Whiting's additional roles until a long-term appointment could be made ahead of the <a href="/wiki/2019_Bahrain_Grand_Prix" title="2019 Bahrain Grand Prix">Bahrain Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-SpeedCafe_20190320_11-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-SpeedCafe_20190320-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> As of the <a href="/wiki/2019_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix" title="2019 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix">2019 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix</a>, Masi remains the Race Director.
</p>

championship.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup><sup </p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Tyres">Tyres</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Australian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Tyres">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">Further information: <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyres#Tyre_summary" title="Formula One tyres">Formula One tyres §&#160;Tyre summary</a></div>
<p>Tyre supplier <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> provided teams with an intermediate range of tyres for the race.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> Under rules introduced in 2019, Pirelli changed the designations of their tyres to rate the hardness of the compounds, with "C1" being the hardest and "C5" the softest.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> The colour-coding system used in previous years was simplified so that the white-coloured tyre wall represents the hardest compound available at any Grand Prix, a yellow tyre wall the medium compound and the red tyre wall the softest tyre.<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> Pirelli nominated the C2 tyre as the white-banded hard compound, C3 as the yellow-banded medium and C4 as the red-banded soft.<sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Points_system_change">Points system change</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Australian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Points system change">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">Further information: <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_points_scoring_systems" class="mw-redirect" title="List of Formula One points scoring systems">List of Formula One points scoring systems</a></div>
<p>The race saw the introduction of a change to the points system. The system in use since <a href="/wiki/2010_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2010 Formula One World Championship">2010</a> will be retained, but drivers who set the fastest lap could be awarded a bonus point provided that they finish the race inside the top ten. The bonus point is also awarded to the constructor of the driver that set the fastest lap as well.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Australian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> was fastest in all three practice sessions. The sessions ran uninterrupted apart from an incident that saw <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Albon" title="Alexander Albon">Alexander Albon</a> spinning out at turn 2 and hitting the wall during the first practice session. Albon was able to return to the pits for repairs.<sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> In the third practice session <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> released <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">Lando Norris</a> into the path of <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> which resulted in the McLaren team receiving a fine.<sup id="cite_ref-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Australian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The first qualifying session passed without incident until the final couple of minutes when <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> hit the wall coming out of turn 10 giving him a puncture, he later attributed this to a sudden increase in grip.<sup id="cite_ref-31" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> The drivers knocked out in Q1 and finishing qualifying in sixteenth to twentieth respectively were <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a>, <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz</a>, <a href="/wiki/George_Russell_(racing_driver)" title="George Russell (racing driver)">George Russell</a> and <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> with <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a> setting the fastest lap time of the session.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_32-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Gasly being knocked out came as a particular surprise and put his early exit down to unexpected track evolution.<sup id="cite_ref-33" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> Sainz was also disappointed with his early exit from qualifying, especially considering his <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">team mate</a> qualified in eighth, Sainz attributed his early exit to having to slow down after Kubica got a puncture in front of him forcing him to slow down on his qualifying lap.<sup id="cite_ref-34" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The second qualifying session passed without incident and resulted with <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Albon" title="Alexander Albon">Alexander Albon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Antonio_Giovinazzi" title="Antonio Giovinazzi">Antonio Giovinazzi</a> and <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a> being knocked out and finishing qualifying ranked eleventh to fifteenth respectively with <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> setting a qualifying lap record to end the session fastest.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_32-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:1_35-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> The surprises came from the two <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renaults</a> of Ricciardo and Hülkenberg being knocked out after being considered as the fourth fastest team following pre season testing and from <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">Lando Norris</a> getting in to the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-36" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-37" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-38" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>For the third qualifying session all of the teams were able to have two attempts to set the fastest time, after the first attempt it was <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> who was leading having beaten the qualifying lap record which Hamilton had set in Q2, Bottas was followed by Hamilton and <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_35-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> During the second attempt in Q3 Hamilton improved his time by half a second to beat Bottas's lap record.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_35-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> As no one else improved their time sufficiently to beat any of top three it was Lewis Hamilton who took <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> followed by Valtteri Bottas and Sebastian Vettel completing the top three.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_32-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> The top ten was completed by <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a>, <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">Lando Norris</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a>. In his post qualifying interview Leclerc confessed that he wasn't happy with his performance in saying that he thought he was able to get into the top three but was unable to due to a untidy lap.<sup id="cite_ref-39" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup> Meanwhile <a href="/wiki/Haas_F1_Team" title="Haas F1 Team">Haas</a> said they were delighted with their performance getting their cars into sixth and seventh positions.<sup id="cite_ref-40" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-40">&#91;40&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Australian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> started the race well from second place, and had the lead going into turn one.<sup id="cite_ref-41" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-41">&#91;41&#93;</a></sup> Further back, <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> was contesting a position with <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a>, in which Ricciardo had to drive on the grass on the right side of the track to avoid Pérez, however Ricciardo drove over a sunken bit of tarmac causing his front wing to fall apart, spilling debris around that part of the track and causing him to drop to last as he drove round with no front wing before pitting for a replacement.<sup id="cite_ref-42" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-42">&#91;42&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:2_43-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:3_44-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> was passed by <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a> who then proceeded to battle his teammate <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>, Vettel kept Leclerc at bay in 5th and Leclerc was reovertaken by Verstappen.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_44-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:4_45-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> also came into the pits on lap 1 after losing his front wing at the start following contact with <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-46" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-46">&#91;46&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On lap 9, <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz</a> pulled over in his <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> at the entrance to the pits, with a large plume of smoke emerging from the rear of his car, which was followed by an onboard fire.<sup id="cite_ref-47" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-47">&#91;47&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> pitted on lap 13 whilst <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a> struggled to make progress from P17, moving up only to P12 despite being in a significantly faster car than those in front of him.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_48-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-48">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:6_49-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-49">&#91;49&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-50" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-50">&#91;50&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> and Pérez then pulled into the pits in search of an undercut, followed by Vettel who put on the medium tyres.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_48-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-48">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:7_51-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:7-51">&#91;51&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> pitted a lap later onto the same tyre compound, and he emerged ahead of Vettel.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_48-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-48">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:6_49-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-49">&#91;49&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:7_51-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:7-51">&#91;51&#93;</a></sup> Romain Grosjean then pitted but an issue on the front left tyre caused him to lose three positions during his pit stop.<sup id="cite_ref-52" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-52">&#91;52&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Antonio_Giovinazzi" title="Antonio Giovinazzi">Antonio Giovinazzi</a> went wide on the approach to turn 13, allowing Grosjean to pass through.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_44-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> Bottas, the race leader, made his pit stop on lap 23, Verstappen followed suit three laps later and Leclerc pitted from second place on lap 29.<sup id="cite_ref-:5_48-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-48">&#91;48&#93;</a></sup> Daniel Ricciardo retired as a precaution following the lap 1 incident.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_43-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-43">&#91;43&#93;</a></sup> Verstappen passed Vettel on lap 31.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_44-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> Grosjean pulled over on to the run off area on the penultimate corner, after his front left wheel came loose.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_44-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-44">&#91;44&#93;</a></sup> Daniil Kvyat went wide trying to pass Lance Stroll at turn 3 on lap 38.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_45-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-45">&#91;45&#93;</a></sup> Bottas retook the fastest lap and won the Grand Prix by over 20 seconds to his teammate Hamilton.<sup id="cite_ref-:8_53-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-53">&#91;53&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:9_54-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:9-54">&#91;54&#93;</a></sup> They were followed by Verstappen in third and the two <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> cars trailing in fourth and fifth.<sup id="cite_ref-:8_53-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-53">&#91;53&#93;</a></sup> Bottas became the first driver to win a bonus point for the fastest lap of the race since <a href="/wiki/Maurice_Trintignant" title="Maurice Trintignant">Maurice Trintignant</a> at the <a href="/wiki/1959_United_States_Grand_Prix" title="1959 United States Grand Prix">1959 United States Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-55" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-55">&#91;55&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
