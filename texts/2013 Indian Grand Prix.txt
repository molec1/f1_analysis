<p>The <b>2013 Indian Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>2013 Formula 1 Airtel Indian Grand Prix</b><sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> held on 27 October 2013 at the <a href="/wiki/Buddh_International_Circuit" title="Buddh International Circuit">Buddh International Circuit</a>, <a href="/wiki/Greater_Noida" title="Greater Noida">Greater Noida</a>, <a href="/wiki/Uttar_Pradesh" title="Uttar Pradesh">Uttar Pradesh</a>, India. It was the sixteenth round of the <a href="/wiki/2013_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2013 Formula One season">2013 Formula One season</a>, and the third <a href="/wiki/Indian_Grand_Prix" title="Indian Grand Prix">Indian Grand Prix</a>. The race, contested over 60 laps, was won by <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>, driving a <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>, from a <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> start. <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> finished second in a <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> third for the <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a> team. Vettel's win confirmed him as <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">2013 Drivers' Champion</a>, as <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> could not pass Vettel's points total with only three races remaining. Red Bull also clinched their fourth consecutive <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a>. 
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Indian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Indian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The Grand Prix was contested by eleven teams, each of two drivers.<sup id="cite_ref-classification_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-classification-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The teams, also known as "<a href="/wiki/Formula_One#Constructors" title="Formula One">constructors</a>", were <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>-<a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>-<a href="/wiki/Mercedes_AMG_High_Performance_Powertrains" title="Mercedes AMG High Performance Powertrains">Mercedes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a>-Renault, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a>-Ferrari, <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a>-Mercedes, <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a>-Renault, <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>-Ferrari, <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a>-Renault and <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia</a>-<a href="/wiki/Cosworth" title="Cosworth">Cosworth</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-classification_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-classification-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Before the race, Red Bull driver Sebastian Vettel led the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">Drivers' Championship</a> with 297 points; Ferrari driver <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> was second on 207 points.<sup id="cite_ref-indiangp_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-indiangp-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> A maximum of 100 points were available in the remaining four races, which meant Alonso could still win the title. Vettel only needed a fifth place or higher to become Drivers' Champion, even if Alonso won.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Behind Vettel and Alonso in the Drivers' Championship, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> was third with 177 points in a Lotus, with <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> on 161 and 148 points respectively. In the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Constructors%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Constructors&#39; Champions">Constructors' Championship</a>, Red Bull were leading on 445 points and Ferrari were second on 297 points, with Mercedes third on 287 points.<sup id="cite_ref-indiangp_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-indiangp-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the week leading up to the race, the organisers of the race, Jaypee Sports International attended a court hearing on a dispute over unpaid entertainment taxes from the previous running of the Grand Prix.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> A court official confirmed, the case would be heard the week after the race, thereby ending speculation that the Grand Prix would be cancelled.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On 21 October, four days before the event's first free practice sessions took place, Toro Rosso confirmed the team was signing <a href="/wiki/GP3_Series" title="GP3 Series">GP3 Series</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a> for the 2014 season, partnering <a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Drag_reduction_system" title="Drag reduction system">drag reduction system</a> (DRS) had two activation zones for the race; one was on the straight between turns 3 and 4, and the second was on the start/finish straight from the final to first corners.<sup id="cite_ref-indiangp_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-indiangp-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The sport's sole tyre supplier <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a>, provided two specifications of slick compound tyres for the race, designated Medium (also referred to as the "prime" tyre) and Soft (also referred to as the "option" tyre).<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> The Medium compound was distinguished by a white-band with the Soft compound being distinguished by a yellow-band. As was the case for all the 2013 Grands Prix, the rules stipulated that all cars should use both types of tyre during the course of the race, and each driver was limited to eleven sets of dry tyres for the weekend.<sup id="cite_ref-f1regulations_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-f1regulations-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice_and_qualifying">Practice and qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Indian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Practice and qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Fernando-alonso-malaysia-2013.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/64/Fernando-alonso-malaysia-2013.jpg/170px-Fernando-alonso-malaysia-2013.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="235" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/64/Fernando-alonso-malaysia-2013.jpg/255px-Fernando-alonso-malaysia-2013.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/6/64/Fernando-alonso-malaysia-2013.jpg 2x" data-file-width="301" data-file-height="416" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Fernando-alonso-malaysia-2013.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Fernando Alonso completed minimal running during Friday's first practice session due to a gearbox problem on his <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> car</div></div></div>
<p>Three practice sessions were held before the Sunday race—two on Friday from 11:00 to 12:00 and 14:00 to 15:30 local time, and a third on Saturday morning between 11:00 to 12:00.<sup id="cite_ref-f1regulations_9-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-f1regulations-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The first practice session took place in dry conditions with an ambient temperature of 30&#160;°C (86&#160;°F).<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> and a track temperature of 32&#160;°C (90&#160;°F).<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Vettel set the session's fastest time with a lap of 1 minute and 26.683, almost two-tenths of a second faster than teammate Webber. The Mercedes of Nico Rosberg set the third fastest time ahead of Renault driver <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> who was fourth fastest. Hamilton set the fifth fastest time in his Mercedes. The two McLaren drivers were sixth and seventh, <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> ahead of <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a>. <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> set the eighth and ninth fastest lap times respectively for Ferrari and Sauber. <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> in the Williams completed the top ten. Alonso's Ferrari car was afflicted by a gearbox problem; this restricted him to completing only six timed laps, and he was 12th overall.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The second practice session was held in similar weather to the first; the only difference was a slightly higher peak track temperature of 40&#160;°C (104&#160;°F).<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> During this session, Vettel set the fastest lap of the day, a 1:25.722; Webber finished with the second-fastest time. Grosjean ended up with the third fastest time. The Mercedes drivers were again quick—Hamilton in fourth and Rosberg sixth. They were split by Alonso; with teammate Massa in seventh. Räikkonen set the eighth fastest time. The McLaren drivers ran slower— Button in ninth and Pérez tenth. Williams driver <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> suffered from a loose wheel nut which deflated his right-front tyre, as a result of an earlier pitstop. Williams were fined €60,000.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Saturday's weather was again dry for third and final practice session, with an ambient temperature of 28&#160;°C (82&#160;°F) and a track temperature of 35&#160;°C (95&#160;°F).<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> The start of session was delayed by 20 minutes as the medical helicopter was unable to take off, due to poor visibility caused by smog.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> The Red Bull drivers were again quickest—Vettel setting the fastest time, a 1:25.332, over half a second faster than Webber. Alonso was able to improve his pace and set the third-fastest time. Hamilton and Rosberg set the eighth and tenth fastest times respectively; they were separated by Button.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="quotebox pullquote floatright" style="width:40%;&#32;;"><style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r887775652">.mw-parser-output .quotebox{background-color:#F9F9F9;border:1px solid #aaa;box-sizing:border-box;padding:10px;font-size:88%;max-width:100%}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatleft{margin:0.5em 1.4em 0.8em 0}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatright{margin:0.5em 0 0.8em 1.4em}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.centered{margin:0.5em auto 0.8em auto}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatleft p,.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatright p{font-style:inherit}.mw-parser-output .quotebox-title{background-color:#F9F9F9;text-align:center;font-size:larger;font-weight:bold}.mw-parser-output .quotebox-quote.quoted:before{font-family:"Times New Roman",serif;font-weight:bold;font-size:large;color:gray;content:" “ ";vertical-align:-45%;line-height:0}.mw-parser-output .quotebox-quote.quoted:after{font-family:"Times New Roman",serif;font-weight:bold;font-size:large;color:gray;content:" ” ";line-height:0}.mw-parser-output .quotebox .left-aligned{text-align:left}.mw-parser-output .quotebox .right-aligned{text-align:right}.mw-parser-output .quotebox .center-aligned{text-align:center}.mw-parser-output .quotebox cite{display:block;font-style:normal}@media screen and (max-width:360px){.mw-parser-output .quotebox{min-width:100%;margin:0 0 0.8em!important;float:none!important}}</style>
<div class="quotebox-quote left-aligned" style="">"The car felt good all weekend and it just got better as the track improved. The car was amazing; it's a great circuit, I really like the flow of it, especially around the middle sector with the high speed corners. If the car behaves the way you want it to through that section then you're always then going to enjoy it, as we did today. It's a great result for the team; Mark is on a different strategy, so we'll see what the race brings tomorrow. We have been getting a lot of questions about the championship this weekend, but we'll keep doing what we've done in the past, just focusing on every single step – we won't change that for tomorrow, or the next couple of races. We've worked hard to get here and tomorrow will be a long race. With the strategy, I think it's always tricky do the right thing, but we have a good package so we should be in good shape."</div>
<p><cite class="left-aligned" style=""><a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>, commenting on taking <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup></cite>
</p>
</div>
<p>Saturday afternoon's qualifying session was divided into three parts. The first part ran for 20 minutes and eliminated the cars from qualifying that finished the session 17th or lower. During this session, the <a href="/wiki/107%25_rule" title="107% rule">107% rule</a> was in effect, which necessitated each driver set a time within 107% of the quickest lap to qualify for the race.<sup id="cite_ref-f1regulations_9-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-f1regulations-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The second part of qualifying lasted 15 minutes and eliminated cars that finished in positions 11 to 16. The final part of qualifying determined the positions from first to tenth, and decided <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-f1regulations_9-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-f1regulations-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The session was held in dry weather; the ambient temperature was 30&#160;°C (86&#160;°F) with a track temperature of 39&#160;°C (102&#160;°F).<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Vettel set the fastest time in the second and final parts of the session, which clinched him <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> with a lap of 1:24.119. Vettel was joined on the front row by Rosberg, who was nearly eight-tenths of a second slower.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ricciardo was the fastest driver not to advance into the final session, qualifying 11th; his best time of 1:25.519 was nearly a second slower than Vettel's pace in the second session. Grosjean (Renault), <a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a> (Marussia), Caterham drivers <a href="/wiki/Giedo_van_der_Garde" title="Giedo van der Garde">Giedo van der Garde</a> and <a href="/wiki/Charles_Pic" title="Charles Pic">Charles Pic</a> and <a href="/wiki/Max_Chilton" title="Max Chilton">Max Chilton</a> (Marussia) failed to advance beyond the first part of qualifying, and thus completed the final rows of the grid. In the first part of qualifying (the only section in which all drivers took part), the entire field was covered by just over two seconds.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Indian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:172px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Sebastian_Vettel_2012_Bahrain_GP.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c9/Sebastian_Vettel_2012_Bahrain_GP.jpg/170px-Sebastian_Vettel_2012_Bahrain_GP.jpg" decoding="async" width="170" height="227" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c9/Sebastian_Vettel_2012_Bahrain_GP.jpg/255px-Sebastian_Vettel_2012_Bahrain_GP.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c9/Sebastian_Vettel_2012_Bahrain_GP.jpg/340px-Sebastian_Vettel_2012_Bahrain_GP.jpg 2x" data-file-width="648" data-file-height="864" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Sebastian_Vettel_2012_Bahrain_GP.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Sebastian Vettel, who took pole and was crowned <a href="/wiki/2013_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2013 Formula One season">2013 Formula One Drivers' Champion</a> as a result of his win (pictured in 2012)</div></div></div>
<p>The race took place in the afternoon from 15:00 local time, in dry and sunny weather, with an ambient temperature of 30&#160;°C (86&#160;°F) and a track temperature of 35&#160;°C (95&#160;°F).<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Vettel, from pole position on the grid, held onto the lead into the first corner. Massa, from fifth, made a good start, moving from 5th to 2nd place by the end of the first lap.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcf1race_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcf1race-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> Maldonado made the best start in the field, moving from 17th to 13th.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_21-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Going into the second corner, Alonso touched Webber, resulting in damage to his Ferrari's front wing.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcf1race_22-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcf1race-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> At the end of the first lap, Vettel led from Massa, Rosberg, Hamilton, Hülkenberg and Räikkönen.<sup id="cite_ref-lapchart_23-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-lapchart-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Vettel began to immediately pull clear of Massa. di Resta and Vergne became the first two drivers to pit by coming in on lap 2.<sup id="cite_ref-autosportrace_21-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosportrace-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Vettel pitted for a new set of medium compound tyres at the end of lap 2, allowing Massa to take the lead.<sup id="cite_ref-gpracenotes_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-gpracenotes-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On lap 40, Webber suffered an alternator failure and became the third retirement of the race.
</p><p>Vettel won his tenth race of the season in a time of 1:31:12.187, at an average speed of 125.598 miles per hour (202.130&#160;km/h). Vettel was crowned the 2013 Drivers' Championship as Alonso could not catch his points total with three races remaining. He became the fourth driver after <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a>, <a href="/wiki/Juan_Manuel_Fangio" title="Juan Manuel Fangio">Juan Manuel Fangio</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alain_Prost" title="Alain Prost">Alain Prost</a> to have won four or more Drivers' Championships.<sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg finished second in his Mercedes car, 29 seconds behind Vettel, with Grosjean third. Massa was fourth in his Ferrari with Pérez fifth for McLaren. Hamilton took sixth place in the other Mercedes and Räikkönen took seventh in the other Lotus car. The Force India cars of di Resta and Sutil were eighth and ninth respectively, with Ricciardo taking the final point in tenth place for Toro Rosso. Alonso finished eleventh with Maldonado trailing behind in twelfth. Vergne, Button, Gutiérrez and Bottas filled the next places, one lap behind the leader, while Chilton and Bianchi were two laps down in seventeenth and eighteenth. Hülkenberg was the final classified driver in nineteenth.
</p>

<p>The top three finishers appeared on the podium to collect their trophies, and in the subsequent press conference. <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> was given a reprimand for failing to drive straight to <a href="/wiki/Parc_ferm%C3%A9" title="Parc fermé">parc fermé</a> after the race, while <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a> were given a €25,000 fine (Rs 1,843,000) fine for failing to adequately instruct him to do so.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
