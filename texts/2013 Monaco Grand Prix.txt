<p>The <b>2013 Monaco Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Grand Prix de Monaco 2013</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> that took place on 26 May 2013 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Monaco" title="Circuit de Monaco">Circuit de Monaco</a>, a <a href="/wiki/Street_circuit" title="Street circuit">street circuit</a> that runs through the principality of <a href="/wiki/Monaco" title="Monaco">Monaco</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-21_Sept._calendar_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21_Sept._calendar-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The race was won by <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> for <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes AMG Petronas</a>, repeating the feat of his father <a href="/wiki/Keke_Rosberg" title="Keke Rosberg">Keke Rosberg</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1983_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="1983 Monaco Grand Prix">1983 race</a>. The race was the sixth round of the <a href="/wiki/2013_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2013 Formula One season">2013 season</a>, and marked the seventy-first time the <a href="/wiki/Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="Monaco Grand Prix">Monaco Grand Prix</a> has been held. Rosberg had started the race from pole.<sup id="cite_ref-qualify_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualify-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Tyres">Tyres</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Tyres">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Tyre supplier <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> brought its yellow-banded soft compound tyre as the harder "prime" tyre and the red-banded super-soft compound tyre as the softer "option" tyre, just as they did the previous two years. It was the second time in the season that the super-soft compound was used at a race weekend, as was the case with the soft tyre compound.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Free_Practice">Free Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Free Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Thursday's Free Practice sessions saw many drivers struggling to find grip on the circuit, as Jean-Éric Vergne and Kimi Räikkönen both suffered major lock-ups at turn 4, and went off into the small run-off area. Lewis Hamilton locked up badly at turn 8, with his teammate, Nico Rosberg, touching the barrier at turn 7. Grosjean and Massa both crashed into the barriers at turn 1, and with Massa's crash being on Saturday morning's Free Practice session, he was unable to compete in qualifying. Adrian Sutil crashed into the outside barrier at turn 2.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The three qualifying sessions were held on 25 May. Q1 and Q2 were rain-affected, with mostly dry conditions for Q3.<sup id="cite_ref-BBC-qual_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBC-qual-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Q1">Q1</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Q1">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Paul_di_Resta" title="Paul di Resta">Paul di Resta</a>, <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Charles_Pic" title="Charles Pic">Charles Pic</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Guti%C3%A9rrez" title="Esteban Gutiérrez">Esteban Gutiérrez</a>, <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Max_Chilton" title="Max Chilton">Max Chilton</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a>, and Ferrari's <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> did not make it out of the first qualifying session. Bianchi stopped on track with an engine issue before setting a timed lap. Massa did not run a lap as his car was still being repaired following a crash in Free Practice Session No. 3. <a href="/wiki/Giedo_van_der_Garde" title="Giedo van der Garde">Giedo van der Garde</a> became the first Caterham driver to advance to the second round of qualifying in the 2013 season.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Q2">Q2</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Q2">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Sauber's <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a>' <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a>, both <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> drivers <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a>, as well as Caterham's Giedo van der Garde were knocked out and did not advance.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Q3">Q3</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Q3">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Mercedes's Nico Rosberg took pole position for the Monaco Grand Prix with a time of 1m13.876s. Mercedes's Lewis Hamilton and Red Bull's <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> set the next two fastest times during the final round of qualifying.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix_-_Sunday.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d9/2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix_-_Sunday.jpg/220px-2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix_-_Sunday.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d9/2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix_-_Sunday.jpg/330px-2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix_-_Sunday.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d9/2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix_-_Sunday.jpg/440px-2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix_-_Sunday.jpg 2x" data-file-width="5184" data-file-height="3456" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix_-_Sunday.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Nico Rosberg in the Massenet curve</div></div></div>
<p>Rosberg led from pole position ahead of teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> at the start and Red Bull's <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a>. <a href="/wiki/Marussia" class="mw-redirect" title="Marussia">Marussia</a>'s Bianchi started from pit lane after an issue on the starting grid.<sup id="cite_ref-GP_report_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GP_report-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Maldonado and van der Garde had contact which forced Maldonado to pit the Williams for a new front wing. On lap 9, Charles Pic pulled over near the pit entry with an engine fire in the Caterham.<sup id="cite_ref-GP_report_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GP_report-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Ferrari's Felipe Massa had a large shunt going into Sainte Devote, very similar to his crash in the third free practice session on Saturday,<sup id="cite_ref-GP_report_8-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GP_report-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> but this time it was caused by a suspension failure.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> The safety car was deployed for the first time in the 2013 season and Rosberg retained the lead after a pit stop.<sup id="cite_ref-GP_report_8-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GP_report-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton dropped to fourth place after leaving too much of a gap to the Red Bulls.
</p><p>On lap 45, Maldonado and Chilton touched near Tabac sending Maldonado into the barriers. The race was <a href="/wiki/Red_flag_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="Red flag (racing)">red flagged</a> to repair damage to the barriers and remove Maldonado's wrecked Williams.<sup id="cite_ref-GP_report_8-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GP_report-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> The race resumed behind the safety car with Rosberg leading Vettel, Webber, and Hamilton. During the red flag stop, <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> was given the position by Alonso, who was trying to avoid a penalty caused by an earlier incident at the chicane.<sup id="cite_ref-GP_report_8-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GP_report-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Lotus's Grosjean crashed into the back of Toro Rosso's Ricciardo exiting the tunnel and the safety car was brought out once again.<sup id="cite_ref-GP_report_8-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GP_report-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> After the race, it was announced that Grosjean would receive a 10-place grid penalty for the Canada Grand Prix because of the crash.<sup id="cite_ref-BBC_Gros_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBC_Gros-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Pérez touched the barriers near the Nouvelle Chicane after trying to overtake Räikkönen on lap 69, Räikkönen had to pit for new tyres following a puncture caused by the collision.<sup id="cite_ref-GP_report_8-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GP_report-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Fernando Alonso, teammate Jenson Button and Räikkönen himself later complained about Pérez's aggressive driving during the race, even though he did not receive any penalties.<sup id="cite_ref-BBC_punch_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-BBC_punch-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Pérez later retired after running down the exit road near Rascasse.<sup id="cite_ref-GP_report_8-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GP_report-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After Vettel set the race's fastest lap, 1:16.577, on lap 77 Red Bull reprimanded their driver over the radio for taking unnecessary risks, saying that there were no points to be gained for setting the fastest lap. Vettel replied "but satisfaction".<sup id="cite_ref-GP_report_8-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GP_report-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Rosberg won the race ahead of Vettel, Webber and Hamilton, having led from start to finish. His victory came 30 years after his father, <a href="/wiki/Keke_Rosberg" title="Keke Rosberg">Keke Rosberg</a>, won the <a href="/wiki/1983_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="1983 Monaco Grand Prix">1983 Monaco Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Guardian-race_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Guardian-race-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Raikkonen passed several cars on the last two laps to take the final points position.<sup id="cite_ref-GP_report_8-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GP_report-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
