<p>The <b>2007 Chinese Grand Prix</b> was the sixteenth race of the <a href="/wiki/2007_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2007 Formula One season">2007 Formula One season</a>. It was held on 7 October 2007 at <a href="/wiki/Shanghai_International_Circuit" title="Shanghai International Circuit">Shanghai International Circuit</a>, <a href="/wiki/Shanghai" title="Shanghai">Shanghai</a>, <a href="/wiki/People%27s_Republic_of_China" class="mw-redirect" title="People&#39;s Republic of China">China</a>. The race was won by <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> with <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> finishing second and <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> finishing third. Championship leader <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> retired from the race after going off at a wet pit entrance on worn tyres left him stuck in the gravel. Räikkönen's win marked the 200th Grand Prix victory for <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Scuderia Ferrari</a>.
</p><p>It was the last Formula One race in which a <a href="/wiki/Tobacco_advertising" class="mw-redirect" title="Tobacco advertising">tobacco</a> brand name (<a href="/wiki/Marlboro_(cigarette)" title="Marlboro (cigarette)">Marlboro</a>) was visible on a car.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (March 2019)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Going into the race, <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> of <a href="/wiki/McLaren_Mercedes" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren Mercedes">McLaren</a> led the Drivers' Championship by 12 points from his team-mate <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>, after Alonso crashed in <a href="/wiki/Fuji_Speedway" title="Fuji Speedway">Fuji Speedway</a>. <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> was the other driver who mathematically had a chance of winning the championship, trailing Hamilton by 17 points. To clinch the championship, Hamilton needed to finish within one point of his team-mate Alonso and within 6 points of Räikkönen. Whatever the result, Hamilton would still be leading the championship after the race.
</p><p>The weather conditions at the track were affected by <a href="/wiki/2007_Pacific_typhoon_season#Typhoon_Krosa_(Ineng)" title="2007 Pacific typhoon season">Typhoon Krosa</a> which hit the east coast of China.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> was facing a drop of ten places on the grid for his collision with <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> in the previous race (the <a href="/wiki/2007_Japanese_Grand_Prix" title="2007 Japanese Grand Prix">Japanese Grand Prix</a>), but this punishment was reduced to a reprimand.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> However, the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a> driver was then dropped five places on the grid for impeding <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> during qualifying.<sup id="cite_ref-Vettel-5_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Vettel-5-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Spyker_F1" title="Spyker F1">Spykers</a> of <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sakon_Yamamoto" title="Sakon Yamamoto">Sakon Yamamoto</a> were both eliminated in the first qualifying session, along with <a href="/wiki/Takuma_Sato" title="Takuma Sato">Takuma Sato</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Super_Aguri_F1" title="Super Aguri F1">Super Aguri</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Wurz" title="Alexander Wurz">Alexander Wurz</a> in his final race for <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a>, <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a> and <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Honda_Racing_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Honda Racing F1">Honda</a>. However, Vettel's penalty caused him to be relegated behind Barrichello. In the second session, <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> in the second Williams, <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Davidson" title="Anthony Davidson">Anthony Davidson</a> in the Super Aguri, <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> in the Renault, <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a> in the Toyota and the two Toro Rossos of Vettel and <a href="/wiki/Vitantonio_Liuzzi" title="Vitantonio Liuzzi">Vitantonio Liuzzi</a> were eliminated.
</p><p>The final session saw <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> come 10th in a rare top ten qualification. The two <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Saubers</a> were 8th and 9th, with <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> ahead of <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a>. The <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bulls</a> of <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> did very well, qualifying 5th and 7th respectively. They were separated by <a href="/wiki/Ralf_Schumacher" title="Ralf Schumacher">Ralf Schumacher</a>'s Toyota. As usual in the 2007 season, the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferraris</a> and <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLarens</a> occupied the top 4 spots in qualifying, with Italian team's <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> pipping McLaren's <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>, to third place.
</p><p>Although <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> of Ferrari had been the fastest driver in all three practice sessions and in the first two sessions of qualifying, <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> managed to beat this time in his McLaren during the third qualifying session and therefore earnt the pole position, the 6th of his career.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Chinese_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:2007_China_GP_before_start.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3f/2007_China_GP_before_start.jpg/220px-2007_China_GP_before_start.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="122" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3f/2007_China_GP_before_start.jpg/330px-2007_China_GP_before_start.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/3f/2007_China_GP_before_start.jpg/440px-2007_China_GP_before_start.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2288" data-file-height="1272" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:2007_China_GP_before_start.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The starting grid before the beginning of the race.</div></div></div>
<p>From the morning of the race onwards, conditions at the circuit were wet; this forced all drivers to use <a href="/wiki/Rain_tyre" title="Rain tyre">intermediate tyres</a>. After the start of the race, the top four remained the same as on the grid with <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> leading in the <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> from <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, his teammate <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> and the other McLaren of <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>. <a href="/wiki/Vitantonio_Liuzzi" title="Vitantonio Liuzzi">Vitantonio Liuzzi</a> was the biggest winner from the start in his <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>, passing <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Honda_Racing_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Honda Racing F1">Honda</a>, <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> in the <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a> and the <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> of <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> on lap 1. He also passed Kubica's teammate <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> before too long to run sixth. <a href="/wiki/Ralf_Schumacher" title="Ralf Schumacher">Ralf Schumacher</a> had his <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyota</a> knocked from behind at the first corner, and slipped to the back of the field.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Anthony_Davidson" title="Anthony Davidson">Anthony Davidson</a>, who had qualified strongly, was the first retirement on lap 11, after his <a href="/wiki/Super_Aguri_F1" title="Super Aguri F1">Super Aguri</a> suffered a brake failure. He had already dropped down to twentieth position. <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> was the next to drop out in the <a href="/wiki/Spyker_F1" title="Spyker F1">Spyker</a>, also from twentieth, after he spun in the wet conditions on lap 25. Schumacher, recovering from his earlier incident, had made it up to twelfth on lap 22, before he spun twice and eventually retired on lap 26.
</p><p>As the track dried Hamilton began to suffer from tyre wear. With his pit stop imminent, McLaren elected to keep Hamilton out and stick to the original strategy. This turned out to be a detrimental choice, because by lap 31, Räikkönen had passed Hamilton when the latter ran wide, allowing Räikkönen to take the lead. Hamilton had to come into the pits. This should not have been a problem, however, as he would still have had enough points to secure the World Championship, had he remained in that position. However, as he entered the pit lane with his tyres worn down to the canvas, he failed to negotiate the sharp left-hander into the pits, beaching his car in the gravel. Despite marshal's efforts to get him back into the race, he suffered the first retirement of his career.
</p><p>An effective strategy from the BMW Sauber team benefited Robert Kubica, who was propelled into the lead after the Hamilton incident. It was short-lived, however, because after one lap, a hydraulics failure deprived him and the BMW Sauber team of a potential first victory each. This left Räikkönen leading from Alonso, who had overtaken Massa in the pitstops, and Jenson Button.
</p><p>Räikkönen eventually came home to win comfortably from Alonso, who after having pulled out a considerable lead from Massa was later pulled back until the Brazilian finished less than three seconds behind him. This result ensured the World Drivers' Championship would be decided in the one remaining round - the <a href="/wiki/2007_Brazilian_Grand_Prix" title="2007 Brazilian Grand Prix">Brazilian Grand Prix</a>. <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> of Toro Rosso managed to overtake Button in the closing stages to claim fourth place after he had started seventeenth. His one stop strategy gave him the best result of his career thus far. Button came home fifth, the best result of the season for both him and the Honda team. Liuzzi brought the other Toro Rosso home in sixth place, the team's best result of its career. Nick Heidfeld was seventh in the BMW Sauber, and <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> held on for the final points position in the Red Bull despite late pressure from <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Alexander_Wurz" title="Alexander Wurz">Alexander Wurz</a> finished twelfth in the <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a>, and announced his immediate retirement from Formula One the day after the race. Williams announced that he would be replaced by <a href="/wiki/Kazuki_Nakajima" title="Kazuki Nakajima">Kazuki Nakajima</a> for the last race of the season.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
