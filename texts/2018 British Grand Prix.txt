<p>The <b>2018 British Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 2018 Rolex British Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_racing" title="Formula One racing">Formula One motor race</a> held on 8 July 2018 at the <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Silverstone" title="Silverstone">Silverstone</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>. The race was the 10th round of the <a href="/wiki/2018_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2018 Formula One World Championship">2018 Formula One World Championship</a>. It marked 73rd running of the <a href="/wiki/British_Grand_Prix" title="British Grand Prix">British Grand Prix</a>, the 69th time that the race had been run as a World Championship event, and the 52nd time that the World Championship event had been held at the Silverstone Circuit.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> entered the race with a one-point lead over <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> in the Drivers' Championship. In the Constructors' Championship, Ferrari led <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> by ten points.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<table class="box-More_citations_needed_section plainlinks metadata ambox ambox-content ambox-Refimprove" role="presentation"><tbody><tr><td class="mbox-image"><div style="width:52px"><a href="/wiki/File:Question_book-new.svg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/9/99/Question_book-new.svg/50px-Question_book-new.svg.png" decoding="async" width="50" height="39" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/9/99/Question_book-new.svg/75px-Question_book-new.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/9/99/Question_book-new.svg/100px-Question_book-new.svg.png 2x" data-file-width="512" data-file-height="399" /></a></div></td><td class="mbox-text"><div class="mbox-text-span">This section <b>needs additional citations for <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Verifiability" title="Wikipedia:Verifiability">verification</a></b>.<span class="hide-when-compact"> Please help <a class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=2018_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit">improve this article</a> by <a href="/wiki/Help:Referencing_for_beginners" title="Help:Referencing for beginners">adding citations to reliable sources</a>. <p>The circuit featured three <a href="/wiki/Drag_reduction_system" title="Drag reduction system">drag reduction system</a> (DRS) zones. The two used in previous years – positioned on the Wellington and Hangar Straights – returned, with a third zone placed on the pit straight. As DRS is deactivated when the driver brakes, drivers were able to use the system through the Abbey and Farm corners as these corners could be taken flat-out when the car is low on fuel. This brought increased risk as DRS reduces drag by cancelling out aerodynamic grip at a time when aerodynamic grip improves the car's ability to take corners at speed. Drivers were able to manually deactivate DRS before the corners if they were unable or unwilling to take the corner with the use of DRS. The race marked the first time that drivers were able to use DRS through corners in a race.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> The decision to allow the use of DRS through the corners drew criticism as drivers felt that using DRS through Abbey corner was unsafe. Drivers found the best approach was to manually deactivate DRS before the corner without slowing before reactivating it on the corner exit. This approach was necessary as slowing the car would prevent the system from being reactivated.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The first practice session saw <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> crash at Abbey, after he left his DRS open whilst taking the corner,<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> stopped on the pit straight with a gearbox issue.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> In second practice, Verstappen crashed at Luffield<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> and <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a> broke down.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> In third practice, <a href="/wiki/Brendon_Hartley" title="Brendon Hartley">Brendon Hartley</a> had a heavy crash after his front left suspension failed at high speed.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Q1 was red flagged as <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a> went into the gravel and his car had to be removed.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> After an early showing from Ferrari, Hamilton took pole, his 6th around Silverstone. Ferrari qualified 2nd and 3rd, with <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> 4th.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Hartley missed qualifying after the heavy crash in FP3, and started from the pit lane,<sup id="cite_ref-:0_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> along with Stroll and <a href="/wiki/Sergey_Sirotkin_(racing_driver)" title="Sergey Sirotkin (racing driver)">Sergey Sirotkin</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc_(racing_driver)" class="mw-redirect" title="Charles Leclerc (racing driver)">Charles Leclerc</a> made Q3 for the second time, qualifying in P9.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Hamilton had a slow start and was jumped by Vettel and Bottas. At Turn 3, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> made contact with Hamilton, spinning him off the track and into last.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> also spun at the start, narrowly avoiding the Williams cars exiting the pit lane.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> Hartley failed to exit the pit lane.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> Räikkönen was given a 10 second time penalty for causing the collision between himself and Hamilton, which he served in the pits on lap 14.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> On lap 19, Leclerc retired after exiting the pits with a loose wheel.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> On lap 32, <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Ericsson" title="Marcus Ericsson">Marcus Ericsson</a> crashed at Abbey, after failing to deactivate his DRS quick enough.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> This brought out a safety car, and both Ferraris and both Red Bulls pitted for a 2nd time, for the soft tyres. Mercedes chose to leave their drivers out to gain track position. On lap 38, Grosjean and <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz</a> crashed at Copse, causing another safety car. On lap 46, Verstappen spun and subsequently retired with a brake by wire issue. The next lap, Vettel overtook Bottas for the lead of the race, which he kept until the chequered flag,<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> making it his second victory at Silverstone, the first since 2009.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Vettel became the first Ferrari driver to win at Silverstone since <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> in <a href="/wiki/2011_British_Grand_Prix" title="2011 British Grand Prix">2011</a>.
</p>
