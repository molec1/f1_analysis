<p>The <b>1987 Italian Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held at <a href="/wiki/Autodromo_Nazionale_di_Monza" title="Autodromo Nazionale di Monza">Monza</a> on 6 September 1987. It was the eleventh race of the <a href="/wiki/1987_FIA_Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="1987 FIA Formula One World Championship">1987 FIA Formula One World Championship</a>. It was the 57th <a href="/wiki/Italian_Grand_Prix" title="Italian Grand Prix">Italian Grand Prix</a> and the 52nd to be held at Monza. The race was held over 50 laps of the 5.8-kilometre (3.6&#160;mi) circuit for a race distance of 290 kilometres (180&#160;mi).
</p><p>The race was won from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> by Brazilian driver <a href="/wiki/Nelson_Piquet" title="Nelson Piquet">Nelson Piquet</a>, driving a <a href="/wiki/Williams_FW11" title="Williams FW11">Williams</a>-<a href="/wiki/Honda_in_Formula_One" title="Honda in Formula One">Honda</a>. Piquet took his third victory of the season by 1.8 seconds from compatriot <a href="/wiki/Ayrton_Senna" title="Ayrton Senna">Ayrton Senna</a>, who led in his <a href="/wiki/Lotus_99T" title="Lotus 99T">Lotus</a>-Honda before running wide at the Parabolica with eight laps to go. Piquet's British team-mate, <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Mansell" title="Nigel Mansell">Nigel Mansell</a>, finished third, nearly 50 seconds adrift. It was also the sixth consecutive victory for the Williams team, a run of wins that had begun at the <a href="/wiki/1987_French_Grand_Prix" title="1987 French Grand Prix">French Grand Prix</a> in early July.
</p><p>French <a href="/wiki/Tyrrell_DG016" title="Tyrrell DG016">Tyrrell DG016</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Philippe_Streiff" title="Philippe Streiff">Philippe Streiff</a> was the first naturally aspirated Jim Clark Trophy car to finish in 12th, three laps down on Piquet.
</p><p>The win strengthened Piquet's championship points lead to 14 points over Senna and 20 over Mansell.
</p><p>During qualifying, Piquet recorded a speed of 352.135&#160;km/h (218.807&#160;mph), the fastest achieved by a Formula One car during the first turbo era of the sport (1977-88).
</p><p>Due to the entries of <a href="/wiki/Automobiles_Gonfaronnaises_Sportives" title="Automobiles Gonfaronnaises Sportives">AGS</a> and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Coloni" title="Scuderia Coloni">Coloni</a>, for the first time in the 1987 season, there would be drivers who would fail to qualify for the race - in this case, it was to be <a href="/wiki/Nicola_Larini" title="Nicola Larini">Nicola Larini</a> in the Coloni and <a href="/wiki/Pascal_Fabre" title="Pascal Fabre">Pascal Fabre</a> in the AGS.
</p>
