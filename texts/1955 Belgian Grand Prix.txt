<p>The <b>1955 Belgian Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Motor_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Motor race">motor race</a> held at <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps" title="Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps">Spa-Francorchamps</a> on June 5, 1955. It was race 4 of 7 in the <a href="/wiki/1955_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1955 World Championship of Drivers">1955 World Championship of Drivers</a>. The 36-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Juan_Manuel_Fangio" title="Juan Manuel Fangio">Juan Manuel Fangio</a> after he started from second position. His teammate <a href="/wiki/Stirling_Moss" title="Stirling Moss">Stirling Moss</a> finished second and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Giuseppe_Farina" title="Giuseppe Farina">Nino Farina</a> came in third.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race_report">Race report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1955_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Race report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>This was the easiest of wins for Mercedes. With the absence of Lancia, there was very little competition. Fangio disappeared into the distance with Moss tracking him round. Private entrant Eugenio Castellotti retired after 16 laps, Jean Behra crashed and Hawthorn's Vanwall had an oil leak.
The domination of the silver cars was such that they came in over 2 minutes ahead of Farina in third place after he had lost a great deal of time attempting unsuccessfully to pass Castellotti.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notes">Notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1955_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Notes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Shared Drive: Car #24: <a href="/wiki/Roberto_Mieres" title="Roberto Mieres">Roberto Mieres</a> (10 laps) and <a href="/wiki/Jean_Behra" title="Jean Behra">Jean Behra</a> (25 laps). They shared the 2 points for fifth place.</li>
<li><a href="/wiki/Harry_Schell" title="Harry Schell">Harry Schell</a> set a grid time but his car was driven by teammate <a href="/wiki/Maurice_Trintignant" title="Maurice Trintignant">Maurice Trintignant</a>.</li>
<li>Team Lancia withdrew from Formula 1 after <a href="/wiki/Alberto_Ascari" title="Alberto Ascari">Alberto Ascari</a>'s fatal accident. <a href="/wiki/Eugenio_Castellotti" title="Eugenio Castellotti">Eugenio Castellotti</a> was a private entrant.</li></ul>
