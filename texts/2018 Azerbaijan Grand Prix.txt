<p>The <b>2018 Azerbaijan Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 2018 Azerbaijan Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_racing" title="Formula One racing">Formula One motor race</a> held on 29 April 2018 at the <a href="/wiki/Baku_City_Circuit" title="Baku City Circuit">Baku City Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Baku" title="Baku">Baku</a>, <a href="/wiki/Azerbaijan" title="Azerbaijan">Azerbaijan</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-18_calendar_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18_calendar-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The race was the 4th round of the <a href="/wiki/2018_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2018 Formula One World Championship">2018 Formula One World Championship</a>, the 2nd running of the <a href="/wiki/Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix" title="Azerbaijan Grand Prix">Azerbaijan Grand Prix</a> as a round of the Formula One World Championship and the 3rd time the <a href="/wiki/Baku_City_Circuit" title="Baku City Circuit">Baku City Circuit</a> was being used to host a Formula One race.<sup id="cite_ref-18_calendar_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18_calendar-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> was the defending race winner.<sup id="cite_ref-AZE_17_race_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-AZE_17_race-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> entered the round with a nine-point lead over <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> in the World Drivers' Championship.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> In the World Constructors' Championship, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> started the round with a one-point lead over Ferrari.<sup id="cite_ref-:1_6-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:1-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc_(racing_driver)" class="mw-redirect" title="Charles Leclerc (racing driver)">Charles Leclerc</a> finished 6th and became the first <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_drivers_from_Monaco" title="Formula One drivers from Monaco">Monégasque driver</a> to score a point since <a href="/wiki/Louis_Chiron" title="Louis Chiron">Louis Chiron</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1950_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="1950 Monaco Grand Prix">1950 Monaco Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:6_7-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Brendon_Hartley" title="Brendon Hartley">Brendon Hartley</a> finished 10th and became the first <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_drivers_from_New_Zealand" title="Formula One drivers from New Zealand">driver from New Zealand</a> to score a point since <a href="/wiki/Chris_Amon" title="Chris Amon">Chris Amon</a> in the <a href="/wiki/1976_Spanish_Grand_Prix" title="1976 Spanish Grand Prix">1976 Spanish Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-:7_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:7-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> This would prove to be the final podium for <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Baku_City_Circuit,_April_9,_2018_SkySat.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6d/Baku_City_Circuit%2C_April_9%2C_2018_SkySat.jpg/220px-Baku_City_Circuit%2C_April_9%2C_2018_SkySat.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="124" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6d/Baku_City_Circuit%2C_April_9%2C_2018_SkySat.jpg/330px-Baku_City_Circuit%2C_April_9%2C_2018_SkySat.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6d/Baku_City_Circuit%2C_April_9%2C_2018_SkySat.jpg/440px-Baku_City_Circuit%2C_April_9%2C_2018_SkySat.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3792" data-file-height="2133" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Baku_City_Circuit,_April_9,_2018_SkySat.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The Baku City Circuit in 2018</div></div></div>
<p>The race was brought forward from its June date to avoid clashing with celebrations for the centenary of the Azerbaijan republic.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> The race filled the berth taken by the <a href="/wiki/Russian_Grand_Prix" title="Russian Grand Prix">Russian Grand Prix</a>, which was moved to a September date. The vacancy created in June was filled by the newly-revived <a href="/wiki/French_Grand_Prix" title="French Grand Prix">French Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-18_calendar_2-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18_calendar-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> tyre compounds selected for the 2018 Azerbaijan Grand Prix were, in order of most hard to least, the soft (yellow), super-soft (red), and ultra-soft (purple).<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> Intermediate (green) and full wet (blue) tyres were available were rain to fall during qualifying or the race, but rain was not a factor and neither were used.
</p>

<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Start">Start</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Start">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>All drivers got off the line from the <a href="/wiki/Standing_start" title="Standing start">standing start</a> without incident. A minor racing incident which led to contact between <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Ocon" title="Esteban Ocon">Esteban Ocon</a> occurred as both navigated turn 2.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> On the straight after turn 2, Sirotkin's car made contact with <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso's</a> McLaren and Nico Hulkenberg's Renault, and caused a double tyre puncture (to Alonso's front right and rear right tyres), and puncture to Sirotkin's front left tyre as well as suspension damage.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:2_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> In the chaos, Force India's <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> struck the back of Räikkönen's car, causing him to make a pit stop.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_16-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> The first major contact in the race was again between Kimi Räikkönen and Esteban Ocon at turn 3, Ocon suffered damage sufficient to force his immediate retirement from the race.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Sergey_Sirotkin_(racing_driver)" title="Sergey Sirotkin (racing driver)">Sergey Sirotkin</a>'s tyre puncture caused him to stop near the turn 3 Räikkönen–Ocon incident. Ocon and Sirotkin's positions on the track caused a first lap <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a>. During the safety car, numerous drivers pitted due to damage, including Räikkönen (front wing change) Pérez (front wing change), Alonso (two tyre punctures, front wing change), and Kevin Magnussen (two tyre punctures).<sup id="cite_ref-:8_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Several safety car laps were needed to clear retired cars and <a href="/wiki/Carbon_fiber_reinforced_polymer" title="Carbon fiber reinforced polymer">carbon fibre</a> debris from the track.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Pérez received a five-second penalty for the contact with Räikkönen, which he served at his first pit stop.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (January 2019)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p><p>The top five drivers restarted in qualifying order, at the lap 6 restart.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_20-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz's</a> Renault, which qualified 10th, showed notable early race strength and was in 5th by lap 7.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_20-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> 4th place <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> reported issues with his <a href="/wiki/Kinetic_energy_recovery_system" title="Kinetic energy recovery system">KERS</a> battery after the restart, allowing the top three runners to pull away while a long queue of cars built behind him.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> The two factory <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renaults</a> of Sainz and <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> soon passed Verstappen to take 4th and 5th.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_20-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> On lap 10, Hülkenberg's rear end came out and struck the wall at turn 4, forcing him to retire.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Mid-race">Mid-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Mid-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>After Hulkenberg's retirement, the race approached a more typical Formula One race rhythm. Sainz pitted from 4th with worn ultrasoft tyres, and the Red Bulls of <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> and Max Verstappen raced 4th and 5th for several laps, occasionally exchanging positions.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_20-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:4_21-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos</a> dropped to 9th after his pitstop, he proceeded to fit the soft tyre but this turned out to be a blunder of a strategy because the tyres would not heat meaning that he had been overcut by both <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bulls</a> and was fighting for low points.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_20-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:4_21-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> The top three drivers (<a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>; <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a>; and <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a>, Mercedes) raced within 10 seconds of each other, 4th place Red Bull Verstappen, meanwhile, was 30 seconds behind race leader Vettel by lap 18.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_21-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton switched out worn supersoft tyres for softs on lap 23.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_21-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Bottas, meanwhile stayed out for a long stint on supersoft tyres. Race leader Vettel pitted for soft tyres on lap 31, intending to finish the race on them, leaving Bottas in the race lead (but still needing to pit).<sup id="cite_ref-:4_21-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> At this point, the main question for the race leaders was whether Bottas could build enough of a gap to pit for ultrasoft tyres and then threaten Vettel for the race win. Red Bull teammates Verstappen and Ricciardo continued close racing, with more position changes and overtaking one another in laps 27 and 28, then again as Ricciardo passed Verstappen to retake 4th place on lap 35.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_20-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Both Red Bull drivers pitted shortly after the lap 35 pass for fresh ultrasoft tyres.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_21-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Lap_40">Lap 40</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Lap 40">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>The tempo of the race was completely changed on lap 40, when Red Bull teammates Verstappen and Ricciardo (then in 4th and 5th place, respectively), made major contact.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>  Verstappen, who had just passed Ricciardo due to an overcut during a tyre change pitstop, made a late defensive move to cover an attempted overtake by Ricciardo which caused a collision between the pair.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_20-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> The collision was sufficient to retire both drivers, leave them both disabled on track, and force the race's second safety car.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_20-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:5_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> Several drivers in the top 10 took advantage of the situation; before the safety car bunched the drivers together, race leader Bottas made his long-awaited single pit stop for new ultrasoft tyres on which to finish the race, and second place Vettel, third place Hamilton, and fourth place Raikkonen also all pitted for new ultrasofts.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_21-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Fifth place <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> chose fresh supersoft tyres.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_21-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> On lap 43, while still under the safety car, 6th place <a href="/wiki/Haas_F1_Team" title="Haas F1 Team">Haas</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> lost traction and hit the wall, forcing his retirement and extending the length of the safety car stint.<sup id="cite_ref-:4_21-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:4-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:5_25-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:5-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The safety car could not pull in until lap 48, and Bottas kept the race lead as the lap began.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_20-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> 2nd placed Vettel attempted an aggressive move to pass Bottas, locked his wheels up and overshot the turn, ultimately leaving him in 4th place with flat-spotted tyres behind Bottas, Hamilton, and Raikkonen.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> Just as lap 49 began, Bottas, leading the race by more than one second over Hamilton, suffered a tyre puncture and was forced to retire.<sup id="cite_ref-:2_16-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:2-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> At the same time, Force India's Pérez passed Vettel to gain a 3rd place podium position as Bottas dropped out.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_20-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Finish">Finish</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Finish">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Bottas's sudden retirement was the last incident that affected the podium order. Hamilton won, Raikkonen was second, and Pérez third.<sup id="cite_ref-:9_27-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:9-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Other notable points finishers included Renault's Sainz in fifth, <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc_(racing_driver)" class="mw-redirect" title="Charles Leclerc (racing driver)">Charles Leclerc</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso's</a> <a href="/wiki/Brendon_Hartley" title="Brendon Hartley">Brendon Hartley</a> (who both earned the first Formula One championship points of their careers).<sup id="cite_ref-:6_7-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:6-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:7_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:7-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-:9_27-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:9-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> drivers Fernando Alonso and <a href="/wiki/Stoffel_Vandoorne" title="Stoffel Vandoorne">Stoffel Vandoorne</a> also both managed to earn points: Alonso finished seventh despite the double puncture he suffered early in the race; while Vandoorne used the safety car situation to his advantage.<sup id="cite_ref-:9_27-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:9-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> After having raced almost the entire afternoon in one of the last few places following an unscheduled extra pit stop due to damaged tyres, while all drivers were suffering with dropping tyre temperatures, Vandoorne opted to make a fourth pit stop from 13th place the lap before the safety car returned to the pits.<sup id="cite_ref-:8_19-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:8-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> With his heated tyres<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (May 2018)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> he was able to move from 14th to ninth after the restart.<sup id="cite_ref-:3_20-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:3-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2018_Azerbaijan_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Race winner Lewis Hamilton expressed surprise at his race win, and gave high praise to his teammate's performance. "Valtteri (Bottas) deserved to win, he did an exceptional job and had a faultless drive. ... I would not have got by him in the remaining laps if he hadn't had his tyre blow."<sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Red Bull team boss Christian Horner was reported to have been very angry after the lap 40 crash that forced the retirement of both his drivers, and called the contact "unacceptable".<sup id="cite_ref-:0_29-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> Horner required both Verstappen and Ricciardo to apologize to all members of the Red Bull staff for the incident. Both drivers received an official reprimand from the FIA for the crash in Baku.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_29-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> Verstappen and Ricciardo's crash drew comparisons to the <a href="/wiki/2010_Turkish_Grand_Prix" title="2010 Turkish Grand Prix">2010 Turkish Grand Prix</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-:0_29-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> where then-teammates <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> and Sebastian Vettel made contact with each other. In that race, Vettel was forced to retire immediately with a tyre puncture, and Webber managed only 3rd place in a race Red Bull had dominated up to that point.<sup id="cite_ref-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
