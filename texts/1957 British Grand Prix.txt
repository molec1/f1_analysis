<p>The <b>1957 British Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Motor_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Motor race">motor race</a> held on 20 July 1957 at the <a href="/wiki/Aintree_Circuit" class="mw-redirect" title="Aintree Circuit">Aintree Circuit</a>, near <a href="/wiki/Liverpool" title="Liverpool">Liverpool</a>. It was the tenth <a href="/wiki/British_Grand_Prix" title="British Grand Prix">British Grand Prix</a> and it was race 5 of 8 in the <a href="/wiki/1957_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1957 World Championship of Drivers">1957 World Championship of Drivers</a>. The race was won by <a href="/wiki/Stirling_Moss" title="Stirling Moss">Stirling Moss</a> and <a href="/wiki/Tony_Brooks_(racing_driver)" title="Tony Brooks (racing driver)">Tony Brooks</a>, who shared driving duties in a <a href="/wiki/Vanwall" title="Vanwall">Vanwall</a>. It was the third and final time that a Grand Prix had been won by two drivers in a shared car. This was the first occasion that a British-built car won a World Drivers' Championship race, a feat achieved with two British drivers at their home Grand Prix.
</p>
