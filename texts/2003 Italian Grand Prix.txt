<p>The <b>2003 Italian Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>LXXIV Gran Premio Vodafone d'Italia</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 14 September 2003 at the <a href="/wiki/Autodromo_Nazionale_di_Monza" title="Autodromo Nazionale di Monza">Autodromo Nazionale di Monza</a>, <a href="/wiki/Monza" title="Monza">Monza</a>, Italy. It was the fourteenth race of the <a href="/wiki/2003_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2003 Formula One season">2003 Formula One season</a> and the eighty-seventh <a href="/wiki/Italian_Grand_Prix" title="Italian Grand Prix">Italian Grand Prix</a>. The 53-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> driving for <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> after starting from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>. <a href="/wiki/Juan_Pablo_Montoya" title="Juan Pablo Montoya">Juan Pablo Montoya</a> finished second in a <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> car, with <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a> third in the other Ferrari.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notes">Notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2003_Italian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Notes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<table class="box-Trivia plainlinks metadata ambox ambox-style" role="presentation"><tbody><tr><td class="mbox-image"><div style="width:52px"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/f/f2/Edit-clear.svg/40px-Edit-clear.svg.png" decoding="async" width="40" height="40" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/f/f2/Edit-clear.svg/60px-Edit-clear.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/f/f2/Edit-clear.svg/80px-Edit-clear.svg.png 2x" data-file-width="48" data-file-height="48" /></div></td><td class="mbox-text"><div class="mbox-text-span">This section contains a <b><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Manual_of_Style/Trivia_sections" title="Wikipedia:Manual of Style/Trivia sections">list of miscellaneous information</a>.</b><span class="hide-when-compact"> Please <a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Handling_trivia#Recommendations_for_handling_trivia" title="Wikipedia:Handling trivia">relocate</a> any relevant information into other sections or articles.</span>  <small class="date-container"><i>(<span class="date">September 2020</span>)</i></small></div></td></tr></tbody></table>
<ul><li>Gené replaced the injured <a href="/wiki/Ralf_Schumacher" title="Ralf Schumacher">Ralf Schumacher</a> for this race, scoring his highest ever Formula One finish and his last points.</li>
<li>The race was completed with the fastest ever average race speed of 247.585&#160;km/h.</li>
<li>This was the shortest ever race not to be red flagged.</li></ul>
