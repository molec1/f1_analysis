<p>The <b>1999 Spanish Grand Prix</b><sup id="cite_ref-formula1.com_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formula1.com-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 30 May 1999 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Catalunya" class="mw-redirect" title="Circuit de Catalunya">Circuit de Catalunya</a> near <a href="/wiki/Barcelona" title="Barcelona">Barcelona</a>, <a href="/wiki/Spain" title="Spain">Spain</a>. It was the fifth race of the <a href="/wiki/1999_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="1999 Formula One season">1999 Formula One season</a>. The 65-lap race was won from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> by <a href="/wiki/Mika_H%C3%A4kkinen" title="Mika Häkkinen">Mika Häkkinen</a>, driving a <a href="/wiki/McLaren_MP4/14" title="McLaren MP4/14">McLaren</a>-<a href="/wiki/Mercedes_AMG_High_Performance_Powertrains" title="Mercedes AMG High Performance Powertrains">Mercedes</a>, with team-mate <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> second and <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> third in a <a href="/wiki/Ferrari_F399" title="Ferrari F399">Ferrari</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1999_Spanish_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1999_Spanish_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Mika_H%C3%A4kkinen" title="Mika Häkkinen">Mika Häkkinen</a>, starting on pole for the fifth time in 1999, got off the line quickly and began building a gap between him and second place. <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jacques_Villeneuve" title="Jacques Villeneuve">Jacques Villeneuve</a>, starting third and sixth respectively, had great starts and were able to take second and third place by the first corner. <a href="/wiki/Eddie_Irvine" title="Eddie Irvine">Eddie Irvine</a>, starting second, had a poor start and fell to fifth place after being passed by Coulthard, Villeneuve, and then in the second corner, his teammate <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a>. <a href="/wiki/Olivier_Panis" title="Olivier Panis">Olivier Panis</a> and <a href="/wiki/Marc_Gen%C3%A9" title="Marc Gené">Marc Gené</a> failed to start, with Gené retiring and Panis able to continue on in last place.<sup id="cite_ref-autosport_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosport-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The McLaren duo of Häkkinen and Coulthard began building up a substantial gap, with the Ferraris being held up by Villeneuve.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> Eddie Irvine, on lap 23, became the first of the front runners to pit. Mika Häkkinen came in the next lap, and had built up such a lead that he only fell to second place. Villeneuve and Schumacher both stopped simultaneously, but Villeneuve's stop was longer and Schumacher was able to pass Villeneuve in the pits. Irvine was also able to capitalize on Villeneuve's slow stop to pass him.<sup id="cite_ref-autosport_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosport-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After pitting, Michael Schumacher was initially caught behind <a href="/wiki/Heinz-Harald_Frentzen" title="Heinz-Harald Frentzen">Heinz-Harald Frentzen</a>, but once past, he began the task of chasing down David Coulthard, fourteen seconds ahead. Schumacher closed to within a second of Coulthard on lap 41, but he pitted the next lap without making an attempt to pass. Mika Häkkinen pitted the lap after Schumacher, and Coulthard pitted on lap 45. Coulthard's stop was slightly faster that Schumacher's, and came out just ahead of Schumacher. Schumacher was able to close up behind Coulthard on cold tires, but was unable to pass Coulthard. Once Coulthard's tires warmed up, he was able to pull away from Schumacher.<sup id="cite_ref-autosport_3-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosport-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Jacques Villeneuve pitted on lap 40, suffering from a broken rear wing element. The mechanics pulled the broken element off the wing, but first gear broke when Villeneuve tried to leave his pit box, and he retired.<sup id="cite_ref-autosport_3-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-autosport-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Michael Schumacher made another late charge on David Coulthard as the two battled through the lap traffic of <a href="/wiki/Damon_Hill" title="Damon Hill">Damon Hill</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Toranosuke_Takagi" title="Toranosuke Takagi">Toranosuke Takagi</a>. Schumacher ran out of time, however, and finished the race in third place behind Coulthard. Mika Häkkinen won the race by over six seconds, marking the first time that both McLarens finished a race in 1999.<sup id="cite_ref-formula1.com_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formula1.com-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
