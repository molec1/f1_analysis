<p>The <b>2012 British Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>2012 Formula One Santander British Grand Prix</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-official_name_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-official_name-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race that took place at the <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone Circuit</a> in Northamptonshire and <a href="/wiki/Buckinghamshire" title="Buckinghamshire">Buckinghamshire</a>, England on 8 July 2012.<sup id="cite_ref-7Dec_calendar_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7Dec_calendar-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It was the ninth round of the <a href="/wiki/2012_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2012 Formula One season">2012 Formula One season</a>, the 63rd time <a href="/wiki/British_Grand_Prix" title="British Grand Prix">the event</a> had been a round of the Formula One World Championship, and the 67th time it had been contested overall. The 52-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a>, who took his second victory of the season. The <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> of <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>, who started the race in pole position, finished 3.0 seconds behind Webber, in second. Webber's teammate, <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>, completed the podium by finishing in third position.
</p><p>As a consequence of the race, Webber narrowed Alonso's lead in the drivers' standings to 13 points. Webber himself was 16 points ahead of Vettel, who had moved ahead of <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> into third in the standings on 100 points. Red Bull extended their lead in the constructors' standings to 64 points. Ferrari moved up from fourth place to second whilst McLaren did the reverse and Lotus stayed in third position. All three teams stayed within 10 points of each other.
</p><p>This would prove to be Webber's final career F1 victory and the last Red Bull Racing victory at Silverstone until <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> won the <a href="/wiki/70th_Anniversary_Grand_Prix" title="70th Anniversary Grand Prix">70th Anniversary Grand Prix</a> there in 2020.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a> was given a ten-place grid penalty for the race after causing an avoidable collision with <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> during <a href="/wiki/2012_European_Grand_Prix" title="2012 European Grand Prix">the previous race</a>. <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a> took a five-place grid penalty for the same offence, having caused an avoidable collision with <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> in Valencia.<sup id="cite_ref-JEV_Kamui_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-JEV_Kamui-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>After out-qualifying <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a> drivers Vergne and <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> in Valencia, <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterham</a> announced plans to bring several upgrades for the <a href="/wiki/Caterham_CT-01" class="mw-redirect" title="Caterham CT-01">CT-01 chassis</a> to the race.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia</a> joined Caterham in bringing significant updates to their car, which the Russian team described as their "first proper windtunnel-generated update"<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> since abandoning their <a href="/wiki/Computational_fluid_dynamics" title="Computational fluid dynamics">computational fluid dynamics</a>-only approach at the start of the 2012 season.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> Test driver <a href="/wiki/Mar%C3%ADa_de_Villota" title="María de Villota">María de Villota</a> was involved in a serious accident while carrying out a straight-line aerodynamic test of the upgraded <a href="/wiki/Marussia_MR01" title="Marussia MR01">MR01 chassis</a> in the week preceding the race at <a href="/wiki/Duxford_Aerodrome" title="Duxford Aerodrome">Duxford Aerodrome</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> She was removed from the car with life-threatening injuries, including skull fractures and the loss of her right eye.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Like the <a href="/wiki/2011_British_Grand_Prix" title="2011 British Grand Prix">2011 British Grand Prix</a>, tyre supplier Pirelli brought its silver-banded hard compound tyre as the harder "prime" tyre and the yellow-banded soft compound tyre as the softer "option" tyre.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Dani_Clos" title="Dani Clos">Dani Clos</a> replaced <a href="/wiki/Narain_Karthikeyan" title="Narain Karthikeyan">Narain Karthikeyan</a> at <a href="/wiki/HRT_Formula_1_Team" title="HRT Formula 1 Team">HRT</a> for the first practice session,<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> having previously driven the car at the <a href="/wiki/2012_Spanish_Grand_Prix" title="2012 Spanish Grand Prix">Spanish Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> took <a href="/wiki/Bruno_Senna" title="Bruno Senna">Bruno Senna</a>'s seat at <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a>, while <a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a> drove <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a>'s car for <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> for the duration of the session.<sup id="cite_ref-wet_wet_wet_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wet_wet_wet-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Free_Practice">Free Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Free Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:2012_British_GP_-_Kovalainen.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/91/2012_British_GP_-_Kovalainen.jpg/220px-2012_British_GP_-_Kovalainen.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="117" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/91/2012_British_GP_-_Kovalainen.jpg/330px-2012_British_GP_-_Kovalainen.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/91/2012_British_GP_-_Kovalainen.jpg/440px-2012_British_GP_-_Kovalainen.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2048" data-file-height="1089" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:2012_British_GP_-_Kovalainen.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The wet conditions in Friday's practice sessions saw limited running.</div></div></div>
<p>The teams were due to test an "experimental" hard tyre compound developed by <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> during the first free practice, with a view to introducing it as a racing tyre later in the season;<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> however, wet conditions made this impossible.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> was the fastest driver in the first session, ahead of <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>. <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> and the Force Indias of <a href="/wiki/Paul_di_Resta" title="Paul di Resta">Paul di Resta</a> and Jules Bianchi elected not to set lap times in the face of the difficult conditions.<sup id="cite_ref-wet_wet_wet_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-wet_wet_wet-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The second session was similarly washed out and saw limited running as teams tried to preserve their wet- and intermediate-compound tyres. Hamilton was fastest, leading <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a> and <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> as Grosjean elected not to set a time.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Bruno_Senna" title="Bruno Senna">Bruno Senna</a> brought out a red flag that resulted in a ten-minute stop to the session when he hit a patch of standing water at Becketts, spinning and crashing heavily into the barriers. Fernando Alonso also encountered trouble, spinning and hitting the wall late in the session, but the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver was able to return to the pits.
</p><p>The third and final session was declared dry, but was run under the threat of rain and saw plenty of activity as the teams pushed hard to make up for lost time running in the wet on Friday. <a href="/wiki/Charles_Pic" title="Charles Pic">Charles Pic</a> stopped on the circuit after twenty minutes, forcing the temporary suspension of the session while <a href="/wiki/Marussia_MR01" title="Marussia MR01">his car</a> was removed. Pic later returned to the circuit, but stopped once again, and his car was removed by marshalls without forcing the session to be stopped. Alonso ended the session fastest overall.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Qualifying started in wet conditions, and with the threat of more rain on the way, a queue of cars formed at the end of pit lane in the minutes before the session began. Once out, many drivers were instructed by their teams to keep going with their flying laps, even if they felt they had been impeded by a slower car because the rapidly evolving nature of the circuit meant that they could still set a fast lap time. <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> was eliminated in eighteenth position when he could not find clear track space. His final flying lap saw him set sector times that would have been fast enough to avoid elimination by over a second and a half, but a spin on the main straight by <a href="/wiki/Timo_Glock" title="Timo Glock">Timo Glock</a> brought out yellow flags, prompting Button to back off, where he was duly eliminated. <a href="/wiki/Vitaly_Petrov" title="Vitaly Petrov">Vitaly Petrov</a> out-qualified teammate <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> for the second time in 2012, with the Caterhams starting in eighteenth and nineteenth once grid penalties had been applied. Despite his spin, Glock's fastest lap saw him qualify twentieth, ahead of both HRT drivers, with <a href="/wiki/Pedro_de_la_Rosa" title="Pedro de la Rosa">Pedro de la Rosa</a> once again out-qualifying <a href="/wiki/Narain_Karthikeyan" title="Narain Karthikeyan">Narain Karthikeyan</a>. <a href="/wiki/Charles_Pic" title="Charles Pic">Charles Pic</a> failed to set a lap time within <a href="/wiki/107%25_rule" title="107% rule">107%</a> of the fastest time in the session, set by <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>.
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:2012_British_GP_-_Raikkonen.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cd/2012_British_GP_-_Raikkonen.jpg/220px-2012_British_GP_-_Raikkonen.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="131" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cd/2012_British_GP_-_Raikkonen.jpg/330px-2012_British_GP_-_Raikkonen.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/cd/2012_British_GP_-_Raikkonen.jpg/440px-2012_British_GP_-_Raikkonen.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2670" data-file-height="1586" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:2012_British_GP_-_Raikkonen.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> qualified his <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a> in sixth position.</div></div></div>
<p>The weather delivered on its promise of more rain, inundating the circuit ahead of Q2. The session was red-flagged with just over six minutes remaining after four cars span off within the space of a minute, and <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> at the top of the time sheets. The stoppage lasted for ninety-two minutes while race officials waited for the rain to stop and the circuit to clear. When the session re-opened, the seventeen drivers would have enough time left over for one set of flying laps, forcing them to choose between the intermediate and wet-weather compounds as the length of a lap in the wet meant that the drivers would not have enough time to pit and change tyres if they made the wrong choice. Pérez chose the intermediate compound which quickly proved to be the wrong choice as he rapidly plummeted down the order, and finished slowest. He would start the race fifteenth after penalties were applied. <a href="/wiki/Paul_di_Resta" title="Paul di Resta">Paul di Resta</a>'s final lap gave him a provisional start in Q3, but he was eliminated in the final moments of the session and started eleventh. <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a> was twelfth, but dropped to seventeenth when his penalty from Valencia was applied. <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> and <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> finished thirteenth and fourteenth, ahead of <a href="/wiki/Bruno_Senna" title="Bruno Senna">Bruno Senna</a> who admitted to being "too conservative" in qualifying. <a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a> qualified sixteenth, but was moved to the back of the grid once his penalty was applied. <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> made it through to Q3, but spun at the end of the period at the Vale chicane, and took no further part in the session.
</p><p>Q3 was fought between the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferraris</a>, <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bulls</a> and <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a>. <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> and <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> quickly locked out the front row of the grid, until late laps from <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> left the Red Bull cars first and second. Massa was unable to improve his lap time and finished fifth, while Schumacher pushed Webber off pole. Webber reclaimed it with his next lap, only to lose it in turn to Alonso on the Spaniard's final lap of the session. <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>, the last driver to set a time, finished sixth, ahead of <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>. <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> was ninth, which became fourteenth when he took a five-place grid penalty for a gearbox change.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<table class="box-Expand_section plainlinks metadata ambox mbox-small-left ambox-content" role="presentation"><tbody><tr><td class="mbox-image"><a href="/wiki/File:Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg" class="image"><img alt="[icon]" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg/20px-Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg.png" decoding="async" width="20" height="14" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg/30px-Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg.png 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/1/1c/Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg/40px-Wiki_letter_w_cropped.svg.png 2x" data-file-width="44" data-file-height="31" /></a></td><td class="mbox-text"><div class="mbox-text-span">This section <b>needs expansion</b>. <small>You can help by <a class="external text" href="https://en.wikipedia.org/w/index.php?title=2012_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=">adding to it</a>.</small>  <small class="date-container"><i>(<span class="date">July 2012</span>)</i></small></div></td></tr></tbody></table>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Fernando_Alonso_-_Inggris_2012.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/db/Fernando_Alonso_-_Inggris_2012.jpg/220px-Fernando_Alonso_-_Inggris_2012.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="125" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/db/Fernando_Alonso_-_Inggris_2012.jpg/330px-Fernando_Alonso_-_Inggris_2012.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/db/Fernando_Alonso_-_Inggris_2012.jpg/440px-Fernando_Alonso_-_Inggris_2012.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2610" data-file-height="1480" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Fernando_Alonso_-_Inggris_2012.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> started the race in pole position and finished in second place after being overtaken by <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> near the end of the race.</div></div></div>
<p>An engine failure on <a href="/wiki/Vitaly_Petrov" title="Vitaly Petrov">Vitaly Petrov</a>'s Caterham on his way round to the grid meant he had to return to the pits and retire the car before the race had even begun. When it did, however, it was polesitter Alonso who maintained his lead into the first corner. Webber and Schumacher followed him round in their same positions, but Vettel fell prey to Massa and Räikkönen, before quickly getting back past the Finn. <a href="/wiki/Paul_di_Resta" title="Paul di Resta">Paul di Resta</a>, in tenth place at the time, suffered a puncture as a result of his right rear tyre coming into contact with <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a>'s front wing at the start of the Wellington straight. Di Resta spun and retired from the damage on the following lap whilst Grosjean pitted on the third lap to replace his front wing and change tyres after his impaired front wing was costing him places. Later on in the race, <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> had an incident at Brooklands at the end of the Wellington Straight. Maldonado tried to overtake Pérez on the inside of the corner under braking, whilst Pérez was defending on the inside line Maldonado's front wing clipped Pérez's car and sent both of them into a spin, the result of this would be Pérez retiring from the race and Maldonado having to make an unscheduled pit stop to repair damage to his front wing. This would be a disaster for his strategy in the race because a lap earlier, he pitted for new tyres, Maldonado would later finish 16th in the race. At the start of lap 50, <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> was closing in on race leader Fernando Alonso, with Vettel shortly behind his team mate in 3rd place. Webber was faster than Alonso due to his tyres being worn more than Webber's, but then with 3 laps to go Webber was within a second of Alonso's car, this would mean he was eligible to use DRS in the allocated DRS zone on the Wellington Straight, this would give him extra speed. Alonso knew he was slower than Webber so he took the inside line as Webber passed Alonso for the lead of the race, Alonso tried to attack on him in the next few corners. Now Vettel would try and close up on Alonso. When the chequered flag fell Mark Webber won his second British Grand Prix, following his victory in <a href="/wiki/2010_British_Grand_Prix" title="2010 British Grand Prix">2010</a>. Alonso was 2nd, Vettel not having the pace to catch Alonso and remaining 3rd. <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> finished 5 seconds behind Vettel, Romain Grosjean recovered from the first lap incident to finish sixth, behind his team mate <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>. The other points scorers were <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Schumacher</a>, <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Hamilton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bruno_Senna" title="Bruno Senna">Senna</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> in 10th.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> from the <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> team received a 10,000 <a href="/wiki/Euro_(currency)" class="mw-redirect" title="Euro (currency)">euro</a> fine for an incident with <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a>.
</p><p>Sauber's other driver <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a> received a 25,000 euro fine for crashing into four pit crew members in a manoeuvre that was described as a 'very dangerous move which had potentially serious implications'.
</p><p>The race also introduced a twist in the post-race television unilateral interview; instead of Webber, Alonso, and Vettel proceeding to the interview room in the media centre, <a href="/wiki/Jackie_Stewart" title="Jackie Stewart">Jackie Stewart</a> conducted the televised interview on the podium, allowing the drivers to address the crowd directly.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> The drivers then proceeded to the interview room for the press conference with assembled media.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> This form of post-race interviewing was used in every race until the <a href="/wiki/2018_Chinese_Grand_Prix" title="2018 Chinese Grand Prix">2018 Chinese Grand Prix</a>.
</p>
