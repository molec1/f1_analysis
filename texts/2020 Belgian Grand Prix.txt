<p>The <b>2020 Belgian Grand Prix</b> (officially known as the <b>Formula 1 Rolex Belgian Grand Prix 2020</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> held on 30 August 2020 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps" title="Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps">Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps</a> in <a href="/wiki/Stavelot" title="Stavelot">Stavelot</a>, <a href="/wiki/Belgium" title="Belgium">Belgium</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The race was the seventh round in the <a href="/wiki/2020_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2020 Formula One World Championship">2020 Formula One World Championship</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>A minute of silence was held before the race to mark the one year anniversary of <a href="/wiki/Anthoine_Hubert" title="Anthoine Hubert">Anthoine Hubert</a>'s death and <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a> wore a tribute helmet for Hubert. All drivers and teams put a star sticker on their chassis and helmets in tribute to Hubert.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Impact_of_the_COVID-19_pandemic">Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">See also: <a href="/wiki/2020_Formula_One_World_Championship#Impact_of_the_COVID-19_pandemic" title="2020 Formula One World Championship">2020 Formula One World Championship §&#160;Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic</a>, and <a href="/wiki/COVID-19_pandemic_in_Belgium" title="COVID-19 pandemic in Belgium">COVID-19 pandemic in Belgium</a></div>
<p>The opening rounds of the <a href="/wiki/2020_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2020 Formula One World Championship">2020</a> championship were heavily affected by the <a href="/wiki/COVID-19_pandemic" title="COVID-19 pandemic">COVID-19 pandemic</a>. Several Grands Prix were cancelled or postponed after the aborted <a href="/wiki/2020_Australian_Grand_Prix" title="2020 Australian Grand Prix">opening round</a> in <a href="/wiki/Australia" title="Australia">Australia</a>, prompting the <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_l%27Automobile" title="Fédération Internationale de l&#39;Automobile">FIA</a> to draft a new calendar. However, the Belgian Grand Prix was not impacted by this change and kept its original date.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In April 2020, the Belgian government extended a ban on mass gatherings until 31 August 2020 in a bid to control the spread of the COVID-19 pandemic. However, the race later received permission to be held <a href="/wiki/Behind_closed_doors_(sport)" title="Behind closed doors (sport)">behind closed doors</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Entrants">Entrants</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Entrants">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">Further information: <a href="/wiki/2020_Formula_One_World_Championship#Entries" title="2020 Formula One World Championship">2020 Formula One World Championship §&#160;Entries</a></div>
<p>The drivers and teams were the same as those on the season entry list with no additional stand-in drivers for either the race or practice.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Tyres">Tyres</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Tyres">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">See also: <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyres" title="Formula One tyres">Formula One tyres</a></div>
<p>Pirelli brought forward the C2, C3 and C4 tyres for the race weekend.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The first practice session took place without major incident and ended with <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> fastest for <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> ahead of teammate <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> and <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a>. <a href="/wiki/Haas_F1_Team" title="Haas F1 Team">Haas</a> drivers <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alfa_Romeo_in_Formula_One" title="Alfa Romeo in Formula One">Alfa Romeo</a>’s <a href="/wiki/Antonio_Giovinazzi" title="Antonio Giovinazzi">Antonio Giovinazzi</a> were all unable to set a lap due to power unit issues for the Haas cars and unexplained issues for Giovinazzi’s Alfa car.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
In the second practice session, Max Verstappen was fastest for Red Bull, ahead of <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a>’s <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> and Hamilton in the Mercedes.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Daniel Ricciardo suffered a hydraulics issue on the Kemmel straight shortly after setting the second fastest time resulting in a virtual safety car. A short red flag period followed soon due to an advertising billboard coming loose and falling on the track at turn 1. In the third and final practice session, Hamilton was fastest followed by <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Ocon" title="Esteban Ocon">Esteban Ocon</a> for Renault and <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">Lando Norris</a> for <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>. <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, who had won the previous Belgian Grand Prix struggled in third practice with <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a> 17th and <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> last.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> took pole, 0.511 seconds ahead of teammate <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> in Q3 (the third part of qualifying) with <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>’s <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> only 0.015 seconds behind Bottas in third. Hamilton dedicated his pole position to <a href="/wiki/Chadwick_Boseman" title="Chadwick Boseman">Chadwick Boseman</a>, who had died in the days preceding the race. <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> finished fourth for <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a> with <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Albon" title="Alexander Albon">Alexander Albon</a> fifth and <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Ocon" title="Esteban Ocon">Esteban Ocon</a> sixth. The rest of the top 10 were <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz Jr.</a> for <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Racing_Point_F1_Team" title="Racing Point F1 Team">Racing Point</a> duo of <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a> followed by <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">Lando Norris</a> in the other McLaren. The <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_AlphaTauri" title="Scuderia AlphaTauri">AlphaTauri</a>’s of <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a> and <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a> were 11th and 12th, followed by the Ferrari’s of <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>. This was the first time since the <a href="/wiki/2014_British_Grand_Prix" title="2014 British Grand Prix">2014 British Grand Prix</a> that Ferrari took no part in Q3. <a href="/wiki/George_Russell_(racing_driver)" title="George Russell (racing driver)">George Russell</a> impressed in 15th, getting through to Q2 (the second part of qualifying) for the fifth time this season for <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a>. Out in Q1 (the first part of qualifying) were both <a href="/wiki/Alfa_Romeo_in_Formula_One" title="Alfa Romeo in Formula One">Alfa Romeos</a>, both <a href="/wiki/Haas_F1_Team" title="Haas F1 Team">Haas</a> drivers and the Williams of <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_Latifi" title="Nicholas Latifi">Nicholas Latifi</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> 
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p><a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>’s <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz Jr.</a> suffered a power unit issue which also damaged his exhaust, meaning he was unable to start the race.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> On lap 10, <a href="/wiki/Alfa_Romeo_in_Formula_One" title="Alfa Romeo in Formula One">Alfa Romeo's</a> <a href="/wiki/Antonio_Giovinazzi" title="Antonio Giovinazzi">Antonio Giovinazzi</a> lost control of his car coming out of turn 14 and crashed into the wall. One of his wheels detached from the car which subsequently hit the front right suspension of <a href="/wiki/George_Russell_(racing_driver)" title="George Russell (racing driver)">George Russell</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> forcing both drivers to retire from the race.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>This caused significant debris resulting in a safety car. All drivers other than two, <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a>, pitted under safety car conditions.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-20">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> At the end of the safety car period the top 6 were <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a>, <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a>, Gasly, Pérez, <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a>.
</p><p>On older tyres and out of position due to not pitting under the safety car Pérez and Gasly both began to fall down the field. None-the-less by the time Ricciardo had passed Gasly and Pérez a few laps later, he was over 10 seconds behind third placed Max Verstappen and despite a much praised race Riccardo would be unable to challenge for third.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_20-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-20">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sergio Pérez pitted at the end of lap 18 for new hard tyres dropping him to 17th, the last of the runners still on track, before starting to gradually make his way up the field using his newer tyres.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_20-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-20">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> On lap 26 Pierre Gasly pitted from 10th position and put on a set of medium tyres that were to see him to the end of the race.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_20-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-20">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> He came out in 16th position, behind every driver other than <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a> who had been forced to make a second pit stop due to a pneumatics issue.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_20-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-20">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Similarly to Pérez before him Gasly used his newer tyres to start to make his way though the field, by lap 36 he was in 10th position behind Pérez in 9th. Gasly passed Pérez on lap 39 before closing the gap to <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a> whom he passed on lap 42 to take 8th position where he finished.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_20-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-20">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Hamilton won the race, having led for every lap, followed by Bottas and Verstappen who had also held second and third respectively for the entire race.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_20-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-20">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Ricciardo completed the fastest lap, a 1:47.483, on the final lap of the race for an extra championship point and closing the gap to Max Verstappen in third to just over three seconds. He finished in fourth, a position he had held the entire race other than briefly during and immediately after the safety car due to Gasly and Pérez not pitting. Ocon was able to overtake Albon on the last lap. The race was also notable for <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> finishing ahead of both Ferraris, including his former teammate Vettel. 
</p>
