<p>The <b>1961 Belgian Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 18 June 1961 at <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps" title="Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps">Spa-Francorchamps</a>. It was race 3 of 8 in both the <a href="/wiki/1961_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1961 World Championship of Drivers">1961 World Championship of Drivers</a> and the <a href="/wiki/1961_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1961 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1961 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>.
</p><p>The organizers of the race invited 25 entries, but were only going to pay starting money to 19: sixteen pre-selected cars plus the 3 fastest of the remaining 9. Three of the cars without starting money decided not to race after practicing. A fourth entry was a single car for <a href="/wiki/Cliff_Allison" title="Cliff Allison">Cliff Allison</a> and <a href="/wiki/Henry_Taylor_(racing_driver)" title="Henry Taylor (racing driver)">Henry Taylor</a>. <a href="/wiki/British_Racing_Partnership" title="British Racing Partnership">UDT Laystall</a> decided to let the fastest driver in practice compete, but Allison wrecked the car on his first practice lap. The <a href="/wiki/Emeryson" title="Emeryson">Emeryson</a> cars were also discarded by Equipe Nationale Belge after discovering terminal chassis damage on one of them, although <a href="/wiki/Willy_Mairesse" title="Willy Mairesse">Willy Mairesse</a> gained the use of a <a href="/wiki/Team_Lotus" title="Team Lotus">Team Lotus</a> spare machine to post a better time on Saturday. The team eventually reached a deal for Mairesse and <a href="/wiki/Lucien_Bianchi" title="Lucien Bianchi">Lucien Bianchi</a> to compete in the race with the older, yet proven, Lotus 18s of non-starters Marsh and Seidel. 
</p><p>The race was completely dominated by the Ferrari team, with the four works drivers finishing 1-2-3-4. This was the last time any constructor achieved this score in a F1 race.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>  While <a href="/wiki/Graham_Hill" title="Graham Hill">Graham Hill</a> took the lead at the start from sixth on the grid, he could not hold off the Italian cars and all had passed him by the end of the first lap. Hill fought with <a href="/wiki/John_Surtees" title="John Surtees">John Surtees</a> for fifth place until he retired with an oil leak on the 24th lap.
</p>
