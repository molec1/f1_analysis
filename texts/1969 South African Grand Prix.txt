<p>The <b>1969 South African Grand Prix</b>, formally the <b>Third AA Grand Prix of South Africa</b> (<a href="/wiki/Afrikaans" title="Afrikaans">Afrikaans</a>: <i>Derde AA Suid-Afrikaanse Grand Prix</i><sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>), was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Motor_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Motor race">motor race</a> held at <a href="/wiki/Kyalami" title="Kyalami">Kyalami Circuit</a> on 1 March 1969. It was race 1 of 11 in both the <a href="/wiki/1969_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1969 World Championship of Drivers">1969 World Championship of Drivers</a> and the <a href="/wiki/1969_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1969 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1969 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>. The 80-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Equipe_Matra_Sports" title="Equipe Matra Sports">Matra</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Jackie_Stewart" title="Jackie Stewart">Jackie Stewart</a> after he started from fourth position. <a href="/wiki/Graham_Hill" title="Graham Hill">Graham Hill</a> finished second for the <a href="/wiki/Team_Lotus" title="Team Lotus">Lotus</a> team and <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Denny_Hulme" title="Denny Hulme">Denny Hulme</a> came in third.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1969_South_African_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Matra_ms10_RSA_1969.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/26/Matra_ms10_RSA_1969.jpg/220px-Matra_ms10_RSA_1969.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="172" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/26/Matra_ms10_RSA_1969.jpg/330px-Matra_ms10_RSA_1969.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/26/Matra_ms10_RSA_1969.jpg/440px-Matra_ms10_RSA_1969.jpg 2x" data-file-width="500" data-file-height="392" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Matra_ms10_RSA_1969.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Jackie_Stewart" title="Jackie Stewart">Jackie Stewart</a> in his <a href="/wiki/Matra_MS10" title="Matra MS10">Matra MS10</a>, equipped with two wings</div></div></div>
<p>In the weeks leading up to the first Formula One race of the <a href="/wiki/1969_Formula_One_season" title="1969 Formula One season">1969</a> season, entries were being announced for the race, with all the teams, apart from <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> expected to nominate two drivers for the race. <a href="/wiki/Enzo_Ferrari" title="Enzo Ferrari">Enzo Ferrari</a>, the managing director of the team he founded, announced that Ferrari were only going to nominate one driver for the race. To most people's surprise, Ferrari at first nominated two drivers for the race without explanation. However, the decision was later changed, with only <a href="/wiki/Chris_Amon" title="Chris Amon">Chris Amon</a> nominated eventually for Ferrari. <a href="/wiki/Brabham" title="Brabham">Brabham</a>, <a href="/wiki/British_Racing_Motors" title="British Racing Motors">BRM</a>, <a href="/wiki/Team_Lotus" title="Team Lotus">Lotus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Matra_International" class="mw-redirect" title="Matra International">Matra</a> and <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> all chose to nominate two drivers for the race meeting.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
