<p>The <b>2007 Canadian Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Auto race">motor race</a> held on 10 June 2007 at <a href="/wiki/Circuit_Gilles_Villeneuve" title="Circuit Gilles Villeneuve">Circuit Gilles Villeneuve</a> in <a href="/wiki/Montreal" title="Montreal">Montreal</a>, Quebec, Canada.<sup id="cite_ref-race_page_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-race_page-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> It was the sixth race of the <a href="/wiki/2007_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2007 Formula One season">2007 Formula One season</a>. The race was won by <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>, starting from pole position, in his first season in the top formula. It was Hamilton's first win in Formula One. <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> finished second and <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Wurz" title="Alexander Wurz">Alexander Wurz</a> was third, making it the first Grand Prix of the 2007 season that drivers from teams other than <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> and <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a> achieved podium positions.
</p><p>The <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a> was deployed an unprecedented four times during the course of the race.<sup id="cite_ref-4safety_car_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4safety_car-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> One of these periods was due to <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a>'s crash, which resulted in him suffering a sprained ankle and concussion. During the race <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> and <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a> were disqualified for exiting the pit lane when the red light was on.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>A test session was held on May 17 and 18 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_Paul_Ricard" title="Circuit Paul Ricard">Paul Ricard circuit</a> in France, with the track configured to replicate the characteristics of the Circuit Gilles Villeneuve for the final two days after it had been in the style of the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Monaco" title="Circuit de Monaco">Monaco</a> for the first two. Despite the fact that the <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a> team had dominated on the Monaco set up of the circuit, <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> were better on the Canadian set up.<sup id="cite_ref-paul_ricard_testing_1_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-paul_ricard_testing_1-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-paul_ricard_testing_2_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-paul_ricard_testing_2-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> had the fastest time on the first day, with a time of 1:28.833. Rain after lunch caused the test to be halted, when the test resumed Räikkönen was forced to finish early when his car experienced electronic problems. <a href="/wiki/Scott_Speed" title="Scott Speed">Scott Speed</a> achieved the second fastest time, two tenths of a second behind Räikkönen, with Seven tenths separating the first eight drivers. The <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> team focused on testing their suspension. <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a> and <a href="/wiki/Christian_Klien" title="Christian Klien">Christian Klien</a> shared testing for the <a href="/wiki/Honda_Racing_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Honda Racing F1">Honda team</a> over the day, however Klien's engine failed on his first lap. <a href="/wiki/James_Rossiter" title="James Rossiter">James Rossiter</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Super_Aguri_F1" title="Super Aguri F1">Super Aguri</a> completed the most laps with 115.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Räikkönen was also the fastest on the second day with a time of 1:28.624, with <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a>  ending the day with the second fastest time. Fisichella also completed 111 laps, the second most of the day, behind James Rossiter's 131. <a href="/wiki/Pedro_de_la_Rosa" title="Pedro de la Rosa">Pedro de la Rosa</a> was third fastest despite completing fewer laps than other drivers due to his engine failing early in the day. The testing session was stopped twice, when <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil's</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button's</a> engines failed. Red Bull continued to perform high and low downforce work and <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW</a> performed low and medium downforce work.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Fisichella was optimistic about the race after these tests saying "he had more grip, the brakes were good and the front suspension gave him a better turn-in".<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During the two weeks leading up to the Grand Prix, Räikkönen and <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> released statements to the press saying they believed that everything would be different in Canada because of the very different conditions—as shown at Paul Ricard.<sup id="cite_ref-raikkonen_pre-race_9-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-raikkonen_pre-race-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> believed McLaren had momentum from their win in Monaco, and <a href="/wiki/Martin_Whitmarsh" title="Martin Whitmarsh">Martin Whitmarsh</a> said that they were realistic about dominating the race.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Practice started on the Friday with Alonso leading at the end of both the two practice sessions. In both sessions, he outpaced his teammate and the closest Ferrari driven by Felipe Massa. <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a> were pleased with 5th place in the first session and 7th in the second from <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> despite his report that the car was "very difficult to drive". But <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a>'s car suffered a fuel leak resulting in him being unable to post a timed lap in the morning session, Kubica also told the press after the fuel leak, "the car has been quite difficult to drive." Both <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> drivers finished in the top ten of the morning practice. <a href="/wiki/Honda_Racing_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Honda Racing F1">Honda</a> were slower than its customer team <a href="/wiki/Super_Aguri_F1" title="Super Aguri F1">Super Aguri</a> in the first practice, but <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Barrichello</a> was faster than <a href="/wiki/Takuma_Sato" title="Takuma Sato">Takuma Sato</a> in the second practice. <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyota's</a> suspension problems meant that their second practice was cut short, but not before <a href="/wiki/Ralf_Schumacher" title="Ralf Schumacher">Ralf Schumacher</a> narrowly missed a <a href="/wiki/Groundhog" title="Groundhog">groundhog</a> that ran across the track. After experiencing problems with <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a>'s suspension, Toyota stopped both drivers for 45 minutes as a precautionary measure. By the time they got the cars back on the track, they only managed a few extra laps until the problem recurred. <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> ended his practice day by going into the wall at turn 7, damaging the suspension. His teammate, <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Fisichella</a>, performed better, staying in the top 8 in both sessions. At the end of the afternoon practice, <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> slowed down unexpectedly before turn 8 causing <a href="/wiki/Scott_Speed" title="Scott Speed">Scott Speed</a> to drive into the back of the Red Bull. Webber had overtaken Speed just before this incident.<sup id="cite_ref-friday_practice_1_11-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-friday_practice_1-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-friday_practice_2_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-friday_practice_2-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-practice_quotes_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-practice_quotes-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-webber_speed_clash_15-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-webber_speed_clash-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The Saturday morning practice session was 17&#160;minutes shorter than the scheduled 60&#160;minutes, due to Kovalainen's <a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a> having a suspected engine failure. The failure caused oil to be split on the track, this failure persisted during the race weekend.<sup id="cite_ref-saturday_prac_description_16-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-saturday_prac_description-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> This resulted in a <a href="/wiki/Racing_flags#Red_flag" title="Racing flags">red flag</a> period, from 10:33&#160;am until 10:50&#160;am local time. Hamilton led the practice with the fastest lap; with Räikkönen, Alonso and Massa posting the second, third and fourth fastest laps respectively. Sato finished the session with the fifth fastest lap in the Super Aguri car, outpacing the Honda works team for the second time during the race weekend. Toyota continued a disappointing race weekend, with the 14th and 16th fastest laps.<sup id="cite_ref-saturday_prac_results_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-saturday_prac_results-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Their suspension problems from the day before had not been resolved, and the two drivers were given instructions to avoid the kerbs at turn 8 to minimise further damage to the suspension.<sup id="cite_ref-toyota_team_instructions_18-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-toyota_team_instructions-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> Red Bull came back with both of the two repaired cars entering the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-saturday_prac_results_17-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-saturday_prac_results-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Kubica_Canada_2007.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6a/Kubica_Canada_2007.jpg/220px-Kubica_Canada_2007.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="165" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6a/Kubica_Canada_2007.jpg/330px-Kubica_Canada_2007.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/6a/Kubica_Canada_2007.jpg/440px-Kubica_Canada_2007.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2348" data-file-height="1758" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Kubica_Canada_2007.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> leaves the pits during qualifying</div></div></div>
<p>In qualifying Hamilton took his first <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> as part of a McLaren one-two. Heidfeld improved on his practice performance to take third position, in front of the Ferraris of Räikkönen and Massa.<sup id="cite_ref-hamilton_qualifies_19-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hamilton_qualifies-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Massa shared the third row with Mark Webber, who did not seem to be suffering the same braking problem that almost put teammate <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> out in the first session of qualifying, he had only one attempt at a lap in the second session as a result.<sup id="cite_ref-coulthard_brakes_20-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-coulthard_brakes-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a>, Robert Kubica, Giancarlo Fisichella and Jarno Trulli completed the top ten.<sup id="cite_ref-qualifying_results_21-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualifying_results-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Consistent with previous form, the Hondas of Barrichello and Button failed to make it into the top ten; they qualified 13th and 15th respectively. <a href="/wiki/Takuma_Sato" title="Takuma Sato">Takuma Sato</a>, once again out-qualified the two Honda cars, just missing out on the top ten, in front of <a href="/wiki/Vitantonio_Liuzzi" title="Vitantonio Liuzzi">Vitantonio Liuzzi</a>. He made it through to the second session of qualifying by 94 hundredths of a second, where he qualified in 14th. <a href="/wiki/Scott_Speed" title="Scott Speed">Scott Speed</a> was the slowest car in the second session of qualifying putting him in 16th position.<sup id="cite_ref-qualifying_results_21-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualifying_results-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Anthony_Davidson" title="Anthony Davidson">Anthony Davidson</a> missed out on the second session of qualifying, putting his Super Aguri in 17th position, 3 places behind his teammate Sato. <a href="/wiki/Ralf_Schumacher" title="Ralf Schumacher">Ralf Schumacher</a> and his team were disappointed with qualifying in 18th. <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> crashed into the wall at turn 5, damaging his rear suspension and losing his rear wing, resulting in the session being stopped temporarily.<sup id="cite_ref-hamilton_qualifies_19-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-hamilton_qualifies-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> Once the debris had been cleared, Kovalainen in his repaired car managed one impressive lap that put him in 13th, after which six drivers outqualified him. Kovalainen then had to make an engine change due to an engine failure and was demoted to the back of the grid.<sup id="cite_ref-kovalainen_engine_22-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-kovalainen_engine-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Wurz" title="Alexander Wurz">Wurz</a>, <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Sutil</a> and <a href="/wiki/Christijan_Albers" title="Christijan Albers">Albers</a> qualified in 19th, 20th and 21st respectively after being pushed up a place by Kovalainen's engine change.<sup id="cite_ref-qualifying_results_21-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualifying_results-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Hamilton_%2B_Alonso_2007_Canada.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/80/Hamilton_%2B_Alonso_2007_Canada.jpg/220px-Hamilton_%2B_Alonso_2007_Canada.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="146" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/80/Hamilton_%2B_Alonso_2007_Canada.jpg/330px-Hamilton_%2B_Alonso_2007_Canada.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/8/80/Hamilton_%2B_Alonso_2007_Canada.jpg/440px-Hamilton_%2B_Alonso_2007_Canada.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3504" data-file-height="2332" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Hamilton_%2B_Alonso_2007_Canada.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> leads at the start of the race, whilst <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> runs wide at the first corner.</div></div></div>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:205px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Kubica_crash.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/e/ed/Kubica_crash.jpg" decoding="async" width="203" height="152" class="thumbimage" data-file-width="203" data-file-height="152" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Kubica_crash.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a>'s violent crash.</div></div></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> led from the start, preventing <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> from overtaking at turn one. Alonso ran wide allowing <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> to overtake him and move into second place. Alonso damaged his car in the process and subsequently ran off the track at turn one a further three times during the race.<sup id="cite_ref-alonso_weekend_23-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-alonso_weekend-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-alonso_mistakes_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-alonso_mistakes-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> had a <a href="/wiki/Gearbox" class="mw-redirect" title="Gearbox">gearbox</a> problem at the start of the race and was unable to select first gear causing him stall on the grid.<sup id="cite_ref-race_driver_quotes_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-race_driver_quotes-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Christijan_Albers" title="Christijan Albers">Christijan Albers</a> started from pit lane. The first accident of the race involved <a href="/wiki/Scott_Speed" title="Scott Speed">Scott Speed</a> who clipped the rear wing of Wurz' <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a> and went into the hairpin with a broken <a href="/wiki/Suspension_(vehicle)" class="mw-redirect" title="Suspension (vehicle)">suspension</a>.
</p><p>Alonso ran across the grass at turn one again on lap 15, which resulted in him falling further behind Hamilton. Three laps later he made a similar mistake at the same turn, resulting in him sliding across the grass for the third time in the race. This caused him to drop back to fourth as it allowed Massa to go round the inside of Alonso into third.<sup id="cite_ref-alonso_weekend_23-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-alonso_weekend-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
Massa moved into second when Heidfeld pitted on lap 20. Hamilton pitted on lap 22, rejoining the race in third place.<sup id="cite_ref-Commentary_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Commentary-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>On lap 22 <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> crashed at turn four and the <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a> had to be deployed; Hamilton had pitted just before the incident.<sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> Thus the pit lane was closed but <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> were forced to pit in order to avoid running out of fuel, and were given ten-second stop-and-go penalties. When the pit lane was re-opened and the rest of the field pitted, Massa and <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a> left the pit lane while the red light was still on. Both were later disqualified from the race on lap 51.<sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:252px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Circuit_Monreal_with_two_location_circled.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/c/c5/Circuit_Monreal_with_two_location_circled.jpg/250px-Circuit_Monreal_with_two_location_circled.jpg" decoding="async" width="250" height="228" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/thumb/c/c5/Circuit_Monreal_with_two_location_circled.jpg/375px-Circuit_Monreal_with_two_location_circled.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/c/c5/Circuit_Monreal_with_two_location_circled.jpg 2x" data-file-width="445" data-file-height="405" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Circuit_Monreal_with_two_location_circled.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Circuit Gilles Villeneuve showing the location of Sutil's (red) and Kubica's (blue) crashes.</div></div></div>
<p>The race was restarted on lap 26, and on the approach to the hairpin later in the lap <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> hit the back of <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Toyota_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Toyota F1">Toyota</a>. The impact forced Kubica onto the grass at the edge of the track, where a bump launched his car into and along the concrete wall just before the hairpin. After losing three wheels, the nose and suffering major damage to the sidepods in the initial impact, the <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW</a> flipped back across the track and came to rest against the barrier in the run-off area on the other side of the track. Although initial reports said that he had broken his leg, Kubica only suffered a sprained ankle and <a href="/wiki/Concussion" title="Concussion">concussion</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-Unscathed_Kubica_out_of_hospital_29-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Unscathed_Kubica_out_of_hospital-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup> The safety car was deployed again after this crash.<sup id="cite_ref-Kubica_escapes_injury_after_crash_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Kubica_escapes_injury_after_crash-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup> The safety car was also deployed on lap 50, due to debris from a crash involving <a href="/wiki/Christijan_Albers" title="Christijan Albers">Christijan Albers</a> on the previous lap, and on lap 55 due to <a href="/wiki/Vitantonio_Liuzzi" title="Vitantonio Liuzzi">Vitantonio Liuzzi</a> crashing into the wall of champions once again.
On Lap 58, Jarno Trulli was trying to pass Wurz but later his front track rod clipped away causing him to crash.
Cement was placed at the hairpin around Kubica's accident. Räikkönen ran wide after he misjudged the braking on the dust. The Super Aguri of <a href="/wiki/Takuma_Sato" title="Takuma Sato">Takuma Sato</a> went past him as a result and moved into 10th place.
On lap 37, Trulli and <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> were battling for position going into turn 1. Both span off the track in almost synchronised fashion without touching each other. This occurrence amused reporters due to it resembling a form of dance. Both drivers were able to keep going. Also on this lap <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Davidson" title="Anthony Davidson">Anthony Davidson</a> made an unscheduled pit stop. He claimed after the race that a <a href="/wiki/Beaver" title="Beaver">beaver</a> had damaged his front wing although Canadian reports said it more likely to have been a <a href="/wiki/Groundhog" title="Groundhog">groundhog</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-groundhog_31-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-groundhog-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> One lap later <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> was forced to retire due to a gearbox problem.<sup id="cite_ref-32" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Ralf_Schumacher" title="Ralf Schumacher">Ralf Schumacher</a> also stopped on this lap and this allowed Massa, Fisichella and Sato to move ahead of both of them. Räikkönen had his second pit stop on lap 52 at the same time as Alonso. The latter was able to pass him just after they had exited the pits side-by-side. This moved him into 5th.<sup id="cite_ref-grand_33-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-grand-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Alonso_%2B_Sato_2007_Canada.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9d/Alonso_%2B_Sato_2007_Canada.jpg/220px-Alonso_%2B_Sato_2007_Canada.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9d/Alonso_%2B_Sato_2007_Canada.jpg/330px-Alonso_%2B_Sato_2007_Canada.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/9d/Alonso_%2B_Sato_2007_Canada.jpg/440px-Alonso_%2B_Sato_2007_Canada.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3016" data-file-height="2011" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Alonso_%2B_Sato_2007_Canada.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>In the closing stages, <a href="/wiki/Takuma_Sato" title="Takuma Sato">Takuma Sato</a> passed Alonso to take sixth position: the best result in the <a href="/wiki/Super_Aguri_F1" title="Super Aguri F1">Super Aguri</a> team's history.</div></div></div>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_2007_Canada.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/69/Lewis_Hamilton_2007_Canada.jpg/220px-Lewis_Hamilton_2007_Canada.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="165" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/69/Lewis_Hamilton_2007_Canada.jpg/330px-Lewis_Hamilton_2007_Canada.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/69/Lewis_Hamilton_2007_Canada.jpg/440px-Lewis_Hamilton_2007_Canada.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1620" data-file-height="1217" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_2007_Canada.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Hamilton led from pole position to take the first victory of his F1 career.</div></div></div>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Podium_2007_Canada.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b8/Podium_2007_Canada.jpg/220px-Podium_2007_Canada.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="170" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b8/Podium_2007_Canada.jpg/330px-Podium_2007_Canada.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/b/b8/Podium_2007_Canada.jpg/440px-Podium_2007_Canada.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1162" data-file-height="897" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Podium_2007_Canada.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>The podium celebrations.</div></div></div>
<p>Including the two disqualifications, ten drivers failed to finish the race, six of them because of accidents including Kubica. Four of them gearbox failures. Several cars were damaged during the race: Wurz's rear wing was damaged in the incident that took out Speed, while Davidson hit a groundhog on the racetrack. This meant that the safety car had to be deployed on four occasions. At the final restart on lap sixty, all of the remaining twelve cars were on the same lap creating some close racing and a lot of overtaking. Hamilton sustained his consistent pace throughout all of the incidents and pulled clear of Heidfeld in the closing stages, taking his first race win relatively comfortably.<sup id="cite_ref-f1.com_race_report_34-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-f1.com_race_report-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Barrichello dropped ten places on his final pit stop. Hamilton's championship lead was strengthened when Sato overtook the reigning world champion to move into sixth place around the outside of the final chicane, having switched to the harder tyres on his final stop.<sup id="cite_ref-alonso_weekend_23-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-alonso_weekend-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-grand_33-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-grand-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Austria" title="Austria">Austria</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Wurz" title="Alexander Wurz">Alexander Wurz</a> drove through the field in his <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a> from 19th at the start to finish third by taking one pitstop, completing 40 laps on super soft tires. By finishing third, Wurz recorded his third career podium in the race that marked ten years since his debut race in F1. <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> finished in second place after trailing Hamilton for the majority of the race. The safety car deployments also allowed <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> to move up to fourth after starting at the back of the grid. The race was the first time in the 2007 season where a driver from a team other than <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> or <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a> made the podium.<sup id="cite_ref-f1.com_race_report_34-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-f1.com_race_report-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Sato overtook the <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a>-<a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_HighPerformanceEngines" class="mw-redirect" title="Mercedes-Benz HighPerformanceEngines">Mercedes</a> of world champion <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> on lap 67, just after overtaking <a href="/wiki/Ralf_Schumacher" title="Ralf Schumacher">Ralf Schumacher</a> and having overtaken Ferrari's <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> earlier in the race. He finished sixth after having a race that had seen him move from the middle of the grid; to the back of the pack and to a high of fifth before a pit-stop error caused him to move back to eleventh; were moved up 5 places in the last 15 laps. Sato was voted "Driver of the Day" on the <a href="/wiki/ITV_(TV_network)" title="ITV (TV network)">ITV</a> website over Hamilton's first win.
This was the last time that <a href="/wiki/Super_Aguri" class="mw-redirect" title="Super Aguri">Super Aguri</a> scored <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> points, and its biggest single points haul. The team's only other point came at the <a href="/wiki/2007_Spanish_Grand_Prix" title="2007 Spanish Grand Prix">2007 Spanish Grand Prix</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Reaction to the race was mainly focused on Hamilton and the fact that it was his first Grand Prix victory, making him the first black driver to ever win a Formula One race. It was also thought impressive that it had taken only six races for him to win and, in the process, take the lead in the drivers' championship at the age of 22. <a href="/wiki/Niki_Lauda" title="Niki Lauda">Niki Lauda</a> said he was "stunned" by the victory and <a href="/wiki/Damon_Hill" title="Damon Hill">Damon Hill</a> believed "People should not underestimate what he had achieved in a short space of time". Speculation began about the possibility of him winning the drivers championship.<sup id="cite_ref-35" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-36" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Alonso and the Ferrari team were disappointed with the outcome of the race. Alonso was now eight points behind Hamilton in the drivers championship, as a result of finishing 7th. Ferrari lost ground in both the constructors and driver championship, after gaining a total of just four points from the race. Alonso believed Hamilton to have been "very lucky" to have won the race and did not congratulate his teammate after the race. Hamilton's performance was felt to have been faultless and all the more impressive due to four separate deployments of the Safety Car.<sup id="cite_ref-37" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>As a result of his crash Kubica was unable to race at the next Grand Prix in the <a href="/wiki/2007_United_States_Grand_Prix" title="2007 United States Grand Prix">United States</a>. The decision to not allow him to race was made by the sport's governing body the <a href="/wiki/FIA" class="mw-redirect" title="FIA">FIA</a>, as they believed it was a too much of a risk for him to race in case of another crash so soon after this one. This was despite the fact Kubica had said in a statement that he was "ready to race". He was replaced by <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> for the next race.<sup id="cite_ref-38" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
