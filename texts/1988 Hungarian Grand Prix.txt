<p>The <b>1988 Hungarian Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 7 August 1988 at the <a href="/wiki/Hungaroring" title="Hungaroring">Hungaroring</a>, <a href="/wiki/Budapest" title="Budapest">Budapest</a>. It was the tenth race of the <a href="/wiki/1988_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="1988 Formula One World Championship">1988 Formula One World Championship</a>.
</p><p>The 76-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Ayrton_Senna" title="Ayrton Senna">Ayrton Senna</a>, driving a <a href="/wiki/McLaren_MP4/4" title="McLaren MP4/4">McLaren</a>-<a href="/wiki/Honda_in_Formula_One" title="Honda in Formula One">Honda</a>, with teammate <a href="/wiki/Alain_Prost" title="Alain Prost">Alain Prost</a> second and <a href="/wiki/Thierry_Boutsen" title="Thierry Boutsen">Thierry Boutsen</a> third in a <a href="/wiki/Benetton_B188" title="Benetton B188">Benetton</a>-<a href="/wiki/Ford_Motor_Company" title="Ford Motor Company">Ford</a>. The win, Senna's sixth of the season, moved him level on points with Prost at the top of the Drivers' Championship.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1988_Hungarian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1988_Hungarian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The tight and twisty Hungaroring offered a chance for the <a href="/wiki/Naturally_aspirated_engine" title="Naturally aspirated engine">naturally-aspirated</a> cars against the <a href="/wiki/Turbocharger" title="Turbocharger">turbos</a>, and they took seven of the top ten positions in qualifying. However, <a href="/wiki/Ayrton_Senna" title="Ayrton Senna">Ayrton Senna</a> still took pole position in his <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>-<a href="/wiki/Honda_in_Formula_One" title="Honda in Formula One">Honda</a>, his eighth of the season, pipping the <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> of <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Mansell" title="Nigel Mansell">Nigel Mansell</a> by 0.1 seconds. Mansell was driving against doctor's orders as he had developed <a href="/wiki/Chickenpox" title="Chickenpox">chickenpox</a>. On the second row of the grid were <a href="/wiki/Thierry_Boutsen" title="Thierry Boutsen">Thierry Boutsen</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Benetton_Formula" title="Benetton Formula">Benetton</a> and <a href="/wiki/Ivan_Capelli" title="Ivan Capelli">Ivan Capelli</a> in the <a href="/wiki/March_Engineering" title="March Engineering">March</a>, and on the third row were <a href="/wiki/Alessandro_Nannini" title="Alessandro Nannini">Alessandro Nannini</a> in the second Benetton and <a href="/wiki/Riccardo_Patrese" title="Riccardo Patrese">Riccardo Patrese</a> in the second Williams. Senna's teammate <a href="/wiki/Alain_Prost" title="Alain Prost">Alain Prost</a> could only manage seventh, while <a href="/wiki/Gerhard_Berger" title="Gerhard Berger">Gerhard Berger</a> was ninth in his <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, the two separated by the second March of <a href="/wiki/Maur%C3%ADcio_Gugelmin" title="Maurício Gugelmin">Maurício Gugelmin</a>. Completing the top ten was <a href="/wiki/Alex_Caffi" title="Alex Caffi">Alex Caffi</a> in the <a href="/wiki/Dallara" title="Dallara">Dallara</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1988_Hungarian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:K02-M5-0003.tif" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/7c/K02-M5-0003.tif/lossy-page1-220px-K02-M5-0003.tif.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="144" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/7c/K02-M5-0003.tif/lossy-page1-330px-K02-M5-0003.tif.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/7c/K02-M5-0003.tif/lossy-page1-440px-K02-M5-0003.tif.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2447" data-file-height="1601" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:K02-M5-0003.tif" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Senna leads in front of the Williams cars and Benetton cars, next to the pit entry</div></div></div>
<p>Mansell made a good start and almost took the lead but Senna held onto his position, and thanks to the superior power of his Honda engine he pulled away slightly down the main straight to head the field into turn 1. Patrese also made an excellent start climbing from 6th to 3rd while Prost was slow away and was only 9th at the end of the first lap. Capelli made a great start and in fact almost overtook Mansell into the first turn before his engine suddenly went sour and he pitted after just 2 laps to have a serious misfire investigated. On lap 12, Mansell lost downforce running close behind the McLaren and spun down to 4th place, leaving Senna to fend off the attacks of the other Williams, that of Patrese. The Italian later dropped back with engine problems, while similar problems were afflicting Nannini.
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:K02-M5-0012.tif" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d3/K02-M5-0012.tif/lossy-page1-220px-K02-M5-0012.tif.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d3/K02-M5-0012.tif/lossy-page1-330px-K02-M5-0012.tif.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/d/d3/K02-M5-0012.tif/lossy-page1-440px-K02-M5-0012.tif.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2442" data-file-height="1631" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:K02-M5-0012.tif" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Senna took the chequered flag less than a second before Prost</div></div></div>
<p>On lap 32, Prost had climbed to 3rd place behind Senna and Boutsen. On lap 37, Mansell pitted to replace tyres, which were ragged and causing vibrations to the car. Prost passed Boutsen on lap 47 and set out chasing down Senna. Two laps later, while Senna prepared to lap <a href="/wiki/Yannick_Dalmas" title="Yannick Dalmas">Yannick Dalmas</a>' <a href="/wiki/Lola_Cars" title="Lola Cars">Lola</a> and <a href="/wiki/Gabriele_Tarquini" title="Gabriele Tarquini">Gabriele Tarquini</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Enzo_Coloni_Racing_Car_Systems" class="mw-redirect" title="Enzo Coloni Racing Car Systems">Coloni</a>, Prost passed all three of them in a breathtaking move down the main straight. However he was too quick going into the first turn and ran wide enabling Senna to regain the lead. Prost set the fastest lap of the race but was forced to drop back with vibrations caused by a loose wheel bearing. On lap 58 Mansell retired still feeling the effects of chickenpox. He later admitted that racing in Hungary was a mistake as he was then forced to miss the next two races. Boutsen closed on Prost, but the Frenchman pressed on and pushed Senna. Just half a second covered the two McLarens as they crossed the line. Boutsen took the third spot on the podium, unable to challenge further due to a broken exhaust. Berger, Gugelmin and Patrese completed the points scorers.
</p>
