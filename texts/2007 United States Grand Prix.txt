<p>The <b>2007 United States Grand Prix</b> (formally the <b>XXXVI RBS United States Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> held at the <a href="/wiki/Indianapolis_Motor_Speedway" title="Indianapolis Motor Speedway">Indianapolis Motor Speedway</a> in <a href="/wiki/Speedway,_Indiana" title="Speedway, Indiana">Speedway</a>, <a href="/wiki/Indiana" title="Indiana">Indiana</a>, in the <a href="/wiki/United_States" title="United States">United States</a> on 17 June 2007.<sup id="cite_ref-race_page_1-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-race_page-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The 73-lap race was the seventh round of the <a href="/wiki/2007_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2007 Formula One season">2007 Formula One season</a> and was won by <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>-<a href="/wiki/Mercedes_AMG_High_Performance_Powertrains" title="Mercedes AMG High Performance Powertrains">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>. His teammate, <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>, finished the race in second position whilst <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver, <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>, completed the podium by finishing third. This was the debut race of the future world champion <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> with <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber_F1_Team" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber F1 Team">BMW Sauber F1 Team</a>.
</p><p>The race was Formula One's final visit to Indianapolis, after an eight-year period. The event was removed from the 2008 calendar a few months later and Formula One did not return to the United States until November 2012, at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_of_the_Americas" title="Circuit of the Americas">Circuit of the Americas</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a>'s large crash at the <a href="/wiki/2007_Canadian_Grand_Prix" title="2007 Canadian Grand Prix">Canadian Grand Prix</a> resulted in the season's first driver change. Although Kubica passed the medical examination and did not seem to show any ill effects, the <a href="/wiki/FIA" class="mw-redirect" title="FIA">FIA</a> medical delegate decided it would be best to rest him for the weekend, and not risk another <a href="/wiki/Concussion" title="Concussion">concussion</a> so close to the previous one. He was replaced in the <a href="/wiki/BMW_Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="BMW Sauber">BMW Sauber</a> team by <a href="/wiki/2007_Formula_Renault_3.5_Series_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2007 Formula Renault 3.5 Series season">Formula Renault 3.5 Series</a> championship leader, 19-year-old <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">German</a> <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>, who made his Formula One debut at the Indianapolis circuit.<sup id="cite_ref-Vettel_to_sub_for_Kubica_at_Indy_2-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Vettel_to_sub_for_Kubica_at_Indy-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Friday's morning practice was dominated by <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> and BMW Sauber, with <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> putting in the fastest time.<sup id="cite_ref-Friday_Practice_1_Results_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Friday_Practice_1_Results-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>  Sebastian Vettel started his first race weekend with a fourth fastest time. <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, who expected that their pace would return at Indianapolis,<sup id="cite_ref-Massa:_Ferrari_poised_to_strike_back_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Massa:_Ferrari_poised_to_strike_back-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> found themselves on either side of <a href="/wiki/WilliamsF1" class="mw-redirect" title="WilliamsF1">Williams</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> with the fifth and seventh fastest times.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> and his <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>-<a href="/wiki/Renault_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Renault F1">Renault</a> were the first to leave the pit lane in part one of the qualifying session. His partner, <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a>, was close behind followed by the <a href="/wiki/Spyker_F1" title="Spyker F1">Spyker</a> and Williams cars. Later in the session, with about six minutes left, Coulthard spun out in turn eight. Fernando Alonso ran first, <a href="/wiki/Nick_Heidfeld" title="Nick Heidfeld">Nick Heidfeld</a> second, and <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> closely behind in third. Although they struggled, both Ferraris made it, as well as Vettel. <a href="/wiki/Scott_Speed" title="Scott Speed">Scott Speed</a> failed to make it out of Q1 in his native country, along with team-mate <a href="/wiki/Vitantonio_Liuzzi" title="Vitantonio Liuzzi">Vitantonio Liuzzi</a>. <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a>, <a href="/wiki/Takuma_Sato" title="Takuma Sato">Takuma Sato</a>, <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Wurz" title="Alexander Wurz">Alexander Wurz</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Christijan_Albers" title="Christijan Albers">Christijan Albers</a> also missed the cut.
</p><p>McLaren and Ferrari began part two of their battle with Hamilton taking the top spot early. However, his team-mate, Fernando Alonso took top spot midway through. <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a>, <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>, and Heidfeld along with the two McLarens made the top five again. Webber made it to the next session, although his team-mate Coulthard was relegated along with <a href="/wiki/2002_United_States_Grand_Prix" title="2002 United States Grand Prix">2002 United States Grand Prix</a> winner <a href="/wiki/Rubens_Barrichello" title="Rubens Barrichello">Rubens Barrichello</a>. Barrichello's partner <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> was also relegated, along with Nico Rosberg, <a href="/wiki/Anthony_Davidson" title="Anthony Davidson">Anthony Davidson</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Ralf_Schumacher" title="Ralf Schumacher">Ralf Schumacher</a>.
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Hamilton_US_%2707.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/35/Hamilton_US_%2707.jpg/220px-Hamilton_US_%2707.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/35/Hamilton_US_%2707.jpg/330px-Hamilton_US_%2707.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/3/35/Hamilton_US_%2707.jpg/440px-Hamilton_US_%2707.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1024" data-file-height="683" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Hamilton_US_%2707.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> on his way to pole position</div></div></div>
<p>In part three Hamilton was first out and set the quickest time with a 1:13:089. Alonso was about four tenths behind, followed by Heidfeld and the Ferraris. <a href="/wiki/Giancarlo_Fisichella" title="Giancarlo Fisichella">Giancarlo Fisichella</a> and <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a>, both Renault drivers, also were near the top, but <a href="/wiki/Jarno_Trulli" title="Jarno Trulli">Jarno Trulli</a> and Webber knocked Fisichella down. With about 5 minutes left, Massa took first spot and Räikkönen took second. Hamilton was bumped to third, Alonso fourth, and Heidfeld remained fifth. About one minute later, Hamilton took the top spot, and Alonso second. Alonso had one lap left,  but could only get within about 2 tenths of a second of rookie teammate Lewis Hamilton. Massa and Räikkönen were dropped to third and fourth respectively. Heidfeld held on to fifth, with Kovalainen in sixth, bouncing back from his disappointing 22nd starting position in Canada. Vettel finished seventh, just two spots behind his temporary partner, Heidfeld. Trulli was 8th, with Red Bull-Renault's Mark Webber ninth and Fisichella tenth.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2007_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>

<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_2007_USA.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/92/Lewis_Hamilton_2007_USA.jpg/220px-Lewis_Hamilton_2007_USA.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="135" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/92/Lewis_Hamilton_2007_USA.jpg/330px-Lewis_Hamilton_2007_USA.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/92/Lewis_Hamilton_2007_USA.jpg/440px-Lewis_Hamilton_2007_USA.jpg 2x" data-file-width="793" data-file-height="488" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Lewis_Hamilton_2007_USA.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Hamilton on his way to victory in the <a href="/wiki/McLaren_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="McLaren (racing)">McLaren</a> <a href="/wiki/McLaren_MP4-22" title="McLaren MP4-22">MP4-22</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>Lewis Hamilton, on the pole, sped off the grid. Fernando Alonso stayed close behind, followed by the Ferraris. The drama in turn one was in the middle of the pack. Vettel ran wide and lost places, but escaped damage. Ralf Schumacher, who started in 12th, turned in late, and ended up crashing into David Coulthard, who he had started alongside. Jenson Button and Nico Rosberg, 13th and 14th respectively, escaped but Rubens Barrichello got caught up. Schumacher was out on the spot, while Coulthard and Barrichello limped back to the pits and retired.
</p><p>Also, Kimi Räikkönen was caught by Nick Heidfeld and Heikki Kovalainen. Up front, the top four were running strongly until the pit stops came around. The top four all went in early, soon followed by Heidfeld in the BMW Sauber, leaving sixth place qualifier Kovalainen in first. He led six or seven laps, and Hamilton then regained the lead.
</p><p>Despite a mid-race challenge from team-mate Fernando Alonso, in which the two McLaren drivers were racing side-by-side for the lead down the main straight, Hamilton maintained the lead to win his second-ever Formula One Grand Prix race, the other victory being in the previous event at <a href="/wiki/2007_Canadian_Grand_Prix" title="2007 Canadian Grand Prix">Montreal</a>. This marked  McLaren's first 1-2 finish at the circuit and their first win at Indianapolis since <a href="/wiki/Mika_H%C3%A4kkinen" title="Mika Häkkinen">Mika Häkkinen</a>'s final Grand Prix win in <a href="/wiki/2001_United_States_Grand_Prix" title="2001 United States Grand Prix">2001</a>.
</p><p>Takuma Sato spun off at turn 3 after managing to pass Adrian Sutil. Sato had been due a drive-through penalty for overtaking under yellow flags, but this was changed to a 10-place grid penalty for <a href="/wiki/2007_French_Grand_Prix" title="2007 French Grand Prix">the next race</a>.
</p><p>There were also three mechanical retirements&#160;– Nico Rosberg in the Williams suffered an engine failure with only five laps to go, while BMW Sauber's Nick Heidfeld and <a href="/wiki/Toro_Rosso" class="mw-redirect" title="Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>'s Vitantonio Liuzzi also retired from the race due to mechanical failure.
</p>
