<p>The <b>1982 Dutch Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Motor_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Motor race">motor race</a> held at <a href="/wiki/Circuit_Park_Zandvoort" class="mw-redirect" title="Circuit Park Zandvoort">Zandvoort</a> on 3 July 1982. The race, contested over 72 laps, was the ninth race of the <a href="/wiki/1982_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="1982 Formula One season">1982 Formula One season</a> and was won by <a href="/wiki/Didier_Pironi" title="Didier Pironi">Didier Pironi</a>, driving a <a href="/wiki/Ferrari_126C" title="Ferrari 126C">Ferrari</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Nelson_Piquet" title="Nelson Piquet">Nelson Piquet</a> second in a <a href="/wiki/Brabham_BT50" title="Brabham BT50">Brabham</a>-<a href="/wiki/BMW_in_Formula_One" title="BMW in Formula One">BMW</a> and <a href="/wiki/Keke_Rosberg" title="Keke Rosberg">Keke Rosberg</a> third in a <a href="/wiki/Williams_FW08" title="Williams FW08">Williams</a>-<a href="/wiki/Ford_Motor_Company" title="Ford Motor Company">Ford</a>.
</p>
<div class="thumb tleft"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Grand_Prix_op_Zandvoort_overzicht_tijdens_start,_Bestanddeelnr_253-8754.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a9/Grand_Prix_op_Zandvoort_overzicht_tijdens_start%2C_Bestanddeelnr_253-8754.jpg/220px-Grand_Prix_op_Zandvoort_overzicht_tijdens_start%2C_Bestanddeelnr_253-8754.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="146" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a9/Grand_Prix_op_Zandvoort_overzicht_tijdens_start%2C_Bestanddeelnr_253-8754.jpg/330px-Grand_Prix_op_Zandvoort_overzicht_tijdens_start%2C_Bestanddeelnr_253-8754.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/a/a9/Grand_Prix_op_Zandvoort_overzicht_tijdens_start%2C_Bestanddeelnr_253-8754.jpg/440px-Grand_Prix_op_Zandvoort_overzicht_tijdens_start%2C_Bestanddeelnr_253-8754.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3674" data-file-height="2434" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Grand_Prix_op_Zandvoort_overzicht_tijdens_start,_Bestanddeelnr_253-8754.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Start of the race</div></div></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Ren%C3%A9_Arnoux" title="René Arnoux">René Arnoux</a> started from pole position, but he crashed out at the notorious Tarzan Corner when his <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a>'s throttle stuck open and he hit the tyre barriers. Arnoux walked away from the crash unharmed.
</p><p>This was the first Grand Prix after the death of <a href="/wiki/Riccardo_Paletti" title="Riccardo Paletti">Riccardo Paletti</a> three weeks earlier in <a href="/wiki/Circuit_Gilles_Villeneuve" title="Circuit Gilles Villeneuve">Montreal</a>. This was also the final win of <a href="/wiki/Didier_Pironi" title="Didier Pironi">Didier Pironi</a>'s Formula One career.
</p><p>Ferrari entered <a href="/wiki/Patrick_Tambay" title="Patrick Tambay">Patrick Tambay</a> to replace <a href="/wiki/Gilles_Villeneuve" title="Gilles Villeneuve">Gilles Villeneuve</a>, who had been killed during qualifying a few race weekends prior, at the <a href="/wiki/1982_Belgian_Grand_Prix" title="1982 Belgian Grand Prix">Belgian Grand Prix</a>.
</p>
