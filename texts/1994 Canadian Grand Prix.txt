<p>The <b>1994 Canadian Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held on 12 June 1994 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_Gilles_Villeneuve" title="Circuit Gilles Villeneuve">Circuit Gilles Villeneuve</a> in <a href="/wiki/Montreal" title="Montreal">Montreal</a>, <a href="/wiki/Quebec" title="Quebec">Quebec</a>, <a href="/wiki/Canada" title="Canada">Canada</a>. It was the sixth race of the <a href="/wiki/1994_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="1994 Formula One World Championship">1994 Formula One World Championship</a> and the 32nd <a href="/wiki/Canadian_Grand_Prix" title="Canadian Grand Prix">Canadian Grand Prix</a>.
</p><p>The 69-lap race was won from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a> by <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a>, driving a <a href="/wiki/Benetton_B194" title="Benetton B194">Benetton</a>-<a href="/wiki/Ford_Motor_Company" title="Ford Motor Company">Ford</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Damon_Hill" title="Damon Hill">Damon Hill</a> second in a <a href="/wiki/Williams_FW16" title="Williams FW16">Williams</a>-<a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jean_Alesi" title="Jean Alesi">Jean Alesi</a> third in a <a href="/wiki/Ferrari_412_T1" title="Ferrari 412 T1">Ferrari</a>. The win, Schumacher's fifth of the season, put him 33 points clear of Hill in the Drivers' Championship.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1994_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1994_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>A temporary chicane was inserted between the hairpin and the Casino corner on the back straight, so as to decrease top speed and increase safety in the wake of Ayrton Senna's fatal crash at Imola.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Simtek" title="Simtek">Simtek</a> entered only one car for Canada, following the injuries to <a href="/wiki/Andrea_Montermini" title="Andrea Montermini">Andrea Montermini</a> at the previous race in <a href="/wiki/1994_Spanish_Grand_Prix" title="1994 Spanish Grand Prix">Spain</a>. <a href="/wiki/Andrea_de_Cesaris" title="Andrea de Cesaris">Andrea de Cesaris</a> replaced the injured <a href="/wiki/Karl_Wendlinger" title="Karl Wendlinger">Karl Wendlinger</a> at <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a>, and would thus make his 200th Grand Prix start at Montreal.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1994_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> took his fifth victory from six races in his <a href="/wiki/Benetton_Formula" title="Benetton Formula">Benetton</a>, finishing nearly 40 seconds ahead of <a href="/wiki/Damon_Hill" title="Damon Hill">Damon Hill</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a>. <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Jean_Alesi" title="Jean Alesi">Jean Alesi</a> finished third, just ahead of teammate <a href="/wiki/Gerhard_Berger" title="Gerhard Berger">Gerhard Berger</a>, the last driver on the lead lap. Hill's teammate <a href="/wiki/David_Coulthard" title="David Coulthard">David Coulthard</a> was fifth, scoring his first points in Formula One, while <a href="/wiki/Christian_Fittipaldi" title="Christian Fittipaldi">Christian Fittipaldi</a> crossed the line sixth but was disqualified when his <a href="/wiki/Footwork_Arrows" title="Footwork Arrows">Footwork</a> was found to be underweight, thus promoting Schumacher's teammate <a href="/wiki/JJ_Lehto" title="JJ Lehto">JJ Lehto</a> to the final point.
</p><p>During the race, <a href="/wiki/%C3%89rik_Comas" title="Érik Comas">Érik Comas</a> became the first-ever F1 driver to be penalised for speeding in the pit lane; he received a ten-second stop-go penalty.
</p>
