<p>The <b>1957 Italian Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Motor_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Motor race">motor race</a> held on 8 September 1957 at <a href="/wiki/Autodromo_Nazionale_di_Monza" title="Autodromo Nazionale di Monza">Monza</a>. It was the eighth and final race in the <a href="/wiki/1957_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1957 World Championship of Drivers">1957 World Championship of Drivers</a>.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notes">Notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1957_Italian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Notes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>Shared Drives:
<ul><li>Car #8: <a href="/wiki/Giorgio_Scarlatti" title="Giorgio Scarlatti">Giorgio Scarlatti</a> (50 laps) and <a href="/wiki/Harry_Schell" title="Harry Schell">Harry Schell</a> (34 laps). They shared the 2 points for fifth place.</li>
<li>Car #28: <a href="/wiki/Andr%C3%A9_Simon_(racing_driver)" title="André Simon (racing driver)">André Simon</a> (40 laps) and <a href="/wiki/Ottorino_Volonterio" title="Ottorino Volonterio">Ottorino Volonterio</a> (32 laps).</li></ul></li>
<li>This was the last time that the season finale was held in Italy.</li>
<li>Behra raced in a Maserati with a V12 engine after Fangio practiced with the V12 car and decided to use his usual straight-6 car after he was quicker in practice with the latter. All other Maseratis that ran in the race had the standard straight-6 engine.</li></ul>
