<p>The <b>1968 Dutch Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_Zandvoort" title="Circuit Zandvoort">Zandvoort Circuit</a> on 23 June 1968. It was race 5 of 12 in both the <a href="/wiki/1968_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1968 World Championship of Drivers">1968 World Championship of Drivers</a> and the <a href="/wiki/1968_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1968 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1968 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>. The 90-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/Equipe_Matra_Sports" title="Equipe Matra Sports">Matra</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Jackie_Stewart" title="Jackie Stewart">Jackie Stewart</a> after he started from fifth position. His teammate <a href="/wiki/Jean-Pierre_Beltoise" title="Jean-Pierre Beltoise">Jean-Pierre Beltoise</a> finished second and <a href="/wiki/British_Racing_Motors" title="British Racing Motors">BRM</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Pedro_Rodr%C3%ADguez_(racing_driver)" title="Pedro Rodríguez (racing driver)">Pedro Rodríguez</a> came in third.
</p>
