<p>The <b>2015 British Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>2015 Formula 1 British Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> that took place on 5 July 2015 at the <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Silverstone" title="Silverstone">Silverstone</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_Kingdom" title="United Kingdom">United Kingdom</a>. The race was the ninth round of the <a href="/wiki/2015_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2015 Formula One season">2015 season</a>, and marked the 70th running of the <a href="/wiki/British_Grand_Prix" title="British Grand Prix">British Grand Prix</a>, and the 51st time that the race was held at the Silverstone Circuit.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> was the defending race winner, and came into the event with a ten-point lead over teammate <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> in the World Drivers' Championship. In the Constructors' Championship, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> were leading <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> by 136 points, with <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> a further 63 points adrift.
</p><p>Hamilton took pole position during Saturday's qualifying, his eighth of the season, ahead of Rosberg and the two Williams of <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> and <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a>. The first lap of the race saw the Massa and Bottas take first and second place respectively through fast starts. They were able to hold their positions until the pit stops. Rain in the latter part of the race gave <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> the chance to overtake the two Williams for the last podium position. Hamilton won the race for the second consecutive year, 11 seconds ahead of his teammate, extending his championship lead to 17 points.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2015_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2015_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:P%C3%A9rez_Force_India_VJM08B_Britain_2015.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/72/P%C3%A9rez_Force_India_VJM08B_Britain_2015.jpg/220px-P%C3%A9rez_Force_India_VJM08B_Britain_2015.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/72/P%C3%A9rez_Force_India_VJM08B_Britain_2015.jpg/330px-P%C3%A9rez_Force_India_VJM08B_Britain_2015.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/7/72/P%C3%A9rez_Force_India_VJM08B_Britain_2015.jpg/440px-P%C3%A9rez_Force_India_VJM08B_Britain_2015.jpg 2x" data-file-width="3965" data-file-height="2645" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:P%C3%A9rez_Force_India_VJM08B_Britain_2015.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> driving the newly introduced <a href="/wiki/Force_India_VJM08" title="Force India VJM08">Force India VJM08B</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>Coming to <a href="/wiki/Silverstone_Circuit" title="Silverstone Circuit">Silverstone</a>, <a href="/wiki/Force_India" title="Force India">Force India</a> introduced a new version (<i>B-spec</i>) of their <a href="/wiki/Force_India_VJM08" title="Force India VJM08">VJM08</a>, featuring a shorter nose with "nostrils".<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> After a problem with a wheelnut had caused problems for <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> at the <a href="/wiki/2015_Austrian_Grand_Prix" title="2015 Austrian Grand Prix">previous race in Austria</a>, <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> chose to revise its wheelnut system for the British Grand Prix.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Manor Marussia</a> secured new sponsorship ahead of the race, thus changing its livery.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> The race marked the 900th Grand Prix contested by at least one car using a <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari#Engine_supply" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> engine.<sup id="cite_ref-spox_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-spox-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> nominated their two hardest dry-weather compounds for the event; the white-banded medium tyre as the option selection, while the orange-banded hard tyre would be the prime compound. The company also supplied their two wet-weather tyres, as they do for all events; the green-banded intermediate tyre and the blue-banded full wet tyre.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Silverstone was used in its revised 2011 Arena Grand Prix layout, which has been described as "one of the fastest circuits in the F1 calendar".<sup id="cite_ref-track_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-track-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> The "fast, flowing circuit"<sup id="cite_ref-track_10-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-track-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> featured 18 turns, with Stowe and Vale corners being the best overtaking opportunities.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> came into the weekend with a ten-point lead over teammate <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-fiaprev_12-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiaprev-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> after Rosberg took victory in Austria.<sup id="cite_ref-nbcprev_13-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-nbcprev-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> In the constructors' championship, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> led Ferrari by 136 points, while third placed <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> had moved closer to Ferrari in Austria.<sup id="cite_ref-fiaprev_12-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiaprev-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> The race stewards for the weekend were <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_l%27Automobile" title="Fédération Internationale de l&#39;Automobile">FIA</a> World Council member Lars Österlind, President of the <a href="/wiki/List_of_FIA_member_organisations" title="List of FIA member organisations">Barbados Motoring Federation</a> Andrew Mallalieu and <a href="/wiki/1992_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="1992 Formula One World Championship">1992</a> world champion <a href="/wiki/Nigel_Mansell" title="Nigel Mansell">Nigel Mansell</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-fiaprev_12-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-fiaprev-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Free_practice">Free practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2015_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Free practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Marussia_MR03B_Britain_15_Stevens.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/90/Marussia_MR03B_Britain_15_Stevens.jpg/220px-Marussia_MR03B_Britain_15_Stevens.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/90/Marussia_MR03B_Britain_15_Stevens.jpg/330px-Marussia_MR03B_Britain_15_Stevens.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/9/90/Marussia_MR03B_Britain_15_Stevens.jpg/440px-Marussia_MR03B_Britain_15_Stevens.jpg 2x" data-file-width="5184" data-file-height="3456" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Marussia_MR03B_Britain_15_Stevens.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Will_Stevens" title="Will Stevens">Will Stevens</a> driving the reliveried <a href="/wiki/Marussia_MR03" title="Marussia MR03">Marussia MR03B</a></div></div></div>
<p>Per the regulations for the <a href="/wiki/2015_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2015 Formula One World Championship">2015</a> season, three practice sessions were held, two one and a half-hour sessions on Friday and another one-hour session before qualifying on Saturday.<sup id="cite_ref-pracreg_14-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pracreg-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> In the first practice session on Friday morning, Nico Rosberg posted the fastest time, after recovering from early hydraulic problems that forced his team to exchange his gearbox.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> Rosberg was eventually 0.07 seconds quicker than teammate Lewis Hamilton. <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> was 1.2 seconds down on Rosberg in third, proving the <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a> chassis was competitive for the weekend.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> Taking part in first practice were test drivers <a href="/wiki/Jolyon_Palmer" title="Jolyon Palmer">Jolyon Palmer</a>, replacing <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> at <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a>, <a href="/wiki/Susie_Wolff" title="Susie Wolff">Susie Wolff</a> in <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a>' <a href="/wiki/Williams_FW37" title="Williams FW37">Williams FW37</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Raffaele_Marciello" title="Raffaele Marciello">Raffaele Marciello</a> for <a href="/wiki/Sauber" class="mw-redirect" title="Sauber">Sauber</a> instead of <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Ericsson" title="Marcus Ericsson">Marcus Ericsson</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The second session on Friday afternoon was red-flagged early on when Romain Grosjean got stuck in a <a href="/wiki/Run-off_area" title="Run-off area">gravel trap</a> at Luffield corner. Nico Rosberg was once again fastest ahead of the two Ferrari drivers and teammate Hamilton, who complained about the setup of his car. <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> and Sauber endured difficult sessions, finishing only ahead of the two Manor Marussia cars. It was Manor Marussia driver <a href="/wiki/Roberto_Merhi" title="Roberto Merhi">Roberto Merhi</a> who brought out a second red flag, when his car beached at the same spot that had caught out Grosjean earlier.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> After the Williams drivers only finished tenth and twelfth, <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> stated that his team was behind <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>In the third and final practice session, held on Saturday morning, World Champion Lewis Hamilton set the fastest time, half a second ahead of his teammate, who spent the better part of the session in the garage while his team investigated a possible gearbox oil leak. Behind the <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> cars were the two Ferraris and the two Toro Rossos, with only <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> coming within a second of Hamilton's time. Meanwhile, <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> was confined to only six laps, ending his session early.<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2015_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Massa_Britiain_2015.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/42/Massa_Britiain_2015.jpg/220px-Massa_Britiain_2015.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="93" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/42/Massa_Britiain_2015.jpg/330px-Massa_Britiain_2015.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/4/42/Massa_Britiain_2015.jpg/440px-Massa_Britiain_2015.jpg 2x" data-file-width="5184" data-file-height="2196" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Massa_Britiain_2015.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> qualified third and would go on to lead the first part of the race.</div></div></div>
<p>Qualifying consisted of three parts, 18, 15 and 12 minutes in length respectively, with five drivers eliminated from competing after each of the first two sessions.<sup id="cite_ref-pracreg_14-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-pracreg-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> Before qualifying began, race directors warned the drivers to respect track limits, especially at Copse corner.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup> Still, eleven drivers ultimately had times disallowed for leaving the track with all four wheels in the corner over the course of qualifying. <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> lost two of his lap times, including his best in Q1, and barely made it into Q2.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-qualautosport_23-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualautosport-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> This came at the expense of the two McLaren drivers, the two Manor Marussia cars and Sauber's <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Nasr" title="Felipe Nasr">Felipe Nasr</a>, who all failed to proceed into the second part. Meanwhile, Kimi Räikkönen was fastest during the session.<sup id="cite_ref-qualautosport_23-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualautosport-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The second part of qualifying (Q2) saw both Lotus drivers eliminated, as well as Max Verstappen who complained about lack of rear grip. <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> qualified eleventh while Grosjean in 12th had his best time disallowed for exceeding the track limits. However, his second best time proved sufficient to keep his position.<sup id="cite_ref-qualautosport_23-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualautosport-23">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Q3 saw the top-ten drivers compete for <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>, which eventually went to Lewis Hamilton, beating teammate Rosberg by 0.122 seconds. Both Mercedes drivers were unable to improve on their first times set in the session, with Hamilton aborting his run and returning to the pit lane before the session was over. After securing two podium finishes in the past two races, Williams confirmed their improved form and took the second row on the grid, Massa ahead of Bottas.<sup id="cite_ref-qualguard_24-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-qualguard-24">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup> It was the twentieth consecutive pole position for Mercedes, being four races shy of a record set by Williams in 
<a href="/wiki/1992_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="1992 Formula One World Championship">1992</a>–<a href="/wiki/1993_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="1993 Formula One World Championship">1993</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcqualireport_25-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcqualireport-25">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2015_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Lotus_accident_Britain_2015.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c9/Lotus_accident_Britain_2015.jpg/220px-Lotus_accident_Britain_2015.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="137" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c9/Lotus_accident_Britain_2015.jpg/330px-Lotus_accident_Britain_2015.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/c/c9/Lotus_accident_Britain_2015.jpg/440px-Lotus_accident_Britain_2015.jpg 2x" data-file-width="5183" data-file-height="3223" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Lotus_accident_Britain_2015.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>After colliding with each other on the opening lap, both <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus</a> drivers retired.</div></div></div>
<p>The sell-out race on Sunday was held in front of a crowd of 140,000.<sup id="cite_ref-crowd_3-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-crowd-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> On the way to the grid, Felipe Nasr experienced gearbox issues and had to retire. After the lights went out, both Mercedes cars did not get away well, allowing the two Williams cars of Massa and Bottas to move into first and third respectively, with Bottas almost getting the better of Lewis Hamilton. Further back, <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> was quick off the line as well, jumping from ninth to fifth at the expense of <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a> and the two Ferraris. At the back of the grid, <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> touched Romain Grosjean into Turn 3, causing Grosjean to turn into his teammate Pastor Maldonado. Fernando Alonso, who had to turn away to avoid the accident, collided with teammate <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a>, who along with both Lotus drivers had to retire. While Alonso made a pit stop for a new nose, the incident brought out the safety car, which led the field until the start of lap four. Hamilton immediately attacked Felipe Massa, only to run wide on Vale, allowing Bottas to take second place on the inside of Club. Meanwhile, Sergio Pérez overtook Sebastian Vettel for ninth, while Max Verstappen retired after a spin in the gravel.
</p><p>Over the next couple of laps, Williams exercised team orders, and told their drivers to avoid racing each other to pull away from the Mercedes cars. Bottas complained that he was faster and requested permission to overtake for the lead, which Williams granted. His pace did not improve, however, but the top four pulled away from the midfield, leading Nico Hülkenberg in fifth by seven seconds on lap 13. Both Ferrari drivers made a pit stop for new tyres early, on laps 14 and 15, apparently set for a two-stop strategy.<sup id="cite_ref-raceguard_26-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-raceguard-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> On lap 20, Lewis Hamilton opted for the undercut,<sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> coming in for a new set of tyres. Massa, then in the lead, and Rosberg, in fourth place came in a lap later. On exit, both fought on the pit lane wheel to wheel, but Massa's lead proved sufficient for provisional second. Bottas came in a lap later, but Hamilton's out-lap, the fastest of the race, saw the world champion take the lead, with Bottas in third; splitting Massa and Rosberg. Daniel Ricciardo retired with an electrical failure on lap 23.
</p><p>By the halfway point, the drivers were told that rain was expected before the end of the race. When <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz Jr.</a> lost speed and retired on track due to an electrical failure, the race saw a brief <a href="/wiki/Safety_car#Virtual_Safety_Car_(VSC)" title="Safety car">Virtual Safety Car</a> period on laps 33 and 34, after which the first drops of rain hit the circuit, initially hitting the area around the pit lane. By lap 38, drivers started to lose traction. Sebastian Vettel managed to overtake teammate Räikkönen on lap 39, with Räikkönen making a pit stop for intermediate tyres at the end of the lap, which proved to be a poor choice as the track was not wet enough yet. Meanwhile, Rosberg took third from Bottas on the same lap, and took second from Massa two laps later. Räikkönen lost additional places and dropped down to ninth on his intermediate tyres. Rosberg, on the slicks, pushed hard and significantly reduced Hamilton's lead, only 3.7 seconds behind by lap 43. In response Hamilton made a pit stop for intermediate tyres himself, which Vettel imitated. The strategy proved effective, as the rain came on just then, with Rosberg and both Williams cars dashing into the pit lane in the subsequent laps, but the stops proved too late as Massa and Bottas fell behind Vettel, with Hamilton managing to improve his lead to nine seconds over Rosberg.
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Alonso_Britain_2015.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/67/Alonso_Britain_2015.jpg/220px-Alonso_Britain_2015.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="81" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/67/Alonso_Britain_2015.jpg/330px-Alonso_Britain_2015.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/6/67/Alonso_Britain_2015.jpg/440px-Alonso_Britain_2015.jpg 2x" data-file-width="4438" data-file-height="1624" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Alonso_Britain_2015.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> scored his first point of the season and first since returning to <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>.</div></div></div>
<p>On lap 49, <a href="/wiki/Will_Stevens" title="Will Stevens">Will Stevens</a> went wide into the barriers, losing his front wing. He was able to continue, but the subsequent nose change demoted him to last place.<sup id="cite_ref-raceguard_26-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-raceguard-26">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton held on to his lead over the last couple of laps, winning at Silverstone for the third time (the last two being consecutive British Grand Prix wins and the first from pole by any driver, since 1994).<sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> In doing so, he also surpassed <a href="/wiki/Jackie_Stewart" title="Jackie Stewart">Jackie Stewart</a>'s 45-year-old record of laps led in eighteen consecutive Grands Prix. Alonso finished tenth to earn his first point since his return to McLaren, while the new B-spec Force India recorded a double-point finish with Hülkenberg and Pérez in seventh and ninth respectively.<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2015_British_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="quotebox pullquote floatright" style="width:30%;&#32;;"><style data-mw-deduplicate="TemplateStyles:r887775652">.mw-parser-output .quotebox{background-color:#F9F9F9;border:1px solid #aaa;box-sizing:border-box;padding:10px;font-size:88%;max-width:100%}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatleft{margin:0.5em 1.4em 0.8em 0}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatright{margin:0.5em 0 0.8em 1.4em}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.centered{margin:0.5em auto 0.8em auto}.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatleft p,.mw-parser-output .quotebox.floatright p{font-style:inherit}.mw-parser-output .quotebox-title{background-color:#F9F9F9;text-align:center;font-size:larger;font-weight:bold}.mw-parser-output .quotebox-quote.quoted:before{font-family:"Times New Roman",serif;font-weight:bold;font-size:large;color:gray;content:" “ ";vertical-align:-45%;line-height:0}.mw-parser-output .quotebox-quote.quoted:after{font-family:"Times New Roman",serif;font-weight:bold;font-size:large;color:gray;content:" ” ";line-height:0}.mw-parser-output .quotebox .left-aligned{text-align:left}.mw-parser-output .quotebox .right-aligned{text-align:right}.mw-parser-output .quotebox .center-aligned{text-align:center}.mw-parser-output .quotebox cite{display:block;font-style:normal}@media screen and (max-width:360px){.mw-parser-output .quotebox{min-width:100%;margin:0 0 0.8em!important;float:none!important}}</style>
<div class="quotebox-quote left-aligned" style="">"What a great race! A classic British Grand Prix which had a bit of everything. In particular, the changeable conditions kept us all on the edge of our seats, with the intensity of the rain being very difficult to read."</div>
<p><cite class="left-aligned" style=""><a href="/wiki/John_Booth_(motor_racing)" title="John Booth (motor racing)">John Booth</a>, Team Principal of <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Manor Marussia</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-quotes_30-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-quotes-30">&#91;30&#93;</a></sup></cite>
</p>
</div>
<p>Media reception of the race was good, with different sources calling it a "thriller"<sup id="cite_ref-crashnet_31-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-crashnet-31">&#91;31&#93;</a></sup> and "a transfusion of fresh blood for the much-criticised sport."<sup id="cite_ref-guardpostrace_32-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-guardpostrace-32">&#91;32&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton's performance in particular was deemed "brilliant."<sup id="cite_ref-bbcpostrace_33-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcpostrace-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> The world champion admitted that Mercedes losing their places at the start "made the race for the fans" and went on to say: "You can't imagine how happy I am. I was tearing up on the last lap."<sup id="cite_ref-bbcpostrace_33-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcpostrace-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Following the early lap fight for the lead with teammate Massa, Valtteri Bottas stated that he was sure he could have pulled a gap to the Mercedes behind. However, he insisted that "No one should let anyone by. That's not racing, but it would have been nice to have been able to race when I had the best opportunities."<sup id="cite_ref-34" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-34">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Red Bull's team principal <a href="/wiki/Christian_Horner" title="Christian Horner">Christian Horner</a> declared after the race that a spin on a damp track had allegedly cost Daniil Kvyat the chance to challenge for a podium position.<sup id="cite_ref-35" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-35">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> Following the race, the stewards decided not to take any action regarding the first-lap incident between the Lotus and McLaren drivers.<sup id="cite_ref-36" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-36">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:Hamilton_Britain_2015_victory.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/20/Hamilton_Britain_2015_victory.jpg/220px-Hamilton_Britain_2015_victory.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="95" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/20/Hamilton_Britain_2015_victory.jpg/330px-Hamilton_Britain_2015_victory.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/2/20/Hamilton_Britain_2015_victory.jpg/440px-Hamilton_Britain_2015_victory.jpg 2x" data-file-width="4302" data-file-height="1866" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:Hamilton_Britain_2015_victory.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> celebrating his third home Grand Prix win</div></div></div>
<p>The victory allowed Hamilton to extend his championship lead over Rosberg to 17 points.<sup id="cite_ref-bbcpostrace_33-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-bbcpostrace-33">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> He also broke the record by leading at least one lap in 18 consecutive races. By placing two drivers on the podium for the ninth time in a row, Mercedes equalled a record set by Ferrari over the course of the <a href="/wiki/1952_Formula_One_season" title="1952 Formula One season">1952</a>–<a href="/wiki/1953_Formula_One_season" title="1953 Formula One season">1953</a> seasons, albeit whilst often racing with more than two cars.<sup id="cite_ref-37" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-37">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>A day after the race, a small controversy surfaced in the media in relation to the Mercedes team allegedly breaking the rules by causing its mechanics to rush to the pit lane on lap 14, for a stop that never eventuated. Stirred by text messages from <a href="/wiki/Susie_Wolff" title="Susie Wolff">Susie Wolff</a> to her husband, Mercedes motorsport boss <a href="/wiki/Toto_Wolff" title="Toto Wolff">Toto Wolff</a>, the latter publicly conceded that the incident was "a bit of a game" that failed to cause Williams to call its cars in the pit lane early.<sup id="cite_ref-38" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-38">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup> While no sanctions followed, FIA Race Director <a href="/wiki/Charlie_Whiting" title="Charlie Whiting">Charlie Whiting</a> responded by deciding to warn all teams against this practice at the subsequent <a href="/wiki/2015_Hungarian_Grand_Prix" title="2015 Hungarian Grand Prix">Hungarian Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-39" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-39">&#91;39&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
