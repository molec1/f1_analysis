<p>The <b>2020 Bahrain Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 Gulf Air Bahrain Grand Prix 2020</b>) is a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_racing" title="Formula One racing">Formula One motor race</a> that is to take place on 29 November 2020 at the <a href="/wiki/Bahrain_International_Circuit" title="Bahrain International Circuit">Bahrain International Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Sakhir" title="Sakhir">Sakhir, Bahrain</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-formal_2-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-2">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The race is the fifteenth round of the <a href="/wiki/2020_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2020 Formula One World Championship">2020 Formula One World Championship</a> and marks the 16th time that the <a href="/wiki/Bahrain_Grand_Prix" title="Bahrain Grand Prix">Bahrain Grand Prix</a> has been run as a round of the Formula One World Championship.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Bahrain_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Impact_of_the_COVID-19_pandemic">Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Bahrain_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">See also: <a href="/wiki/2020_Formula_One_World_Championship#Impact_of_the_COVID-19_pandemic" title="2020 Formula One World Championship">2020 Formula One World Championship §&#160;Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic</a>, and <a href="/wiki/COVID-19_pandemic_in_Bahrain" title="COVID-19 pandemic in Bahrain">COVID-19 pandemic in Bahrain</a></div>
<p>The race was originally due to take place on 22 March 2020 as the second round of the championship, but the response to the <a href="/wiki/COVID-19_pandemic" title="COVID-19 pandemic">COVID-19 pandemic</a> led to event organisers initially announcing that no spectators would be permitted to attend the race.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> On 13 March the race was indefinitely postponed.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> It was the second time the race was postponed after the <a href="/wiki/2011_Bahrain_Grand_Prix" title="2011 Bahrain Grand Prix">2011 Bahrain Grand Prix</a> which was ultimately cancelled.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> In its place, an <a href="/wiki/Esports" title="Esports">esports</a> virtual Grand Prix was held on 22 March 2020 featuring race drivers, celebrities and esports racers with the race being won by <a href="/wiki/Guanyu_Zhou" title="Guanyu Zhou">Guanyu Zhou</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
