<p>The <b>1960 Dutch Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Motor_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Motor race">motor race</a> held at <a href="/wiki/Circuit_Park_Zandvoort" class="mw-redirect" title="Circuit Park Zandvoort">Zandvoort</a> on 6 June 1960. It was race 4 of 10 in the <a href="/wiki/1960_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1960 World Championship of Drivers">1960 World Championship of Drivers</a> and race 3 of 9 in the <a href="/wiki/1960_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1960 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1960 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>.
</p><p>Due to a crash by <a href="/wiki/Dan_Gurney" title="Dan Gurney">Dan Gurney</a>, a spectator, who was in a prohibited area, was killed during this event.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>This race marked the Formula One debut of future World Champion <a href="/wiki/Jim_Clark" title="Jim Clark">Jim Clark</a>.
</p>
