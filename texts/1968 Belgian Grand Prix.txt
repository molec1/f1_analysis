<p>The <b>1968 Belgian Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race held at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Spa-Francorchamps" title="Circuit de Spa-Francorchamps">Spa-Francorchamps Circuit</a> on 9 June 1968. It was race 4 of 12 in both the <a href="/wiki/1968_World_Championship_of_Drivers" class="mw-redirect" title="1968 World Championship of Drivers">1968 World Championship of Drivers</a> and the <a href="/wiki/1968_International_Cup_for_Formula_One_Manufacturers" class="mw-redirect" title="1968 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers">1968 International Cup for Formula One Manufacturers</a>. The 28-lap race was won by <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Bruce_McLaren" title="Bruce McLaren">Bruce McLaren</a> after he started from sixth position. <a href="/wiki/Pedro_Rodr%C3%ADguez_(racing_driver)" title="Pedro Rodríguez (racing driver)">Pedro Rodríguez</a> finished second for the <a href="/wiki/British_Racing_Motors" title="British Racing Motors">BRM</a> team and <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Jacky_Ickx" title="Jacky Ickx">Jacky Ickx</a> came in third.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Notes">Notes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=1968_Belgian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Notes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<ul><li>After the introduction of 'dive plane' wings on the nosecone on an F1 car by <a href="/wiki/Team_Lotus" title="Team Lotus">Lotus</a> at the previous race, the <a href="/wiki/1968_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="1968 Monaco Grand Prix">1968 Monaco Grand Prix</a>, Ferrari added a strut mounted negative incidence wing - to their lead driver <a href="/wiki/Chris_Amon" title="Chris Amon">Chris Amon</a>'s car<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> and he nabbed pole position, and was 4 seconds faster in qualifying than the next fastest car of <a href="/wiki/Jackie_Stewart" title="Jackie Stewart">Jackie Stewart</a>, though Amon claimed to have performed similar lap times without the wings.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> Amon's teammate <a href="/wiki/Jacky_Ickx" title="Jacky Ickx">Jacky Ickx</a> did not have wings on his car.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> The Brabham team also fitted a rear wing to <a href="/wiki/Jack_Brabham" title="Jack Brabham">Jack Brabham</a>'s car, paired with dive planes on the nose to counteract lift; he qualified 10th.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Wings were added to Ickx's car (and many other teams copied the idea for their cars) for the next race at Zandvoort. As 1968 season progressed many F1 teams utilized strut mounted wings attached directly to suspension elements - copying Chaparral sports car practice - to increase cornering speeds, reducing lap times. Ferrari never utilized strut mounted wings attached to suspension, continuing with strut mounted wings mounted directly to the chassis.</li>
<li>On the seventh lap Brian Redman went off the circuit when his suspension failed and he crashed into and over a concrete barrier and into a parked car. His Cooper caught fire but Redman escaped with a severely broken right arm and a few minor burns.</li>
<li><b>Records:</b>
<ul><li>Fastest Fastest Lap Speed - <a href="/wiki/John_Surtees" title="John Surtees">John Surtees</a> (241.140&#160;km/h)</li>
<li>Most Races Contested - <a href="/wiki/Jack_Brabham" title="Jack Brabham">Jack Brabham</a> (100)</li>
<li>Fastest Average Race Speed - <a href="/wiki/Bruce_McLaren" title="Bruce McLaren">Bruce McLaren</a> (236.797&#160;km/h)</li></ul></li>
<li><b>Milestones:</b>
<ul><li>100th Race Entry <a href="/wiki/Jack_Brabham" title="Jack Brabham">Jack Brabham</a> <i>(Driver)</i></li>
<li>10th Fastest Lap <a href="/wiki/John_Surtees" title="John Surtees">John Surtees</a> <i>(Driver)</i></li>
<li>1st Win - <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> <i>(Car Manufacturer)</i></li></ul></li>
<li><b>Last win:</b> <a href="/wiki/Bruce_McLaren" title="Bruce McLaren">Bruce McLaren</a></li></ul>
