<p>The <b>2013 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>2013 Formula 1 Etihad Airways Abu Dhabi Grand Prix</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> that was held at the <a href="/wiki/Yas_Marina_Circuit" title="Yas Marina Circuit">Yas Marina Circuit</a> during the <a href="/wiki/Solar_eclipse_of_November_3,_2013" title="Solar eclipse of November 3, 2013">solar eclipse on 3 November 2013</a>. The race was the seventeenth round of the 2013 season, and marked the 5th running of the Abu Dhabi Grand Prix.
</p><p>The race, contested over 55 laps, was won by <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>, driving a <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>. <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a> finished second, and by <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> driving a <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> was third. Vettel won the race 30.8s ahead of his Red Bull Team mate Mark Webber.
Vettel's victory equalled <a href="/wiki/Alberto_Ascari" title="Alberto Ascari">Alberto Ascari</a> and <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a>'s record in <a href="/wiki/1953_Formula_One_season" title="1953 Formula One season">1953</a> and <a href="/wiki/2004_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2004 Formula One season">2004</a>  record for most consecutive wins in a season. This was  Red Bull's 100th podium. <a href="/wiki/Adrian_Sutil" title="Adrian Sutil">Adrian Sutil</a> scored his last ever World Championship points at this race, <a href="/wiki/Paul_di_Resta" title="Paul di Resta">Paul di Resta</a> also scored his final points.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Partial_solar_eclipse">Partial solar eclipse</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Partial solar eclipse">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">Main article: <a href="/wiki/Solar_eclipse_of_November_3,_2013" title="Solar eclipse of November 3, 2013">Solar eclipse of November 3, 2013</a></div>
<p>During the race it was possible to observe a partial <a href="/wiki/Solar_eclipse" title="Solar eclipse">solar eclipse</a> from 17:22 local time to 17:39 local time when the <a href="/wiki/Sun" title="Sun">Sun</a> set, and this was shown briefly during the race live broadcast.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Tyres">Tyres</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Tyres">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Like the <a href="/wiki/2012_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix" title="2012 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix">2012 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix</a>, tyre supplier Pirelli brought its white-banded medium compound tyre as the harder "prime" tyre and the yellow-banded soft compound tyre as the softer "option" tyre.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Free_Practice">Free Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Free Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> were top in the first practice session.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> drivers were fastest in the second practice session with Vettel leading Webber by a tenth of a second.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>During third practice Mercedes and Red Bull were close at the top and the session ended with Vettel two tenths of a second ahead of Webber who in turn was less than a hundredth of a second faster than Hamilton’s Mercedes<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Webber qualified in pole position ahead of his Red Bull team mate Vettel who qualified in second. The Mercedes drivers of Hamilton and Rosberg qualified on the second row of the grid. <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a> was the highest placed driver in the World Championship standings who failed to qualify for the final round of qualifying.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Jules_Bianchi" title="Jules Bianchi">Jules Bianchi</a> was penalised before the start of qualifying five places on the grid for an unscheduled gearbox change.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-qualifying">Post-qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Post-qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p><a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> was excluded from qualifying and was relegated to twenty-second place on the grid having originally qualified fifth place on the grid after his car failed a floor deflection test.<sup id="cite_ref-Kimi_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Kimi-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The race began with Vettel in second on the grid overtaking Webber into the first corner and then leading until the chequered flag. Rosberg moved up to second position, while <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>, starting from the back of the grid, broke his front-right suspension after contact with <a href="/wiki/Giedo_van_der_Garde" title="Giedo van der Garde">Giedo van der Garde</a>, causing Räikkönen to retire from the race. German <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a>, during his first pit-stop was deemed to have been unsafely released and was given a drive through penalty.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
Grosjean, advanced from sixth on the grid to fourth after the start. During the first half of the race, <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> led his team-mate Alonso, but was overtaken by Alonso in the latter stages of the race. After Alonso's second pit-stop, he came out of the pitlane and moved around backmarker <a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a>, going off the racetrack with all four wheels in a move that consequently caused Massa to run wide into turn five. Vettel won the race by 30.8s over Webber.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post_race">Post race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2013_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Post race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>During the cool down lap Vettel and Webber performed <a href="/wiki/Doughnut_(driving)" title="Doughnut (driving)">doughnuts</a>. As Vettel had done one after the <a href="/wiki/2013_Indian_Grand_Prix" title="2013 Indian Grand Prix">previous race in India</a>, this time he incurred no penalty from the stewards.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Alonso was sent for precautionary medical checks after a kerb impact measured at <a href="/wiki/G-force" title="G-force">25g</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
