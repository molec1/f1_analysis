<p>The <b>2017 Italian Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 Gran Premio Heineken d'Italia 2017</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_racing" title="Formula One racing">Formula One motor race</a> held on 3 September 2017 at the <a href="/wiki/Autodromo_Nazionale_di_Monza" title="Autodromo Nazionale di Monza">Autodromo Nazionale di Monza</a> in <a href="/wiki/Monza" title="Monza">Monza, Italy</a>. It was the thirteenth round of the <a href="/wiki/2017_FIA_Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="2017 FIA Formula One World Championship">2017 FIA Formula One World Championship</a>, and marked the 87th running of the <a href="/wiki/Italian_Grand_Prix" title="Italian Grand Prix">Italian Grand Prix</a> and the 82nd time the race was held at Monza.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> entered the round with a seven-point lead over <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> in the World Drivers' Championship, with <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> forty-one points behind in third. In the World Constructors' Championship, <a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> led Ferrari by thirty-nine points with <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a> in third. Hamilton started the race from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>, the sixty-ninth of his career. With this pole position, he broke <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a>'s record for the most pole positions in a career.
</p><p>The race concluded with Hamilton leading the championship by 3 points ahead of Vettel.
</p><p>The race was considered somewhat farcical, due to the number of grid penalties that were taken in the race equalling 150 and making the grid almost unrecognisable from the qualifying results. Only 4 drivers started in their qualifying positions, with only Hamilton of the four not having taken a penalty. Sergio Pérez, meanwhile, moved up 1 place on the grid despite having a 5-place grid penalty.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Italian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Italian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Mercedes drivers Lewis Hamilton and Valtteri Bottas set the fastest times in first<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> and second<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> practice respectively, with times of 1:21.537 and 1:21.406.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_8-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Third practice was disrupted by heavy rain on the circuit, and the green flag was only shown with 16 minutes remaining.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Williams driver <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> set the quickest time with a 1:40.660.<sup id="cite_ref-:0_8-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-:0-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Italian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The heavy rains that delayed the start of Free Practice 3 remained over the region, causing qualifying to be interrupted. The session started on time, but was red-flagged when <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> spun into the barriers. After almost two hours of waiting for the track to clear up, the session resumed with Lewis Hamilton taking pole ahead of the two Red Bulls but because of grid penalties it promoted <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a> to second to become the youngest front row starter in a Formula One race ever.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (September 2017)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup> <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Ocon" title="Esteban Ocon">Esteban Ocon</a> got promoted to third ahead of Bottas, Räikkönen and Vettel.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_Italian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The race was welcomed by clear sky with Hamilton on pole ahead of Stroll and Ocon. Hamilton got away well as Stroll was conservative into turn 1 and was passed by Ocon. Further behind there was contact between Massa and Perez. A few laps later, Verstappen moved to the outside at turn 1 but hit Massa which caused Verstappen to get a right front tyre puncture. The stewards took no further action. There were good battles further behind including Räikkönen, Ocon and Stroll. During the only round of pit stops (most drivers made only one stop in the race), all these drivers retained their positions. Further behind, Ricciardo was 5th but overtook Räikkönen into turn 1 and closed in on Vettel. In the end, Mercedes finished with a 1–2, with Lewis Hamilton winning ahead of the second-placed Valtteri Bottas. Vettel finished third ahead of a charging Ricciardo. On the final lap, Massa attacked Stroll but couldn't overtake him and finished 8th. Verstappen recovered from the early puncture to finish 10th after small incidents with the two Haas drivers (<a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> and <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a>).
</p>
