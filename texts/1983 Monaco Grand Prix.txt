<p>The <b>1983 Monaco Grand Prix</b> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Motor_race" class="mw-redirect" title="Motor race">motor race</a> held at <a href="/wiki/Circuit_de_Monaco" title="Circuit de Monaco">Monaco</a> on 15 May 1983. The race, contested over 76 laps, was the fifth race of the <a href="/wiki/1983_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="1983 Formula One season">1983 Formula One season</a> and was won by <a href="/wiki/Keke_Rosberg" title="Keke Rosberg">Keke Rosberg</a>, driving a <a href="/wiki/Williams_FW08" title="Williams FW08">Williams</a>-<a href="/wiki/Ford_Motor_Company" title="Ford Motor Company">Ford</a>, with <a href="/wiki/Nelson_Piquet" title="Nelson Piquet">Nelson Piquet</a> second in a <a href="/wiki/Brabham_BT52" title="Brabham BT52">Brabham</a>-<a href="/wiki/BMW_in_Formula_One" title="BMW in Formula One">BMW</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alain_Prost" title="Alain Prost">Alain Prost</a> third in a <a href="/wiki/Renault_RE40" title="Renault RE40">Renault</a>.
</p><p>Thirty years later, Rosberg's son <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico</a> won the <a href="/wiki/2013_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="2013 Monaco Grand Prix">2013 race</a>, making them the first father and son to win in the principality.
</p>
