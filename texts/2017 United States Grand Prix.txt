<p>The <b>2017 United States Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>2017 Formula 1 United States Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_racing" title="Formula One racing">Formula One motor race</a> held on 22 October 2017 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_of_the_Americas" title="Circuit of the Americas">Circuit of the Americas</a> in Austin, Texas, United States as the seventeenth round of the <a href="/wiki/2017_FIA_Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="2017 FIA Formula One World Championship">2017 FIA Formula One World Championship</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-prov._calendar_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-prov._calendar-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> The race marked the forty-seventh running of the <a href="/wiki/United_States_Grand_Prix" title="United States Grand Prix">United States Grand Prix</a>, the thirty-ninth time that the race was run as a World Championship event since the inaugural season in <a href="/wiki/1950_Formula_One_season" title="1950 Formula One season">1950</a>, and the sixth time that a World Championship round was held at the Circuit of the Americas in Austin, Texas.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> driver <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> entered the round with a 59-point lead over <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari's</a> <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> in the World Drivers' Championship. Hamilton's teammate <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> was third, a further 13 points behind. In the World Constructors' Championship, Mercedes held a lead of 155 points over Ferrari, with <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull Racing</a> a further 92 points behind in third place.
</p><p>Mercedes clinched its fourth successive World Constructors' Championship after Hamilton won and Bottas finished 5th.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Driver_changes">Driver changes</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Driver changes">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:BrendonHartleyF1Debut.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fb/BrendonHartleyF1Debut.jpg/220px-BrendonHartleyF1Debut.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="156" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fb/BrendonHartleyF1Debut.jpg/330px-BrendonHartleyF1Debut.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/fb/BrendonHartleyF1Debut.jpg/440px-BrendonHartleyF1Debut.jpg 2x" data-file-width="1391" data-file-height="984" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:BrendonHartleyF1Debut.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div><a href="/wiki/Brendon_Hartley" title="Brendon Hartley">Brendon Hartley</a> made his Formula One debut with <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Scuderia Toro Rosso</a>.</div></div></div>
<p><a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz Jr.</a> moved to <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a> and replaced <a href="/wiki/Jolyon_Palmer" title="Jolyon Palmer">Jolyon Palmer</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a> returned to <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a> after having missed the <a href="/wiki/2017_Malaysian_Grand_Prix" title="2017 Malaysian Grand Prix">Malaysian</a> and <a href="/wiki/2017_Japanese_Grand_Prix" title="2017 Japanese Grand Prix">Japanese Grands Prix</a>, taking over Sainz's car.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/2015_FIA_World_Endurance_Championship" title="2015 FIA World Endurance Championship">2015 World Endurance Champion</a> <a href="/wiki/Brendon_Hartley" title="Brendon Hartley">Brendon Hartley</a> made his Formula One début, taking <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a>'s (formerly Kvyat's) seat at Toro Rosso.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-Grid_Penalties1_10-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Grid_Penalties1-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> This was the first time a New Zealander participated in a Formula One race in 33 years.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Tyres">Tyres</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2017_United_States_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Tyres">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Tyre supplier <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> provided teams with the ultra-soft, super-soft and soft compounds of tyre. The purple branding on the ultra-soft compound was replaced by pink for the race to raise awareness of <a href="/wiki/Breast_cancer" title="Breast cancer">breast cancer</a>. Several teams also incorporated pink into their liveries to support the cause (except Force India, whose cars were pink to begin with).
</p>
