<p>The <b>2019 Abu Dhabi Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 Etihad Airways Abu Dhabi Grand Prix 2019</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race that was held on 1 December 2019 at the <a href="/wiki/Yas_Marina_Circuit" title="Yas Marina Circuit">Yas Marina Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Abu_Dhabi" title="Abu Dhabi">Abu Dhabi</a>, <a href="/wiki/United_Arab_Emirates" title="United Arab Emirates">United Arab Emirates</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The race was the twenty first and final round of the <a href="/wiki/2019_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2019 Formula One World Championship">2019 Formula One World Championship</a> and marked the eleventh running of the <a href="/wiki/Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix" title="Abu Dhabi Grand Prix">Abu Dhabi Grand Prix</a> and the eleventh time that the race was run as a World Championship event since the inaugural season in <a href="/wiki/1950_Formula_One_season" title="1950 Formula One season">1950</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> This was also the first time that a race was held in December since the <a href="/wiki/1963_South_African_Grand_Prix" title="1963 South African Grand Prix">1963 South African Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> It was the final race for <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>, as the team was rebranded to <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_AlphaTauri" title="Scuderia AlphaTauri">AlphaTauri</a> for the <a href="/wiki/2020_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2020 Formula One World Championship">2020</a> season.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Championship_standings_before_the_race">Championship standings before the race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Championship standings before the race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Heading into the race weekend the top two places in the <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_World_Drivers%27_Champions" title="List of Formula One World Drivers&#39; Champions">World Drivers' Championship</a> had already been determined with <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> as champion ahead of team mate <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a>. However third place was yet to be decided between <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a>, 260 points and <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a>, 249 points, with <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>, 230 points, still able to overtake Leclerc for fourth place in the standings but too far behind Verstappen to take third. The top four places in the <a href="/wiki/World_Constructors_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="World Constructors Championship">World Constructors Championship</a> were decided with <a href="/wiki/Mercedes_in_Formula_One" class="mw-redirect" title="Mercedes in Formula One">Mercedes</a> in first ahead of <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a>, <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a> and <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>. Fifth place was still undecided with <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a> eight points ahead of <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Toro Rosso</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Entries">Entries</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Entries">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">Further information: <a href="/wiki/2019_Formula_One_World_Championship#Entries" title="2019 Formula One World Championship">2019 Formula One World Championship ¬ß&#160;Entries</a></div>
<p>The drivers and teams entered were the same as those for the previous race with no additional stand-in drivers for the race or practice.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The first session saw the <a href="/wiki/Red_flag_(racing)" class="mw-redirect" title="Red flag (racing)">red flag</a> come out twice. The first time when <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> suffered an engine failure and a second time after <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> crashed his car at turn 19 in the closing minutes of the session, after which the session was not restarted. The session ended with <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> fastest followed by <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup> The second practice session saw one red flag after Bottas and <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> collided at turn 11, Bottas would later receive a reprimand for the incident.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> Bottas finished the session fastest ahead of Hamilton and <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup> The third practice session ran uninterrupted and ended with Verstappen fastest followed by Hamilton and Bottas.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2019_Abu_Dhabi_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>Prior to the race, it was announced that the fuel mass in <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a>'s car as declared by <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> was "significantly different" to the amount measured by the <a href="/wiki/FIA" class="mw-redirect" title="FIA">FIA</a>. It was decided that this would be investigated by the stewards after the race.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup> 
</p><p>The front-runners all maintained their positions into the first corner, with <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> leading from <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>. Further back down the grid, <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a> collided with the rear of <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a>, causing Gasly to make contact with <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio P√©rez">Sergio P√©rez</a> in front of him and resulting in Gasly losing his front wing. Gasly was forced to pit for a replacement which caused him to drop to the back of the grid, over a minute behind 19th place. The incident was not investigated by the stewards. <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> lost 2nd place to Leclerc on the straight between turns 7 and 8, meanwhile <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> had made up five places on the first lap after starting in last place.
</p><p>On lap 4 it was announced that <a href="/wiki/Drag_reduction_system" title="Drag reduction system">DRS</a> was disabled for all cars due to a technical issue. It was later revealed that the system was deliberately disabled by the race director after a server crash caused communications with the cars to be disrupted, meaning that it could not be ensured that cars only used DRS in the permitted zones of the track.<sup id="cite_ref-17" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-17">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup> Shortly after, Stroll complained of difficulties caused by damage from his first lap collision with Gasly. Stroll would later go on to retire from a brake problem after running near the back for most of the race. <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">Lando Norris</a> was the first driver to make a scheduled pit stop on lap 8. Ferrari brought both drivers in for pit stops on lap 12, with <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> suffering with a slow stop causing him to be stationary for nearly 7 seconds. 
</p><p>DRS was eventually enabled on lap 18. Bottas passed <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico H√ºlkenberg">Nico H√ºlkenberg</a> on the same lap to clear the midfield after starting from the back. <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> and <a href="/wiki/Antonio_Giovinazzi" title="Antonio Giovinazzi">Antonio Giovinazzi</a> both suffered floor damage after colliding during an overtake attempt by Giovinazzi. Verstappen pitted from 2nd on lap 25, followed by Hamilton a lap later who maintained the lead upon exiting the pits. Bottas was the last of the front-runners to pit on lap 29. No positions were gained or lost amongst the top six after the round of pit stops. On lap 32, Verstappen retook 2nd place from Leclerc going into turn 8, having lost the position on the first lap. The Ferrari drivers made a second round of pit stops on lap 38, with Leclerc maintaining 3rd and Vettel dropping from 4th to 6th. Bottas passed <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Albon" title="Alexander Albon">Alexander Albon</a> for 4th place soon afterwards. 
</p><p>On lap 41, <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz Jr.</a> made a second pit stop from 9th place after he was concerned that his ageing tyres would cause him to be overtaken towards the end of the race, losing a points-scoring position and handing 6th place in the World Drivers' Championship to Gasly. Sainz exited the pits in 14th place. The final two laps saw multiple overtakes; Vettel passed Albon for 5th place, <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">Lando Norris</a> lost 7th place to P√©rez, and Sainz successfully passed H√ºlkenberg to take 10th place and claim a point, securing 6th place in the championship. Hamilton crossed the line to win the race, taking his 11th victory of the season and the 84th of his career. Having also led every lap, taken pole position and recorded the fastest lap of the race, this marked Hamilton's sixth "grand slam", his last of which came at the <a href="/wiki/2017_British_Grand_Prix" title="2017 British Grand Prix">2017 British Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Post-race, Ferrari were handed a ‚Ç¨50,000 fine for the pre-race fuel infringement, although Leclerc was allowed to keep his 3rd-place finish.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> In an interview, Gasly said he was "disgusted" by the collision with Stroll at the start of the race, as his car "clearly had the potential to keep sixth place [in the drivers' championship]".<sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Gasly ended the season in 7th place with 95 points, one short of Sainz's 96.
