<p>The <b>2020 Styrian Grand Prix</b> (officially known as the <b>Formula 1 Pirelli Großer Preis der Steiermark 2020</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> that took place on 12 July 2020 at the <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Ring" title="Red Bull Ring">Red Bull Ring</a> in <a href="/wiki/Spielberg,_Styria" title="Spielberg, Styria">Spielberg</a>, <a href="/wiki/Austria" title="Austria">Austria</a>. The race was the second round of the <a href="/wiki/2020_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2020 Formula One World Championship">2020 Formula One World Championship</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> It was the first running of the <b>Styrian Grand Prix</b>, and was held exactly one week after the <a href="/wiki/2020_Austrian_Grand_Prix" title="2020 Austrian Grand Prix">2020 Austrian Grand Prix</a> on the same track, due to the rescheduling of the season because of the <a href="/wiki/COVID-19_pandemic" title="COVID-19 pandemic">COVID-19 pandemic</a>.
</p><p>In the race, <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> won from pole position, ahead of his teammate <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a>. The result saw Hamilton close to within 6 points of the championship leader, Bottas, and Mercedes extend their lead in the Constructors' Championship to 41 points.
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Impact_of_the_COVID-19_pandemic">Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">See also: <a href="/wiki/2020_Formula_One_World_Championship#Impact_of_the_COVID-19_pandemic" title="2020 Formula One World Championship">2020 Formula One World Championship §&#160;Impact of the COVID-19 pandemic</a>, and <a href="/wiki/COVID-19_pandemic_in_Austria" title="COVID-19 pandemic in Austria">COVID-19 pandemic in Austria</a></div>
<p>The originally scheduled calendar for the <a href="/wiki/2020_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="2020 Formula One World Championship">2020</a> championship was heavily affected by the <a href="/wiki/COVID-19_pandemic" title="COVID-19 pandemic">COVID-19 pandemic</a>. Several Grands Prix were cancelled or postponed after the aborted <a href="/wiki/2020_Australian_Grand_Prix" title="2020 Australian Grand Prix">opening round</a> in Australia, prompting the <a href="/wiki/F%C3%A9d%C3%A9ration_Internationale_de_l%27Automobile" title="Fédération Internationale de l&#39;Automobile">Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile</a> to draft a new calendar. The start of the championship was delayed until 5 July, with the <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Ring" title="Red Bull Ring">Red Bull Ring</a> hosting the <a href="/wiki/Austrian_Grand_Prix" title="Austrian Grand Prix">Austrian Grand Prix</a> as the opening round of the championship. Organisers of the race signed a contract with Liberty Media, the sport's commercial rights holder, to host a second round at the circuit on 12 July (a week after the first race) to be known as the "Styrian Grand Prix".<sup id="cite_ref-4" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-4">&#91;2&#93;</a></sup> The race was named for <a href="/wiki/Styria" title="Styria">Styria</a>, the state of Austria that the Red Bull Ring is located in. The back-to-back Austrian races marked the first time that a country hosted back-to-back races in the same season since <a href="/wiki/1995_Formula_One_World_Championship" title="1995 Formula One World Championship">1995</a> when Japan hosted the <a href="/wiki/1995_Pacific_Grand_Prix" title="1995 Pacific Grand Prix">Pacific</a> and <a href="/wiki/1995_Japanese_Grand_Prix" title="1995 Japanese Grand Prix">Japanese</a> Grands Prix.<sup id="cite_ref-5" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-5">&#91;c&#93;</a></sup> The race was also the first time in the sport's history that the same venue and circuit layout hosted back-to-back World Championship races.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Car_upgrades">Car upgrades</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Car upgrades">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferrari</a> brought and introduced a wide range of updates to the <a href="/wiki/Ferrari_SF1000" title="Ferrari SF1000">Ferrari SF1000</a>. The upgrades were originally scheduled for the <a href="/wiki/2020_Hungarian_Grand_Prix" title="2020 Hungarian Grand Prix">Hungarian Grand Prix</a> but were brought forward after a poor performance at the <a href="/wiki/2020_Austrian_Grand_Prix" title="2020 Austrian Grand Prix">Austrian Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Mercedes_in_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Mercedes in F1">Mercedes</a> trialed a reliability upgrade by testing new gearbox components designed to solve the electrical problems at the first race of the season.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup> McLaren used a new chin spoiler design.<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Championship_standings_before_the_race">Championship standings before the race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Championship standings before the race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>After the first round at the <a href="/wiki/2020_Austrian_Grand_Prix" title="2020 Austrian Grand Prix">2020 Austrian Grand Prix</a>, <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> led the championship by 7 points from <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a> of <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Scuderia Ferrari</a> with <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">Lando Norris</a> of <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> a further 2 points behind in third place. Defending Drivers' Champion <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> was fourth in the standings, 13 points off the championship lead. In the Constructors' Championship, defending champions Mercedes led the championship with 37 points, McLaren was in second with 26 points with Ferrari placed third with 19 points.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Entrants">Entrants</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Entrants">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">Further information: <a href="/wiki/2020_Formula_One_World_Championship#Entries" title="2020 Formula One World Championship">2020 Formula One World Championship §&#160;Entries</a></div>
<p>The drivers and teams were the same as the season entry list with no additional stand-in drivers for the race.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Jack_Aitken" title="Jack Aitken">Jack Aitken</a> and <a href="/wiki/Robert_Kubica" title="Robert Kubica">Robert Kubica</a> drove in the first practice session for <a href="/wiki/Williams_Grand_Prix_Engineering" title="Williams Grand Prix Engineering">Williams</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alfa_Romeo_in_Formula_One" title="Alfa Romeo in Formula One">Alfa Romeo Racing</a> respectively. Aitken replaced <a href="/wiki/George_Russell_(racing_driver)" title="George Russell (racing driver)">George Russell</a>, while Kubica replaced <a href="/wiki/Antonio_Giovinazzi" title="Antonio Giovinazzi">Antonio Giovinazzi</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Tyres">Tyres</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Tyres">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div role="note" class="hatnote navigation-not-searchable">See also: <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyres" title="Formula One tyres">Formula One tyres</a></div>
<p>The C2, C3 and C4 tyre compounds were made available for teams to use by <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a>, the same compound selections used at the <a href="/wiki/2020_Austrian_Grand_Prix" title="2020 Austrian Grand Prix">Austrian Grand Prix</a> on the same circuit the week before.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Practice">Practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The first practice session ended with <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> fastest for <a href="/wiki/Racing_Point_F1_Team" title="Racing Point F1 Team">Racing Point</a> ahead of <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_Racing" title="Red Bull Racing">Red Bull</a>'s <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> and <a href="/wiki/Mercedes_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Mercedes F1">Mercedes's</a> <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-16" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-16">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">Lando Norris</a> was given a three-place grid penalty after overtaking another car under <a href="/wiki/Racing_flags#Yellow_flag" title="Racing flags">yellow flag</a> conditions,<sup id="cite_ref-NOR_pen_17-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-NOR_pen-17">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup> which came after <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_Latifi" title="Nicholas Latifi">Nicholas Latifi</a> was forced to pull over to the side of the track with gearbox issues.<sup id="cite_ref-18" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-18">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a> was unable to set a lap time during the session due to battery problems.<sup id="cite_ref-19" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-19">&#91;16&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-20" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-20">&#91;17&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The second practice session ended with Verstappen fastest, followed by Bottas and Pérez.<sup id="cite_ref-21" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-21">&#91;18&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a>'s session ended early when his <a href="/wiki/Renault_R.S.20" title="Renault R.S.20">Renault R.S.20</a> left the track at turn nine and crashed heavily into the barriers at the exit of the corner, bringing out the <a href="/wiki/Racing_flags#Red_flag" title="Racing flags">red flags</a>. Ricciardo confirmed over the radio that he was fine and was taken to the circuit's medical centre where he was declared fit to race.<sup id="cite_ref-22" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-22">&#91;19&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> finished the session in sixth, seven-tenths adrift of teammate Bottas. <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Albon" title="Alexander Albon">Alexander Albon</a> recovered from a spin at turn three to record the seventh-fastest time, and complained about a "snappy" <a href="/wiki/Red_Bull_RB16" class="mw-redirect" title="Red Bull RB16">Red Bull RB16</a> – which then led to another spin into the gravel going through the turn 7–8 chicane with 15 minutes of the session to go.<sup id="cite_ref-23" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-23">&#91;20&#93;</a></sup> Lando Norris cited chest and back pain as the reason for doing fewer laps in practice than teammate <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz Jr.</a>, however his condition had improved by the following morning.<sup id="cite_ref-24" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-24">&#91;21&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The third practice session was cancelled due to heavy rain.<sup id="cite_ref-25" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-25">&#91;22&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The session was the first entirely wet qualifying session at the Red Bull Ring since the <a href="/wiki/1998_Austrian_Grand_Prix" title="1998 Austrian Grand Prix">1998 Austrian Grand Prix</a> and the first at the circuit where non-<a href="/wiki/Slick_tyres" class="mw-redirect" title="Slick tyres">slick tyres</a> were used since the <a href="/wiki/2015_Austrian_Grand_Prix" title="2015 Austrian Grand Prix">2015 Austrian Grand Prix</a>.<sup class="noprint Inline-Template Template-Fact" style="white-space:nowrap;">&#91;<i><a href="/wiki/Wikipedia:Citation_needed" title="Wikipedia:Citation needed"><span title="This claim needs references to reliable sources. (July 2020)">citation needed</span></a></i>&#93;</sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Q1_and_Q2">Q1 and Q2</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=9" title="Edit section: Q1 and Q2">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The first qualifying session began at 15:46 local time, forty-six minutes after the session was originally scheduled to start.  All drivers started on the <a href="/wiki/Rain_tyre" title="Rain tyre">wet compound</a> <a href="/wiki/Formula_One_tyres" title="Formula One tyres">tyres</a>, and all would continue to use it for the entirety of qualifying. The track was extremely wet, with the cars prone to <a href="/wiki/Aquaplaning" title="Aquaplaning">aquaplaning</a>. The only major incident of the session came towards the end, when <a href="/wiki/Antonio_Giovinazzi" title="Antonio Giovinazzi">Antonio Giovinazzi</a> lost control of his <a href="/wiki/Alfa_Romeo_in_Formula_One" title="Alfa Romeo in Formula One">Alfa Romeo</a> after turn seven; his car slid onto the grass and collided gently with a barrier, scattering some debris. Giovinazzi kept the engine running, but soon stopped at turn four, leading to the session being <a href="/wiki/Racing_flags#Red_flag" title="Racing flags">red-flagged</a> with 13 seconds remaining. Giovinazzi was eliminated from Q1 along with <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a>, <a href="/wiki/Nicholas_Latifi" title="Nicholas Latifi">Nicholas Latifi</a> and <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a>, who had not set a lap due to an issue with his <a href="/wiki/Energy_recovery" title="Energy recovery">energy recovery</a> system.<sup id="cite_ref-26" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-26">&#91;23&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/George_Russell_(racing_driver)" title="George Russell (racing driver)">George Russell</a> reached Q2 for the first time in his career, the first time a <a href="/wiki/Williams_Racing" class="mw-redirect" title="Williams Racing">Williams</a> driver had achieved this since the <a href="/wiki/2018_Brazilian_Grand_Prix" title="2018 Brazilian Grand Prix">2018 Brazilian Grand Prix</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-27" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-27">&#91;24&#93;</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-28" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-28">&#91;25&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>The rain intensity increased during Q2 however the session proceeded without incident. Those eliminated were <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a>, George Russell, <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a>, <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Kevin_Magnussen" title="Kevin Magnussen">Kevin Magnussen</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Q3">Q3</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=10" title="Edit section: Q3">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Heavy rain continued into Q3 but gradually decreased over the course of the session. At various points, <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>, <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> were on provisional <a href="/wiki/Pole_position" title="Pole position">pole position</a>. In the end, Hamilton took pole with a lap time of 1:19:273, over 1.2 seconds faster than his nearest rival, Verstappen, who spun on his final attempt. <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz Jr.</a> qualified third, the first driver to do so for <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a> on pace alone since <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> at the <a href="/wiki/2014_British_Grand_Prix" title="2014 British Grand Prix">2014 British Grand Prix</a> (Sainz's teammate <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">Lando Norris</a> had started third at the <a href="/wiki/2020_Austrian_Grand_Prix" title="2020 Austrian Grand Prix">previous race</a>, but had been promoted to that position after Hamilton received a penalty).<sup id="cite_ref-29" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-29">&#91;26&#93;</a></sup> Hamilton's teammate Bottas qualified in fourth place, followed by <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Ocon" title="Esteban Ocon">Esteban Ocon</a>, Lando Norris, <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Albon" title="Alexander Albon">Alexander Albon</a>, <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a>, <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a>, and <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a>.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-qualifying">Post-qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=11" title="Edit section: Post-qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Norris's penalty issued during the first practice session demoted him from sixth to ninth on the grid. Three drivers were investigated for breaches of the sporting regulations after qualifying; Pérez for ignoring <a href="/wiki/Racing_flags#Yellow_flag" title="Racing flags">yellow flags</a> during Q1, Räikkönen and Leclerc for breaches of red-flag protocol during Q1, and Leclerc again for impeding Kvyat during Q2. No action was taken regarding the first three allegations, however Leclerc was issued a three-place grid penalty for impeding Kvyat, demoting him from 11th to 14th on the grid.<sup id="cite_ref-30" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-30">&#91;27&#93;</a></sup> On the morning of the race, Giovinazzi received a five place grid penalty for an unscheduled gearbox change,<sup id="cite_ref-GIO_pen_31-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GIO_pen-31">&#91;28&#93;</a></sup> whilst Romain Grosjean was forced to start from the pit lane due to a breach of <a href="/wiki/Parc_ferm%C3%A9" title="Parc fermé">parc fermé</a> regulations by his team, who worked on his car for over 3 hours more than allowed by the regulations, without the presence of a scrutineer. Whilst the normal punishment in such cases is disqualification from the Grand Prix weekend, Grosjean avoided this as the team blamed an ERS water pump problem that had to be repaired in order to allow him to participate.<sup id="cite_ref-GRO_pen_32-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-GRO_pen-32">&#91;29&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=13" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race_report">Race report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=14" title="Edit section: Race report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Opening_laps">Opening laps</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=15" title="Edit section: Opening laps">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>The race was run in dry conditions, in contrast to the entirely wet qualifying meaning all 20 drivers had a free choice of starting tyres as is permitted by  the Sporting Regulations.<sup id="cite_ref-36" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-36">&#91;33&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> maintained his lead from pole. At turn one <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> fell to 12th place behind <a href="/wiki/George_Russell_(racing_driver)" title="George Russell (racing driver)">George Russell</a> and <a href="/wiki/Lance_Stroll" title="Lance Stroll">Lance Stroll</a>. In an attempt to overtake his teammate on the inside of turn three, <a href="/wiki/Charles_Leclerc" title="Charles Leclerc">Charles Leclerc</a> collided with Vettel, breaking Vettel's rear wing. The <a href="/wiki/Safety_car" title="Safety car">safety car</a> was then deployed to clear debris from the turn three incident. Both <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Ferrari" title="Scuderia Ferrari">Ferraris</a> entered the pits at the end of the opening lap and Vettel's car was retired from the race. Leclerc's car had suffered front wing and floor damage and his front wing was replaced. Racing resumed on lap four. Russell, running in 11th place, left the track at turn six and went into the gravel. He was able to continue racing but rejoined at the back of the field in 18th place. At the end of lap four, Ferrari determined that the damage to Leclerc's car was too great to continue and he entered the pits to retire from the race. By lap eight, <a href="/wiki/Carlos_Sainz_Jr." title="Carlos Sainz Jr.">Carlos Sainz Jr.</a> had fallen from third to fifth having been passed by <a href="/wiki/Valtteri_Bottas" title="Valtteri Bottas">Valtteri Bottas</a> and <a href="/wiki/Alexander_Albon" title="Alexander Albon">Alexander Albon</a>. On lap 14, <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a> entered the points positions by passing <a href="/wiki/Lando_Norris" title="Lando Norris">Lando Norris</a> for tenth place, having started 17th.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Mid-race">Mid-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=16" title="Edit section: Mid-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>On lap 24, second-placed <a href="/wiki/Max_Verstappen" title="Max Verstappen">Max Verstappen</a> was the first driver to make a scheduled pit stop. On the following lap, <a href="/wiki/Esteban_Ocon" title="Esteban Ocon">Esteban Ocon</a> entered the pits to retire from the race, his car suffering from a cooling issue.<sup id="cite_ref-37" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-37">&#91;34&#93;</a></sup> Race leader Hamilton pitted on lap 27, with teammate Bottas inheriting the lead. On lap 32, Sainz suffered a slow pit stop which would go on to cost him three positions, and two laps later Bottas made his first stop to hand the lead back to Hamilton. After all drivers had made their first stops, the order was Hamilton, Verstappen, Bottas, Albon, Ricciardo, Stroll, Pérez, Sainz, Norris and <a href="/wiki/Pierre_Gasly" title="Pierre Gasly">Pierre Gasly</a>.
</p><p>Pérez began to move further forward after his pit stop, passing teammate Stroll on lap 46 and then <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> two laps later to claim fifth place. Pérez then set a number of <a href="/wiki/Fastest_lap" title="Fastest lap">fastest laps</a> whilst moving closer towards Albon in fourth.<sup id="cite_ref-race_report_38-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-race_report-38">&#91;35&#93;</a></sup> Gasly later became the first driver to make two pit stops, having started seventh but fallen to 13th on his hard-compound tyres. He would only recover to 15th by the end of the race. On lap 62 Sainz let Norris pass for eighth.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Closing_laps">Closing laps</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=17" title="Edit section: Closing laps">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Bottas, who had been slowly catching Verstappen since the first round of pit stops, overtook him for second place on lap 66. The two cars battled through the remainder of the lap, with Verstappen briefly reclaiming the place, before Bottas overtook again on the following lap. Verstappen and ninth-placed Sainz then pitted with two laps remaining in attempts to claim the fastest lap of the race, both drivers having large enough gaps behind as to avoid losing positions. On the penultimate lap, Pérez attempted an overtake on Albon for fourth place at turn four. The cars made contact resulting in Pérez breaking his front wing. The 15-second gap behind Pérez then began to close as the damage caused him to fall back. Stroll, who had been close behind Ricciardo for almost 30 laps, made an overtake attempt for sixth place at turn three, running deep and causing both drivers to take to the runoff area. Stroll took the position and the move caused Ricciardo to fall to eighth place behind Norris. On the final lap, Norris passed Stroll for sixth place on the straight after turn three, before catching the ailing Pérez at the final corner to take fifth.
</p><p>Hamilton took the <a href="/wiki/Racing_flags#Chequered_flag" title="Racing flags">chequered flag</a> to claim his first victory of the season and the 85th of his career, followed by Bottas and Verstappen to complete the podium, with Albon and Norris behind. Pérez, Stroll and Ricciardo crossed the finish line three-wide, separated by just over 0.2 seconds, but maintained those respective positions. Sainz finished ninth and claimed his first <a href="/wiki/List_of_Formula_One_drivers_who_set_a_fastest_lap" title="List of Formula One drivers who set a fastest lap">fastest lap in Formula One</a> and the second consecutive fastest lap for <a href="/wiki/McLaren" title="McLaren">McLaren</a>. Sainz's fastest lap of 1:05 619  on lap 68 set a new race lap  record for the circuit.<sup id="cite_ref-39" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-39">&#91;36&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a> claimed the final point in tenth place.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2020_Styrian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=18" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>Stroll was investigated by the race stewards for his penultimate-lap overtake on Ricciardo, however this was deemed a racing incident and no further action was taken. Ricciardo later described the move as "desperate" and stated that he expected Stroll to receive a penalty.<sup id="cite_ref-40" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-40">&#91;37&#93;</a></sup> Leclerc apologised for causing the opening-lap collision with Vettel, remarking "I am just disappointed in myself. I’ve done a very bad job today. I let the team down." Vettel stated he was "very surprised" that Leclerc attempted the overtake, noting that the team had intended to assess the performance of the updates brought to the <a href="/wiki/Ferrari_SF1000" title="Ferrari SF1000">SF1000</a> for the race.<sup id="cite_ref-41" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-41">&#91;38&#93;</a></sup>
