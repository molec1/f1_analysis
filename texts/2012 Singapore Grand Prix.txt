<p>The <b>2012 Singapore Grand Prix</b> (officially the <b>2012 Formula 1 SingTel Singapore Grand Prix</b>) was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> motor race that took place at the <a href="/wiki/Marina_Bay_Street_Circuit" title="Marina Bay Street Circuit">Marina Bay Street Circuit</a> in <a href="/wiki/Marina_Bay,_Singapore" title="Marina Bay, Singapore">Marina Bay</a>, <a href="/wiki/Singapore" title="Singapore">Singapore</a> on 23 September 2012 as the fourteenth round of the <a href="/wiki/2012_Formula_One_season" class="mw-redirect" title="2012 Formula One season">2012 season</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-7Dec_calendar_3-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7Dec_calendar-3">&#91;3&#93;</a></sup> The race was the thirteenth time that a <a href="/wiki/Singapore_Grand_Prix" title="Singapore Grand Prix">Singapore Grand Prix</a> has been held, and the fifth time it was a round of the Formula One World Championship.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> started the race from pole.<sup id="cite_ref-HAM_MAL_front_row_4-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-HAM_MAL_front_row-4">&#91;4&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> won the race, his second of the season, after Hamilton's gearbox failed early in the race.<sup id="cite_ref-VET_win_5-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-VET_win-5">&#91;5&#93;</a></sup>
</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Report">Report</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Singapore_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: Report">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Singapore_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>Having been unable to race in Italy two weeks earlier because of a one-race ban he received for causing a serious collision in <a href="/wiki/2012_Belgian_Grand_Prix" title="2012 Belgian Grand Prix">Belgium</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-Grosjean_EX_6-0" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-Grosjean_EX-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> was back in the driving seat again with <a href="/wiki/Lotus_F1" title="Lotus F1">Lotus F1</a> for the race in Singapore.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>Like the <a href="/wiki/2011_Singapore_Grand_Prix" title="2011 Singapore Grand Prix">2011 Singapore Grand Prix</a>, tyre supplier Pirelli brought its yellow-banded soft compound tyre as the harder "prime" tyre and the red-banded supersoft compound tyre as the softer "option" tyre.<sup id="cite_ref-8" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-8">&#91;8&#93;</a></sup>
</p><p>At Singapore's Marina Bay circuit, <a href="/wiki/Ma_Qinghua" title="Ma Qinghua">Ma Qinghua</a> once again drove <a href="/wiki/Narain_Karthikeyan" title="Narain Karthikeyan">Narain Karthikeyan</a>'s car in the first free practice session,<sup id="cite_ref-9" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-9">&#91;9&#93;</a></sup> as he had during free practice at the Italian Grand Prix in <a href="/wiki/2012_Italian_Grand_Prix" title="2012 Italian Grand Prix">Monza</a>,<sup id="cite_ref-10" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-10">&#91;10&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Free_practice">Free practice</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Singapore_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Free practice">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The first practice session on Friday evening was held in damp conditions. Rain earlier in the day had soaked the circuit, but it had started drying out as the session began. <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> set the fastest time of the session, five hundredths of a second faster than <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-11" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-11">&#91;11&#93;</a></sup> Although several drivers went off the circuit, they were able to rejoin without damage and the session was uninterrupted. By the time the second session began, the circuit was completely dry. Vettel was once again fastest, this time three tenths of a second faster than <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-12" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-12">&#91;12&#93;</a></sup> The session was briefly interrupted when <a href="/wiki/Bruno_Senna" title="Bruno Senna">Bruno Senna</a> clipped a barrier coming out from under the grandstand at turn 19 before spinning to a halt at the next corner. Vettel topped the third and final session on Saturday evening, three-tenths faster than Hamilton.<sup id="cite_ref-13" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-13">&#91;13&#93;</a></sup> The session was cut short two minutes before the chequered flag when <a href="/wiki/Vitaly_Petrov" title="Vitaly Petrov">Vitaly Petrov</a> hit the wall at Turn 21, breaking his right-rear suspension and as a consequence, his Caterham stopped in the entrance to the pitlane and by doing so, blocked it causing a red flag until the session ended.
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Qualifying">Qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Singapore_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>The first qualifying period saw several teams caught out by the difference in performance between the harder prime tyres and the softer options, which was estimated to be up to a second and a half per lap. The end result was that several teams had to use up a set of soft tyres to guarantee a place in the second period. <a href="/wiki/Williams_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="Williams F1">Williams'</a> <a href="/wiki/Bruno_Senna" title="Bruno Senna">Bruno Senna</a> clipped the wall on his final lap while faced with the threat of elimination, but made it through to the second period by a tenth of a second when <a href="/wiki/Kamui_Kobayashi" title="Kamui Kobayashi">Kamui Kobayashi</a> failed to better his lap time. <a href="/wiki/Vitaly_Petrov" title="Vitaly Petrov">Vitaly Petrov</a> out-qualified teammate <a href="/wiki/Heikki_Kovalainen" title="Heikki Kovalainen">Heikki Kovalainen</a> to start nineteenth, putting the <a href="/wiki/Caterham_F1" title="Caterham F1">Caterhams</a> ahead of <a href="/wiki/Marussia_F1" title="Marussia F1">Marussia</a> duo of <a href="/wiki/Timo_Glock" title="Timo Glock">Timo Glock</a> and <a href="/wiki/Charles_Pic" title="Charles Pic">Charles Pic</a>. <a href="/wiki/HRT_F1" class="mw-redirect" title="HRT F1">HRT</a> once again filled the final row of the grid, with <a href="/wiki/Narain_Karthikeyan" title="Narain Karthikeyan">Narain Karthikeyan</a> out-qualifying <a href="/wiki/Pedro_de_la_Rosa" title="Pedro de la Rosa">Pedro de la Rosa</a> for the second race in succession with a time that was over a second faster than the Spaniard's. De la Rosa started qualifying with the knowledge that he would receive a five-place grid penalty for an unscheduled gearbox change regardless of where he started, but as he qualified twenty-fourth and last, the effect of the penalty was negated.
</p><p>The second qualifying period started with Senna and <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> crashing. While Grosjean was able to continue after mechanics inspected his car, Senna – who had once again hit the wall at Turn 19, his third such incident at that corner – returned to the pit with terminal damage. With his failure to set a lap time, he was relegated to seventeenth on the grid. At the end of the fifteen-minute session, <a href="/wiki/Nico_H%C3%BClkenberg" title="Nico Hülkenberg">Nico Hülkenberg</a> was eliminated in eleventh, and later admitted that he felt he could have gone faster in the first sector. <a href="/wiki/Kimi_R%C3%A4ikk%C3%B6nen" title="Kimi Räikkönen">Kimi Räikkönen</a> qualified alongside him in twelfth, ahead of <a href="/wiki/Felipe_Massa" title="Felipe Massa">Felipe Massa</a> and <a href="/wiki/Sergio_P%C3%A9rez" title="Sergio Pérez">Sergio Pérez</a>. <a href="/wiki/Scuderia_Toro_Rosso" title="Scuderia Toro Rosso">Scuderia Toro Rosso</a> drivers <a href="/wiki/Daniel_Ricciardo" title="Daniel Ricciardo">Daniel Ricciardo</a> and <a href="/wiki/Jean-%C3%89ric_Vergne" title="Jean-Éric Vergne">Jean-Éric Vergne</a> had a quiet qualifying session, and ultimately finished fifteenth and sixteenth respectively.
</p><p><a href="/wiki/Mercedes-Benz_in_Formula_One" title="Mercedes-Benz in Formula One">Mercedes</a> elected not to take part in the final period of qualifying, allowing <a href="/wiki/Michael_Schumacher" title="Michael Schumacher">Michael Schumacher</a> and <a href="/wiki/Nico_Rosberg" title="Nico Rosberg">Nico Rosberg</a> to save a set of tyres and giving them the choice of which compound on which to start the race. As a result, only eight drivers took part in the final ten minutes of qualifying. Lewis Hamilton prevailed, taking pole position by half a second from <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a>. After being consistently fast through free practice, <a href="/wiki/Sebastian_Vettel" title="Sebastian Vettel">Sebastian Vettel</a> could only manage third overall, ahead of <a href="/wiki/Jenson_Button" title="Jenson Button">Jenson Button</a> and <a href="/wiki/Fernando_Alonso" title="Fernando Alonso">Fernando Alonso</a>, the latter admitting that he was not expecting to be fast during the Singapore Grand Prix weekend. <a href="/wiki/Paul_di_Resta" title="Paul di Resta">Paul di Resta</a> qualified sixth, ahead of <a href="/wiki/Mark_Webber" title="Mark Webber">Mark Webber</a>, leaving <a href="/wiki/Romain_Grosjean" title="Romain Grosjean">Romain Grosjean</a> as the eighth and final driver to set a time.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-qualifying">Post-qualifying</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Singapore_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=5" title="Edit section: Post-qualifying">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>In an interview after qualifying, <a href="/wiki/Lewis_Hamilton" title="Lewis Hamilton">Lewis Hamilton</a> publicly implored <a href="/wiki/Pastor_Maldonado" title="Pastor Maldonado">Pastor Maldonado</a> to be careful during the start of the race, given the latter's reputation for reckless driving and frequent on-track incidents.<sup id="cite_ref-14" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-14">&#91;14&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Pre-race">Pre-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Singapore_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Pre-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p>A minute of silence was held for <a href="/wiki/Sid_Watkins" title="Sid Watkins">Sid Watkins</a>, who died on 12 September 2012. A book of remembrance was also available for people to sign their condolences over the weekend.<sup id="cite_ref-15" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-15">&#91;15&#93;</a></sup>
</p>
<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Race">Race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Singapore_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<div class="thumb tright"><div class="thumbinner" style="width:222px;"><a href="/wiki/File:1_singapore_f1_night_race_2012_city_skyline.jpg" class="image"><img alt="" src="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f0/1_singapore_f1_night_race_2012_city_skyline.jpg/220px-1_singapore_f1_night_race_2012_city_skyline.jpg" decoding="async" width="220" height="147" class="thumbimage" srcset="//upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f0/1_singapore_f1_night_race_2012_city_skyline.jpg/330px-1_singapore_f1_night_race_2012_city_skyline.jpg 1.5x, //upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/f/f0/1_singapore_f1_night_race_2012_city_skyline.jpg/440px-1_singapore_f1_night_race_2012_city_skyline.jpg 2x" data-file-width="2048" data-file-height="1367" /></a>  <div class="thumbcaption"><div class="magnify"><a href="/wiki/File:1_singapore_f1_night_race_2012_city_skyline.jpg" class="internal" title="Enlarge"></a></div>Day 2</div></div></div>
<p>Hamilton led lap 1 with Maldonado falling to 4th with Vettel and Button overtaking into P2 and P3 respectively.
</p><p>On lap 23, Hamilton's gearbox failed forcing him to retire allowing Vettel to take P1 which continued until the end of the race. It was noted to be the 2nd mechanical retirement for the Briton.
</p><p>On lap 30, Karthikeyan crashed at turn 18 causing yellow flags and a safety car to be deployed. Maldonado retired  with hydraulic issues before the safety car came in. He had a call 1 lap before he came to the pits and retired. 2 laps later, Schumacher and Vergne collided, with Schumacher failing to brake properly, and consequently crashing into the back of Vergne's Toro Rosso, resulting in their retirement. After the crash, Michael Schumacher climbed out of his car and went over to apologise to Vergne.
</p><p>Paul di Resta managed to keep Mercedes driver Nico Rosberg at bay to claim his career-best finish of P4, gaining him two places in the Drivers' Championship to stand 11th.
</p><p>The race finished two laps early as the two-hour limit was reached, the first time this had happened since the <a href="/wiki/2008_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="2008 Monaco Grand Prix">2008 Monaco Grand Prix</a>.
</p>
<h4><span class="mw-headline" id="Post-race">Post-race</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2012_Singapore_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=8" title="Edit section: Post-race">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h4>
<p>After the race, the stewards gave Michael Schumacher a ten-place grid penalty at the next race, Suzuka, after he crashed into Jean-Eric Vergne and caused them both to retire. This penalty was applied in accordance with article 16.1 of the FIA Formula One Sporting Regulations. Mark Webber was also demoted by a place, losing the 10th place and the point to finish 11th, due to a move deemed by the FIA to be advantageous as it resulted in an unfair advantage.
</p>
