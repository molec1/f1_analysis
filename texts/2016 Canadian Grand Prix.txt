<p>The <b>2016 Canadian Grand Prix</b> (formally known as the <b>Formula 1 Grand Prix du Canada 2016</b>)<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> was a <a href="/wiki/Formula_One" title="Formula One">Formula One</a> <a href="/wiki/Auto_racing" title="Auto racing">motor race</a> that took place on 12 June 2016 at the <a href="/wiki/Circuit_Gilles_Villeneuve" title="Circuit Gilles Villeneuve">Circuit Gilles Villeneuve</a> in <a href="/wiki/Montreal" title="Montreal">Montreal, Quebec</a>, Canada.<sup id="cite_ref-formal_1-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-formal-1">&#91;1&#93;</a></sup> The race was the seventh round of the <a href="/wiki/2016_FIA_Formula_One_World_Championship" class="mw-redirect" title="2016 FIA Formula One World Championship">2016 FIA Formula One World Championship</a> and marked the fifty-third running of the <a href="/wiki/Canadian_Grand_Prix" title="Canadian Grand Prix">Canadian Grand Prix</a> as a round of the Formula One World Championship since the series' inception in <a href="/wiki/1950_Formula_One_season" title="1950 Formula One season">1950</a>. The race was won by Lewis Hamilton.
</p>

<h3><span class="mw-headline" id="Background">Background</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=2016_Canadian_Grand_Prix&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Background">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h3>
<p><a href="/wiki/Daniil_Kvyat" title="Daniil Kvyat">Daniil Kvyat</a> and <a href="/wiki/Marcus_Ericsson" title="Marcus Ericsson">Marcus Ericsson</a> both took three-place grid penalties for causing avoidable accidents during the previous race in <a href="/wiki/2016_Monaco_Grand_Prix" title="2016 Monaco Grand Prix">Monaco</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-6" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-6">&#91;6&#93;</a></sup> After heavily damaging his car in an accident during the Monaco Grand Prix, <a href="/wiki/Renault_in_Formula_One" title="Renault in Formula One">Renault</a> prepared a brand new <a href="/wiki/Renault_R.S.16" title="Renault R.S.16">Renault R.S.16</a> chassis for <a href="/wiki/Jolyon_Palmer" title="Jolyon Palmer">Jolyon Palmer</a> for the race.
</p><p>Tyre supplier <a href="/wiki/Pirelli" title="Pirelli">Pirelli</a> made the soft, supersoft and ultrasoft tyres available to teams for the race.<sup id="cite_ref-7" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-7">&#91;7&#93;</a></sup> Renault and <a href="/wiki/Haas_F1_Team" title="Haas F1 Team">Haas</a> chose radical tyre allocation strategies, with both teams avoiding the supersoft compound entirely.
</p>
